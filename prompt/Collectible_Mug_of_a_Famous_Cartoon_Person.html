How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for celebrity mug designs, collectible art, or caricature toys in entertainment merch. Preserve "hyper-detailed 3D collectible mug," "[FAMOUS PERSON]," "exaggerated cartoon features," and "Pixar-meets-Clash-Royale style" for whimsical energy. Do not change the glowing rim light or pedestal. Changing person to "Einstein" could yield genius visuals, leading to expressive eyes, while original creates premium, detailed results.

Prompt

A hyper-detailed 3D collectible mug designed as [FAMOUS PERSON], exaggerated cartoon features sculpted into the ceramic, glossy porcelain textures with vibrant colors, Pixar-meets-Clash-Royale style, oversized expressive eyes, surreal whimsical energy, lighting like a product ad with glowing rim light, placed on a dramatic pedestal with mist swirling around, whimsical yet premium collectible design

This prompt is effective due to its hyper-detailed 3D collectible mug design with exaggerated cartoon features the glossy porcelain textures and vibrant colors add fun and premium quality the dramatic pedestal with mist enhances the whimsical yet collectible appeal making the image perfect for merchandise product launches or fan art.
