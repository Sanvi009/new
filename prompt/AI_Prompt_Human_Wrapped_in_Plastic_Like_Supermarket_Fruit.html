"AI Prompt: Human Packaged Like Supermarket Food" | Brainloom

AI Prompts
Published: 20-09-2025
Author: Brainloom

Create a hyperrealistic AI image of a person packaged like supermarket fruit. The human is placed in a red styrofoam tray, wrapped tightly in plastic with water droplets on the surface. A price tag with a barcode is stuck on their forehead, showing a serious expression. With a dark background and dramatic lighting, this prompt creates a surreal, unsettling photo that looks ultra-realistic and cinematic.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a deeply unsettling and thought-provoking piece of AI art by merging the mundane with the macabre. It creates a hyperrealistic image of a human being presented as a commercial product, specifically packaged like supermarket fruit. The creative purpose is to evoke a strong emotional response—discomfort, curiosity, and reflection on themes of consumerism, dehumanization, and the surreal nature of modern life. By taking something utterly familiar, like a styrofoam tray of fruit, and placing a human within it, the prompt forces a cognitive dissonance that is both captivating and disturbing. The inclusion of a specific price, 'FACE CUT – PHP 355.99/K', and a barcode directly on the forehead intensifies this commodification, turning a person into a purchasable item. The serious expression on the subject's face, as opposed to fear or sadness, adds a layer of passive acceptance that makes the scene even more chilling, suggesting a normalization of this absurd and horrifying situation. This prompt falls squarely within the surreal and conceptual AI art category, aiming to create a viral, shareable image that sparks conversation. The promptText is meticulously crafted to guide the AI toward a very specific visual outcome. Every element is a critical instruction. The phrase 'hyperrealistic surreal image' sets the paradoxical goal: the scene must be fantastical in concept but rendered with the fidelity of a photograph. Specifying the 'red styrofoam container' and 'tightly sealed with transparent plastic' grounds the image in a recognizable supermarket aesthetic. The detail of 'water droplets and condensation' is crucial for realism; it mimics the refrigerated environment of a grocery store and adds a layer of tactile texture that makes the plastic wrap feel tangible. The 'supermarket price sticker reading 'FACE CUT – PHP 355.99/K' with a barcode attached to the forehead' is the conceptual anchor, directly applying the language and iconography of product labeling to the human body. The instruction for a 'serious expression' on the subject is a masterstroke of direction, as it avoids caricature and maintains the unsettling calm. Finally, the technical directives—'dramatic dark background, cinematic lighting, tense and unsettling atmosphere, ultra-detailed, 8K photorealistic style'—are essential for the mood and quality. The dark background isolates the subject like a specimen in a display case, while the cinematic lighting sculpts the form and highlights the plastic's sheen and the condensation, creating a professional, high-stakes visual that feels like a still from a psychological thriller. The realism level demanded by '8K photorealistic' ensures that every pore, every reflection on the plastic, and every shadow is rendered with excruciating detail, which is precisely what makes the surreal concept so believable and impactful. Composition is implied to be tight and focused, centered on the tray and the subject's face, making the viewer confront the image directly. Certain elements within this prompt are non-negotiable for maintaining its core unsettling effect. The 'red styrofoam tray' is a classic, almost archetypal symbol of supermarket packaging; changing it would alter the familiar context. The 'plastic wrap and condensation' are the primary signifiers of the 'packaged' state and must be kept for maximum realism, as they are the physical barrier between the subject and the viewer. The 'barcode and price sticker' are the semantic heart of the consumerism critique; removing them would weaken the concept. The 'serious expression' is also vital, as a different emotion would change the narrative from one of disturbing compliance to one of overt horror or comedy. However, the promptVariations offer safe and creative avenues for modification. Changing the 'red styrofoam tray' to a 'clear plastic container' shifts the aesthetic to something more modern and sterile, like high-end or prepared food packaging, which could make the image feel colder and more clinical. Moving the 'barcode sticker on forehead' to a 'label across the chest' subtly alters the focal point and can suggest different forms of product labeling, like a price tag on a piece of meat. Switching the 'serious expression' to a 'blank expression' can make the subject seem even more detached and object-like, potentially increasing the sense of unease. Adding a background of 'bright supermarket shelves' completely transforms the context from a dark, dramatic studio shot to a jarringly commercial environment, making the scene feel like a product display gone horribly wrong, which can be a powerful variation. To improve accuracy and visual quality, it's critical to start with a high-quality, well-lit source photo of the person. The AI needs clear data on facial structure, skin tone, and texture to seamlessly integrate them into the surreal scene. When writing the prompt, ensure the keywords are strongly emphasized. A common mistake is to rely on the AI to invent the person, but this prompt is designed for face-swapping or inpainting based on an uploaded photo. Without that source, the result will be a generic, AI-generated face, which loses the personal and jarring connection of seeing a 'real' person in this situation. Another pitfall is neglecting the lighting keywords; omitting 'cinematic lighting' or 'dramatic dark background' might result in a flat, brightly lit image that looks more like a silly photoshop job than a piece of unsettling surrealist art. The tension and atmosphere are built entirely on the contrast between the clinical lighting of the subject and the void-like background, so maintaining those directives is key to a successful, high-impact generation.

The Prompt 

A hyperrealistic surreal image of a human ("photo") packaged like supermarket fruit. The human is placed inside a red styrofoam container and tightly sealed with transparent plastic, with water droplets and condensation visible on the surface. A supermarket price sticker reading 'FACE CUT – PHP 355.99/K' with a barcode is attached to the forehead, with a serious expression. Dramatic dark background, cinematic lighting, tense and unsettling atmosphere, ultra-detailed, 8K photorealistic style.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload a photo of the person you want to use. The AI will place them inside a supermarket-style fruit package with plastic wrap and a price sticker. You can edit the price tag text, barcode, or packaging color to customize the look, but keep the plastic wrap and condensation for maximum realism.

Why This Prompt Works

It instantly grabs attention because it’s weird, disturbing, and realistic at the same time. The supermarket packaging makes it feel familiar, but putting a human inside it makes it unsettling and thought-provoking. That mix of normal and surreal is what makes people stop, stare, and share.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “red styrofoam tray” → “clear plastic container” for a more modern packaging look.
  • Replace “barcode sticker on forehead” → “label across the chest” for variety.
  • Switch “serious expression” → “blank expression” to change the mood.
  • Add “bright supermarket shelves in background” instead of dark background for a more commercial vibe.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is this meant to look creepy or funny?
    It leans toward dark and unsettling, but you can lighten it up by changing the text on the price tag or adding a playful background.
  • Do I need to add the barcode and price sticker?
    Not necessarily. The image works even without them, but the sticker makes it feel more like real supermarket packaging, which sells the surreal effect.
