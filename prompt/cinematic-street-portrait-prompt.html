Prompt Explanation

This prompt is expertly designed to generate a sophisticated, ultra-realistic street portrait that mimics the look of professional DSLR photography. Its creative purpose is to capture a moment of profound stillness and introspection amidst the chaos of a bustling city, creating a powerful visual metaphor for focus and individuality. The core concept is a study in contrasts: the sharp, detailed stillness of the subject against the streaked, unfocused motion of the passing crowd. This technique, reminiscent of long-exposure photography, elevates a simple street scene into a dramatic, emotional, and highly stylized portrait, perfect for personal avatars, artistic projects, or conceptual photography. The 'promptText' is a masterclass in directing an AI towards a specific photographic outcome. It begins with the crucial instruction for facial consistency: 'exact same facial and physical appearance and structure from the uploaded image.' This grounds the image in a specific person, making the result deeply personal. The prompt then establishes the core dynamic: 'standing still in a crowded city street, sharp focus on my face with calm, intense expression' juxtaposed with a 'long-exposure effect with people moving around my creating strong directional motion blur.' This clearly delineates what should be static and sharp (the subject) versus what should be dynamic and blurred (the crowd). The use of 'strong directional motion blur' and 'streaked crowd movement' is particularly effective, as it guides the AI to create a realistic sense of speed and direction in the blur, rather than just a generic fuzziness. The technical photography terms that follow—'shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh, DSLR quality, 85mm lens look, f/1.8, high dynamic range, 8K, professional color grading'—are the prompt's stylistic engine. They tell the AI to emulate the specific characteristics of a portrait taken with a fast prime lens on a high-end camera: a very narrow focal plane that isolates the subject, a smooth, aesthetically pleasing background blur (bokeh), and a wide dynamic range that captures detail in both shadows and highlights. Descriptors like 'realistic skin texture, subtle freckles, neutral makeup' push the AI towards photorealism and away from a smooth, idealized, or illustrated look. The clothing—'dark winter coat and scarf'—is simple and classic, providing a solid, non-distracting visual anchor that fits the 'introspective, urban storytelling' mood. The final instruction, 'do according to my gender,' ensures the AI correctly applies the reference image's characteristics. The interplay between the subject and the environment is the heart of this prompt's visual impact. The 'natural soft daylight' provides even, flattering illumination that prevents harsh shadows from interfering with the detailed skin texture. The 'shallow depth of field' and '85mm lens look' are critical for compressing the perspective, making the background crowd feel closer and more immersive while keeping the subject sharply separated. The 'long-exposure effect' is not just a visual trick; it's a narrative device. The blurred, streaked crowd represents the relentless, impersonal flow of city life, while the subject's stillness and sharp focus represent individuality, contemplation, and a moment of pause. This creates a powerful, emotional contrast that is central to the prompt's 'urban storytelling photography' goal. The elements that must not be changed are the core dynamic of a sharp, still subject versus a motion-blurred background, the use of a reference image for the subject's face, and the overall aim for ultra-realistic, DSLR-quality output. Altering the motion blur effect, for example, would collapse the entire conceptual foundation of the image. However, the prompt is designed with considerable flexibility to explore different moods and settings. The lighting is a primary variable: changing 'natural soft daylight' to 'neon night lights' would transform the mood from introspective to gritty and atmospheric, with the motion blur capturing colorful light trails instead of anonymous figures. Swapping 'golden hour sunset' for the daylight would introduce a warm, romantic, and melancholic tone. The location is also adaptable; a 'busy train station' or a 'festival crowd' would create different types of motion blur and environmental context, shifting the urban storytelling narrative. The subject's expression can be modified from 'calm, intense' to 'smiling warmly' or 'looking away thoughtfully,' which would alter the emotional connection with the viewer. Even the clothing and lens choice can be adjusted to fit different seasons or stylistic preferences, such as changing the '85mm lens' to a '35mm lens' for a wider environmental portrait that shows more of the street context. To achieve optimal results, users must follow the 'howToInstructions' carefully. Uploading a clear, front-facing reference photo is non-negotiable for face consistency. In Midjourney, while the prompt doesn't specify parameters, using `--ar 9:16` is essential for the vertical portrait format. Adding `--style raw` and `--v 6` or higher would further enhance the photographic realism. In Stable Diffusion, using a realistic checkpoint model and enabling face restoration are key. A common mistake is not being specific enough about the 'directional' nature of the motion blur, which can result in a chaotic, non-sensical blur. Another pitfall is overemphasizing the crowd, causing the AI to generate distinct faces in the background, which would ruin the long-exposure effect. By meticulously combining a personal reference with precise photographic terminology, this prompt consistently generates a stunning, ultra-realistic street portrait that tells a compelling visual story of an individual's quiet moment amidst the rush of urban life.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic cinematic street portrait of me with exact same facial and physical appearance and structure from the uploaded image . standing still in a crowded city street, sharp focus on my face with calm, intense expression. Long-exposure effect with people moving around my creating strong directional motion blur, streaked crowd movement while the subject remains perfectly still. Natural soft daylight, shallow depth of field, creamy bokeh, realistic skin texture, subtle freckles, neutral makeup dark winter coat and scarf. Emotional, introspective mood, urban storytelling photography, DSLR quality, 85mm lens look, f/1.8, high dynamic range, 8K, professional color grading. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the entire prompt text. In your AI image generator, upload a clear reference photo of a face first. Then paste the prompt. Adjust the clothing description (like 'dark winter coat') to match your subject if needed. The generator will use your uploaded image to create a realistic street portrait with motion blur.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it uses specific photography terms like '85mm lens', 'f/1.8', and 'long-exposure effect' that AI models recognize for style. 'Sharp focus on face' and 'subject remains perfectly still' tells the AI where to put detail, while 'motion blur' and 'streaked crowd' create contrast. Mentioning 'realistic skin texture' and 'DSLR quality' pushes the AI toward a photorealistic result instead of an illustration.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'natural soft daylight' to 'neon night lights' or 'golden hour sunset' for different moods.
  • Swap 'crowded city street' for 'busy train station' or 'festival crowd'.
  • Replace 'calm, intense expression' with 'smiling warmly' or 'looking away thoughtfully'.
  • Change 'dark winter coat' to 'leather jacket' or 'hoodie' for a different season.
  • Use '50mm lens' or '35mm lens' instead of '85mm lens' for a slightly wider field of view.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3 all work well. Use the 'upload image' feature for the character reference.
  • What kind of photo should I upload for the best result?
    Upload a clear, well-lit front-facing portrait photo. The AI will copy the facial structure, so a good quality photo gives a better match.
  • Can I use this prompt without uploading an image?
    Yes, but the AI will generate a random person's face. To get 'exact same facial appearance', you must upload a reference image.
