How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, well-lit reference photo of your face and body to your AI image generator. 2. Copy the full prompt and paste it into the tool. 3. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 or Portrait. 4. For best results with facial preservation, use Midjourney with image reference or Stable Diffusion with face swap/extensions. 5. The multiple "no change" instructions emphasize to the AI that your exact features must be maintained.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it contains multiple redundant instructions reinforcing the same requirement: preserve the exact facial and physical identity. Terms like "skull geometry," "jawline," "stubble," and "bone structure" give the AI specific anatomical features to lock onto. The contrast between the ultra-realistic preservation request and the retro styling (windbreaker, boombox) creates an interesting visual tension. The studio background keeps focus on the subject.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the windbreaker to a leather jacket or vintage denim vest.
  • Replace the boombox with a vintage camera or retro cassette player.
  • Adjust the studio background color to match the windbreaker.
  • Add period-appropriate props like vinyl records or neon lights.
  • Modify the pose to holding the boombox at hip level or both hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure the AI preserves my exact facial features?
    Use a high-quality reference photo and an AI tool with strong image reference capabilities. The prompt includes multiple preservation instructions: 'exact same facial and physical identity,' 'preserve original skull geometry,' 'identical jawline and stubble,' and multiple 'no change' commands. This redundancy helps guide the AI.
  • What does 'no age shift' mean in this prompt?
    It instructs the AI not to make you look older or younger than in your reference photo. Some AI generators tend to smooth skin or change perceived age. This term prevents that, ensuring your exact age appearance is maintained.
  • Why specify 'skull geometry' and 'bone structure'?
    These anatomical terms give the AI specific aspects of your face to preserve beyond just surface features. They help maintain the underlying structure that makes your face uniquely yours, preventing the AI from altering proportions while adding the retro styling.
  • What's the best AI generator for this prompt?
    Midjourney with strong image reference weights or Stable Diffusion with face swap extensions like Roop/Reactor work best. DALL-E 3 may struggle with precise facial preservation. The prompt is optimized for tools that can maintain identity while changing clothing and adding props.
  • Will this work if my reference photo isn't in a studio?
    Yes, the prompt adds 'studio background' regardless of your original background. The AI will replace the background while preserving you. A clear, well-lit reference photo with good visibility of your face and body shape will yield the best results.
