The Prompt 

A highly stylized, dramatic top-down editorial portrait of me with exact same facial and physical apperance from the uploaded image standing alone at the center of the frame. i gazes upward directly into the camera with a calm, curious, and thoughtful expression. The background is a clean, seamless gradient with smooth tonal transitions. Soft studio lighting creates a focused circular spotlight around me, with gentle falloff and controlled shadows i wears a brown overshirt layered over a light knit sweater, paired with dark trousers and casual minimalist shoes. Shot with a wide-angle lens that introduces subtle perspective distortion, emphasizing isolation and depth. Modern fashion editorial photography style, high detail, sharp focus, cinematic mood, professional studio quality. do according to my gender , aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create an editorial fashion portrait with top-down perspective. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3 for professional photography styles.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt includes professional photography terms ('top-down editorial portrait', 'seamless gradient background', 'circular spotlight') that AI models recognize from fashion photography training data. 'Wide-angle lens with subtle perspective distortion' creates the distinctive editorial look while 'exact same facial appearance' ensures likeness accuracy.

Prompt Variations

  • Change to colored gradient background (blue to purple)
  • Try different lighting (rim light, split lighting, backlight)
  • Create monochrome version with high contrast black and white
  • Make it street style with urban background instead of gradient
  • Change outfit to formal suit or evening dress for different fashion style

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What's the best reference photo for this prompt?
    Use a clear headshot or upper body photo with good lighting. The AI will adapt it to the top-down perspective and outfit described.
  • Can I change the clothing colors?
    Yes, replace 'brown overshirt' and 'light knit sweater' with your preferred colors and clothing items for different fashion looks.
  • How do I make the spotlight effect stronger?
    Change 'soft studio lighting' to 'dramatic spotlight' or 'strong circular pool of light' for more intense lighting effects.
