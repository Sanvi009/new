Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a high-end, hyper-realistic cinematic fashion portrait that incorporates a complex visual effect known as ghost duplicates or time-slice motion. Its creative purpose is to merge the precision of luxury editorial photography with a dynamic, almost surreal sense of movement, resulting in an image that feels both static and fluid. The subject is the undeniable focal point, rendered with razor-sharp clarity, while four symmetric blurred duplicates on either side suggest a fleeting passage through time, adding a layer of narrative and artistic depth that standard portraits lack. The instruction for a waist-up composition against a muted teal studio background ensures that the viewer's attention remains fixed on the subject and the sophisticated interplay of sharpness and blur, rather than being distracted by a busy environment. This specific backdrop also provides a perfect color contrast to the all-black attire, making the clothing and the subject's silhouette pop with a refined, premium aesthetic. The prompt_text guides the AI by establishing a hierarchy of importance. The phrase 'exact facial and physical identity from the uploaded image' is paramount, instructing the model to treat the reference photo as the primary source of truth for likeness. This is followed by a cascade of descriptive modifiers that build the scene: the 'slim lean build' suggests a particular physique that complements the structured clothing. The detailed outfit description—'luxury all-black suit, black shirt (top buttons open), structured black coat, thin black chain, round dark sunglasses'—is not just a list but a series of visual cues that signal wealth, style, and a carefully curated, undone formality. The open buttons and the thin chain are crucial details that prevent the look from becoming too rigid or somber, injecting a touch of modern, fashion-forward nonchalance. The pose, 'hands in pockets, calm intense gaze into camera,' conveys a sense of controlled power and confidence, which is essential for a luxury portrait. The technical photographic instructions are equally vital. 'Cinematic studio lighting' sets a professional, controlled mood, while 'shallow DOF' and '85mm look' are specific lens and camera effect requests that tell the AI to mimic the compression and beautiful bokeh of a classic portrait lens, ensuring the background and duplicates remain subordinate to the main figure. The request for 'ultra-sharp eyes' is a critical detail that adds life and intensity to the central subject, a non-negotiable element in high-end portraiture. The lighting in this prompt is the invisible architect of the mood. It must be soft enough to flatter and create a smooth, editorial finish, yet directional enough to sculpt the subject's features and cast subtle shadows that define the form. This lighting logic ensures the subject stands out three-dimensionally from the teal background. The proportion and symmetry are explicitly dictated by the 'symmetric blurred ghost duplicates (2 left, 2 right).' This is a high-risk, high-reward element; the AI must understand not just to create duplicates, but to place them in perfect balance on either side, with a gradient of blur that suggests forward or backward motion. The materiality of the clothing is another layer where the prompt_text does heavy lifting. Words like 'structured coat' and 'thin black chain' ask the AI to render fabric with body and weight, and metal with a fine, delicate reflectivity. The realism level is set to 'hyper-realistic,' which pushes the AI to render skin texture, the sheen of the sunglasses, and the weave of the suit fabric with photographic accuracy. The composition is centered and waist-up, creating a powerful, iconic frame that is both intimate and commanding. The elements that must NOT be changed are the core identifiers of the subject's identity and the fundamental structure of the effect. Altering the facial features or swapping the symmetric ghost duplicates for random placements would break the entire concept. The specific count of 'four' duplicates is also crucial; changing this number would unbalance the symmetry. However, several elements can be safely modified to create variations on this theme. The promptVariations section offers excellent alternatives, such as changing the number of duplicates to three in a different arrangement, or swapping the blurred effect for color gradients, which would shift the aesthetic from motion to a more graphic, pop-art feel. Changing the outfit from a luxury suit to streetwear would completely transform the subject's persona from a high-fashion model to an urban style icon, altering the target audience and mood. The background could be changed to solid white or black for a more minimalist, classic studio look, though this would reduce the sophisticated color contrast provided by the teal. To improve accuracy and visual quality, the most important practical tip is to use a high-resolution reference photo with even, front-facing lighting. The AI needs a clear map of the face to replicate it. In Midjourney, using a high image weight parameter (--iw 2) will force the AI to prioritize the reference image over the prompt's description of the person, which is exactly what you want. For the ghost duplicates, if the AI struggles, a technique is to generate the best possible central figure first, and then use an inpainting or outpainting tool to manually add and blend the duplicates, or composite them in Photoshop. A common mistake is to use a low-quality or poorly lit reference photo, which will result in a distorted or generic face. Another mistake is not specifying the aspect ratio as 9:16, which is essential for the vertical, mobile-friendly, magazine-cover composition this prompt aims for. Users also often forget to emphasize the 'blurred' nature of the duplicates, leading to sharp clones that look like a glitch rather than an artistic choice. The instructions to 'maintain the look from the attached photo without distortions' and to focus on 'skin texture' are critical, as they directly combat the AI's tendency to default to idealized, airbrushed versions of faces, which would ruin the hyper-realistic intent. Ultimately, this prompt is a masterclass in using precise, layered language to command an AI to execute a complex, multi-faceted artistic vision that blends technical photographic knowledge with high-fashion creativity.

The Prompt 

Hyper-realistic cinematic fashion portrait of me with exact facial and physical identity from the uploaded image , slim lean build, waist-up, centered on muted teal studio background. Luxury all-black suit, black shirt (top buttons open), structured black coat, thin black chain, round dark sunglasses. Hands in pockets, calm intense gaze into camera. Razor-sharp central subject with four symmetric blurred ghost duplicates (2 left, 2 right) creating a time-slice motion effect. Cinematic studio lighting, shallow DOF, 85mm look, ultra-sharp eyes. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear photo of your face. 2. Copy the full prompt into Midjourney, DALL·E, or Stable Diffusion. 3. Set aspect ratio to 9:16 (--ar 9:16 in Midjourney). 4. Generate. The ghost duplicate effect works best in Midjourney V6 or Stable Diffusion with composition-aware generation. 5. Upscale the central subject for maximum detail.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt introduces a sophisticated, difficult-to-execute effect (symmetric ghost duplicates) with clear specifications (2 left, 2 right, blurred, time-slice). The contrast between razor-sharp central subject and soft duplicates creates depth and narrative. Luxury fashion keywords (structured coat, thin chain, all-black suit) signal premium aesthetic. The teal background provides sophisticated color contrast to black clothing.

Prompt Variations

  • Three duplicates only (one left, one right, one above)
  • Color gradient duplicates instead of blurred
  • Streetwear outfit instead of luxury suit
  • Solid white or black background minimal
  • Profile view with duplicates trailing behind
  • Evening gown version for female-presenting subjects
  • Gold chain and accessories instead of black

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a 'time-slice motion effect'?
    It's a photographic effect where multiple moments are combined into one frame, showing the subject in slightly different positions. Here it's interpreted as four blurred duplicate images flanking the sharp central subject, suggesting movement through time.
  • Will all AI tools generate the ghost duplicates correctly?
    Midjourney V6 and Stable Diffusion with good prompt adherence can produce this effect. DALL·E 3 may struggle with exact symmetry and count. You may need multiple generations or Photoshop compositing for perfect results.
  • What does 'buttons open' add to the look?
    It adds a relaxed, slightly undone element to an otherwise formal all-black suit. This creates visual interest and a fashion-forward, less rigid silhouette.
  • Why round dark sunglasses indoors?
    They contribute to the luxury editorial aesthetic, add mystery, and create a distinctive silhouette. Round frames contrast with the sharp lines of the structured coat.
  • What is 'shallow DOF' and why include it?
    Shallow depth of field keeps the central subject sharp while blurring the ghost duplicates and background slightly, emphasizing the main figure. It mimics high-end fashion photography.
