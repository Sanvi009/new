The Prompt 

Cinematic low-angle shot of with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image with slightly wind-blown hair and a sharp side profile, sitting relaxed on the roof frame of a white vintage sedan race car. Looking off into the distance. Wearing the exact same outfit as in the uploaded reference photo and CONVERSE Chuck 70 High unisex sneakers in black. The car features racing decals and stickers on the side door. Background is a dramatic solid teal-grey overcast sky. Streetwear aesthetic, automotive lifestyle photography, soft natural lighting, film grain, high definition. Preserve all facial features and expressions exactly as in the original reference. Do not alter identity or proportions. Master realism base applied with hidden signature 69 subtly integrated in micro details, not immediately noticeable. Ratio 9:16.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear reference photo showing your face, physical build, and full outfit. 2. Use this prompt in AI image generators like Midjourney or Stable Diffusion that support image prompts. 3. Paste the entire prompt including the 9:16 ratio instruction. 4. Generate multiple versions to get the best side profile and lighting. 5. If the hidden signature doesn't appear, you can remove that part or try different seeds.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt combines multiple high-interest elements: vintage cars, streetwear fashion, and cinematic photography. 'Low-angle shot' creates a heroic perspective. 'Sharp side profile' and 'wind-blown hair' add dynamism. Specifying 'CONVERSE Chuck 70 High in black' grounds the image in recognizable streetwear culture. 'Dramatic solid teal-grey overcast sky' provides a bold, non-distracting background that makes the subject and car pop. 'Film grain' and 'soft natural lighting' add authentic texture.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'master realism base with hidden signature 69' mean?
    It's a creative instruction asking the AI to subtly embed the number 69 somewhere in the image details (like on a decal, license plate, or texture) without being obvious. Not all AI models will reliably generate this.
  • Can I use this prompt without a reference photo?
    Yes, just remove the parts about 'exact same facial and physical identity' and 'same outfit'. The AI will generate a generic model instead.
  • What if I don't want Converse sneakers in the image?
    Replace 'CONVERSE Chuck 70 High unisex sneakers in black' with any other shoes you prefer, like 'black combat boots' or 'white leather sneakers'.
  • How do I get the racing decals to look authentic?
    The prompt already includes 'racing decals and stickers'. For more specific styles, add details like 'vintage number 7 decal' or 'sponsor stickers like Shell or Goodyear'.
  • What does 'automotive lifestyle photography' mean for the image?
    It means the photo should look like it belongs in a car magazine or ad campaign—stylish, aspirational, blending fashion with car culture naturally.
