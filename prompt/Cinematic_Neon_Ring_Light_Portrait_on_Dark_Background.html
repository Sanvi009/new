How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it in portrait photography, book covers, or motivational posters for dramatic and minimalist themes. Use generators by replacing "[young man/woman]" and "[dark jacket/high neck/coat]" with "young woman" and "high neck," keeping "cinematic portrait," "glowing neon ring," "dark, soft gradient background," and "dramatic and somber atmosphere" intact. Do not alter the side profile or edge light. Changing colors to "blue and purple" could produce sci-fi visuals, leading to shadowy results, while original warm tones might create introspective art with powerful, emotional outcomes.

Prompt

A cinematic portrait of a [young man/woman] with a side profile, wearing a [dark jacket/high neck/coat] illuminated by a glowing neon ring behind their heads. The background is a dark, soft gradient background with warm golden orange and yellow... Lighting creates a strong edge light around the head and shoulders, while some faces remain in the shadow. The image has a dramatic and somber atmosphere — clean, minimalist, powerful.

Why this prompt works

This prompt works by using dramatic lighting and a minimalist composition to create a powerful cinematic portrait the glowing neon ring behind the head provides a strong edge light that highlights the profile while keeping some faces in shadow adding mystery and depth the dark soft gradient background with warm golden orange and yellow tones creates a somber yet striking atmosphere this style is perfect for album covers promotional posters or artistic portraits that aim to convey emotion and intensity with a clean modern aesthetic.
