How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, incorporate it into food and beverage marketing, lifestyle blogs, or cultural cuisine promotions for East African themes. Use generative AI by separating the food and drink prompts, keeping details like "golden-brown mandazi," "spiced chai," "weathered Swahili door," and camera specs such as "f/2.2" and "Leica M11 realism" intact. Do not change the morning light, condensation, or macro elements. For example, emphasizing "mandazi" could result in bakery ads with textured dough visuals, while focusing on "coffee" might create moody cafe shots, offering versatile content for social media or menu designs.

Prompt

Food Prompt: Lifestyle shot of golden-brown mandazi (Swahili donuts) piled on a brass tray, powdered sugar dusted unevenly. A glass cup of spiced chai sits beside it, cinnamon sticks and cardamom pods floating atop. Background: a weathered Swahili door with peeling paint. Morning light spills across the scene, casting delicate shadows. Shot at f/2.2 to blur the background, with condensation on the chai glass. Leica M11 realism. .Drink Prompt: Extreme close-up of a freshly brewed cup of Kenyan AA coffee in a handmade clay mug. Crema swirls on the surface, with a coffee bean floating. Side elements: a burlap sack of beans spilling open, a vintage grinder. Dark moody lighting, single spotlight (f/4.5). Shot with a macro lens to capture steam curls and oil sheen.
