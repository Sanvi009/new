Prompt Explanation

This prompt is crafted to produce a striking, narrative-driven portrait that places the subject within a dark fantasy or supernatural scene. Its creative purpose is to merge photorealism with cinematic horror, creating an image where the viewer is both a character and a canvas for ethereal elements. The prompt_text guides the AI by establishing a clear three-layer composition: the grounded, realistic subject in the foreground, a supernatural entity in the middle ground, and a thematic background. The foundation is the instruction to preserve the exact facial and physical features, including hairstyle, from the uploaded image. This ensures the portrait remains personal and identifiable. The subject's pose is specified as a confident, one-sided stance with a slight look toward the camera, which adds a layer of narrative engagement. The core creative element is the semi-transparent shadow entity made of swirling black smoke with glowing white eyes, which must emerge from behind and wrap naturally around the body, blending into the floor. This instruction dictates the interaction between the real and the supernatural, making the entity feel integrated into the scene rather than pasted on top. The lighting is described as moody and cinematic, with high contrast, which is essential for creating the dramatic, shadowy atmosphere that defines the genre. The black-and-white ghost-themed background further reinforces the mood and ensures the focus remains on the high-contrast interaction between the subject and the smoke entity. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's core identity—their face, physical build, and hairstyle. The AI must treat the reference photo as the immutable source for the person in the scene. Elements that can be safely modified are extensive, as shown in the promptVariations. The background can be changed to more specific locations like an abandoned mansion or foggy graveyard to enhance the narrative. The entity's eyes can be given different colors, and its form can be altered from smoke to ethereal wisps or crackling energy. The subject's pose can also be changed to convey different emotional states, from defensive crouching to ethereal floating. The color palette can be shifted from black and white to desaturated colors or a single accent color for a different visual impact. Practical tips for accuracy involve using a clear, well-lit reference photo and ensuring the AI tool used has strong image reference capabilities. To improve visual quality, emphasizing the 8k ultra-realistic and high-contrast requirements is key. A common mistake to avoid is making the entity too opaque, which would obscure the subject, or too faint, making it disappear into the background. The prompt's phrasing, let the smoke wrap naturally, is crucial for guiding the AI to create a believable interaction. This prompt effectively integrates keywords like ultra-realistic, cinematic, dark fantasy, and ghost portrait, transforming them from simple descriptors into a cohesive and compelling visual story.

The Prompt 

Create a 8k portrait ultra-realistic dark, cinematic ghost fantasy scene photography of me exact same facial and pysical features from uploaded image with same hair style. me standing confidently one side pose with looking slightly at the camera. Add a semi-transparent shadow entity made of swirling black smoke with glowing white eyes emerging from behind. Let the smoke wrap naturally around the body and blend into the floor. Use a black-and-white ghost-themed background, high contrast, moody cinematic lighting, ultra-realistic. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the full prompt text. 3. For best results, adjust the prompt to specify your desired gender (e.g., 'man' or 'woman') if not implied. 4. Use the 9:16 aspect ratio for a vertical portrait. 5. Generate the image and refine with variations if needed.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt combines specific technical requests ('8k ultra-realistic', 'high contrast') with strong stylistic keywords ('cinematic', 'moody lighting', 'black-and-white') that AI models recognize. It clearly layers the subject, the supernatural smoke entity, and the background, giving the AI a structured scene to build. Keywords like 'swirling black smoke' and 'glowing white eyes' provide clear visual anchors for the generator.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'ghost-themed background' to 'abandoned mansion hallway' or 'foggy graveyard' for a stronger setting.
  • Try 'blue or green glowing eyes' instead of white for a different eerie effect.
  • Replace 'standing confidently' with 'crouching defensively' or 'floating ethereally' for a different pose.
  • Swap 'black and white' for 'desaturated color with a single accent color' on the eyes.
  • Change the entity from 'smoke' to 'ethereal wisps', 'crackling energy', or 'tendrils of darkness'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I use my own face in this AI ghost portrait?
    Most AI image generators have an 'upload image' feature. Use a clear, well-lit frontal photo of yourself as a reference image, then use this prompt.
  • Which AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    This prompt works well in Midjourney for artistic style, DALL-E 3 for prompt understanding, and Stable Diffusion with proper checkpoints for photorealism.
  • Can I get a color version of this ghost portrait?
    Yes. Simply remove the 'black-and-white' phrase from the prompt or change it to 'desaturated colors' or 'cool color palette'.
