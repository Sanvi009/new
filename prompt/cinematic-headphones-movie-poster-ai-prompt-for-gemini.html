How to Use

This prompt is engineered for a premium, high-drama close-up suitable for a film poster. For the best results in Gemini, upload a high-resolution reference photo with clear, well-lit eyes and neutral expression. The AI will map your exact facial identity onto the composition. The Audeze CRBN2 headphones are a specific, high-end electrostatic model with a distinctive open-back grille and suspension headband. If the AI renders a generic pair of headphones, add more specific descriptors: `Audeze CRBN2 electrostatic headphones with visible silver mesh grille and carbon fiber headband.` The 'intense black background' and 'high contrast' lighting are designed to make the subject emerge from shadow. If the result is too dark and obscures the face, adjust the lighting cue to `dramatic Rembrandt lighting` or `single key light from the side creating a triangle of light on the shadowed cheek.` The 85mm lens and 4:5 ratio are locked for the poster aesthetic; do not change the aspect ratio.

FAQ

  • The Audeze CRBN2 headphones don't look right. They look like generic gaming headphones. How can I get the exact model?
    The Audeze CRBN2 is a very specific electrostatic headphone with a distinct visual profile: a large circular ear cup, a visible silver mesh grille on the outside, and a thin carbon fiber suspension headband. To improve accuracy, add these physical descriptors to the prompt: `wearing Audeze CRBN2 headphones with large circular black ear cups, visible silver electrostatic mesh grille, and a thin carbon fiber headband.` The more visual cues you provide about the geometry and materials, the less the AI will default to a generic 'black headphone' shape. If absolute accuracy is required for a product shot, you may need to use a reference image of the headphones themselves or inpaint them in post-production.
  • The 'intense black background' is swallowing my hair and shoulders. The image looks like a floating head. How do I ground it?
    This is a common issue with `emerging from black` prompts. The AI needs a subtle gradient or edge light to define the transition between subject and void. Add a rim light instruction: `A very subtle, soft back rim light separates the shoulders and hair from the pure black background.` Alternatively, change `intense black background` to `deep charcoal void with a faint, almost imperceptible vignette.` This provides just enough tonal separation for the AI to render the contours of the neck and clothing without breaking the dark, dramatic poster aesthetic.
  • I want to use this for an actual short film poster. Can I add my film's title?
    Yes, you can replace the generic 'movie poster' instruction with your specific title. Change `create a movie poster` to `Create a movie poster titled 'MIDNIGHT FREQUENCY' in bold red serif font at the bottom.` However, AI text rendering is often unreliable for perfect typography. For a professional film poster, the best workflow is to use this prompt to generate the stunning, high-contrast 8K portrait of you with the headphones. Then, import that image into Canva, Photoshop, or Photopea. You can overlay your perfect vector title, tagline, and billing block there. This guarantees a polished, festival-ready poster while keeping the AI's beautiful portrait rendering intact.
