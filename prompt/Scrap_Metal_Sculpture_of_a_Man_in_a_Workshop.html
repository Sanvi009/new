How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for sculpture art, recycling awareness campaigns, or industrial design exhibits in galleries and environmental education. Apply it with uploaded photos in AI tools, preserving "hyper-realistic life-size sculpture," "waste scrap metal pieces," "old garage workshop," and "realistic" style for gritty authenticity. Do not change the outfit from the image or dim sunlight. Optionally vary metal types; for example, adding more "gears" could yield steampunk visuals, resulting in intricate details, whereas emphasizing "chains" might create rugged industrial art with textured, narrative effects.

Prompt

Hyper-realistic photograph of a life-size sculpture of a man, in uploaded photo, entirely crafted from waste scrap metal pieces—gears,pipes,rusted chains,bolts, and sheet metal—assembled with intricate detailing to capture the facial features of uploaded photo,wearing outfit shown in image.The sculpture is set inside an old garage workshop,surrounded by piles of metal parts,welding tools,and dim,dusty sunlight filtering through broken windows. Realistic

Why this prompt works

This prompt is effective due to its creative reuse of materials and hyper-realistic approach crafting a man’s sculpture from waste scrap metal tells a story of recycling and artistry the setting in an old garage workshop with dim dusty sunlight adds a gritty authentic atmosphere that enhances the realism the requirement to capture facial features and outfit from a photo ensures personalization and accuracy this image can serve as powerful art for environmental campaigns portfolio pieces or unique decorative art that sparks conversation.

Prompt Variations

  • Material: Swap “waste scrap metal pieces” → “discarded electronic components (circuit boards, wires)” for a cyberpunk sculpture. Swap “waste scrap metal” → “reclaimed wood and nails” for a rustic sculpture.
  • Subject Outfit: Swap “wearing outfit shown in image” → “wearing a sculpted suit of armor made from scrap”.
  • Setting: Swap “old garage workshop” → “a post-apocalyptic junkyard” for an outdoor scene. Swap “old garage workshop” → “a modern art gallery” for a contrasting setting.
  • Lighting: Swap “dim, dusty sunlight” → “a single bright spotlight” for a gallery effect. Swap “sunlight” → “the glow from a welding torch” for a dramatic in-action shot.
