Cinematic Meadow Portrait AI Prompt for Realistic Self Image

AI Image Prompts 2026-04-22 Brainloom

This prompt creates an ultra-realistic cinematic photograph of a person lying in a wild meadow, preserving exact facial identity and natural features. The scene captures a serene, melancholic moment with the subject reclining in tall grass, eyes closed, wearing a slightly disheveled formal suit with a loosened tie. A small wildflower rests delicately on the cheek, with subtle traces of nature like pollen and grass fragments adding authenticity. The lighting is soft and diffused, mimicking overcast or golden-hour conditions, while the color grading leans into muted, desaturated earthy tones with a fine film grain texture. The shallow depth of field keeps the subject sharp against a softly blurred background, creating a poetic, introspective still that feels like a frame from an indie film. The prompt explicitly restricts facial alteration, artificial beautification, and studio lighting to ensure the result looks like a genuine, unposed photograph of the specific individual.

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic cinematic photograph of a person (replace with my exact face, gender, facial structure, proportions, and skin tone — strict identity preservation). Do not beautify or stylize in a way that alters identity. Preserve natural asymmetries, pores, skin texture, eye shape, nose structure, lips, and all unique facial features. The result must look exactly like me captured in a real photograph, not an interpretation or idealization.

A serene, melancholic outdoor scene in a wild meadow filled with tall, soft grass and small wildflowers. The subject is lying on their back, slightly reclined in the grass, with their head gently tilted to one side. Eyes closed, expression calm, introspective, almost dreamlike — conveying stillness and quiet emotion.
Camera angle: slightly above eye level, medium close-up framing (chest and head visible). The subject is centered but organically integrated into the environment, partially surrounded by grass and flowers.
The subject wears a slightly disheveled formal outfit:
Black or very dark suit jacket (slightly wrinkled, natural wear)
White dress shirt, subtly wrinkled and imperfect (not pristine)
Black tie, loosened and slightly off-center
The styling should feel natural, as if the subject has been lying there for a while — not staged.
Detalles clave (muy importante):
A small wildflower (like a daisy) rests delicately on the subject's cheek
A few blades of grass lightly touch the face and clothing
Subtle traces of nature (tiny dirt marks, pollen, grass fragments) on skin and clothes

Iluminación:
Soft, diffused natural light — overcast or golden-hour muted lighting. No harsh shadows.
Light should feel warm but desaturated, giving a nostalgic, cinematic tone.
Skin tones must remain realistic and true-to-life.
Color grading y estilo visual:
Muted, earthy tones — greens, browns, soft yellows.
Low saturation, film-like color grading.
Slight vintage cinematic look (similar to analog film photography).
Subtle grain, very fine texture (film grain effect).
Profundidad de campo:
Shallow depth of field — subject in sharp focus, background softly blurred (bokeh).
Foreground elements (grass/flowers) slightly out of focus to add depth.
Lente y configuración simulada: by Lagz.
50mm or 85mm lens
Aperture: f/1.8 – f/2.8
ISO: low (clean but with added film grain)
Natural light only, no artificial lighting
Estética general:
Poetic, introspective, calm, slightly melancholic.
Feels like a still from an indie film or a nostalgic memory.
No exaggerated drama — everything subtle and emotionally grounded.
Restricciones (IMPORTANTE):
No alterar identidad facial
No embellecimiento artificial
No estilo cartoon, anime o pintura
No iluminación de estudio
No poses forzadas
ar 9:16

How to Use

To use this prompt, paste the entire text directly into Gemini's image generation interface or any compatible AI image model. Before generating, locate the section that says 'replace with my exact face' and substitute that placeholder text with a detailed, objective description of your own facial features. Avoid subjective terms like 'beautiful' or 'handsome' and stick to specifics: shape of jawline, distance between eyes, bridge width of nose, lip thickness, skin undertones, and any distinct marks or asymmetries. For best results, use a reference photo upload alongside the text prompt if the platform allows it. You can also tweak the environment by changing 'wild meadow' to a specific location like 'wheat field at dusk' or 'overgrown garden.' Adjust the color grading keywords from 'muted, earthy' to 'cool, desaturated blues' if you prefer a colder emotional tone. The aperture and lens settings are cues for the AI's rendering engine, not literal camera controls, so you can modify these to influence sharpness and blur. If the AI adds too much blur, change 'f/1.8' to 'f/4.0' in the text. Always keep the restriction list at the end intact to prevent the model from drifting into illustration or artificial retouching.

FAQ

  • How do I ensure the AI actually generates MY face and not a generic person?
    The prompt is structured with strict negative constraints ('No alterar identidad facial') to prevent the model from defaulting to a generic attractive face. However, the most effective method is to use this prompt in a platform that supports 'Face Swap' or 'Identity Reference' uploads alongside text prompts. In Gemini or similar advanced models, you should upload 3-5 clear reference photos of yourself from different angles before pasting the prompt. In the prompt text where it says 'replace with my exact face,' you can also add a technical description of your features (e.g., 'hooded almond eyes, wide nasal bridge, prominent cupid's bow, slight under-eye hollows') to lock the model into your specific geometry rather than an idealized average.
  • Why does the prompt specify 'wrinkled suit' and 'dirt marks' if I want a clean photo?
    These details are included to break the AI's tendency to render sterile, studio-perfect images. When generating realistic human portraits, AI models often default to 'beauty mode'—flawless skin, ironed clothes, and perfect lighting. By explicitly requesting wrinkles, dirt, and pollen, you are forcing the rendering engine to prioritize texture and imperfection, which paradoxically results in a *more* realistic and believable photograph. It signals to the AI that this is a candid, documentary-style shot rather than a commercial headshot. If you want a cleaner look, you can soften those keywords to 'softly creased' or remove the 'dirt marks' line, but be aware the result may look slightly more staged.
  • Can I change the aspect ratio to 16:9 for a desktop wallpaper?
    Yes, the prompt ends with 'ar 9:16' which is a vertical portrait orientation. You can simply change this to 'ar 16:9' for a horizontal, cinematic widescreen look. However, keep in mind that changing the aspect ratio will affect the composition. The current prompt describes a 'medium close-up framing (chest and head visible).' In a 16:9 horizontal frame, this same framing will leave empty space on the left and right of the subject. For a better 16:9 result, you might want to adjust the framing description in the prompt from 'medium close-up' to 'medium shot, subject off-center following rule of thirds' so the environment fills the wider frame more naturally.
