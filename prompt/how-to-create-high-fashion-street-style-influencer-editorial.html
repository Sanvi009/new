How to Create a High-Fashion Street Style Influencer Editorial

AI Image Prompts 2026-03-20 Brainloom

Learn how to generate a striking, paparazzi-style streetwear editorial photo. This AI prompt creates a high-contrast, direct-flash portrait of an influencer at a major fashion week, featuring trendy Y2K apparel, a custom graphic tee, and luxurious urban aesthetics. Perfect for fashion bloggers, digital artists, and clothing brands looking to showcase edgy, photorealistic street style.

The Prompt

A candid yet highly stylized "street style" editorial shot of a (female/male) influencer during Paris Fashion Week. (She/he) is wearing a vintage-wash t-shirt with "I ❤️ AI SLOP" printed in a distressed Y2K font. He’s layered with a faux-fur oversized coat slung off the shoulders and diamond-encrusted sunglasses. Direct flash photography, high contrast, vibrant colors, urban luxury background, effortless cool pose, extremely detailed fabric textures.use the uploaded image as the subject , ar 9:16

How to Use

To customize this prompt, start by selecting the gender of your subject by removing either '(female/male)' and '(She/he)'. You can personalize the graphic tee by replacing '"I ❤️ AI SLOP"' with your own brand name or slogan. To change the overall vibe, swap 'Paris Fashion Week' with 'Tokyo Fashion Week' or 'New York Fashion Week'. You can also alter the wardrobe; try changing the 'faux-fur oversized coat' to a 'leather trench coat' or 'vintage denim jacket', and adjust the accessories like the 'diamond-encrusted sunglasses' to fit your desired aesthetic.

FAQ

  • How do I change the custom text on the t-shirt?
    To customize the typography, simply locate the phrase '"I ❤️ AI SLOP"' in the prompt and replace it with your desired text. Keep the phrase relatively short (1-4 words) for the best results, as AI image generators handle shorter text strings much more accurately.
  • What gives this prompt its specific paparazzi editorial look?
    The unique visual style is driven by the keywords 'direct flash photography' and 'high contrast'. These terms instruct the AI to mimic the harsh lighting, deep shadows, and bright highlights typical of candid, on-the-go fashion week photography.
  • How can I ensure the AI uses my uploaded face correctly?
    The prompt includes the instruction 'use the uploaded image as the subject'. When using tools like Midjourney, you should combine this prompt with an image prompt (URL) or use the '--cref' (character reference) parameter to strongly guide the AI to replicate your facial features and identity.
