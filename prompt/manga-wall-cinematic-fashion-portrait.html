Prompt Explanation

This prompt is an exceptionally detailed and rigorous instruction set designed to generate a high-fashion editorial portrait that marries classic luxury aesthetics with a contemporary pop culture twist. Its creative purpose is to place the subject, rendered with photographic perfection, in front of a visually striking backdrop of black-and-white manga panels. This juxtaposition creates a powerful tension between the timeless elegance of the tailored beige suit and the dynamic, graphic energy of the comic art, resulting in an image that feels both sophisticated and edgy, suitable for a high-end magazine spread. The portrait is not just a photograph; it is a statement about the intersection of different cultural and artistic languages. The prompt_text is the most comprehensive and legally precise of the set, functioning almost like a contract with the AI. It opens with an ironclad clause on likeness: 'Face must be 100% identical to the uploaded reference image — exact same facial features, bone structure, jawline, eyes, nose, lips, skin tone, hairstyle, and overall identity. Do not alter or reinterpret the face. Preserve likeness perfectly.' This level of redundancy and specificity is necessary because AI models have a strong tendency to 'idealize' or 'smooth' faces. By listing every feature individually and using commanding language like 'Do not alter,' the prompt aggressively combats this default behavior, demanding that the AI treat the reference photo as a sacred, unchangeable template. Following this, the outfit is described with the precision of a stylist's mood board: 'a tailored beige suit jacket over a deep navy blue dress shirt, paired with a green tie featuring subtle yellow polka dots and a matching pocket square.' This is not a simple color scheme; it's a carefully considered palette. The beige suit provides a soft, luxurious base. The deep navy shirt adds depth and contrast. The green tie with yellow polka dots is the statement piece, a playful yet refined detail that injects personality and prevents the outfit from being too safe. The matching pocket square shows an attention to detail that signals true style. The pose is interactive and natural: 'adjusting my tie with one hand, showcasing a luxury black wristwatch.' This serves two purposes: it creates a dynamic, slightly candid moment within a formal portrait, and it specifically highlights an accessory, adding another layer of luxury and detail. The 'round black sunglasses' and 'mysterious composed expression' contribute to an air of enigmatic sophistication. The technical photography instructions that follow are exhaustive and read like a photographer's shot list. 'Dramatic studio lighting with soft shadows and high contrast' is a specific lighting recipe that creates a sculpted, three-dimensional look with a touch of drama. 'Ultra-detailed skin texture' is a direct counter to the AI's smoothing instinct, reinforcing the earlier command for realism. 'Sharp focus, editorial fashion photography style, symmetrical centered composition, 9:16 aspect ratio, photorealistic, luxury aesthetic, cinematic color grading' are all aesthetic and technical anchors that collectively define the final look. The prompt then culminates in what looks like camera metadata: 'Camera: Medium close-up Lens: 85mm portrait lens Aperture: f/2.0 Lighting: Soft key light + subtle rim light 8K resolution, ultra realistic, high detail.' This is brilliant prompting. By using terms that are deeply embedded in photographic knowledge, it tells the AI to mimic the exact optical characteristics of a professional portrait shoot: the flattering compression of an 85mm lens, the shallow depth of field from an f/2.0 aperture that blurs the manga wall just enough to keep the focus on the subject, and the professional lighting setup. The '8K resolution' and 'high detail' are the final quality assurance checks. The lighting logic is thus not just about mood but about technical replication of a studio setup. The soft key light provides the main illumination, while the subtle rim light separates the subject from the manga wall behind them, adding depth and a touch of glamour. The composition is explicitly 'symmetrical and centered,' creating a classic, powerful, and iconic frame that is ideal for a fashion portrait. The realism level is set to the maximum with 'photorealistic,' '8K,' and 'ultra-detailed skin texture.' The elements that absolutely must NOT be changed are the subject's facial identity and the core of the outfit and pose. Altering the face would defeat the entire purpose. The specific interaction of the beige suit, navy shirt, and polka-dot tie is a carefully designed color and pattern story that should be preserved. However, the promptVariations show that the concept is highly adaptable. The suit can be swapped for an evening gown or casual streetwear, completely changing the subject's persona. The manga wall can be replaced with graffiti for a grittier urban feel, or a minimalist white backdrop for a purer, more classic fashion look. The lighting can be adjusted from dramatic studio to neon noir or golden hour, shifting the entire mood. Even the expression can be changed from mysterious to a confident smile. To improve accuracy, the reference photo is everything. It must be high-resolution, with the face clearly visible and evenly lit. In Midjourney, using a very high image weight parameter (--iw 2) is non-negotiable to force facial consistency. A common mistake is to use a reference photo with a different hairstyle or glasses, which will confuse the AI. Another mistake is to overlook the instruction 'do according to my gender,' which is crucial for the AI to correctly interpret the suit and tie for a female-presenting subject. Finally, forgetting the camera settings can result in an image that lacks the specific optical character of a professional portrait lens. This prompt is a masterclass in how to use ultra-detailed, technically precise language to exert maximum control over an AI, guiding it to produce a highly specific, high-quality, and conceptually rich piece of fashion art.

The Prompt 

A cinematic fashion portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded photo standing against a wall fully covered with black-and-white manga comic panels. Face must be 100% identical to the uploaded reference image — exact same facial features, bone structure, jawline, eyes, nose, lips, skin tone, hairstyle, and overall identity. Do not alter or reinterpret the face. Preserve likeness perfectly. i wears a tailored beige suit jacket over a deep navy blue dress shirt, paired with a green tie featuring subtle yellow polka dots and a matching pocket square. i adjusting my tie with one hand, showcasing a luxury black wristwatch. Round black sunglasses, mysterious composed expression, neatly slicked-back hair. Dramatic studio lighting with soft shadows and high contrast, ultra-detailed skin texture, sharp focus, editorial fashion photography style, symmetrical centered composition, 9:16 aspect ratio, photorealistic, luxury aesthetic, cinematic color grading. do according to my gender Camera: Medium close-up Lens: 85mm portrait lens Aperture: f/2.0 Lighting: Soft key light + subtle rim light 8K resolution, ultra realistic, high detail

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo of the person you want to feature. 2. Copy the full prompt and paste it into your AI image generator (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion). 3. For best results, use the same aspect ratio (9:16) and include the camera settings if your tool allows parameters. 4. Run the generator. If the face isn't a perfect match on the first try, adjust the reference image weight or run a few variations.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines specific technical photography instructions (85mm lens, f/2.0 aperture, studio lighting) with a strong visual concept (manga panel wall). The detailed description of the outfit, pose, and required facial accuracy gives the AI clear constraints, reducing random elements and increasing the chance of a usable, high-end fashion portrait.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the suit to an evening gown or casual streetwear.
  • Swap the manga panels for graffiti art or a minimalist white backdrop.
  • Adjust the lighting to neon noir or golden hour glow.
  • Change the expression to a confident smile or a serious gaze.
  • Make it a full-body shot instead of a medium close-up.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this prompt to make a portrait of someone else?
    Yes, just upload their clear photo as the reference image instead of your own. The prompt is designed to preserve the likeness of whoever is in the uploaded image.
  • Which AI tool works best for this prompt?
    Midjourney and Stable Diffusion are excellent choices because they handle both specific camera settings and character consistency well. DALL-E 3 can also produce great results, especially with the detailed description.
  • What does 'do according to my gender' mean?
    This instruction tells the AI to apply the clothing description (suit, tie) in a way that fits the gender of the person in the uploaded photo, ensuring the final image looks natural.
  • How do I get the face to look exactly like my photo?
    Use a high-quality, well-lit reference photo where the face is clearly visible. In tools like Midjourney, you can use the '--iw' parameter to set a higher image weight, telling the AI to prioritize the likeness of the reference image.
