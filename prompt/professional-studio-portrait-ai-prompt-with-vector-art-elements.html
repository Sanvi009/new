Prompt Explanation

This prompt is ingeniously designed to create a composite image that blends two distinct visual identities into one frame: the real person and their graphic alter ego. Its creative purpose is to produce a sophisticated piece of personal branding material. It goes beyond a simple headshot by incorporating a stylized vector portrait on the wall beside the subject, creating a visual dialogue between the person's real, photorealistic self and a simplified, iconic representation. This is a powerful concept for professionals in creative fields, tech, or anyone wanting to convey a multifaceted personal brandâ€”one that is both approachably human and sharply defined. The image suggests a person who is not only present and authentic but also understands the power of design and symbolic representation. The prompt_text is a blueprint for a studio photoshoot with a conceptual art twist. It begins with the uncompromising demand for an 'Ultra-realistic 8K full-body portrait using the exact face from the reference image (Face100% match).' This sets the highest possible bar for the fidelity of the main subject. It then describes the subject's attire in precise, professional detail: 'White dress shirt with turned-up collar and lapel mic, dark navy trousers, brown dress shoes.' The lapel mic is a brilliant detail, subtly suggesting the person is a speaker, presenter, or someone of note, adding a layer of professional context without needing a complex background. The pose instruction, 'Casual lean against a light-gray studio wall, hands in pockets, one leg crossed, relaxed confidence,' is equally important. It guides the AI to create a specific body language that is approachable yet confident, perfectly balancing the formality of the attire with a relatable human quality. The core of the prompt's creative concept is the next sentence: 'Beside him on the wall: black-and-white vector portrait of his face and chest, sharp angular polygonal style. Text below: Brainloom online.' This is the element that elevates the image from a standard portrait to a piece of personal branding art. The vector art acts as a logo or a symbol, a deconstructed version of the person that speaks to a digital or design-centric identity. The specific placement 'beside him on the wall' and the 'sharp angular polygonal style' are crucial for the AI to execute the concept correctly. The final instructions, 'Photorealistic, studio lighting. do according to my gender,' ground the entire scene in a professional photography aesthetic. The lighting described as 'studio lighting' is the foundation for achieving the 'ultra-realistic' look. It implies a controlled, soft, and even illumination that flatters the subject and creates a clean, professional finish. The composition is cleverly split. On one side, you have the three-dimensional, fully realized person. On the other, on the same two-dimensional plane (the wall), you have a flat, graphical representation of that same person. This creates a fascinating visual tension about identity and representation. The proportions are critical: the subject must be full-body and dominant in the frame, while the vector portrait must be large enough to be a clear, secondary element but not so large that it competes for attention. The element that must not be changed is the core concept of the dual portrait: the real person and the vector representation existing in the same space. The 'Face100% match' instruction is also non-negotiable for a personalized branding image. However, almost every other element is a variable that can be customized. As the prompt variations suggest, the clothing can be changed to a 'black turtleneck' for a more artistic, Steve Jobs-like vibe, or to a 'blazer and shirt' for a more traditional corporate look. The background can be swapped from a minimalist studio wall to an 'office environment' to add more context. Most creatively, the style of the vector art can be completely transformed. Replacing 'polygonal style' with 'line art' or 'watercolor' would create a different artistic feel, changing the nature of the person's graphic alter ego. The text below the vector art is also a powerful customizable element, allowing for the inclusion of a personal brand name, a website, or a tagline. For practical tips to improve accuracy, as noted in the FAQ, generating this in one pass can be challenging for AI. A two-step approach is often more effective: first, generate the photorealistic portrait of the person against the plain wall using the first part of the prompt. Then, use an inpainting or image-to-image tool with a more specific prompt to add the vector art element to the wall, or composite it in manually using photo editing software. A common mistake is not being specific enough about the style of the vector art. Just saying 'vector portrait' might yield a flat, generic result. The prompt's inclusion of 'sharp angular polygonal style' gives the AI a clear, modern aesthetic to aim for. Another frequent error is expecting the AI to perfectly render the text. As with the mugshot prompt, the text should be seen as a stylistic element that suggests branding, and may need to be cleaned up or replaced in post-production for a truly professional finish. This prompt is a prime example of how AI can be used not just to replicate reality, but to construct a layered visual statement about identity and personal brand.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic 8K full-body portrait using the exact face from the reference image (Face100% match). White dress shirt with turned-up collar and lapel mic, dark navy trousers, brown dress shoes. Casual lean against a light-gray studio wall, hands in pockets, one leg crossed, relaxed confidence. Beside him on the wall: black-and-white vector portrait of his face and chest, sharp angular polygonal style. Text below: Brainloom online . Photorealistic, studio lighting. do according to my gender , aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your face reference photo to your AI generator. 2. Copy and paste this prompt. 3. Ensure your AI tool references your uploaded image for 'face100% match'. 4. Generate to create a professional studio portrait with vector art elements. 5. Change clothing colors or background to match your brand.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt provides specific clothing details (white dress shirt, lapel mic), pose instructions (lean against wall, hands in pockets), and clear composition elements (vector portrait beside subject) that AI can execute precisely. 'Studio lighting' and 'photorealistic' trigger realistic rendering models.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'white dress shirt' to 'black turtleneck' or 'blazer and shirt'
  • Swap 'light-gray studio wall' to 'concrete wall', 'bookshelf backdrop', or 'office environment'
  • Replace 'polygonal style' vector with 'line art', 'watercolor', or 'silhouette' style
  • Try 'standing pose' or 'sitting pose' instead of 'lean against wall'
  • Modify 'Brainloom online' text to your own brand name or tagline

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get AI to use my exact face in a portrait?
    Upload a clear, front-facing photo of yourself first. Use 'face100% match' or similar phrasing in your prompt. Most AI generators have specific commands for image reference (like /imagine in Midjourney).
  • Can I use this for LinkedIn or professional profiles?
    Yes, this creates business-appropriate portraits. The professional attire and studio setting work well for LinkedIn, company websites, or speaker profiles.
  • Why does the vector art sometimes look blurry or misplaced?
    AI can struggle with multiple elements in one image. For better results, generate the portrait first, then add vector elements separately in editing software, or use more specific positioning terms.
