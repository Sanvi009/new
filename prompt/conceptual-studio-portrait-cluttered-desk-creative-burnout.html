Prompt Explanation

This prompt represents a sophisticated fusion of fashion editorial photography, environmental storytelling, and psychological portraiture. It moves beyond simple image generation into conceptual art direction, using every visual element to build a narrative about creative professional life, burnout culture, and the tension between personal style and workspace reality. The prompt opens with a critical identity instruction: "Use uploaded images face reference and keep face 100 percent accurate." This explicit requirement acknowledges that AI models often default to generating generic attractive faces rather than preserving specific identities. By demanding 100 percent accuracy and including "exact same facial and physical identity," the prompt establishes non-negotiable parameters that prevent the AI from taking creative liberties with the subject's appearance. The mention of "sharp angular features" provides additional guidance for emphasizing existing bone structure. The pose description creates a specific psychological portrait. "Sitting relaxed" suggests comfort or resignation, while "contemplative expression" indicates internal focus rather than engagement with the viewer. This combination creates a subject who exists within their own thoughts, oblivious to or unconcerned with being observed—a powerful narrative stance that invites viewers to observe without participating. The black office chair positions the subject as a worker, but the relaxed posture suggests this workspace is deeply familiar. The fashion direction reads like a stylist's notes. The oversized beige pinstripe suit with wide lapels references classic 1980s power dressing but with contemporary volume that softens the aggressive silhouette. The vest adds formality while the wide-leg trousers and white sneakers introduce streetwear comfort. This combination tells viewers the subject understands fashion but prioritizes personal expression over corporate conformity. The black cat-eye sunglasses being adjusted rather than worn adds motion and suggests the subject is either arriving or preparing to leave—adding temporal dimension to a static image. The desk environment functions as visual autobiography. The cluttered metal desk with open Apple MacBook laptop, scattered notebooks, pens, white desk lamp, stacked books, and coffee cups creates a specific portrait of creative work. Unlike sterile office photography, this space shows evidence of actual use—thinking, writing, reading, caffeinating. The crumpled papers overflowing onto the floor extend the narrative beyond the desk, suggesting ideas discarded and work that has escaped containment. This overflow transforms a cluttered desk into a metaphor for mental state. The location elements ("minimalist room with muted green walls and wooden floor") provide essential contrast. The minimalist architecture means the chaos isn't environmental but personal—the room itself is orderly, the subject creates the disorder. Muted green walls add color psychology; green suggests creativity and growth but muted versions imply exhaustion or staleness. Wooden floors add warmth that prevents the image from feeling cold or institutional. The technical photography direction demonstrates sophisticated understanding of editorial image making. "High-contrast soft lighting" describes a specific quality achieved through large diffusion placed close to the subject—soft light that wraps around forms while maintaining dramatic shadow areas. Emphasizing textures like wool fabric and glossy paper adds tactile dimension that distinguishes professional photography from generic AI rendering. Wool has distinct fiber patterns; glossy paper reflects light differently than matte surfaces—these details matter for authenticity. The conceptual framework ("creative professional burnout or designer workspace shoot for a magazine like Vogue or Dazed") provides both thematic direction and aesthetic reference. Creative burnout is a culturally relevant concept in 2026, resonating with audiences who understand the pressures of knowledge work and creative industries. Referencing Vogue and Dazed establishes quality standards—these magazines represent the pinnacle of fashion editorial photography, so images should aim for that level of sophistication, lighting, styling, and narrative cohesion. The 9:16 aspect ratio optimizes for mobile viewing and social media platforms, acknowledging that fashion editorial content increasingly lives on Instagram, TikTok, and other vertical platforms. This practical consideration ensures the generated images work across publishing contexts. Common mistakes when using this prompt include providing reference images that don't match the contemplative mood—if the reference shows high-energy expression, the AI may struggle to generate contemplative versions while preserving identity. Another issue is insufficient attention to the specific items listed for desk clutter; the AI needs explicit direction to include all elements, and omitting any can change the narrative. Users also sometimes underestimate how many generations are needed to achieve perfect hand positioning with the sunglasses adjustment—this specific action often requires multiple attempts. To optimize results, consider generating the environment first without the subject to establish desk arrangement and lighting, then adding the subject in subsequent generations. This layered approach can help isolate variables and achieve better control. Adding specific designer references ("Raf Simons-inspired suit," "Martin Margiela proportions") can further refine fashion direction if the initial suit styling doesn't hit the intended mark. The key to mastering this prompt is understanding that every element serves narrative purpose—from the crumpled papers to the adjusted sunglasses to the muted wall color—and the most successful generations will be those where all elements work together to tell a coherent story about the person at the center.

How to Use This Prompt

Begin by uploading a high-quality reference image of the person you want to feature. The prompt requires 100 percent facial and physical identity accuracy, so choose a photo with clear front-facing views, good lighting, and minimal obstructions. The AI needs to analyze facial structure, proportions, and unique features to maintain consistency throughout generation. Once your reference is uploaded, paste the complete prompt into your preferred AI image generator. Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion with fine-tuned photographic capabilities will work best. This prompt combines multiple complex elements—specific fashion, detailed environment, precise pose, and conceptual mood—so expect to generate several versions to achieve optimal results. The pose requires careful attention: the subject should sit relaxed in a black office chair with one hand adjusting black cat-eye sunglasses on their face and the other resting on the desk. If the hand positioning looks awkward or unnatural, regenerate with added terms like "natural hand placement" or "relaxed fingers." The contemplative expression should convey thoughtfulness without appearing forced or staged. The desk clutter is extensive and specific. The prompt lists an open Apple MacBook laptop, scattered notebooks, pens, a white desk lamp, stacked books, coffee cups, and crumpled papers that overflow onto the floor. If the clutter looks too tidy or sparse, add "extreme disarray" or "creative chaos" to emphasize the overwhelming quantity. The papers overflowing onto the floor are particularly important for conveying the burnout concept. The oversized beige pinstripe suit with wide lapels, vest, and wide-leg trousers creates a specific fashion silhouette that defines the editorial style. If the suit looks ill-fitting in an unintended way, add "tailored oversized" or "deliberately voluminous" to clarify the intentional aesthetic. The white sneakers ground the formal look in contemporary streetwear fusion. The lighting should be high-contrast but soft, emphasizing textures like wool fabric and glossy paper. If textures appear flat, add "texture emphasis" or "fabric detail enhancement." The overall cinematic quality should evoke creative professional burnout editorial spreads from Vogue or Dazed magazine.

By placing this requirement early and capitalizing "100 percent," the prompt signals to the AI that identity preservation is non-negotiable. Adding "sharp angular features" provides specific facial structure guidance that helps the AI emphasize existing features rather than genericizing them. The pose description ("sitting relaxed in a black office chair at a cluttered metal desk") establishes a specific power dynamic. The subject is relaxed, suggesting comfort with or resignation to the surrounding chaos—this psychological nuance elevates the image beyond simple portraiture. The contemplative expression reinforces this internal narrative. The fashion choices tell their own story. The oversized beige pinstripe suit with wide lapels and vest references classic tailoring but with contemporary volume, suggesting a creative professional who respects tradition but operates outside corporate constraints. The white sneakers ground the look in practicality and modernity. One hand adjusting black cat-eye sunglasses adds an element of studied nonchalance—the subject is aware of being photographed but not performing for the camera. The desk environment functions as visual biography. The chaotic arrangement of MacBook laptop, scattered notebooks, pens, stacked books, coffee cups, and crumpled papers tells viewers this is someone who lives at their workspace. The papers overflowing onto the floor extend the chaos beyond the desk boundaries, suggesting the disarray has become uncontainable—a powerful metaphor for mental state. The location details ("minimalist room with muted green walls and wooden floor") provide contrast to the desk chaos. The minimalist environment means the clutter isn't competing with other visual elements—all attention focuses on the subject and their immediate workspace. Muted green walls add subtle color without distracting from the beige suit and white/black accents. The style direction ("conceptual fashion editorial photography, high-contrast soft lighting emphasizing textures") combines technical and artistic guidance. Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure the oversized suit looks intentional rather like a poor fit?
    Add 'deliberately voluminous silhouette,' 'fashion-forward oversized tailoring,' or 'designed oversized fit' to clarify the intentional aesthetic. Reference specific designers known for oversized cuts like Thom Browne or Maison Margiela for additional guidance.
  • The desk clutter keeps coming out too organized. How can I make it messier?
    Include phrases like 'extreme chaotic disarray,' 'papers literally spilling everywhere,' 'complete creative chaos,' or 'organized mess' doesn't work—this needs to look genuinely chaotic.' You can also add specific items like 'tipped-over coffee cup' or 'crumpled papers on floor.'
  • What does 'high-contrast soft lighting' mean and how do I achieve it?
    High-contrast soft lighting means deep shadows but smooth transitions—achieved with large light sources placed close to the subject. Add 'large softbox overhead' or 'diffused key light with dramatic fall-off' to help the AI understand this specific quality.
