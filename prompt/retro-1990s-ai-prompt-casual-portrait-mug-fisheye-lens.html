Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a highly personalized, nostalgic portrait that transports the subject back to the 1990s, capturing a specific cultural and aesthetic moment. Its creative purpose is to blend the user's contemporary identity with a carefully constructed retro environment, resulting in an image that feels both familiar and historically situated. The prompt functions as a detailed instruction set for the AI, guiding it to reconstruct the user's likeness within a scene defined by vintage photographic techniques and period-specific details. The core of the prompt, the 'prompt_text,' is a masterclass in directive language. It begins by anchoring the output to the user's identity with the critical phrase 'me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image,' ensuring the AI prioritizes likeness over generic generation. From there, it builds the world: the action ('drinking from a mug'), the wardrobe ('casual 1990s-inspired outfit'), and the setting ('aged interior space with glossy tiles and diffused natural light'). This level of specificity leaves little to the AI's imagination, cornering it into producing a cohesive scene. The instruction for a 'dramatic, wide-angle composition... slightly distorted by the fisheye lens' is particularly important, as it dictates not just what we see, but how we see it. The fisheye effect is a powerful stylistic choice that immediately evokes a sense of 90s alternative or skate photography, adding a layer of authenticity that a standard lens could not achieve. The lighting described as 'diffused natural light coming from a small window' is crucial for setting a soft, intimate mood that complements the vintage feel, while the request for 'soft shadows and highlights' prevents the image from looking flat or artificially lit. The prompt places a heavy emphasis on materiality and realism, asking for 'realistic textures' for the 'ceramic mug, fabrics, [and] natural skin detail.' This is a direct challenge to the AI to move beyond surface-level rendering and consider the tactile qualities of objects. The inclusion of 'subtle film grain' and a 'warm vintage filter' are the final touches that unify the aesthetic, mimicking the imperfections of analog photography. The element that must absolutely not be changed is the core instruction regarding the user's identity. Any deviation from '100% use my real face' would defeat the entire purpose of a personalized portrait. The aspect ratio of 9:16 is also a fixed requirement, dictating a vertical format suitable for mobile viewing or social media stories. However, many elements are designed to be modular. The prompt variations provided are excellent examples of safe and creative modifications. Changing the mug to a 'soda can' or an 'old video game controller' would amplify the 90s nostalgia. Swapping the 'glossy tiles' for a 'wood-paneled wall' would shift the interior from perhaps a kitchen to a more suburban living room feel. Altering the lens effect or color grading would change the entire photographic style, moving it from a gritty 90s documentary look to something else entirely. For improved accuracy, users should ensure their uploaded photo is well-lit and shows their face clearly. When generating, using a high 'image weight' parameter (like --iw in Midjourney) will help the AI adhere more strictly to the reference photo. A common mistake is to use a low-quality or poorly lit source image, which forces the AI to guess at facial details. Another is to neglect the 'do according to my gender' part, which can lead to the AI defaulting to a different presentation if not explicitly directed. By intricately weaving together directives for identity, setting, photographic technique, and texture, this prompt transcends a simple description and becomes a functional blueprint for a very specific and evocative piece of retro & vintage photography.

The Prompt 

me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image, i drinking from a mug, seated or standing in a casual 1990s-inspired outfit, relaxed and confident pose. The background suggests a slightly aged interior space with glossy tiles and diffused natural light coming from a small window. Dramatic, wide-angle composition emphasizes the subject’s presence, slightly distorted by the fisheye lens. Colors are muted, vintage, and slightly desaturated, giving a nostalgic, retro film-like atmosphere. Textures are realistic: ceramic mug, fabrics, natural skin detail. Subtle film grain, warm vintage filter, soft shadows and highlights enhance depth and cinematic mood. High-resolution, sharp focus, enhanced realism and tactile detail using Nano Banana Pro capabilities. 100% use my real face.do according to my gender , aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Upload a clear photo of your face. Copy and paste the entire prompt. The AI will generate a vertical (9:16) retro-style portrait using your likeness, placing you in a 90s outfit with a mug, using a fisheye lens effect and vintage color grading.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt effectively combines a specific era aesthetic ('1990s-inspired', 'nostalgic, retro film-like') with distinctive photographic techniques ('fisheye lens', 'wide-angle composition', 'desaturated colors'). The detailed scene setting ('aged interior space with glossy tiles', 'diffused natural light') and textural focus ('ceramic mug, fabrics, natural skin detail') provide concrete visual anchors. Mentioning a fictional but specific technical capability ('Nano Banana Pro') can sometimes act as a unique style token, encouraging the AI to push for enhanced detail and a unique look.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the prop from a 'mug' to a 'soda can' or 'old video game controller' for a stronger 90s vibe.
  • Swap the 'glossy tiles' background for a 'wood-paneled wall' or 'curtained window'.
  • Alter the lens effect from 'fisheye' to 'standard wide angle' or 'tilt-shift' for a different feel.
  • Make the colors more vibrant for an '80s retro' look or more sepia-toned for an 'old photograph' feel.
  • Specify a particular 90s outfit, like 'flannel shirt and jeans' or 'colorful windbreaker'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a 'fisheye lens' effect?
    A fisheye lens creates an extreme wide-angle view with pronounced barrel distortion, making straight lines appear curved and exaggerating the sense of space. In this prompt, it gives the portrait a distinctive, dramatic, and slightly surreal 90s alternative photography style.
  • What does 'Nano Banana Pro capabilities' refer to?
    This is likely a fictional or niche technical term used within certain AI art communities as a 'style token'. It signals to the AI to aim for hyper-realistic texture and detail, as if rendered by a advanced, specialized system. You can treat it as a keyword for ultra-high detail.
  • How can I make the 90s style more accurate?
    You can be more specific with fashion keywords ('baggy jeans', 'crop top', 'denim jacket', 'chunky sneakers'), hairstyles ('curtain bangs', 'frosted tips'), and props ('Discman', 'CRT television'). The more era-specific details you add, the stronger the thematic result.
