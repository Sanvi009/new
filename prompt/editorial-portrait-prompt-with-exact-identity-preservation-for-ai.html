Editorial Portrait Prompt With Exact Identity Preservation for AI

AI Image Prompts 2026-04-22 Brainloom

This prompt is designed to generate a full-body editorial portrait that strictly preserves your exact facial identity, skin texture, and natural proportions. The scene is a minimalist studio setup with a muted olive-green backdrop and soft beige floor, creating a cinematic, high-fashion atmosphere. The subject wears an oversized brown blazer, loosely tucked beige shirt, thin brown tie, and wide-leg checkered trousers, holding a small bouquet of wilted pastel flowers. The expression is introspective and calm, with a gaze directed off-camera. The lighting is soft and diffused, mimicking large studio softboxes, while the color grading leans toward desaturated Kodak Portra film tones. The result is a raw, unretouched-looking photograph that emphasizes fabric weave, skin pores, and natural asymmetry without any beautification filters or smoothing algorithms.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic full-body cinematic photograph of a person (replace with my exact face, gender, facial structure, proportions, and skin tone — strict identity preservation). Do NOT beautify, idealize, or stylize in a way that alters identity. Preserve natural asymmetries, pores, skin texture, eye shape, nose structure, lips, and all unique facial features. The result must look like a real photograph of me, not a reinterpretation.
Escena y composición: by Lagz.
A minimalist studio fashion portrait with a muted olive-green background and a soft beige floor. Full-body framing, subject standing slightly off-center with a relaxed but confident pose. One hand resting inside the pants pocket, another one is holding a small bouquet of slightly wilted flowers (soft pastel tones, subtle and understated). Body weight shifted to one leg for a natural asymmetrical stance.
Vestuario (clave del estilo):
Oversized brown blazer with soft structure and slightly dropped shoulders
Light beige or off-white button-up shirt, slightly wrinkled and loosely tucked
Thin brown tie, worn loosely and slightly off-center
High-waisted, wide-leg trousers with a subtle checkered pattern (brown tones)
Black leather shoes with a minimal, polished finish
Fit intentionally relaxed and androgynous, leaning toward editorial fashion styling.
Expresión y pose: by Lagz.
Neutral, introspective facial expression. Slightly distant gaze looking off-camera (not directly at viewer). Lips relaxed, eyes soft. Head slightly tilted upward or to the side, conveying calm confidence and quiet detachment.
Iluminación (muy importante):
Soft, diffused studio lighting with a cinematic feel.
Key light from one side (soft, indirect, large light source)
Gentle shadows, no harsh contrast
Subtle falloff across the body to create depth
Slight warm tone overall
Color y estética:
Muted, earthy color palette: olive green, beige, brown.
Low saturation, slightly desaturated cinematic grading.
Film-like color science (Kodak Portra / editorial magazine tone).
Soft contrast, slightly matte finish.
Lente y cámara:by Lagz.
Shot on 50mm or 85mm lens
Full-frame camera look
Slight depth of field but mostly sharp across subject
Editorial fashion photography quality
Texturas y detalles:
Visible fabric texture (wool blazer, soft cotton shirt, structured trousers).
Natural wrinkles and folds in clothing (important for realism).
Skin texture preserved (no plastic smoothing).
Estilo general:
High-end fashion editorial, minimalist, androgynous aesthetic, cinematic realism, subtle storytelling through pose and styling.
Negative prompt (MUY IMPORTANTE):
no beauty filter, no skin smoothing, no unrealistic symmetry, no exaggerated features, no cartoon, no anime, no over-sharpening, no glossy skin, no artificial lighting, no extreme contrast, no distortion, no identity change
--ar 9:16

How to Use

Copy the entire prompt text and paste it directly into Gemini AI or a similar image generation model that supports detailed negative prompting. The most critical step is replacing the bracketed phrase 'replace with my exact face...' with your actual reference photo or a detailed text description of your features if the model supports face reference uploads. To get the best match, ensure your reference image is well-lit and shows your natural facial structure clearly. You can adjust the color of the blazer, shirt, or background by changing the specific color names in the 'Vestuario' and 'Escena' sections—for instance, swapping 'olive-green' for 'dusty rose' or 'slate blue.' If the clothing appears too stiff in the result, emphasize 'fabric wrinkles' more strongly in the prompt. The aspect ratio is set to 9:16 for a vertical phone wallpaper or Instagram story format, but you can change this to 4:5 for standard posts. The negative prompt is essential to prevent the AI from applying default 'beauty filters' that erase skin pores and alter identity.

FAQ

  • How do I get the AI to actually use my face instead of a generic model?
    This prompt relies on strict 'identity preservation' language which works best with AI models that support image-to-image referencing. If you are using Gemini or a similar advanced model, upload a clear, well-lit photo of your face and instruct the AI to use that as a reference for the 'person' in the prompt. The text in the prompt forces the model to retain your bone structure and skin texture rather than defaulting to its training data averages. Avoid using beauty filters on your reference photo, as the AI will interpret that smoothness as part of your identity.
  • What does 'by Lagz' mean in the prompt structure?
    In the context of AI prompt crafting, 'by Lagz' acts as a style trigger token or an artist influence tag. It signals to the image generator that you want the composition, lighting style, and editorial framing associated with that specific reference within the model's training data. It helps steer the AI away from generic stock photo looks and toward a more curated, high-fashion editorial magazine aesthetic. You do not need to replace it unless you have a different specific artist style you wish to emulate.
  • Can I change the gender of the clothing while keeping the same face?
    Yes, absolutely. The 'Fit intentionally relaxed and androgynous' line already leans toward a fluid style, but you can edit the wardrobe section to be more feminine or masculine as you see fit. For example, swap the blazer for a long wool skirt or replace the trousers with a structured dress. The key is to keep the 'strict identity preservation' and negative prompt instructions intact so the face and natural body proportions remain exactly yours while the silhouette of the clothing changes.
