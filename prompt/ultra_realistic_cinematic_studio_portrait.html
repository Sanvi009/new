Ultra-Realistic Cinematic Studio Portrait — Instagram Viral Prompt

AI Image Prompts 2025-09-20 Brainloom

AI art prompt to create a cinematic studio portrait in 9:16 format with a clean golden gradient background. The subject wears a cream blazer, pastel shirt, tailored trousers, and loafers, posed elegantly on a modern chair. Styled with dramatic lighting, shallow depth of field, and low-angle capture, this prompt emphasizes luxury, detail, and Instagram-viral aesthetics.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic cinematic studio portrait in 9:16 format, captured from a low-angle with an 85mm lens and shallow depth of field. The background is a perfectly clean, smooth warm golden gradient with no clutter or distractions, creating a minimal yet luxurious atmosphere. The subject’s hairstyle is styled in a modern, voluminous, neatly set look that frames the face with precision. Outfit: cream blazer over a pastel shirt, tailored trousers, and polished loafers.He (attached photo) is seated elegantly on a sleek modern chair, legs crossed, one hand lightly touching his chin. His expression blends dreamy softness with a subtle intense (angry-mode) vibe, radiating confidence, charisma, and sophistication. Lighting: dramatic cinematic key light with a soft rim glow, emphasizing ultra-detailed textures of skin, hair, fabric, and facial contours. Designed for Instagram-viral impact.

How to Use

Copy the prompt into your image generator. Use a reference photo if available. Make sure to set the aspect ratio to 9:16, and emphasize cinematic lighting, clean gradient background, and shallow depth of field. Guide the model to highlight textures in skin, hair, and clothing. Adjust negative prompts to keep the background minimal and distraction-free.
