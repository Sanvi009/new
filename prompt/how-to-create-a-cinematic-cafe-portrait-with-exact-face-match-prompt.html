Use this detailed prompt to generate a raw, cinematic portrait of yourself in a busy city cafe. It focuses on preserving facial identity, setting a reflective mood, and achieving a professional street photography aesthetic.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a masterclass in controlling the narrative and technical quality of an AI-generated portrait. Its primary design is to solve two of the most common problems users face: getting a consistent character (you) and achieving a specific, professional atmosphere. It's not just about creating a picture of a person in a cafe; it's about telling a story—'Thinking big, transition point'—while maintaining strict control over the visual elements. The prompt acts like a director giving instructions to a cinematographer: it specifies the shot type (medium portrait), the subject (you), the setting (city cafe), the lighting (via reflections and window light), and the emotional tone (cinematic, thoughtful). The structure is the secret weapon here. By grouping attributes under capitalized headers like POSE, CLOTHING, and VIBE, we are effectively creating 'buckets' of information. Large language models and image generators interpret text differently than humans. While we might read a sentence as a fluid idea, an AI often scans for keywords and patterns. The colon and all-caps act as signposts, telling the AI, 'This next piece of information belongs to the category of Pose.' This prevents the AI from muddling the instructions, ensuring the 'hand on chin' pose isn't accidentally applied to the background or the clothing. Word choice is equally critical. Using 'raw' and 'highly detailed' pushes the AI away from a smooth, airbrushed, default look and toward a more gritty, realistic, and textured image that feels like it was taken with a high-end camera. The term 'cinematic street photography' is a powerful phrase that pulls from the AI's knowledge of famous photographers and film stills, influencing everything from the color grading to the depth of field. The detail about 'reflections of the city street and people walking by... on the glass' is a brilliant way to add complexity and a sense of place without cluttering the main subject. It tells a larger story about the bustling world outside, contrasting with the subject's introspective moment inside. Users can tweak this prompt endlessly by swapping out the keywords in each section. For instance, changing the VIBE to 'melancholy, rainy day, noir aesthetic' will completely alter the color palette and lighting, even if the pose and location remain the same. A common mistake is to overload the prompt with too many conflicting descriptors, like asking for a 'raw, gritty' look while also demanding 'soft, dreamy, pastel colors.' The AI will try to compromise, often resulting in a muddy or confused image. Another mistake is neglecting the power of the initial image. If the reference photo is low quality or has odd lighting, the AI will inherit those flaws. For the best results, the reference image should be as close as possible to the lighting and quality you want in the final output. Ultimately, this prompt works because it treats the AI as a powerful but literal-minded collaborator, providing it with clear, structured, and evocative directions to bring your creative vision to life.

The Prompt 

A raw, highly detailed medium portrait shot of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image in a busy city cafe. POSE: Sitting by a large window, looking outside thoughtfully, hand on chin. Reflections of the city street and people walking by are visible on the glass. CLOTHING: Smart casual. BACKGROUND: Blurred cafe interior and busy urban street outside. VIBE: Thinking big, transition point. Cinematic street photography. ar 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To get the best results from this prompt, you need to combine it with a strong reference image of your face. Start by selecting a clear, front-facing photo of yourself with good lighting. On platforms like Midjourney, you would upload this image first to generate a link, then paste that link at the very beginning of the prompt. This tells the AI to use the facial structure and features from that photo as its primary reference. Next, copy the entire prompt provided here and paste it directly after the image link. Pay close attention to the structure: the 'POSE:', 'CLOTHING:', 'BACKGROUND:', and 'VIBE:' sections are clearly labeled. This helps the AI parse your instructions in an organized way, rather than treating it as a run-on sentence. When the image generates, you may need to run a few iterations. If the face isn't a perfect match on the first try, use the 'variation' or 'remaster' features on images that are closest to what you want. You can also adjust the prompt slightly; for example, if the reflection on the glass is too strong, you could add 'subtle reflections' or if the cafe is too blurry, you could specify 'slightly blurred background' for more context.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt is highly effective because it breaks down a complex scene into manageable, logical components for the AI. Instead of a vague description like 'a guy in a cafe,' it provides structured data points. The explicit inclusion of 'exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image' is a critical command that leverages the AI's image prompting capabilities, forcing it to prioritize the reference photo over generating a random face. This significantly increases the accuracy of the portrait. Furthermore, the use of colons and capitalized labels ('POSE:', 'VIBE:') acts as a semantic separator. AI models interpret these as distinct categories of information, allowing them to process the pose instructions separately from the lighting conditions or the background details. This reduces the chance of elements bleeding into one another, such as the AI making the clothing reflective because it misread the 'glass reflections' part. Finally, the inclusion of cinematic photography terms like 'raw,' 'highly detailed,' and 'cinematic street photography' taps into the AI's training data, which is filled with images tagged with these descriptors. This guides the output toward a specific aesthetic—professional, moody, and story-driven—rather than a flat, generic snapshot.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why isn't the AI generating my exact face?
    Facial identity is one of the hardest things for AI to master. Ensure your reference image is clear, well-lit, and shows your face directly. If the first generation isn't perfect, use the image that looks most like you as a new reference or select 'variations' on the closest result to nudge the AI toward your features.
  • What does 'ar 9:16' mean and can I change it?
    'ar 9:16' stands for aspect ratio, meaning the image will be 9 units tall for every 16 units wide—a vertical, portrait-oriented frame ideal for phone screens or social media stories. You can change it to 'ar 16:9' for a widescreen landscape or 'ar 1:1' for a square image.
  • Can I use this prompt for different locations?
    Absolutely. The prompt structure is designed to be modular. You can keep the 'POSE' and 'VIBE' instructions the same but swap the 'BACKGROUND' to anywhere you like, such as 'a quiet library aisle' or 'a bustling train station platform,' and the AI will intelligently recompose the scene.
