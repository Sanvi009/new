How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for action figure designs, collectible toys, or cosplay merch in gaming and comics. Preserve "version of a collectible doll," "wearing clothes as per the photo," "style of (Spanish) action figures," and "articulated base" for dynamic pose. Do not change the joints or buffalo warrior inspiration. Optionally vary pose; for example, more aggressive could yield warrior visuals, resulting in PVC details, while original creates branded, realistic outcomes.

Prompt

Create a version of a collectible doll. The doll is wearing clothes as per the photo. The doll must follow the style of (Spanish)action figures, such as (buffalo warrior) . The doll must have joints in the elbows, wrists, knees, and feet. It must be on an articulated base and in a dynamic pose. It for ChatGPT image generation.
