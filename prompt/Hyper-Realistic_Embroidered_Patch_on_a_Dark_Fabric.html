The Prompt 

A hyper-realistic embroidered patch of [your logo or icon description], showing detailed thread texture, realistic stitching along the edges, and subtle fabric fraying for authenticity. The patch should have vibrant colors exactly matching the logo design, with visible thread direction and soft shadows for depth. Place it on a dark fabric background with diagonal textured lines, lit with soft studio lighting for a premium look. Ultra-detailed, 8K resolution, photorealistic, close-up macro shot, aspect ratio 1:1.
