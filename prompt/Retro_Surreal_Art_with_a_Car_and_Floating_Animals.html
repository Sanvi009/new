How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into surreal art, car ads, or pop-art exhibits for retro and playful themes. Preserve "retro-surreal cinematic artwork," "[SKY COLORS]," "[CHARACTER: description, clothes, attitude]," and "pop-art surrealism aesthetic" for contrast. Do not modify the oversized animals or neon palette. Replacing character with "cowboy" could produce western visuals, leading to floating elements, while original yields bold, detailed results.

Prompt

Retro-surreal cinematic artwork, hyper-saturated [SKY COLORS] with [CLOUD COLORS]. A [CHARACTER: description, clothes, attitude] is seen with a [CAR TYPE / COLOR]. Around the car, oversized [ANIMALS] move both across reflective cracked water pavement and floating mid-air. Pop-art surrealism aesthetic, playful yet cinematic, bold high-contrast neon palette, hyper-detailed retro style.

Why this prompt works

This prompt works by creating a retro-surreal cinematic artwork with pop-art aesthetics the oversized animals and reflective cracked water pavement add whimsy and motion the bold neon palette and hyper-detailed style ensure visual impact making the image ideal for music albums festival posters or branding that aims to be playful and vibrant.

Prompt Variations

  • Sky Colors: Swap “[SKY COLORS]” → “deep purples and magentas” for a psychedelic sky. Swap “[SKY COLORS]” → “acid green and yellow” for a toxic, surreal sky.
  • Character & Car: Swap “[CHARACTER: description, clothes, attitude]” → “a punk rocker with a leather jacket” and “[CAR TYPE / COLOR]” → “[a pink convertible]”.
  • Character & Car: Swap “[CHARACTER: description, clothes, attitude]” → “a punk rocker with a leather jacket” and “[CAR TYPE / COLOR]” → “[a pink convertible]”.
  • Animals: Swap “[ANIMALS]” → “oversized goldfish” for a unexpected element. Swap “[ANIMALS]” → “oversized rabbits” for a more whimsical feel.
