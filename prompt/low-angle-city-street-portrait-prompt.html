Prompt Explanation

This prompt is engineered to produce a powerful, cinematic street photograph that elevates the subject to an almost heroic stature. Its creative purpose is to place a specific individual within a grand, architectural urban landscape, using perspective and scale to create a narrative of confidence and presence. The image is designed to feel like a still from a movie or a high-fashion editorial, where the city itself becomes a dramatic backdrop that frames and amplifies the subject. The prompt_text is a meticulously crafted set of instructions that balances identity preservation with complex scene generation. It opens with a non-negotiable demand for 'exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image,' which is the cornerstone for any personalized result. The scene is then set: the subject stands 'confidently in the middle of a modern city street,' a pose that immediately conveys self-assurance. The architectural element is the most visually striking part of the prompt: 'surrounded by towering skyscrapers curving inward toward the sky.' This specific directive for a 'curving inward' effect is brilliant, as it creates a funnel-like composition that draws the viewer's eye directly to the subject, while also imparting a sense of awe and scale. The wardrobe is detailed and contemporary: an oversized gray t-shirt with 'bold lettering text BRAINLOOM,' camouflage cargo shorts, a backpack, black headphones around the neck, and a water bottle in one hand. Each item contributes to a casual, urban, and slightly athletic aesthetic. The lighting is described as 'natural and dramatic,' a seemingly paradoxical phrase that asks for the realism of natural light combined with the high contrast and visual interest of dramatic skies, achieved by specifying 'clear blue skies and scattered clouds above.' Finally, the aspect ratio is locked to 9:16, the vertical format essential for emphasizing height and for platforms like Instagram Stories or TikTok. The lighting is crucial for grounding this fantastic scene in reality. 'Natural and dramatic' lighting means the primary light source is the sun, but its effect is enhanced by the 'scattered clouds,' which can act as diffusers or create dynamic patterns of light and shadow on the subject and the buildings. This interplay of light and shadow is what gives the image its cinematic quality. The proportions are guided by the 'low-angle' shot, which will exaggerate the subject's height and the skyscrapers' towering nature. The materials are varied: the soft cotton of the t-shirt, the rugged fabric of the cargo shorts, the plastic and metal of the headphones and water bottle, and the glass, steel, and concrete of the skyscrapers. The AI must render all these materials convincingly under the same lighting conditions. The composition is explicitly low-angle and vertical (9:16), forcing a perspective that prioritizes the sky and the upper reaches of the buildings. The element that must remain absolutely unchanged is, again, the subject's identity. The prompt is built around the concept of 'me' in this scene, and any deviation in facial features or physique breaks the core illusion. The wardrobe is also highly specific but offers room for variation. The promptVariations provide a fantastic toolkit for exploring different moods and settings. Changing the lighting to 'sunset or golden hour' would instantly warm the scene and create longer, more dramatic shadows. Replacing the modern skyscrapers with 'futuristic neon-lit buildings at night' would transform the image into a cyberpunk-inspired portrait. The t-shirt text can be swapped for any other brand or phrase, allowing for personalization. The cargo shorts can be modified to solid-color joggers or jeans to shift the style. Adding 'rain or wet street reflections' would introduce a whole new layer of texture and visual interest. Changing the camera angle to eye-level would create a more intimate and less imposing portrait. To achieve the highest accuracy, users must follow the howToInstructions diligently. Using a reference photo with a high image weight in Midjourney (`--iw 2`) is critical for locking in the face and physique. For Stable Diffusion, using an image-to-image workflow with a low denoising strength can help preserve the original structure while generating the new background. A common mistake is to not fully appreciate the complexity of the 'skyscrapers curving inward' effect. It might require multiple generations or even a separate AI tool for architectural rendering to get it just right. Another mistake is to overlook the interaction between the subject and the environment; the lighting on the subject must match the lighting on the buildings and sky. If the subject is lit from the front but the clouds suggest a top-down light source, the image will feel synthetic. Ultimately, this low-angle city street portrait prompt is a powerful tool for creating an image that is both personally significant and cinematically grand, transforming an ordinary person into the protagonist of their own urban story.

The Prompt 

A hyper-realistic, cinematic low-angle portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image standing confidently in the middle of a modern city street, surrounded by towering skyscrapers curving inward toward the sky. i wears a casual oversized gray t-shirt with bold lettering text BRAINLOOM, camouflage cargo shorts, and carries a backpack. Black headphones rest around his neck, and he holds a water bottle in one hand. The lighting is natural and dramatic, with clear blue skies and scattered clouds above. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt text exactly. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use high image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to lock identity and clothing details. 6. Specify low-angle view if your tool supports camera angle prompts.

Why This Prompt Works

It demands exact identity preservation and gender accuracy, solving the common AI problem of altered faces. It describes a specific architectural effect (skyscrapers curving inward) that creates a striking, cinematic frame. It names concrete wardrobe items with a custom brand text (BRAINLOOM) that AI can render. Natural lighting with blue sky anchors the scene in a believable outdoor environment.

Prompt Variations

  • Change to sunset or golden hour lighting
  • Replace skyscrapers with futuristic neon-lit buildings at night
  • Swap t-shirt text to another brand or phrase
  • Modify cargo shorts to solid color joggers or jeans
  • Add rain or wet street reflections
  • Change camera angle to eye-level or aerial
  • Replace water bottle with coffee cup or phone

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will the AI write BRAINLOOM correctly on the shirt?
    Yes, bold lettering text is clearly specified. For best results, use Midjourney v6 or DALL-E 3 which handle custom text more reliably. Generate multiple times.
  • What does 'low-angle' mean for this scene?
    Camera is positioned low, looking upward at the subject. This makes the person appear powerful and emphasizes the height of surrounding skyscrapers.
  • How do I keep my face exactly the same?
    Use reference image with high image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney). The prompt includes 'exact same facial and physical identity' and 'do according to my gender' to reinforce this.
  • Can I change the clothing colors?
    Yes, modify gray t-shirt, camouflage shorts, or backpack color while keeping the overall silhouette and prop list.
  • What does 'skyscrapers curving inward' look like?
    Buildings tilt slightly toward the center of the frame, creating a funnel or tunnel effect that draws focus to the subject. Inspired by tilt-shift or architectural photography.
