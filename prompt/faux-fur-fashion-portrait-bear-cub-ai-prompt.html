Prompt Explanation

This AI prompt is a masterclass in constructing a surreal, high-fashion narrative that blends luxury, texture, and an element of wild, adorable fantasy. Its creative purpose is to produce an image that feels both impossibly chic and utterly unique, placing a specific individual in a scenario that is part editorial fashion spread and part fairy tale. The scene, featuring a person in a voluminous burgundy faux fur coat holding a live white bear cub on their neck, is deliberately incongruous and striking. It leverages the inherent visual tension between the opulence of the fashion world, represented by the high-end coat and studio setting, and the untamed, living element of the bear cub. This juxtaposition creates an image that is immediately memorable and ripe for narrative interpretation. The creative direction is to present the subject not just as a model, but as a character in a story, someone who commands both the runway and the natural world with equal ease, all while maintaining their authentic, personal identity through the strict identity lock.The prompt text guides the AI through a complex, layered construction. It begins by setting an extremely high technical benchmark, specifying an ultra-detailed close-up photograph, ultra-photorealistic, 8K resolution, and even naming the specific camera and lens, a Hasselblad H6D-400c with a 100mm f/2.8 portrait lens. This signals to the AI that the output should mimic the highest standards of professional photography, influencing everything from color science to depth of field. The absolute identity lock is then forcefully reiterated, with exhaustive detail on what must be preserved: 100% of facial features, bone structure, proportions, expression, skin tone, and hairstyle, with emphatic prohibitions against any changes, beautification, stylization, or distortion. This is the anchor of the entire concept, ensuring the final image remains deeply personal. The composition is then meticulously choreographed: a high-angle, top-down view of the subject, who is dressed in the specified burgundy faux fur coat. The bear cub is positioned on the subject's neck, held with both hands, creating an intimate and protective gesture. The camera angle is then defined by the cub's perspective, at its eye level, meaning the subject looks up at the camera from below. This creates a fascinating dynamic where the viewer sees the scene from the cub's point of view, making the subject appear both powerful and tender. The scene is set against a clean light gray studio background, with soft studio lighting to keep the focus on the textures and forms.Lighting, proportion, and material texture are the pillars of this prompt's success. The soft studio lighting is essential for evenly illuminating the subject and the cub without creating harsh shadows that could obscure detail or create a mood that conflicts with the high-fashion, editorial style. It allows the voluminous faux fur coat to be rendered with all its plush, tactile detail and ensures the subject's skin texture is visible and natural. The proportions are carefully managed through the specified high angle and the camera's position at the cub's eye level. This forces a unique perspective that is both dynamic and intimate, making the subject's upward gaze a central point of connection with the viewer. The materials are the true stars: the burgundy faux fur coat and the live white bear cub. The prompt explicitly demands emphasis on the textures of the costume, and by extension, the cub's fur. This dual textural focus creates a rich visual contrast between the manufactured, luxurious softness of the coat and the organic, living softness of the animal. The realism level is set to ultra-photorealistic, meaning the AI must render these textures with such fidelity that they are virtually tangible, making the surreal premise of the image feel oddly, compellingly plausible.The elements that must not be changed are the core pillars of the concept. The absolute identity lock is paramount; changing the subject's appearance would break the personal connection the prompt is built on. The presence of the white bear cub and the subject wearing the burgundy faux fur coat are the central visual hooks and are non-negotiable. The compositional dynamic created by the camera being at the bear cub's eye level, with the subject looking up, is also critical, as it defines the emotional relationship between the two figures. However, many elements can be varied to create new, interesting iterations. The coat itself can be changed in color and material, such as to a black leather jacket for a tougher look or a white cashmere sweater for a softer, more minimalist aesthetic. The animal can be replaced with other creatures like a rabbit or a small dog, which would alter the narrative from one of wildness to one of domesticity and charm. The background offers a simple variable; moving from a clean light gray to a colorful gradient background could inject energy or match a specific brand palette. The lighting is another key variable; swapping soft studio lighting for dramatic rim lighting would create a more mysterious, high-contrast silhouette effect, while natural window light could make the scene feel more candid and less staged. Finally, the angle could be shifted from a high angle to an eye-level portrait or even a low angle shot, which would fundamentally change the power dynamic and the viewer's relationship to the subject.To improve accuracy and visual quality, the user must provide a pristine reference photo, as the identity lock is so stringent. The face should be clearly visible and well-lit. When generating, it is crucial to use an AI model that excels at photorealistic output and understands complex compositional instructions. For the bear cub, it may be necessary to generate several variations, as realistic animal generation can be challenging for some AI; the model might produce anatomical errors. Patience and multiple attempts are key. A common mistake is to overlook the specific instruction that the camera is at the bear cub's eye level. If this is not carefully weighted in the prompt, the AI might default to a more conventional portrait angle, losing the unique perspective. Another frequent error is in rendering the faux fur; without the emphasis on texture and the high-resolution requirements, the coat might look like a flat mass of color rather than a plush, tactile garment. Users should ensure their chosen AI model is capable of rendering complex textures. Finally, the instruction that the subject is holding it with both hands is important for creating a sense of security and preventing the cub from looking like it's merely perched precariously. By respecting the detailed composition and focusing on the textural and identity-locking elements, this prompt can consistently produce a stunning and utterly original piece of high-fashion art.

The Prompt 

Ultra-detailed close-up photograph, ultra-photorealistic, 8K resolution, shot on a Hasselblad H6D-400c medium format digital camera with a 100mm f/2.8 studio portrait lens. Flawless sharpness, extreme detail, and cinematic depth of field. Absolute identity lock: of me from the uploaded image strictly preserve 100% of the original facial features, bone structure, proportions, expression, skin tone, and hairstyle exactly as in the first attached reference photo — no changes, no beautification, no stylization, no distortion .. Strictly preserve as in the first attached photo, without distortion. Shot from a high angle looking down, top-down view. i dressed in a voluminous faux fur coat of burgundy color. A live white bear cub is sitting on my neck. i holding it with both hands. The camera is at the eye level of the bear cub, i looks up at the camera from below. Clean light gray studio background. High fashion, editorial style, soft studio lighting, sharp focus, 8K resolution. Emphasis on facial details, skin texture, as well as the textures of the costume.

How to Use This Prompt

Copy this prompt into your AI image generator. Upload your reference photo when prompted. The 'identity lock' instruction will help maintain your exact appearance. Adjust 'i' to 'man' or 'woman' based on your subject. The prompt creates a high-fashion studio portrait with specific camera and lighting details.

Why This Prompt Works

Specific camera mentions (Hasselblad H6D-400c, 100mm lens) signal high-quality photography to AI models. 'Absolute identity lock' phrasing helps maintain facial consistency when using reference images. Clear compositional details ('camera at eye level of bear cub') create consistent perspective. Fashion terminology ('editorial style', 'high fashion') produces professional-looking results.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'burgundy faux fur coat' to 'black leather jacket' or 'white cashmere sweater'
  • Replace 'white bear cub' with 'rabbit' or 'small dog' for different animals
  • Try 'colorful gradient background' instead of 'light gray studio background'
  • Swap 'soft studio lighting' for 'dramatic rim lighting' or 'natural window light'
  • Change 'high angle' to 'eye-level portrait' or 'low angle shot'

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'identity lock' mean in AI prompts?
    Identity lock tells the AI to preserve exact facial features from your reference photo without changing, beautifying, or stylizing the person's appearance.
  • Can I use this prompt without uploading a photo?
    The prompt works best with a reference image. Without one, change 'me' to 'a person' and remove 'identity lock' instructions for a generic fashion portrait.
  • Which AI tools work with animal portraits like bear cubs?
    Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion all handle animal portraits well. Some may require careful prompting to get realistic animals.
  • How important is mentioning Hasselblad camera in the prompt?
    Mentioning high-end camera brands helps AI generate professional-quality images with accurate color grading and sharpness that mimics premium photography equipment.
