The Prompt 

Ultra-detailed close-up photograph, ultra-photorealistic, 8K resolution, shot on a Hasselblad H6D-400c medium format digital camera with a 100mm f/2.8 studio portrait lens. Flawless sharpness, extreme detail, and cinematic depth of field. Absolute identity lock: of me from the uploaded image strictly preserve 100% of the original facial features, bone structure, proportions, expression, skin tone, and hairstyle exactly as in the first attached reference photo — no changes, no beautification, no stylization, no distortion .. Strictly preserve as in the first attached photo, without distortion. Shot from a high angle looking down, top-down view. i dressed in a voluminous faux fur coat of burgundy color. A live white bear cub is sitting on my neck. i holding it with both hands. The camera is at the eye level of the bear cub, i looks up at the camera from below. Clean light gray studio background. High fashion, editorial style, soft studio lighting, sharp focus, 8K resolution. Emphasis on facial details, skin texture, as well as the textures of the costume.

How to Use This Prompt

Copy this prompt into your AI image generator. Upload your reference photo when prompted. The 'identity lock' instruction will help maintain your exact appearance. Adjust 'i' to 'man' or 'woman' based on your subject. The prompt creates a high-fashion studio portrait with specific camera and lighting details.

Why This Prompt Works

Specific camera mentions (Hasselblad H6D-400c, 100mm lens) signal high-quality photography to AI models. 'Absolute identity lock' phrasing helps maintain facial consistency when using reference images. Clear compositional details ('camera at eye level of bear cub') create consistent perspective. Fashion terminology ('editorial style', 'high fashion') produces professional-looking results.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'burgundy faux fur coat' to 'black leather jacket' or 'white cashmere sweater'
  • Replace 'white bear cub' with 'rabbit' or 'small dog' for different animals
  • Try 'colorful gradient background' instead of 'light gray studio background'
  • Swap 'soft studio lighting' for 'dramatic rim lighting' or 'natural window light'
  • Change 'high angle' to 'eye-level portrait' or 'low angle shot'

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'identity lock' mean in AI prompts?
    Identity lock tells the AI to preserve exact facial features from your reference photo without changing, beautifying, or stylizing the person's appearance.
  • Can I use this prompt without uploading a photo?
    The prompt works best with a reference image. Without one, change 'me' to 'a person' and remove 'identity lock' instructions for a generic fashion portrait.
  • Which AI tools work with animal portraits like bear cubs?
    Midjourney, DALL-E 3, and Stable Diffusion all handle animal portraits well. Some may require careful prompting to get realistic animals.
  • How important is mentioning Hasselblad camera in the prompt?
    Mentioning high-end camera brands helps AI generate professional-quality images with accurate color grading and sharpness that mimics premium photography equipment.
