Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a comprehensive template for generating a full-body, hyper-realistic portrait that sits at the intersection of street style and high-fashion editorial photography. Its creative purpose is to place the user within a specific aesthetic world, defined by avant-garde clothing, a moody atmosphere, and professional-grade photographic techniques. The prompt_text acts as a detailed creative brief, instructing the AI on every aspect from personalization to the minutiae of fabric texture. It opens with the essential personalization command: hyper-realistic DSLR cinematic full-body portrait of me with exact same facial and physical features. This anchors the entire generation to the user's likeness. The description of the subject's stance—a confident, dynamic pose captured in natural yet dramatic lighting—sets the tone for a powerful, unposed-yet-stylized look. The clothing description is the heart of the street-fashion aesthetic. It is incredibly detailed, specifying an oversized distressed dark brown and beige patchwork knit sweater with contrasting thick stitching, intentional worn holes, and long hanging cords. This is not just a sweater; it is a complex garment with specific textures, colors, and deconstructed elements that define the raw, avant-garde look. Pairing it with black relaxed-fit cargo pants grounds the outfit in streetwear functionality. The inclusion of black over-ear gloomyboy style headphones with a visible wired connection and a minimal metal ring adds layers of character and subcultural reference, making the portrait feel authentic and grounded in a specific style tribe. The prompt then layers on the photographic and atmospheric qualities: editorial street-fashion aesthetic with ultra-detailed fabric textures, realistic skin tones, subtle film grain, cinematic color grading, shallow depth of field, high contrast, and a moody yet stylish atmosphere. These instructions collectively push the AI to simulate a professional photo shoot. Shallow depth of field isolates the subject, cinematic color grading creates a unified color palette, and subtle film grain adds a layer of tactile realism that counteracts digital sterility. The lighting is described as natural yet dramatic, suggesting the use of available light manipulated to create contrast and depth, which is perfect for the moody atmosphere. Elements that must not be changed are the core personalization (the face), the level of detail expected in the clothing (ultra-detailed fabric textures), and the overall moody, high-contrast, cinematic style. Removing these would collapse the specific aesthetic the prompt is built to create. However, the individual components of the outfit and the setting are highly variable. Changing the oversized distressed sweater for a tailored suit or a leather jacket would completely shift the character from streetwear aficionado to something else entirely. Swapping the moody atmosphere for bright sunny lighting would transform the image from dark and introspective to light and energetic. Replacing the cargo pants with tailored trousers would tilt the style from street fashion toward a more polished, smart-casual look. Practical tips for improving accuracy include using a photorealistic checkpoint in Stable Diffusion, such as Juggernaut XL or Realistic Vision, which are specifically trained to render detailed textures like knitwear and skin. In Midjourney, using the --stylize parameter at a lower value can sometimes help adhere more strictly to the literal description of the clothing. A common mistake is to be too general about the clothing; simply saying cool sweater is insufficient. The prompt's power comes from its specificity about patchwork, distressing, and drawstrings. Another error is to overlook the importance of the headphones and ring, which are small but crucial details that add authenticity to the street-fashion narrative. By meticulously combining personal identity with a hyper-detailed fashion concept and professional photographic language, this prompt successfully guides the AI to generate images that are not just portraits, but fully realized style editorials.

The Prompt 

Create a hyper-realistic DSLR cinematic full-body portrait of me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image with sharp, well-defined facial features and a confident, dynamic pose. i captured in natural yet dramatic lighting with cinematic depth of field, crisp focus, and professional DSLR clarity.i wears an oversized distressed dark brown and beige patchwork knit sweater with contrasting thick stitching, intentional worn holes, and long hanging cords or drawstrings for a raw, avant-garde look, paired withblack relaxed-fit cargo pants. i has black over-ear “gloomyboy” style headphones with a clearly visible wired connection and a minimal metal ring on my hand. Editorial street-fashion aesthetic with ultra-detailed fabric textures, realistic skin tones, subtle film grain, cinematic color grading, shallow depth of field, high contrast, and a moody yet stylish atmosphere. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the full prompt text into your AI image generator (like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E 3). If your tool supports it, upload a reference photo of yourself when prompted. The AI will use your facial features to create a cinematic fashion portrait matching the detailed clothing and lighting description.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it uses specific photography terms like 'DSLR cinematic', 'shallow depth of field', and 'cinematic color grading' which AI models recognize as styles. Listing detailed clothing items like 'distressed patchwork knit sweater' and 'gloomyboy headphones' gives the AI clear visual anchors to build a coherent, detailed image with a consistent editorial aesthetic.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'moody atmosphere' to 'bright sunny lighting' for a different feel.
  • Swap 'street fashion' for 'high fashion runway' to change the context.
  • Replace 'cargo pants' with 'tailored trousers' or 'denim jeans'.
  • Try 'studio lighting with a solid color backdrop' instead of natural light.
  • Add 'looking directly at the camera with a smile' to alter the expression and pose.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to upload a photo to use this prompt?
    For the most accurate personalization, yes. The prompt is designed to work with an uploaded reference image to copy your facial features. However, you can still use it without one to generate a generic model.
  • Which AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    Midjourney and DALL-E 3 are excellent for this style due to their strong understanding of photographic styles and detailed textures. Stable Diffusion can also produce great results, especially with a photorealistic model checkpoint.
  • How do I get the clothing details right?
    The prompt is very specific. If the AI misses a detail like 'hanging cords' or 'patchwork', try rephrasing that part or adding weight to those keywords (e.g., putting them in quotes or using '::' emphasis in some tools).
