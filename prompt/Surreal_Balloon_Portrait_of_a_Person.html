How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into caricature art, toy designs, or surreal portraits for animation and collectibles. Preserve "surreal 3D render," "(PERSON)," "caricature face inflated like a balloon," and "Pixar-level detailing" for playful uncanny. Do not modify the glossy shine or cinematic DOF. Optionally vary expression; for example, surprised could yield wide eyes, leading to knot ties, while pouty keeps sculptural, poster-realism outcomes.
