Blue and Magenta Split Lighting Portrait Black Turtleneck AI Prompt

AI Image Prompts 2026-02-12 Brainloom

Generate an ultra-realistic cinematic portrait with dramatic blue and magenta split lighting. Subject wears black turtleneck and dark jacket against deep blue gradient background. High-end editorial lighting, 9:16.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic cinematic medium close-up portrait of the reference person, slightly turned to the side while looking toward the camera. Wearing a black turtleneck and dark jacket, modern and minimalist fashion style. Deep blue background with a smooth gradient, combined with contrasting magenta and blue lighting. Strong blue light coming from one side and magenta light from the opposite side, both colors reflecting clearly on the face and skin, creating a dramatic color contrast. High-end editorial lighting, sharp focus, realistic skin texture, cinematic mood, shallow depth of field. aspect ration 9:16

How to Use

1. Upload your reference photo as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use high image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to preserve facial identity. 6. Generate multiple times to achieve clean color separation on skin.

FAQ

  • What is split lighting with colored gels?
    One side of the face is lit with blue light, the opposite side with magenta light. The colors mix subtly across the nose and cheekbones. Common in high-fashion and music portraiture.
  • Will the blue background match the blue lighting?
    The prompt specifies 'deep blue background with a smooth gradient' and 'contrasting magenta and blue lighting'. Background blue should be complementary but not identical to the key light blue.
  • What is 'shallow depth of field' here?
    Face and shoulders in sharp focus, background slightly soft. Separates subject from gradient background. Typical of cinematic portraiture.
