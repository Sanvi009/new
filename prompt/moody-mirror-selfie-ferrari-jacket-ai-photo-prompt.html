The Prompt 

A moody cinematic mirror selfie of me use image for face reference with the same facial and pysical feature from image, wearing a black Ferrari racing jacket with red and yellow stripes and motorsport patches. i have a silver chain necklace and multiple rings, holding a modern smartphone in one hand. How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt text into your AI image generator. For the face to look like you, first upload a clear reference photo of yourself. The AI will use that photo to match your facial features in this cinematic mirror selfie scene.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt combines specific fashion elements ('Ferrari racing jacket'), a detailed environment ('dark elevator interior'), and professional photography techniques ('low-key dramatic lighting', 'shallow depth of field', '35mm'). This gives the AI clear visual cues to create a cohesive, stylized portrait with a strong mood.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'Ferrari racing jacket' to 'leather biker jacket' or 'designer hoodie'.
  • Replace 'elevator interior' with 'dirty public bathroom' or 'neon-lit hallway' for a different vibe.
  • Use 'looking at phone screen' instead of 'holding a smartphone'.
  • Try 'vibrant color grading' or 'black and white' instead of 'cinematic color grading'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make the AI use my face in the picture?
    You need to upload a clear photo of your face as an image reference when using this prompt in your AI generator (like Midjourney's /describe or Stable Diffusion's img2img). The prompt instructs the AI to use that reference.
  • What AI tools work best for this kind of portrait prompt?
    Midjourney, Stable Diffusion with a good checkpoint model, and DALL-E 3 can all produce excellent results. For accurate facial features, tools that support strong image referencing are best.
  • Can I change the clothing or background?
    Yes, you can edit any part of the prompt. Swap 'Ferrari jacket' for another item or change 'elevator' to a different location to create a new style of mirror selfie.
