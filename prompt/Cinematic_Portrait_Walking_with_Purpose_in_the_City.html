How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it in motivational imagery, action film posters, or fitness branding for dynamic urban scenes. Implement it with uploaded faces, preserving "portrait of me," "walking forward with purpose," "wind catching my coat," and "cinematic lighting" for powerful energy. Do not change the focused eyes or city background. Changing coat to "leather jacket" could yield edgy visuals, resulting in heroic strides, while "trench coat" might create detective-themed shots with determined, high-impact outcomes.

Prompt

Portrait of me walking forward with purpose, wind catching my coat, urban city street in the background, cinematic lighting, focused eyes, power in every step same face

Why this prompt works

This prompt is effective due to its dynamic action and urban cinematic style the image of walking forward with purpose wind catching the coat and focused eyes conveys determination and energy the city street background with cinematic lighting adds context and drama making the subject appear powerful and goal-oriented the instruction to keep the same face ensures consistency for personal branding this type of portrait is ideal for professional profiles motivational content or storytelling that requires a strong narrative of movement and ambition.
