Prompt Explanation

This prompt is engineered for one primary creative purpose: to transform a standard personal photo into a powerful, high-impact professional headshot that exudes authority and sophistication. It moves beyond a simple portrait by incorporating dramatic, almost theatrical lighting and composition techniques typically found in high-fashion photography or executive portraiture. The goal is to create an image that is not just a record of what someone looks like, but a statement of their persona—confident, dominant, and elegantly powerful. This makes it ideal for use in professional contexts like LinkedIn, corporate websites, or personal branding materials where making a strong first impression is key. The prompt achieves this by focusing on a very specific interplay of elements. The 'stark cinematic lighting and intense contrast' are the primary drivers of the mood. This isn't flat, even lighting; it's light that carves out the subject's features, creating deep shadows and bright highlights that add drama and dimension. The 'slightly low, upward-facing angle' is a deliberate compositional choice that literally elevates the subject, making them appear more powerful and commanding, while simultaneously dramatizing the jawline and neck for a sculptural, almost heroic quality. This angle, combined with the lighting, works to create the described feeling of 'dominance and sculptural elegance.' The background, a 'deep, saturated crimson red,' is not an afterthought. It's a crucial element chosen for its psychological and visual impact. Red is a color of power, passion, and intensity. Its saturation creates a bold, unapologetic backdrop that makes the subject, with their 'luminous skin and sleek black suit,' pop forward with incredible vibrancy. The clash between the warm, intense red and the subject's skin tones and dark clothing is what creates the image's dynamic energy. The instruction 'DON’T CHANGE ANY FACIAL FEATURES' is the absolute, non-negotiable core of this prompt. It is a portrait, and its entire value depends on the subject being instantly and perfectly recognizable. Any deviation in the facial features renders the image useless for its intended purpose. This command must be respected above all else. While the face is sacred, other elements offer creative flexibility. The prompt variations smartly illustrate this: changing the background to 'deep navy blue' would transform the image from passionate intensity to calm, corporate trustworthiness. An 'emerald green background with gold lighting accents' could evoke a sense of luxury and growth. Even the outfit can be modified from a 'sleek black suit' to a 'white shirt with black blazer,' which would increase the contrast against the red background and create a sharper, more modern look. Adding 'subtle smoke or mist' could introduce an atmospheric, almost mysterious depth, though this should be used sparingly to avoid detracting from the clean, professional aesthetic. For practical tips to improve accuracy, the advice to use a high-resolution reference photo is paramount. The AI needs clear data to work with. In Midjourney, using a high image weight (`--iw 2`) will heavily prioritize the reference photo's features. In Stable Diffusion, enabling the 'restore faces' feature after upscaling can correct any minor distortions. A common mistake is using a photo with poor lighting or an expression that doesn't match the desired 'dominant' vibe. The reference photo should ideally have a neutral or slightly serious expression to align with the prompt's tone. Another mistake is not specifying the desired aspect ratio (1080x1920) if a specific platform requires it. By meticulously combining technical photography terms with a bold color palette and an unyielding demand for facial likeness, this prompt consistently delivers a professional headshot that is both dramatically striking and personally authentic.

The Prompt 

Please create a vertical potrait shot in 1080x1920 format of me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image  using the exact same face features, characterized by stark cinematic lighting and intense contrast. Captured in a slightly low, upward-facing angle that dramatized the subject’s jawline and neck, the composition evokes quite dominance and sculptural elegance. The background is a deep, saturated crimson red, creating a bold visual clash with the model’s luminous skin and sleek black suit. DON’T CHANGE ANY FACIAL FEATURES.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create a dramatic portrait with crimson background. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. Use high-quality reference photos for best facial matching.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt specifies technical photography terms like 'stark cinematic lighting', 'intense contrast', and 'low upward-facing angle' that AI models understand. 'Deep saturated crimson red' creates a strong visual element, while 'exact same facial features' ensures likeness preservation.

Prompt Variations

  • Change background to deep navy blue for corporate look
  • Try emerald green background with gold lighting accents
  • Create monochrome version with black and white high contrast
  • Add subtle smoke or mist effect for atmospheric depth
  • Change outfit to white shirt with black blazer for contrast

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What makes this good for professional headshots?
    The dramatic lighting, clean crimson background, and professional black suit create authoritative business portraits suitable for LinkedIn and professional profiles.
  • How do I ensure the facial features match exactly?
    Use high-resolution reference photos with clear facial details. DALL-E 3 and Midjourney v6 have excellent facial matching capabilities.
  • Can I change the suit color?
    Yes, replace 'sleek black suit' with your preferred color like 'navy blue suit' or 'charcoal grey suit' in the prompt.
