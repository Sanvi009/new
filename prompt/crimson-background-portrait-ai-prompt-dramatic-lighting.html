An AI image prompt that creates dramatic professional portraits with intense contrast, cinematic lighting, and deep crimson backgrounds using your reference photo.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Please create a vertical potrait shot in 1080x1920 format of me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image  using the exact same face features, characterized by stark cinematic lighting and intense contrast. Captured in a slightly low, upward-facing angle that dramatized the subject’s jawline and neck, the composition evokes quite dominance and sculptural elegance. The background is a deep, saturated crimson red, creating a bold visual clash with the model’s luminous skin and sleek black suit. DON’T CHANGE ANY FACIAL FEATURES.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create a dramatic portrait with crimson background. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. Use high-quality reference photos for best facial matching.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt specifies technical photography terms like 'stark cinematic lighting', 'intense contrast', and 'low upward-facing angle' that AI models understand. 'Deep saturated crimson red' creates a strong visual element, while 'exact same facial features' ensures likeness preservation.

Prompt Variations

  • Try emerald green background with gold lighting accents
  • Create monochrome version with black and white high contrast
  • Add subtle smoke or mist effect for atmospheric depth
  • Change outfit to white shirt with black blazer for contrast

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What makes this good for professional headshots?
    The dramatic lighting, clean crimson background, and professional black suit create authoritative business portraits suitable for LinkedIn and professional profiles.
  • How do I ensure the facial features match exactly?
    Use high-resolution reference photos with clear facial details. DALL-E 3 and Midjourney v6 have excellent facial matching capabilities.
  • Can I change the suit color?
    Yes, replace 'sleek black suit' with your preferred color like 'navy blue suit' or 'charcoal grey suit' in the prompt.
