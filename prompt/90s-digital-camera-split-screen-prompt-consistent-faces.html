Create three vertical split-screen photos with identical faces using 1990s digital camera aesthetics including harsh flash, red eye, and CCD sensor look.

The Prompt 

Use the reference photo as the face guide. The face must remain 100 percent identical to the reference. Do not change facial structure, proportions, base expression, or any facial details.
Vertical split screen composition. Three images stacked vertically from top to bottom. Seamless edge to edge layout. No borders. No padding. Aspect ratio 9:16.
Top panel. Wide shot. Female subject and male subject posing like runway models. Wearing the exact same outfits as in the uploaded photo. The female subject crosses her arms with a stoic expression. The male subject is smoking, with visible smoke coming out. Cool fit vibe. Hard direct on camera flash. Red eye effect visible. In the background, there is a small detail of the number 69 naturally blended into the scene, such as within wall texture or room objects. Subtle and not noticeable.
Middle panel. Extreme close up macro mode selfie. The male subject wears black sunglasses with a slight smile. The female subject laughs excitedly. Noses appear distorted. Goofy expressions. Heads pressed together. Forehead appears overexposed due to flash. The number 69 is subtly hidden within light reflections or digital noise. Difficult to notice.
Bottom panel. Full body shot. The male subject stands casually with both hands on his hips. The female subject stands beside him, holding the male subject’s arm with both hands in an affectionate and excited way. Posters of well known bands are attached to the wall and appear worn. One of the posters includes a small number 69 as part of its design. Feels natural. Chaotic and playful atmosphere.
Visual specifications. Sony Cyber shot and Canon PowerShot A400 aesthetic. CCD sensor look. Early 1990s digital camera style. Direct on camera flash. Harsh shadows. Low resolution. Visible digital noise. High contrast JPEG. Oversharpened look. Slightly red skin tones. Blooming highlights. Messy bedroom background. Trashy Y2K vibe.
Avoid semi realism. Avoid CGI and 3D render. Avoid smooth skin and beauty filters. No doll like or plastic appearance. Avoid perfect symmetry. Do not use studio lighting or magazine editorial style. No retouching. Avoid soft glam. No perfect skin. Not stock photography. Avoid hyper realistic style, digital art, HDR, or render engines. Avoid bad anatomy. No extra fingers or fused limbs. No floating objects. No facial distortion that alters identity. Avoid overly clean composition.
Do not use black or white bars. No borders, panel lines, grid lines, or comic style separators.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a reference photo of your subject's face to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste this prompt. 3. The AI will create three different photos stacked vertically while keeping the face identical. 4. Use in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL·E with reference image features enabled.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt uses specific technical photography terms (CCD sensor, direct flash, blooming highlights) that AI recognizes as distinct visual styles. The detailed scene descriptions help maintain consistent facial features across different poses by providing clear context for each panel.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'messy bedroom' to 'mall food court' or 'amusement park' for different Y2K backgrounds
  • Replace 'band posters' with 'video game posters' or 'movie posters'
  • Try 'polaroid camera aesthetic' instead of 'digital camera' for film grain
  • Change lighting to 'disposable camera flash' or 'club lighting'
  • Swap 'stoic expression' for 'smiling' or 'laughing' in different panels
