Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a highly specific, narrative-driven cinematic portrait that blends photorealism with a stylized cyberpunk gangster aesthetic. Its creative purpose is to produce an image that feels like a still from a high-budget film, focusing on a character who exudes a dangerous, confident coolness. The setting, a dark underground parking garage, immediately establishes a sense of isolation, grit, and urban tension, which is a classic trope in action and crime cinema. By placing a stylishly dressed man with a spiked metal bat next to a rugged black pickup truck, the prompt creates a story of a lone figure, perhaps a protector or an antagonist, ready for a confrontation. The combination of high-end fashion elements with a weapon and a gritty location is what defines the cyberpunk gangster vibe, suggesting a world where style and violence coexist. The prompt text meticulously guides the AI by layering specific visual instructions. It begins with the core requirement of 'ultra-realistic 8K cinematic portrait,' setting the bar for image quality and style. It then anchors the scene with concrete nouns: a dark underground parking garage, a rugged black pickup truck, a black leather jacket, sunglasses, and a spiked metal bat. This leaves little room for the AI to interpret the core elements incorrectly. The subsequent descriptive phrases—'moody dramatic lighting,' 'soft shadows,' 'detailed textures'—act as modifiers that tell the AI how to render these elements. They instruct the AI to consider the light source, the quality of shadows, and the tactile feel of the leather and metal, which is crucial for achieving the desired hyper-detailed cinematic shot. The instruction for 'DSLR depth of field' is a critical technical component. It tells the AI to simulate a shallow focus, where the subject is sharp and the background blurs slightly. This technique is essential in cinematic portraits to draw the viewer's eye directly to the character and create a sense of three-dimensional space, separating him from the garage environment. The lighting described as 'moody dramatic' suggests a single, strong light source, perhaps from overhead or the side, creating deep shadows that sculpt the subject's face and form, emphasizing the textures of his outfit and the vehicle. The softness of the shadows prevents the image from becoming harsh, maintaining a stylish, polished look even within a dark setting. The element that must absolutely not be changed is the subject's face. The prompt explicitly ends with '100% face matching,' and the instructions reiterate uploading a reference photo. This is non-negotiable for a portrait intended to represent a specific person. Altering the facial features would defeat the entire purpose of the prompt. The clothing, while integral to the style, offers some flexibility. The prompt variations suggest replacing the 'black leather jacket' with a 'dark trench coat,' which would shift the aesthetic from a classic biker-gangster to a more mysterious, neo-noir detective feel. Similarly, swapping the 'spiked metal bat' for a 'glowing neon sword' would push the image further into pure science fiction cyberpunk, altering the character from a gritty street fighter to a futuristic warrior. Changing the setting from the 'underground parking garage' to a 'dimly lit urban alley' maintains the gritty urban feel but changes the geometry and context of the scene, perhaps making it feel more claustrophobic and immediate. For practical tips, the instructions are already excellent. Using the correct aspect ratio parameters like `--ar 2:3` in Midjourney is vital for the vertical cinematic framing. In Stable Diffusion, enabling face restoration and using a high-resolution fix will be key to achieving the '8K' detail and perfect facial likeness. A common mistake to avoid is a poorly lit reference photo; the uploaded image should be well-lit and clear for the AI to capture the facial structure accurately. Another mistake is not using negative prompts to prevent common AI artifacts, such as extra fingers on the hand holding the bat or distorted proportions of the truck. Fine-tuning the prompt by specifying the exact type of 'moody' lighting, such as 'light from a single fluorescent tube above,' could also yield more controlled and interesting results, further enhancing the underground parking garage atmosphere and the overall cinematic portrait quality.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic 8K cinematic portrait of a stylish young man in a dark underground parking garage, wearing a black leather jacket, black outfit, and sunglasses. He is crouched down confidently next to a rugged black pickup truck, holding a spiked metal bat on the ground. Moody dramatic lighting, soft shadows, detailed textures on clothes and car, cyberpunk gangster vibe, DSLR depth of field, hyper-detailed cinematic shot. 100% face matching.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload the reference photo to maintain exact facial features. Copy the prompt into your AI generator. Maintain a vertical or cinematic framing for full-body presence. For Midjourney, add `--ar 2:3 --v 5 --q 2`. For Stable Diffusion, ensure face restoration is on and enable high detail for clothing and background textures. Adjust lighting and shadows for moody cinematic effects, and fine-tune negative prompts to avoid extra limbs or distorted objects.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines a precise reference face with cinematic composition, dramatic lighting, and rich textural details. The crouched pose, underground parking garage setting, and cyberpunk gangster aesthetic give a strong sense of mood and storytelling. Specifying props and clothing ensures the AI output aligns with the intended edgy, stylish look.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace 'black leather jacket' with 'dark trench coat' for a mysterious neo-noir style.
  • Replace 'underground parking garage' with 'dimly lit urban alley' for a street-gang vibe.
  • Replace 'spiked metal bat' with 'glowing neon sword' for a cyberpunk futuristic effect.
  • Replace 'crouched down confidently' with 'standing with arms crossed' for a commanding full-body pose.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I maintain exact facial resemblance in the AI output?
    Always upload a high-quality reference photo and enable face restoration in your generator. Mention '100% face matching' in the prompt to prioritize likeness.
  • Can I adapt this portrait for different props or settings?
    Yes. You can swap the bat with other items, change lighting, or alter the background while keeping the face reference intact. Adjust prompts to maintain cinematic mood and style.
