Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create an epic, personalized movie poster in the distinctive and dramatic style of South Indian cinema. Its creative purpose is to place an ordinary person into the larger-than-life world of a film hero, combining an intense, emotional close-up with a dynamic action sequence to craft a narrative of power, drama, and personal mythology. The prompt_text is a masterfully structured directive that functions like a film director's shot list, breaking down the complex poster into manageable, composable layers. The most dominant feature of the text is its use of emphatic, locking language: "exact same facial and physical appearance," "100% exact face," "no changes at all," "same hairstyle," "same riding pose," and the repeated "— no change." This is a crucial strategy to combat the AI's tendency to invent or genericize, forcing it to adhere strictly to the provided reference image. The prompt is then divided into clear sections: Main Portrait, Secondary Scene, Background, Lighting & Color, and Style & Quality. This structure is vital for guiding the AI to assemble a coherent multi-scene poster. The main portrait is an extreme close-up side profile with a serious, emotional expression, which is a classic trope of South Indian posters, conveying intensity and inner conflict. The specific outfit of a "Deep Blue Coat with white t shirt" is a fixed element that adds to the character's visual identity. The secondary scene places the subject on a classic vintage motorcycle riding downhill with dramatic dust, injecting action and energy into the lower frame, a common layout for such posters. The background is locked to a specific landscape: a cloudy, muted sky over mountain terrain, providing an epic, cinematic scale. The lighting and color are precisely defined with "earth-tone cinematic color grading," "high contrast," and a "film-grain movie look," which are the visual hallmarks of the South Indian film aesthetic, giving the image a gritty, realistic, and timeless quality. The style commands of "hyper-realistic skin texture," "premium VFX," and "16K ultra-detailed UHD render" push the AI to deliver a final image with the polish and impact of a real movie poster. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's core identity, the specific blue coat and white t-shirt, and the fundamental layout of a foreground portrait with a background action scene. The variations show how the poster's entire genre can be shifted. Changing "South-Indian cinematic" to "Hollywood action" would likely result in a cleaner, more color-graded (teal and orange) aesthetic with a different typographic style, losing the specific cultural and textural feel of Indian cinema. Swapping the vintage motorcycle for a horse transforms the scene from a gritty road chase to something more classical, heroic, or even rustic. To improve accuracy, users must provide a reference photo that clearly shows the desired hairstyle and, if possible, the coat. A common mistake is not using a high enough image weight in the AI tool, which can cause the AI to blend the subject's features with those of a generic 'hero' model. This prompt's power lies in its rigid, almost contractual language that locks the subject's identity while simultaneously providing a rich, detailed template for the AI to fill with the epic visual language of South Indian cinematic posters, creating a personalized piece of art that is both a portrait and a fantasy.

Ultra-realistic 16K UHD cinematic movie-poster of me with exact same facial and physical appearance and structure from the uploaded image 
(use my uploaded photo for 100% exact face, skin tone, hair match — no changes at all)
Signature: I am me
Main Portrait (Foreground):
• Extreme close-up side profile portrait, looking intensely downward
• Serious, deep, emotional expression
• Same hairstyle, same style
• Deep Blue Coat with white t shirt outfit and texture — no change
Secondary Scene (Bottom Frame):
me riding a classic vintage motorcycle downhill
• Dust and soil flying dramatically
• Same riding pose, same angle — no change
Background:
• Same cloudy, muted cinematic sky
• Same mountain terrain and depth
• Epic scale poster composition — no change
Lighting & Color:
• Soft dramatic highlights on face
• Earth-tone cinematic color grading
• High contrast, film-grain movie look
Style & Quality:
• South-Indian cinematic movie poster
• Hyper-realistic skin texture
• Ultra-sharp focus, premium VFX
• 16K ultra-detailed UHD render
do according to my gender
aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

First, upload a clear photo of your face to your AI image generator. Copy and paste the entire prompt text. The AI will use your uploaded photo to create your exact face and features in a dramatic South Indian-style movie poster with a portrait and action scene.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it uses very specific, directive language ('no change', 'same', 'exact same') to lock the AI onto the features from your uploaded reference. Terms like 'South-Indian cinematic movie poster', 'film-grain movie look', and 'premium VFX' are strong style indicators AI models recognize. The structured layout description (Foreground, Secondary Scene, Background) helps the AI compose the complex poster correctly.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'South-Indian cinematic' to 'Bollywood style' or 'Hollywood action' for a different film aesthetic.
  • Swap 'classic vintage motorcycle' for 'sports bike' or 'horse' to change the action element.
  • Replace 'earth-tone color grading' with 'teal and orange grading' or 'vibrant colorful grading'.
  • Change 'mountain terrain' background to 'city skyline' or 'beach sunset'.
  • Use 'front-facing portrait' instead of 'side profile' for a different look.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the AI to copy my face exactly?
    You must use the image upload feature in your AI tool (like Midjourney's /describe or Stable Diffusion's img2img). Upload a clear, front-facing photo before running the prompt.
  • What does 'South-Indian cinematic movie poster' style mean?
    It refers to the dramatic, high-contrast, often epic poster style from Indian Tamil, Telugu, or Malayalam films, known for intense portraits and dynamic action scenes.
  • Can I use this for a commercial project like a real movie?
    AI-generated images may have copyright restrictions. For a commercial movie poster, consult the terms of your AI generator and consider involving a graphic designer for final artwork.
