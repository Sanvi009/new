FAQ

  • How do I use my own photo with this prompt?
    To use your own image, upload it to your chosen AI image generator that supports image prompts, such as Midjourney (using image URLs with --cref or --sref) or Stable Diffusion (using img2img). The prompt includes '[UPLOAD YOUR IMAGE]' as a placeholder. Replace that with your uploaded image reference, and the AI will maintain your facial and physical identity while generating the scene with the floating holographic elements.
  • What does 'glassmorphism design' mean in this context?
    Glassmorphism is a modern UI design trend characterized by translucent, frosted-glass-like elements with subtle shadows, soft borders, and a blurred background effect. In this prompt, it describes the holographic music player interfaces that should appear as semi-transparent panels with 'soft rounded corners, subtle blur transparency, and glowing orange edges,' creating a sleek, futuristic augmented reality aesthetic popular in current app design.
  • How can I achieve the best results with the floating holographic elements?
    For optimal results, ensure your prompt emphasizes depth layering and positioning by specifying that the UI panels 'float at different depths around the subject.' Include technical terms like 'Octane render' and 'Unreal Engine 5' which signal high-quality 3D rendering. Use a shallow depth of field to keep the subject sharp while slightly blurring the background, making the holographic elements appear naturally integrated into the 3D space around the person.
