How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for drink commercials, digital billboards, or social media ads in beverage marketing. Feed it into tools with "--ar 16:9," preserving "hyper-realistic close-up," "Choco Malt drink bottle exploding," "condensation," and "bold text: 'Choco Refreshing Malt'" for energetic aesthetics. Do not change the orange background or studio-lit style. Changing text to "Mint Fresh" could yield cooling visuals, resulting in splash effects, while original creates fiery, high-resolution outcomes.

Prompt

"Hyper-realistic close-up of a Choco Malt drink bottle exploding with ice-cold splash, condensation covering the surface, bright orange background with fiery splash effects. Add bold text: 'Choco Refreshing Malt' in playful typography. Studio-lit commercial shot, high-resolution, ultra-detailed, 8k --ar 16:9"

Why this prompt works

This prompt is highly effective for creating a refreshing and energetic commercial shot the exploding bottle with ice-cold splash and condensation emphasizes the drink’s coldness and vitality the bright orange background with fiery effects adds excitement and boldness that can attract attention the playful typography reinforces the brand message making the image cohesive and promotional this setup is ideal for beverage advertising point-of-sale displays or digital campaigns that need to highlight refreshment and fun.
