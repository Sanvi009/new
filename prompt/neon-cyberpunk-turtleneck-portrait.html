Prompt Explanation

This prompt is masterfully crafted to generate a stylized, cinematic portrait that merges the subject's exact likeness with a distinct neon cyberpunk aesthetic. Its creative purpose is to transform a standard personal photo into a piece of modern, almost editorial fashion art, placing the individual within a moody, futuristic world defined by vibrant color and dramatic lighting. The prompt_text is exceptionally detailed, functioning as a comprehensive checklist for the AI. It begins with the non-negotiable foundation: the subject's exact facial and physical identity must be preserved. This instruction to match the uploaded image accurately, including natural facial structure and skin tone, is paramount; without it, the personalization that makes the image compelling is lost. The description then builds the scene, specifying the fitted ribbed turtleneck and sleek retro-futuristic dark sunglasses, which are key costume pieces that immediately communicate the cyberpunk genre. The pose, gazing slightly upward with a calm yet confident expression, adds a layer of narrative and attitude. The true genius of the prompt lies in its meticulous lighting design. It dictates a two-light system: soft magenta and pink underlighting that casts dramatic shadows upwards, and a gentle cyan and blue rim light that outlines the jawline and hair. This combination is classic cinematic portraiture, creating depth, separation from the deep black background, and a powerful color contrast that is visually arresting. The instruction for the sunglasses to reflect abstract neon geometric patterns is a brilliant touch, adding complexity and a subtle nod to the digital, tech-infused world of cyberpunk without letting the reflection dominate the portrait. The final technical directives for a clean, modern digital illustration style with realistic skin texture, detailed fabric definition, and a smooth high-resolution finish ensure the output is polished and professional, bridging the gap between illustration and photorealism. The aspect ratio of 9:16 is crucial for a vertical, portrait-oriented composition, ideal for sharing on social media platforms. The core elements that must remain unchanged are the subject's identity, the core wardrobe (turtleneck and retro sunglasses), and the primary lighting setup (magenta/cyan with underlighting and rim light). Altering these would fundamentally change the prompt's specific aesthetic. However, there is significant room for creative variation. Changing the turtleneck to a leather jacket or metallic top shifts the texture and vibe while staying within the futuristic theme. Swapping the neon colors to green and purple creates an entirely different, perhaps more sinister, cyberpunk atmosphere. Replacing the solid black background with a cityscape places the subject in a wider world, adding environmental storytelling. The reflection in the sunglasses can be changed to a city skyline for a more direct narrative link. Finally, adjusting the shot from a close-up to a medium shot allows for a greater focus on the outfit and posture. To improve accuracy and visual quality, users must start with a clear, front-facing, and well-lit source photo to give the AI the best chance at preserving identity. If the initial results aren't a perfect match, tools like Midjourney allow for increasing the image weight parameter (--iw) to prioritize the reference image. A common mistake is using a low-resolution or obscured source image, which will inevitably lead to a poor likeness. Another is neglecting the 'do according to my gender' instruction, which helps the AI appropriately style the clothing. Over-complicating the reflection description can also lead to a cluttered result; the prompt's call for subtle reflections is wise. Users should avoid adding conflicting lighting terms that might confuse the AI's interpretation of the specific magenta underlight and cyan rim light setup.

The Prompt 

A cinematic close-up low-angle portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image wearing a fitted ribbed turtleneck sweater and sleek retro-futuristic dark sunglasses, gazing slightly upward with a calm yet confident expression. The face should match the uploaded reference image accurately, preserving natural facial structure, skin tone, and identity without distortion. The lighting features vibrant neon tones with soft magenta and pink underlighting casting subtle shadows across the face and highlighting the texture of the ribbed sweater. A gentle cyan and blue rim light outlines the jawline and hair, separating the subject from a deep black background. The sunglasses reflect abstract neon geometric patterns in cyan and pink, adding a subtle cyberpunk touch without overpowering the portrait. Rendered in a clean, modern digital illustration style with realistic skin texture, detailed fabric definition, cinematic lighting, and smooth high-resolution finish. How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, front-facing photo of the person whose likeness you want to use. 2. Copy the entire prompt and paste it into your AI image generator (Midjourney, DALL-E 3, Stable Diffusion). 3. If your tool supports parameters, include --ar 9:16 for the correct vertical aspect ratio. 4. Generate the image. If the face isn't a perfect match, try increasing the image weight (--iw in Midjourney) or run multiple variations.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines precise technical details (low-angle, underlighting, rim light) with a strong aesthetic concept (cyberpunk, retro-futuristic). The specific color instructions (magenta/pink underlighting, cyan/blue rim light) give the AI clear direction, while the request for realistic skin texture and fabric detail ensures a high-quality, professional finish.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the turtleneck to a leather jacket or a metallic fabric top.
  • Swap the neon colors to green and purple for a different cyberpunk vibe.
  • Use a cityscape background instead of solid black.
  • Change the reflection in the sunglasses to a city skyline.
  • Make it a medium shot including the shoulders and upper body.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'retro-futuristic dark sunglasses' look like?
    They are typically sleek, dark-lensed sunglasses with a design inspired by 80s sci-fi movies—often with angular or wraparound shapes and reflective lenses.
  • Can I use this for a female portrait?
    Yes, the 'do according to my gender' instruction tells the AI to adapt the clothing and style naturally to fit the person in your reference photo, whether male or female.
  • What is 'underlighting' in photography?
    Underlighting means the light source is positioned below the subject's face. It creates dramatic, often moody shadows and is a classic technique in cinematic and cyberpunk imagery.
  • What's the difference between this and the manga wall prompt?
    This prompt has a darker, more futuristic cyberpunk aesthetic with neon lighting and a black background, while the manga wall prompt is an editorial fashion concept with a pop-art inspired backdrop.
