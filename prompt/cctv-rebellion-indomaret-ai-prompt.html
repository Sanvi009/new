The Prompt 

POV CCTV inside an Indomaret at night. The camera position is in the upper corner of the room, sharply pointing downward to the center area of the store where the snacks are located, so the ceiling is not visible in the frame. A typical high-angle surveillance camera perspective, focused on the floor, shelves, and subject, with a slight fisheye effect.
The subject is me standing in front of the snack shelf in the corner area of the room with my body slightly turned away from the CCTV camera. My back and shoulders are partially visible. My head turns toward the CCTV camera. My face appears fairly close-up and clear while staring at the camera with a nonchalant, cold, and rebellious expression. The subject raises the middle finger toward the CCTV camera as a gesture of rebellion.
Skater outfit: oversized black hoodie, loose white pants, Adidas sandals, relaxed but cool streetwear style. Relaxed yet confident posture, one hand holding a snack, one hand raising the middle finger.
CCTV recording quality: low resolution, grainy, digital noise, slight motion blur, faded colors with a greenish-blue tint, highlighting the face from a tilted angle.
Background: snack shelves full of colorful products, price labels, corner of the store wall, promo posters, drink refrigerator in the distance, typical Indonesian minimarket interior. Quiet Indonesian minimarket atmosphere at night.
There is an Indonesian format timestamp overlay (14/02/2026 23.06.06) and the text (CAM 06 IDS MKS) in the top left corner of the frame.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Copy the full prompt text. 2. Paste it into your AI image generator (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3). 3. For best results, use a photorealistic model or checkpoint. 4. If your generator allows negative prompts, add 'clean, polished, professional photography, well-lit, high definition' to enhance the gritty CCTV effect. 5. Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines a highly specific cultural setting (Indomaret, Indonesian minimarket) with precise technical details of CCTV footage (grain, fisheye, greenish tint, timestamp). The narrative element (rebellious gesture, cold expression) adds emotional impact, making the generated image feel like a captured moment from a viral video. The 9:16 aspect ratio matches phone and social media formats.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the location to a 7-Eleven, Circle K, or other convenience store.
  • Alter the timestamp to a different date or camera ID.
  • Make the subject smile or show a different emotion.
  • Change the gesture to a peace sign or holding up a product.
  • Set it in daytime with different lighting and color tones.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Indomaret?
    Indomaret is a major Indonesian convenience store chain, similar to 7-Eleven or Circle K, found throughout the country. It's a recognizable cultural setting for many Indonesians.
  • How do I get the grainy CCTV effect?
    The prompt includes 'low resolution, grainy, digital noise, slight motion blur, faded colors with a greenish-blue tint'. Using these keywords helps the AI generate that authentic security camera look.
  • What does 'POV' mean in this prompt?
    POV stands for Point of View. Here it means the image is from the perspective of the CCTV camera, as if you are looking through the surveillance footage.
  • Can I remove the timestamp overlay?
    Yes, simply omit the line about the timestamp and camera text when you paste the prompt, or use image editing software to remove it after generation.
  • Which AI generator is best for realistic results?
    Stable Diffusion with photorealistic checkpoints (like Juggernaut or Realistic Vision) often produces the most convincing results for this type of prompt. Midjourney can also work well with the right settings.
