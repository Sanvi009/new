Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a masterclass in using specificity to generate a hyper-realistic, narrative-driven image that mimics the aesthetic of surveillance footage. Its creative purpose is to produce a POV, or point-of-view, image from a CCTV camera inside an Indomaret, an Indonesian convenience store, capturing a moment of quiet rebellion. The prompt constructs a complete and compelling story: a skater, alone in the store at night, looks directly into a security camera and offers a middle finger, creating a viral-style image that feels raw, authentic, and emotionally charged. The prompt text guides the AI with extraordinary precision, starting with the camera's physical placement. By specifying the camera is in the upper corner, sharply pointing downward so the ceiling isn't visible, it forces a specific, constrained perspective that is immediately recognizable as surveillance footage. The instruction for a slight fisheye effect further enhances this illusion, mimicking the wide-angle lenses commonly used in security cameras. The description of the subject is equally detailed, with the body turned away but the head facing the camera, ensuring the face is clear and the rebellious expression is the focal point. The skater outfit of an oversized black hoodie, loose white pants, and Adidas sandals is not just a fashion choice but a crucial part of the narrative, establishing a specific subculture and a relaxed, streetwise persona. The technical directions for the image quality are perhaps the most critical for achieving realism. Telling the AI to produce a low-resolution, grainy image with digital noise, slight motion blur, and a faded greenish-blue tint directly counteracts its default tendency to generate clean, high-definition pictures. This instruction is the heart of the CCTV rebellion aesthetic. It forces the AI to degrade the image in a way that feels authentic to cheap surveillance equipment, complete with the color shifts associated with night-vision or older camera systems. The inclusion of an Indonesian format timestamp and camera ID text in the top left corner is a brilliant touch of hyper-realism, anchoring the image in a specific time and place and completing the illusion of a genuine CCTV still. The background details, from snack shelves and price labels to promo posters and a drink refrigerator, build out the typical Indonesian minimarket interior, grounding the scene in a culturally specific reality. The elements that must not be changed are the core technical and narrative components that define the prompt's unique identity: the POV CCTV perspective, the low-quality grainy aesthetic, the specific rebellious gesture, and the Indonesian minimarket setting. Changing any of these would dissolve the entire concept. However, the prompt provides excellent variations that allow for creative exploration while preserving the core structure. Changing the location to a 7-Eleven or Circle K shifts the cultural context while maintaining the convenience store atmosphere. Altering the emotion or gesture changes the narrative from rebellion to something else, like a peace sign or a candid smile, which would create a different but equally compelling story. To improve accuracy and visual quality, following the how-to instructions is crucial. Using negative prompts like clean, polished, or high definition actively pushes the AI away from its standard output and towards the desired gritty look. Generating multiple variations is also key, as the random nature of AI noise and grain means some outputs will hit the mark more authentically than others. A common mistake would be to use a generator that overly beautifies the subject, as the prompt specifically calls for a cold, nonchalant expression that should not be glamorized. Another error is to neglect the timestamp; while it can be edited out later, its presence during generation helps the AI understand the context and place the text correctly within the frame. Ultimately, this prompt succeeds by constructing a complete, believable world in text. It combines a specific cultural backdrop with a universal theme of youthful rebellion and packages it in the highly recognizable visual language of surveillance footage. The result is a prompt capable of generating images that feel less like AI art and more like captured moments from reality, ready to go viral on social media, perfectly aligning with its category of street photography through its raw, unposed, and documentary-style approach.

The Prompt 

POV CCTV inside an Indomaret at night. The camera position is in the upper corner of the room, sharply pointing downward to the center area of the store where the snacks are located, so the ceiling is not visible in the frame. A typical high-angle surveillance camera perspective, focused on the floor, shelves, and subject, with a slight fisheye effect.
The subject is me standing in front of the snack shelf in the corner area of the room with my body slightly turned away from the CCTV camera. My back and shoulders are partially visible. My head turns toward the CCTV camera. My face appears fairly close-up and clear while staring at the camera with a nonchalant, cold, and rebellious expression. The subject raises the middle finger toward the CCTV camera as a gesture of rebellion.
Skater outfit: oversized black hoodie, loose white pants, Adidas sandals, relaxed but cool streetwear style. Relaxed yet confident posture, one hand holding a snack, one hand raising the middle finger.
CCTV recording quality: low resolution, grainy, digital noise, slight motion blur, faded colors with a greenish-blue tint, highlighting the face from a tilted angle.
Background: snack shelves full of colorful products, price labels, corner of the store wall, promo posters, drink refrigerator in the distance, typical Indonesian minimarket interior. Quiet Indonesian minimarket atmosphere at night.
There is an Indonesian format timestamp overlay (14/02/2026 23.06.06) and the text (CAM 06 IDS MKS) in the top left corner of the frame.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Copy the full prompt text. 2. Paste it into your AI image generator (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E 3). 3. For best results, use a photorealistic model or checkpoint. 4. If your generator allows negative prompts, add 'clean, polished, professional photography, well-lit, high definition' to enhance the gritty CCTV effect. 5. Generate multiple variations and select the one with the most authentic surveillance camera aesthetic.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines a highly specific cultural setting (Indomaret, Indonesian minimarket) with precise technical details of CCTV footage (grain, fisheye, greenish tint, timestamp). The narrative element (rebellious gesture, cold expression) adds emotional impact, making the generated image feel like a captured moment from a viral video. The 9:16 aspect ratio matches phone and social media formats.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the location to a 7-Eleven, Circle K, or other convenience store.
  • Alter the timestamp to a different date or camera ID.
  • Make the subject smile or show a different emotion.
  • Change the gesture to a peace sign or holding up a product.
  • Set it in daytime with different lighting and color tones.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Indomaret?
    Indomaret is a major Indonesian convenience store chain, similar to 7-Eleven or Circle K, found throughout the country. It's a recognizable cultural setting for many Indonesians.
  • How do I get the grainy CCTV effect?
    The prompt includes 'low resolution, grainy, digital noise, slight motion blur, faded colors with a greenish-blue tint'. Using these keywords helps the AI generate that authentic security camera look.
  • What does 'POV' mean in this prompt?
    POV stands for Point of View. Here it means the image is from the perspective of the CCTV camera, as if you are looking through the surveillance footage.
  • Can I remove the timestamp overlay?
    Yes, simply omit the line about the timestamp and camera text when you paste the prompt, or use image editing software to remove it after generation.
  • Which AI generator is best for realistic results?
    Stable Diffusion with photorealistic checkpoints (like Juggernaut or Realistic Vision) often produces the most convincing results for this type of prompt. Midjourney can also work well with the right settings.
