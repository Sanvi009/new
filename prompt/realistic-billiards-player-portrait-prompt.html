Prompt Explanation

This prompt is crafted to generate a hyper-realistic, character-driven portrait of an individual intensely focused on a game of billiards. Its creative purpose is to capture a moment of concentrated action, transforming a simple sports scene into a study of focus and precision. The image aims to look less like a generic stock photo and more like a professional sports portrait or a frame from a cinematic sequence, emphasizing the player's determination and the specific, technical details of the game. By placing the subject in a recognizable setting with accurate equipment, the prompt seeks to create a believable and immersive character study. The prompt_text guides the AI with a combination of photographic terminology and precise object descriptions. Starting with ultra-realistic medium close-up immediately sets the standard for image quality and framing, instructing the AI to focus on the subject from the chest up, which allows for detailed facial expression while still showing the essential elements of the pose and the table. The instruction same face as reference image 100% accuracy is critical for personalization, ensuring the generated portrait can feature a specific individual's likeness, making it ideal for custom avatars or personal projects. The description of the outfit, a browm polo athletic jersey with TECH patch on left sleeve, number 07 on right, and a black smartwatch, adds a layer of realism and narrative. It suggests a player on a team or in a league, giving the character a backstory beyond just this single moment. The pose is described with technical billiards terminology: left hand forming bridge, cue stick aimed at white cue ball with red dot, red object ball visible. This level of detail is crucial. Simply saying playing pool might result in an incorrect or generic pose. By specifying the bridge hand and the presence of both a cue ball and a red object ball, the prompt ensures the AI renders a realistic and accurate billiards stance, which is essential for the image's credibility. The lighting, while not explicitly detailed with terms like moody or dramatic, is implied by the goal of ultra-realism and the setting. The vibrant blue billiard table acts as a key color element, providing a strong, saturated focal point that contrasts with the subject's attire and skin tones. The unspoken lighting assumption is that it should be clear and sharp to showcase the hyper-detailed skin texture and the sheen on the felt and the balls. The elements that must NOT be changed are the core components that define the scene as a realistic billiards portrait. Removing the specific pose instructions or the details about the cue ball and red object ball would risk generating an inaccurate or less compelling image. The requirement for facial accuracy is also central to its purpose as a personalized portrait. However, many elements are safely modifiable. As suggested in the variations, the jersey color can be changed to black with gold numbers for a different aesthetic, or the entire scene can be shifted to a dark, moody pool hall with dramatic overhead lighting, transforming the feel from a bright sports portrait to a film noir-inspired scene. The gender of the player can be changed, or the sport itself can be swapped to darts or chess, applying the same logic of focused concentration to a different context. To improve accuracy and visual quality, using a high-resolution reference photo is essential for the face swap. When generating, employing an upscaler after the initial creation will enhance the hyper-realistic details, making skin texture and fabric weaves more convincing. A common mistake is to be too vague about the pose. Just saying leaning over a pool table might cause the AI to generate an incorrect hand position. The prompt's success hinges on the specific term forming a bridge. Another mistake is neglecting the color of the table; vibrant blue is a specific choice that adds visual pop and is a common color for professional pool tables. Without it, the AI might choose a less visually interesting green or brown. This prompt masterfully combines elements of AI sports photography and detailed character portrait generation. It demonstrates how using precise, domain-specific language (like bridge hand) alongside standard photographic terms (medium close-up) can guide an AI to produce an image that is both technically accurate and artistically compelling, firmly placing it within the Photorealistic Portraits category. The result is a focused, believable snapshot of an athlete in their element.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic medium close-up of a me (same face as reference image 100% accuracy), dark hair, focused serious gaze.Outfit: browm polo athletic jersey with TECH patch on left sleeve, number 07 on right, black smartwatch. Pose: leaning over vibrant blue billiard table, left hand forming bridge, cue stick aimed at white cue ball with red dot, red object ball visible.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Copy the prompt text. 2. Open your AI image tool like Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 3. If you have a reference photo of a face, upload it as instructed by your tool. 4. Paste the prompt. 5. Generate the image. Use an upscaler for higher detail.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it gives clear, photographic details. 'Ultra-realistic medium close-up' sets the style and framing. Specifying the outfit, pose, and key objects (cue stick, colored balls) gives the AI concrete elements to render. Mentioning '100% accuracy' for a reference face guides AI tools that support image prompting to match facial features closely, leading to a consistent and believable character portrait.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the jersey color to black with a gold number for a different team look.
  • Set the scene in a dark, moody pool hall with dramatic overhead lighting.
  • Make the player a woman with a determined expression.
  • Show the moment of impact, with the cue ball striking the red ball.
  • Change the sport to darts or chess for a different focused activity.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'medium close-up' mean in an AI prompt?
    It means the photo is framed from the chest or shoulders up, focusing on the subject's face and upper body while still showing some of the surroundings, like the pool table.
  • Can I use this prompt without a reference image?
    Yes. The AI will generate a random person with dark hair and a serious expression that fits the rest of the description.
  • Why is the cue ball described with a red dot?
    This specifies a 'cue ball with a red circle logo', a common detail on real pool balls. It helps the AI create a more accurate and recognizable billiards scene.
  • Which AI tool is best for this type of character portrait?
    Midjourney and DALL-E 3 are very good at understanding detailed clothing and pose descriptions to create realistic human portraits.
