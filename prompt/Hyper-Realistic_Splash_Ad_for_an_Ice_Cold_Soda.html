How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for soda commercials, digital ads, or product hero shots in beverage marketing. Preserve "hyper-realistic close-up," "ice-cold Fanta Apple can," "exploding splash," and "--ar 16:9" for cinematic appeal. Do not change the condensation or bold typography. Changing flavor to "Orange" could yield citrus visuals, resulting in fiery effects, while Apple keeps green, glossy outcomes with energetic splashes.

Prompt

Hyper-realistic close-up of an ice-cold Fanta Apple can, condensation droplets covering the surface, exploding splash of water and ice cubes around it, bright fiery red-orange background with energetic splash and flame-like effects. Bold playful typography on the side reads: “THE TASTE OF FUN.” Studio-lit commercial photography, glossy high-resolution 8K, cinematic lighting, product hero shot, --ar 16:9

Why this prompt works

This prompt is effective due to its vibrant and energetic commercial appeal the ice-cold can with condensation and exploding splash emphasizes refreshment and fun the bright fiery background and playful typography create a dynamic and attention-grabbing composition the studio lighting and high-resolution details ensure a polished product hero shot perfect for beverage advertising social media campaigns or point-of-sale displays that need to stand out.

Prompt Variations

  • Flavor: Swap “Fanta Apple” → “Fanta Orange” for the classic flavor (and color change). Swap “Fanta Apple” → “Fanta Grape” for a purple theme.
  • Splash Element: Swap “exploding splash of water and ice cubes” → “exploding splash of the soda itself” for a sticky, energetic effect.
  • Background: Swap “bright fiery red-orange background” → “a green background” to match an apple theme. Swap “with energetic splash and flame-like effects” → “with floating apple slices”.
  • Typography: Swap “The Taste of Fun” → “Get Funky!” for a different slogan. Remove typography entirely for a pure product shot.
