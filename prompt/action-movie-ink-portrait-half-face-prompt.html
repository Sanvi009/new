Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a striking black and white ink illustration in the style of a vintage action movie poster, specifically focusing on a half-face portrait. Its creative purpose is to transform a standard photograph into a piece of art that evokes the gritty, hand-crafted aesthetic of 19th-century engravings and classic film advertising. The prompt guides the AI by layering specific artistic directives. It begins by establishing the core subject and style, demanding a high-contrast black and white ink illustration that captures the exact facial identity from an uploaded image. This is immediately followed by the compositional constraint of an extreme close-up with only the right half of the face visible, a technique that simplifies the image for the AI, allowing it to focus intensely on detailed linework and likeness rather than the entire facial structure. The instruction for an 'intense serious expression' directs the emotional tone, which is crucial for the action movie aesthetic. The prompt then delves into the technical artistry by specifying 'detailed cross-hatching and stippling technique' and a 'hand-drawn pen and ink engraving style.' These are not mere stylistic suggestions but direct commands that tell the AI to render the image using specific shading methods. Cross-hatching involves layering lines to create tone and depth, while stippling uses dots. This combination guides the AI away from smooth, photographic blending and toward a textured, linear quality. The inclusion of 'dramatic shadows' and 'strong texture lines on skin' further reinforces this, ensuring the final image has the weight and contrast characteristic of ink work. The environment and finishing touches are equally important. The 'off-white textured paper background with ink splatter effects' grounds the illustration in a physical, traditional medium, moving it away from a sterile digital look. This detail, combined with the command for 'bold cinematic typography on the left side reading YUSUF,' completes the poster illusion. The typography is not an afterthought; it is positioned and described to mimic authentic movie poster design, with a minimalist layout to keep the focus on the intense portrait. The prompt also explicitly instructs the AI to 'replace the face with the uploaded photo while keeping realistic facial structure and transforming it into detailed ink cross-hatching illustration,' which is a critical guide for achieving a true likeness within the new artistic medium. The lighting described as 'dramatic lighting' is less about a natural light source and more about the contrast created by the linework itself. In this context, lighting is implied through the density and direction of the cross-hatching, which carves out the facial planes and creates the illusion of illumination. The composition, dictated by the half-face and 9:16 aspect ratio, is already highly structured, leaving the AI to focus on the quality of the line art and the fidelity to the reference photo. Certain elements are core to the prompt's success and must not be changed. The 'extreme close-up half-face composition' is non-negotiable, as it reduces complexity. The 'cross-hatching and stippling' techniques are the heart of the style, and replacing them would fundamentally alter the result. The specific background, 'off-white textured paper,' and the inclusion of 'ink splatter' are key to the vintage, hand-made feel. Finally, while the typography text 'YUSUF' is variable, its position on the left side and its 'bold cinematic' style are structural requirements for the poster layout. Several elements can be safely modified to create variations. The name in the typography can be changed to any custom text, which is the most obvious personalization. One could also specify using the left half of the face instead of the right, which would simply flip the composition's focus. The core technique can be swapped for other printmaking styles like woodcut or linocut, which would change the line quality from fine hatching to bolder, carved lines. The background can be altered from off-white to kraft paper or black to shift the mood and contrast. Adding a subtitle below the main name could create a more complex, movie-poster-like layout. Even the expression can be changed from 'serious' to 'stoic' or 'defiant,' altering the character's emotional impact while keeping it within the action genre. For practical tips to improve accuracy and visual quality, users should start by uploading a high-resolution, well-lit reference photo with clear facial features. Following the instructions to use a high image weight (like --iw 2 in Midjourney) is crucial for preserving the likeness. Generating multiple iterations is advisable because achieving perfect, clean cross-hatching linework can require several attempts, as the AI sometimes produces smoother results than desired. Specifying black and white output is also a necessary step if the chosen generator defaults to color. A common mistake is to upload a photo that is too dark or blurry, which will result in a poor likeness. Another is to use the prompt in a tool that doesn't support high image weight or image prompting effectively. Users might also forget to set the aspect ratio to 9:16, which is essential for the poster format. Finally, expecting the AI to perfectly replicate the 'ink splatter' on the first try can lead to disappointment; it often requires some refinement and multiple generations to get the balance of deliberate, stylistic splatter rather than a messy overlay. This prompt is a sophisticated guide for creating a specific, stylized illustration portrait. It successfully blends technical art terminology with compositional constraints to steer an AI toward a highly desirable and repeatable aesthetic, turning a personal photograph into a piece of dramatic, vintage-inspired poster art.

The Prompt 

High-contrast black and white ink illustration portrait of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image in the style of a gritty action movie poster, extreme close-up half-face composition (only right half of the face visible), intense serious expression, detailed cross-hatching and stippling technique, hand-drawn pen and ink engraving style, dramatic shadows, strong texture lines on skin, , sharp jawline Off-white textured paper background with ink splatter effects. Bold cinematic typography on the left side reading “YUSUF” in large uppercase black letters, minimalist layout. Ultra-detailed linework, vintage engraving aesthetic, poster design, high resolution, dramatic lighting. Replace the face with the uploaded photo while keeping realistic facial structure and transforming it into detailed ink cross-hatching illustration. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use high image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to preserve facial identity. 6. Specify black and white output if your tool defaults to color. 7. Generate multiple times to achieve clean cross-hatching linework.

Why This Prompt Works

It names two specific engraving techniques (cross-hatching, stippling) that AI illustration models recognize. It demands extreme close-up half-face composition, which reduces complexity and improves likeness accuracy. It includes a custom text element (YUSUF) positioned left side, creating authentic poster layout. Vintage engraving and hand-drawn pen style guide the model away from smooth digital airbrush rendering toward textured linework.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace cross-hatching with woodcut or linocut style
  • Alter background from off-white to kraft paper or black
  • Add subtitle text below main name
  • Switch ink splatter to torn paper edges

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will the AI draw only half my face?
    Yes, the prompt explicitly states 'extreme close-up half-face composition (only right half of the face visible)'. The left half should be cropped out of frame.
  • How does it turn a photo into ink cross-hatching?
    The AI analyzes your facial features and redraws them using line-based shading instead of continuous tones. Cross-hatching uses overlapping lines; stippling uses dots.
  • Can I change 'YUSUF' to another name?
    Yes, replace the text in quotes with any uppercase name. Keep the positioning 'on the left side' and 'bold cinematic typography' for consistent layout.
  • What is 'vintage engraving aesthetic'?
    Inspired by 19th-century book illustrations and banknote engravings. Features sharp, deliberate linework, high contrast, and textured paper appearance.
  • Why off-white textured paper background?
    It mimics traditional printmaking materials and makes the black ink and white highlights pop. More authentic than pure white digital background.
  • Will the ink splatter look messy?
    Controlled ink splatter adds authentic hand-drawn character. The prompt says 'ink splatter effects', not 'messy'—it should look deliberate and stylistic.
