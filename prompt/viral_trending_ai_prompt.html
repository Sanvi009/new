Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines nostalgia, realism, and personality. By keeping the faces unchanged, it preserves identity, while the Polaroid blur and flash lighting make it feel like a real, candid moment. Adding cute poses makes the image feel lively, relatable, and shareable.
