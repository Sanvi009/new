Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed for creating a high-impact, professional headshot that places the subject in an authoritative and aspirational setting: at a podium, addressing an audience. Its creative purpose is to generate imagery suitable for corporate websites, LinkedIn profiles, speaker one-sheets, or promotional materials for conferences and events. It aims to capture not just a likeness, but a persona—that of a confident, charismatic leader or expert in their element. The prompt elevates a simple portrait by embedding it in a context rich with professional signifiers: the wooden podium, the array of microphones, the stage lighting, and the aesthetic of broadcast news. This instantly communicates authority, expertise, and public presence, making it a powerful tool for personal branding. The prompt_text is a masterclass in using specific, real-world references to guide the AI. It starts by establishing the character as 'charismatic' while demanding an exact match of 'facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image.' This balances an aspirational quality with the necessity of personal likeness. The instruction to use the 'exact same outfit from the image' is a crucial detail for personal branding, ensuring consistency across a person's visual identity. The description of the podium is functional but detailed: 'wooden' gives it a classic, high-quality material, and 'several microphones and lot's of different types of microphone are attached' creates a sense of a major, professionally-staffed event. This isn't a small gathering; it's a significant speaking engagement. The background, a 'blurred blue event screen or curtain,' is a perfect choice. It provides a professional, non-distracting backdrop that implies a large space (a stage) while the color blue is often associated with trust, intelligence, and calmness. The most technically precise part of the prompt is the photography direction: 'Bright stage lighting sharp focus on the face, telephoto lens style, 4K news broadcast aesthetic.' This combination of instructions is what guarantees a polished, professional result. 'Bright stage lighting' ensures the subject is well-lit and stands out from the background. 'Sharp focus on the face' directs the AI's attention to the most important area. 'Telephoto lens style' is a brilliant inclusion; a telephoto lens compresses perspective, making the subject and the background feel closer together and creating that flattering, slightly shallow depth of field often seen in professional portraits and news broadcasts. Finally, '4K news broadcast aesthetic' ties it all together, demanding a level of clarity, color grading, and overall polish associated with high-end television production. The lighting described is paramount. The combination of 'bright stage lighting' and 'sharp focus' ensures the subject is the undeniable focal point, with every detail of their expression and outfit rendered crisply. The 'telephoto lens style' affects the composition by compressing the distance between the subject and the blurred background, making the podium and the event screen feel like an immediate part of their environment. This creates a cohesive and professional scene where the subject looks naturally placed, not pasted in. The element that must not be changed is the core commitment to using the subject's real face and outfit. The phrase '100% use my real face' is an emphatic instruction that must be respected for the prompt to fulfill its purpose of creating a personalized professional portrait. The setting itself—a podium with microphones—is also central to the concept. However, many elements can be safely modified to tailor the image to a specific context. The prompt variations offer excellent alternatives. Changing the background to a 'corporate boardroom presentation setting' shifts the scene from a public stage to a private, high-stakes meeting. Specifying a 'TED Talk stage with red carpet and audience' creates an even more prestigious and specific event atmosphere. The hand gesture can also be changed to 'pointing finger' for emphasis, or 'hands on podium' for a more grounded, steady pose, each conveying a slightly different aspect of a speaker's demeanor. For practical tips to improve accuracy, the source photo is critical. As the FAQ suggests, using a reference photo with business attire will yield the best results, as the AI will directly transpose that outfit. For the best lighting and facial clarity, the reference photo should be well-lit and front-facing. When using the prompt, adding a negative prompt like 'blurry, distorted face, multiple faces, bad anatomy' can help prevent common AI artifacts, especially when generating hands in a gesturing pose. A common mistake is not providing enough context for the '4K news broadcast aesthetic.' This phrase works because it's a well-known visual standard, but users might also benefit from adding terms like 'high contrast, professional color grading' to reinforce it. Another frequent error is expecting the AI to perfectly render every single microphone. The strength is in the idea of 'lots of different types' of microphones, which the AI will interpret as a cluster of shapes that collectively read as professional audio equipment, rather than perfectly detailed individual mics. Ultimately, this prompt succeeds because it translates the specific, technical language of professional photography and broadcast media into a clear set of instructions for the AI, building a believable and aspirational reality for the subject.

The Prompt 

A charismatic character with  exactly same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image   standing at a wooden podium giving a speech. i gesture with his right hand raised, palm open. i wearing exact same outfit from the image . Several microphones and lot's of different types of microphone are attached to the podium. The background is a blurred blue event screen or curtain. Bright stage lighting sharp focus on the face, telephoto lens style, 4K news broadcast aesthetic. 100% use my real face. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create a professional speaker portrait. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. The outfit from your reference photo will be used.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt includes specific event photography terms ('telephoto lens style', '4K news broadcast aesthetic') and professional elements ('wooden podium', 'multiple microphones') that AI recognizes. 'Bright stage lighting sharp focus on the face' ensures proper lighting, while '100% use my real face' maintains facial accuracy.

Prompt Variations

  • Change to TED Talk stage with red carpet and audience
  • Try corporate boardroom presentation setting
  • Create webinar background with digital screens
  • Make it political rally with flags and larger audience
  • Change background color to red or purple event curtain

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I change the hand gesture?
    Yes, replace 'right hand raised, palm open' with 'pointing finger', 'hands on podium', or 'holding notes' for different speaking poses.
  • What if my reference photo has casual clothes?
    The prompt uses 'exact same outfit from the image' - for professional results, use a business attire photo as reference.
  • How do I make it look more like a conference?
    Add 'large audience in blurred background' or 'conference logo on podium' to enhance the event atmosphere.
