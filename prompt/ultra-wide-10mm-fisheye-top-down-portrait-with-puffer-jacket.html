Prompt Explanation

This prompt is built around a specific creative vision: taking the classic streetwear fashion portrait and pushing it into more experimental territory through extreme lens choice and perspective. The 10mm fisheye from a high top-down angle isn't a traditional portrait setup—it's a deliberate artistic choice that signals this image is meant to be bold, modern, and editorial. The structure follows a logical flow from technical specification to styling details. It opens with the lens and angle because these choices will affect everything else—how the subject is positioned, how the clothing drapes, and how the final image feels. By getting this foundation right first, the AI can build the rest of the image around these parameters. The phrase 'strong perspective distortion (larger head, compressed body)' is particularly important. Some users might see fisheye distortion as a flaw to minimize, but this prompt embraces it as a feature. It tells the AI exactly what kind of distortion is desired, preventing it from trying to correct the perspective or make it look more natural. The result is an image that deliberately plays with proportion for artistic effect. The action of adjusting sunglasses is a small detail that makes a huge difference. Static portraits can sometimes feel stiff or posed, but by including a specific gesture, the prompt creates a sense of movement and spontaneity. The subject isn't just standing there—they're in the middle of doing something. This adds a layer of authenticity and makes the image feel like a captured moment rather than a constructed scene. Color-block puffer jackets have been a staple of streetwear fashion for years, and the grey/black combination is both versatile and sophisticated. Grey and black work well together because they create subtle contrast without the boldness of primary colors. This allows the silhouette and texture to take center stage. The black tee underneath is a classic layering piece, the silver chain adds a touch of edge, and white sneakers keep the look fresh and contemporary. The white studio background is a classic choice for fashion photography because it eliminates distractions and creates a clean, professional look. The 'smooth gradient' prevents it from being a flat, boring white—it adds just enough depth to feel intentional and designed. High contrast lighting ensures that the subject stands out dramatically against this background, with sharp shadows defining the form and adding visual interest. Common mistakes users might make with this prompt include using a reference photo that doesn't show their full head and shoulders clearly, or uploading an image where their face is partially obscured. The AI needs to see your features to replicate them, even if they'll eventually be partially covered by sunglasses. Another mistake is not generating enough variations—fisheye distortion can be tricky, and it often takes several attempts to get the perspective exactly right. Users can experiment with this concept by changing the color scheme entirely—a red and black puffer with matching sunglasses creates a completely different energy. The background could shift from white to black for a more moody, dramatic feel. The gaze could change from confident upward to looking away thoughtfully. Each variation maintains the core technical foundation while exploring different emotional territories.

The Prompt 

Convert uploaded photo into ultra-wide 10mm fisheye portrait from dramatic high top-down angle with strong perspective distortion (larger head, compressed body). Add black oversized sunglasses, left hand adjusting sunglasses, confident upward gaze. Color-block puffer jacket (grey / black), black tee, silver chain, white sneakers. Bold white studio background, smooth gradient, high contrast, sharp editorial lighting, 8k. ar-9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Begin by uploading a clear, well-lit photo of yourself to your AI image generator. The reference photo should show your full face without obstructions, even though the final image will add sunglasses. This gives the AI the best chance of matching your facial features accurately. Copy the prompt exactly as written and paste it alongside your uploaded image. Make sure your platform supports strong image referencing—some tools have a parameter like `--iw` (image weight) that lets you prioritize how closely the AI follows your reference photo. Set this high to preserve your identity. Generate multiple variations and study each result. The key elements to check are: the top-down perspective with larger head and compressed body, the oversized sunglasses being added naturally, the left hand adjusting them, and the confident upward gaze. The fisheye distortion should be noticeable but not overwhelming—you want that exaggerated editorial look. If the clothing details aren't quite right, many platforms now offer inpainting tools. You can select the jacket area and regenerate it with more emphasis on the color-block pattern. The same goes for the silver chain and white sneakers. Pay attention to the lighting—it should be 'sharp editorial lighting' with high contrast against the clean white background.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt is a masterclass in combining extreme photographic techniques with precise fashion direction. Starting with 'ultra-wide 10mm fisheye portrait from dramatic high top-down angle' immediately establishes a bold, unconventional composition that grabs attention. A 10mm fisheye is about as wide as it gets—it creates that signature curved distortion where the center of the image bulges outward. By specifying 'strong perspective distortion (larger head, compressed body),' the prompt embraces this effect rather than fighting it, turning a technical limitation into a creative statement. The high top-down angle is crucial here. Shooting from above with a fisheye creates that distinctive look where the head becomes the focal point while the body tapers away. It's a popular technique in streetwear and editorial fashion photography because it emphasizes the subject's face and attitude while creating dynamic, almost playful proportions. The styling instructions are beautifully concise yet complete. 'Black oversized sunglasses' adds instant cool factor and mystery. The specific action—'left hand adjusting sunglasses'—creates a candid, in-the-moment feel rather than a static pose. It gives the subject something to do, which often results in more natural-looking images. The 'confident upward gaze' works perfectly with the top-down angle; looking up into the camera from below creates an engaging connection with the viewer. The outfit is pure contemporary streetwear: 'color-block puffer jacket (grey/black), black tee, silver chain, white sneakers.' Each item is described with enough detail to guide the AI but not so much that it becomes restrictive. Color-block puffer jackets are a specific style with distinct panels of color, and the grey/black combination is versatile and modern. The silver chain adds a touch of bling without being flashy, and white sneakers keep the look fresh and grounded. Setting the scene against a 'bold white studio background, smooth gradient' is a smart choice. White backgrounds are classic in editorial fashion because they eliminate distractions and let the subject and clothing take center stage. The smooth gradient adds just enough visual interest without competing for attention. High contrast and sharp editorial lighting ensure the image pops—deep shadows and bright highlights create that punchy, magazine-ready look.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 10mm fisheye mean and why use it?
    A 10mm fisheye lens is an ultra-wide lens that creates extreme barrel distortion, making the center of the image appear larger while curving the edges. It's used in fashion for dramatic, attention-grabbing portraits with a unique perspective.
  • How do I ensure my face still looks like me with such strong distortion?
    The distortion primarily affects proportions, not features. Your face will appear larger but should maintain your actual features. Using a high-quality reference photo and setting a high image weight parameter helps preserve your likeness.
  • Why specify a white background for this style of portrait?
    A clean white background creates maximum contrast with the subject and clothing, making the colors and details pop. It's a classic editorial choice that keeps the focus entirely on you and your outfit.
