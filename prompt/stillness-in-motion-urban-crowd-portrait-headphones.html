Create a cinematic editorial portrait where a single subject stands still wearing headphones while blurred figures move around them. This prompt preserves facial identity from an uploaded image and captures urban rhythm and transformation through stillness.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to capture a powerful conceptual image that visualizes the theme of inner calm and focus amidst the chaos of urban life. Its creative purpose is to create a cinematic editorial portrait that contrasts the sharp, defined stillness of an individual with the blurred, frantic motion of a crowd, suggesting a sense of personal transformation achieved through remaining centered. The image aims to feel both like a candid street photography moment and a stylized fashion editorial, using the central figure's composure as a narrative anchor. The prompt guides the AI by first establishing the non-negotiable element: the subject must have the exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image, ensuring the portrait is personal. It then sets up the core visual conflict: the subject stands completely still, wearing headphones, while blurred figures move around them. This instruction is key, as it asks the AI to simulate a photographic technique—long exposure or slow shutter speed—where stationary objects remain sharp and moving objects become ghost-like streaks. The inclusion of the headphones is a subtle but important detail; it implies the subject is in their own world, tuned into something private, which provides a plausible reason for their detachment from the surrounding hustle. The subject wears a hoodie and maintains a minimal expression, contributing to the modern editorial realism and a sense of anonymous, cool detachment. The phrase a feeling of transformation through stillness elevates the prompt from a simple visual description to a conceptual directive, asking the AI to imbue the image with a mood of introspective change. The parameters --ar 9:16 and --raw are technical instructions, setting the vertical aspect ratio for a portrait-oriented, magazine-cover feel and, in Midjourney, telling the model to produce a less-processed image that retains more realistic detail and is better suited for post-processing. The lighting, proportion, and composition are implied through the scene: the lighting must be such that the subject is well-lit and sharp against the background of motion, likely requiring a consistent, possibly overcast or urban ambient light to make the blur effect believable. The proportion is full-body, placing the subject within their environment. The realism level is set by modern editorial realism, aiming for a polished but authentic look, not a fantastical or overly stylized one. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's identity and the core contrast of a sharp, still subject against a blurred, moving crowd. The hoodie and headphones are also central to the modern, understated aesthetic. The prompt variations show what can be safely modified to shift the mood or setting. Changing the setting to a rainy street with blurred umbrellas would introduce a layer of melancholic beauty. Replacing the crowd with motion-blurred trains in a subway station would create a more industrial and isolated feel. Modifying the time of day to evening and including blurred car lights would transform the scene into a more solitary, contemplative night piece. For practical tips, generating multiple versions is essential because the quality and intensity of the motion blur can vary significantly between runs. Adding phrases like long exposure photography or slow shutter speed effect can help reinforce the desired effect for models that might not fully grasp it from the initial prompt. A common mistake is using a reference photo where the subject is already in a complex pose or cluttered background, which can confuse the AI when it tries to separate them from the moving elements. Another mistake is forgetting the --raw parameter, which can result in an overly smooth, AI-generated look that lacks the gritty, realistic texture essential for this street photography concept. This prompt brilliantly uses a simple, powerful visual metaphor—stillness versus motion—to create a portrait that is not just a likeness, but a statement about the individual's relationship with the world around them, leveraging AI's ability to blend photographic techniques with conceptual storytelling.

The Prompt 

A photo of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image standing completely still, wearing headphones. Blurred figures move around them, creating a sense of urban rhythm. The model wears a hoodie, maintaining a minimal expression. Motion-blurred bodies passing by, modern editorial realism, and a feeling of transformation through stillness. --ar 9:16 --raw

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear reference photo of yourself. 2. Use this prompt in Midjourney (supports --ar and --raw) or other AI generators. 3. Paste the full prompt including parameters. 4. Generate multiple versions to get the best motion blur effect. 5. Adjust the intensity of motion blur by adding words like 'more motion blur' or 'slower shutter speed effect' if needed.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt creates visual contrast between stillness and motion, which is inherently dramatic. 'Blurred figures moving around' tells the AI to create ghost-like crowd movements while the main subject stays sharp. 'Modern editorial realism' ensures a fashion-magazine aesthetic. 'Transformation through stillness' adds conceptual depth, making the image feel meaningful beyond just a portrait.

Prompt Variations

  • Standing still in rain with blurred umbrellas moving past, wet streets reflecting city lights
  • Subway station setting with motion-blurred trains instead of people, industrial mood
  • Bright daylight scene with sharply dressed crowd and subject in white hoodie for contrast
  • Evening cityscape with motion-blurred car lights and fewer people, more solitary feel
  • Subject holding a coffee cup instead of headphones, steam rising, early morning commute vibe

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does --ar 9:16 and --raw do in the prompt?
    --ar 9:16 sets the aspect ratio to vertical (portrait mode). --raw in Midjourney reduces the default beautification, giving you more realistic, less processed images that are easier to edit later.
  • How do I get the blurred figures to look realistic?
    The prompt already includes 'motion-blurred bodies passing by'. For better results, you can add 'long exposure effect' or 'slow shutter speed' to your variations.
  • Can I use this without uploading a photo of myself?
    The prompt is designed to preserve a specific face from an uploaded image. Without it, the AI will generate a random person. You can replace 'me' with 'a model' if you don't need identity preservation.
  • What style of headphones work best?
    Over-ear headphones usually look more editorial and visible. The prompt doesn't specify, so the AI will choose—if you want a specific type, add it like 'vintage headphones' or 'white AirPods'.
  • How can I make the crowd look busier?
    Add phrases like 'dense crowd', 'rush hour station', or 'packed city sidewalk' to your prompt variations.
