The Prompt 

Render [insert template] as a cold metallic can with condensation, standing vertically in the center of the frame. Around it — a splash of glowing liquid in a color matching the flavor. Lighting — cinematic, with ultra-sharp focus and contrast in a studio style.

How to Use This Prompt

Replace [insert template] with your logo, brand, or design you want displayed on the can. You can also change the liquid color to match the drink flavor (e.g., orange for citrus, red for cherry). Keep the cinematic studio lighting keywords intact for professional results.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt mimics high-end product photography by combining condensation, sharp cinematic lighting, and a glowing liquid splash. It highlights the can as the centerpiece while reinforcing brand identity through the color-matched liquid. The result feels polished, realistic, and instantly marketable — perfect for showcasing products in ads, presentations, or e-commerce visuals.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “metallic can” → “frosted glass bottle” for a premium look.
  • Change “glowing liquid splash” → “ice cubes exploding around it” for an energetic vibe.
  • Change “cinematic studio lighting” → “soft natural daylight” for a fresh lifestyle look.
  • Change “cold metallic can” → “matte black can” for a sleek modern aesthetic.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this prompt to mock up my brand logo on the can?
    Yes — just replace [insert template] with your logo or label design to see it rendered realistically on the can.
  • How do I change the splash color to match my drink?
    Add keywords like “orange glowing splash” or “green neon liquid burst” so the liquid visually represents the flavor.
