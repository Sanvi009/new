The Prompt 

A full-body studio shot featuring me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image  with uploaded face as reference sitting relaxed in a sculptural cobalt blue armchair where the base forms the 3D block letters "Brainloom". The subject wears a matching blue utility jumpsuit with rolled cuffs, white ribbed crew socks, and chunky beige sneakers. The setting is a seamless, monochromatic cyan-blue infinity cove background creating a color-blocked aesthetic. Lighting is soft and high-key with diffuse shadows, captured from a low angle  using an 85mm lens for a commercial fashion look, highly detailed fabric textures, 8k resolution, photorealistic rendering. 100% use my real face. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear reference photo of your face and body to your AI image generator. 2. Copy the full prompt and paste it into the tool. 3. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 or Portrait. 4. Generate the image. For best results with branded text, use Midjourney or Stable Diffusion which handle 3D lettering reasonably well. 5. The prompt includes "100% use my real face" to emphasize facial likeness priority.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it combines branded product photography with fashion portraiture. The sculptural armchair with "Brainloom" text creates a unique prop that tells a story. The color blocking (different blues with beige accents) creates visual harmony. Technical specifications like 85mm lens, high-key lighting, and 8K resolution push the AI toward professional commercial photography results. The infinity cove background ensures the focus stays on the subject and chair.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the armchair color to emerald green or magenta with matching text.
  • Replace the jumpsuit with a coordinated two-piece outfit in complementary colors.
  • Modify the background to a gradient or different monochromatic shade.
  • Try different poses like leaning on the armrest or standing next to the chair.
  • Swap the sneakers for designer heels or minimalist loafers.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the 'Brainloom' text to appear correctly in the chair base?
    AI generators can struggle with precise text rendering. For best results, use Midjourney or Stable Diffusion with strong prompt adherence. The phrase "3D block letters" helps the AI understand the text should be dimensional. You may need multiple generations or post-processing to get perfect text. Some users combine AI generation with Photoshop for text refinement.
  • What is an infinity cove background?
    An infinity cove is a seamless curved background used in photography that creates the illusion of an endless, shadow-free backdrop. In this prompt, the cyan-blue infinity cove eliminates corners and horizon lines, keeping all focus on the subject and chair while maintaining the color-blocked aesthetic.
  • What does 'high-key lighting' mean?
    High-key lighting is a style characterized by bright, even illumination with minimal shadows and low contrast between light and dark areas. It creates a clean, upbeat, commercial look often used in fashion and product photography. In this prompt, it complements the color-blocked aesthetic.
  • Why an 85mm lens for this shot?
    An 85mm lens is a classic portrait focal length that provides natural perspective without distortion, beautiful background compression, and flattering subject isolation. The low angle combined with 85mm creates an interesting perspective while maintaining professional fashion photography standards.
  • How do I ensure the AI uses my exact face?
    The prompt explicitly states "100% use my real face" and references the uploaded image. Use an AI tool with strong reference image capabilities. In Midjourney, include your photo URL. In Stable Diffusion, use reference or face swap extensions. A clear, well-lit reference photo will improve results.
