The Prompt 

A medium shot photograph of me with exact same facial and physical structure from the uploaded image  in a black turtleneck and glasses, holding a silver Apple MacBook and looking down at a silver wristwatch on his left wrist. my right hand supports the laptop from underneath. The lighting is dramatic side light from the left against a completely dark background, casting strong highlights and shadows on his face and the laptop. Size 9:16 ratio image generate

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear photo of yourself to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste this entire prompt. 3. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 vertical. 4. Generate the image. This prompt works well in most AI image tools, especially those good with objects and realistic lighting like DALL-E 3, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by clearly defining a scene with specific objects (MacBook, watch), clothing (black turtleneck, glasses), and lighting (dramatic side light). The AI can easily visualize this common 'tech professional' archetype and the strong side lighting creates an instantly recognizable, bold visual style.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'silver Apple MacBook' to 'Microsoft Surface laptop' or 'closed notebook'.
  • Replace 'black turtleneck' with 'gray blazer' or 'crewneck sweater'.
  • Try 'looking at camera' instead of 'looking down at watch'.
  • Swap 'dramatic side light' for 'soft window light' or 'warm desk lamp glow'.
  • Change 'completely dark background' to 'modern office shelf' or 'city skyline at night'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I get the AI to show the MacBook logo clearly?
    Mentioning 'silver Apple MacBook' often cues the AI to include the iconic logo. For more clarity, you can add 'with visible Apple logo' to the prompt. Note that some AI platforms may have policies against generating specific brand logos.
  • Can I use this for a LinkedIn profile picture?
    Yes, this is an excellent prompt for a modern, professional LinkedIn headshot. It conveys competence, tech-savviness, and a serious, focused demeanor suitable for industries like tech, business, or consulting.
  • What if the AI gets the hand positions wrong with the laptop and watch?
    Hands and complex poses can be challenging. Using a reference photo where you are holding an object can help. You can also simplify the prompt to 'holding a laptop' and 'glancing at wristwatch' without specifying which hand, giving the AI more flexibility.
  • How do I make the side lighting more dramatic?
    Use stronger keywords like 'hard side light', 'chiaroscuro lighting from the left', or 'strong cinematic shadows'. You can also add 'no fill light' to ensure the shadow side remains very dark, increasing contrast.
