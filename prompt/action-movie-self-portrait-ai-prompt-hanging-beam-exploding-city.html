How to Use This Prompt

Copy the entire prompt into your AI image generator. For the best personal result, upload a clear photo of yourself first. The prompt is formatted for tools like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion. It will create a vertical (9:16) action movie-style image.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt works by combining a strong, clear character pose ('hanging from a beam', 'hand stretched forward') with specific, visually rich environment details ('giant fireballs', 'volumetric smoke'). Using industry terms like 'high-contrast cinematic lighting', '8K ultra-realism', and 'Hollywood action movie poster look' instructs the AI to replicate a professional film photography style, ensuring a dynamic and dramatic output.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the setting from a modern city to a sci-fi metropolis or a fantasy castle under siege.
  • Swap the 'olive green military jacket' for 'tactical armor' or a 'scorched leather jacket'.
  • Alter the perspective to a 'low-angle shot looking up' at the character for a heroic feel.
  • Replace 'explosions' with a 'massive tidal wave' or 'alien invasion laser beams'.
  • Change the character's expression from 'intense focused' to 'desperate scream' or 'defiant glare'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this to put myself in an action movie poster?
    Yes, this prompt is designed specifically for that. Use a clear selfie as a reference image with your AI tool to insert your likeness into the cinematic disaster scene.
  • Which AI generator is best for this hyper-realistic style?
    Midjourney V6+ and DALL-E 3 are excellent for cinematic realism and detail. For ultimate control over elements like smoke and sparks, use Stable Diffusion with a photorealistic model checkpoint.
  • What does 'top-down perspective' mean in the prompt?
    It means the virtual camera is looking down at the subject from a higher angle. This creates a feeling of height, vulnerability, and dramatic scale, showing the vast, exploding city below.
