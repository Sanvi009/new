"Dark Grunge Collage Portrait with Pegasus and Police Car AI Prompt" | Brainloom

Generate an artistic portrait where you stand against a black-and-gray collage featuring a police car, flying Pegasus, perfume bottle, newspaper text, and record label. Hand on face, loose black shirt, light beige pants, soft white outline glow, 9:16.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create a sophisticated and highly artistic form of portraiture that blends the subject with a rich, symbolic collage. Its creative purpose is to produce an image that feels like a piece of contemporary mixed-media art, where the person is not just photographed but placed within a world of fragmented imagery and meaning. The goal is to evoke a 'dark grunge mood' that feels both edgy and aesthetically curated, as if the portrait is a cover for an alternative music album or a page from an avant-garde fashion magazine. The subject, with their 'one hand on my face' pose, becomes a contemplative figure amidst a chaotic but carefully chosen collection of visual symbols. The prompt text works by establishing a clear hierarchy between the subject and the background. It first locks in the subject with precise, unchangeable details: 'use my photo same to same,' the specific 'hand on my face' pose, and the outfit of a 'loose black shirt and light beige pants.' This anchors the image in reality and personal identity. Then, it builds the world around them with the instruction for an 'artistic black-and-gray collage background.' This color palette is crucial; by restricting the collage to monochrome, it ensures the background doesn't overwhelm the subject with color, and it contributes heavily to the 'dark grunge' aesthetic. The list of collage elements—'police car, flying (Pegasus), perfume bottle, newspaper text, record label, and abstract paint textures'—is a stroke of genius. Each item is a concrete, recognizable object that the AI can reliably generate, but together they form a surreal and thought-provoking collection. The police car might symbolize authority or the state, the flying Pegasus represents fantasy and escape, the perfume bottle suggests luxury and image, while the newspaper text and record label ground the collage in the worlds of media and music. The abstract paint textures then tie it all together, giving it the look of a physical, hand-crafted collage. The final key instruction is the 'soft white outline glow around the person.' This is a vital compositional tool. It acts as a visual separator, a subtle halo that lifts the subject off the busy background, ensuring they remain the focal point and preventing them from visually merging with the collage elements. This glow, combined with 'realistic lightings,' gives the subject a three-dimensional presence that contrasts beautifully with the flat, layered look of the collage background. The element that must absolutely not be changed is the subject's identity and pose. The entire artwork is built around 'me' from the uploaded image. The specific 'hand on my face' pose is integral to the mood of contemplative introspection. Changing the pose would alter the emotional core of the portrait. The outfit colors, 'black shirt and light beige pants,' are also important as they provide a neutral base that works well against the black-and-gray background. However, the collage elements are entirely modular, as the prompt variations brilliantly demonstrate. Replacing the 'Pegasus' with a 'dragon' shifts the fantasy element from mythical grace to monstrous power. Swapping the 'police car' for a 'vintage motorcycle' changes the urban reference from institutional authority to personal freedom and rebellion. Changing the 'perfume bottle' to an 'hourglass' would introduce a theme of time and mortality. Even the color of the outline glow can be changed from 'soft white' to a 'warm amber,' which would alter the mood from cool, editorial separation to a warmer, more intimate feel. For practical tips, using a high image weight (`--iw 2` in Midjourney) is non-negotiable for preserving facial features. It might also be beneficial to use a 'style low' or 'style raw' parameter to prevent the AI from overly smoothing or beautifying the subject, as you want them to look natural and real against the stylized collage. A common mistake is not generating enough variations. Collage prompts are complex, and it may take several attempts for the AI to place all the requested elements in a visually pleasing arrangement. Another mistake is being too rigid about the placement of the collage items; allowing the AI some creative freedom often results in a more organic and interesting composition. By fusing a demand for personal likeness with a deliberately chaotic and symbolic collage aesthetic, this prompt guides the AI to create a portrait that is both a recognizable likeness and a piece of evocative, contemporary collage art.

The Prompt 

use my photo same to same from the uploaded image posing with one hand on my face , wearing a loose black shirt and light beige pants. i standing against an artistic black-and-gray collage background. The collage includes elements like a police car, flying (Pegasus), perfume bottle, newspaper text, record label, and abstract paint textures. Aesthetic dark grunge mood with soft white outline glow around the person, realistic lightings ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your portrait photo as a reference image. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use a high image weight setting (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to preserve your facial features and clothing. 6. Generate multiple times until collage elements appear clearly.

Why This Prompt Works

It lists specific, concrete collage objects (police car, Pegasus, perfume bottle) that AI can reliably generate. It demands identity preservation so the output remains a recognizable portrait. It describes a distinct aesthetic (dark grunge, soft white glow) that separates the subject from the busy background. The 9:16 ratio fits vertical portrait composition and mobile viewing.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace Pegasus with dragon or angel wings
  • Swap police car with taxi or vintage motorcycle
  • Change perfume bottle to wine glass or hourglass
  • Use warm amber glow instead of white outline
  • Make collage full color instead of black-and-gray
  • Add handwritten quotes instead of newspaper text
  • Change pose to both hands in pockets or arms crossed

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will the AI keep my exact face from the photo?
    Yes, if you use a strong image weight (--iw 1.5–2 in Midjourney) or reference strength (0.8–1 in DALL-E). The prompt explicitly says 'same to same from the uploaded image'.
  • What does 'soft white outline glow' look like?
    A thin, subtle halo of white light tracing the edges of your body. It separates the subject from the collage background and adds a surreal, editorial effect.
  • Can I change the collage items to my own ideas?
    Absolutely. The prompt structure works with any list of objects. Just replace the five items while keeping the 'black-and-gray collage' style.
  • What style of collage does this produce?
    Digital mixed media, similar to contemporary pop surrealism or dark street art. Think cut-and-paste aesthetic with textured paper and paint overlays.
  • Do I need to specify the Pegasus is flying?
    Yes, the prompt includes 'flying (Pegasus)' to ensure wings are spread and the creature appears mid-air, not standing on ground.
