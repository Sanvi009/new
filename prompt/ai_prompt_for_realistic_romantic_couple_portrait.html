The Prompt 

An advertising poster for the Scottish single malt whisky brand "Glenfiddich". The bottle is surrounded by moss and stones, arranged in a spiral pattern on top of water splashes. In the center, there's green grass with tree branches intertwined around it. On the bottom --ar 4:5 --v 7

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into whisky branding, product ads, or bar decor for Scottish heritage promotions. Use AI generators with "--ar 4:5 --v 7," keeping "Scottish single malt whisky brand 'Glenfiddich'," "bottle surrounded by moss and stones," "spiral pattern," and central grass for artistic appeal. Do not modify the water splashes or tree branches. Optionally vary patterns; for example, changing to "circular" could produce balanced visuals, leading to intertwined elements, whereas spiral maintains dynamic, premium advertising results.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by setting a sophisticated natural scene for a whisky brand advertisement the spiral pattern of moss and stones around the bottle with water splashes and tree branches creates a harmonious and elegant composition that reflects the brand’s heritage and quality the outdoor elements evoke a sense of tradition and craftsmanship making the product appear premium and authentic this image is perfect for print ads packaging or social media content that aims to connect the brand with nature and refinement.

Prompt Variations

  • Brand: Swap “Glenfiddich” → “Macallan” for a different whisky brand.
  • Central Element: Swap “green grass with tree branches” → “a small, clear stream of water” cutting through the center. Swap “moss and stones” → “heather and rough granite stones” for a Scottish highland feel.
  • Container: Add “the bottle is made of frosted glass” for a different texture. Imply the bottle is open: “with a single drop of whisky falling from the bottle into the water below”.
  • Aspect Ratio: Swap “--ar 4:5” → “--ar 16:9” for a cinematic widescreen poster.
