How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, deploy it for fashion photography, urban lifestyle blogs, or personal branding in city-themed campaigns. Use AI with uploaded faces, keeping "portrait of me," "standing in the middle of a city street," "fitted ribbed gray sweater," and "shallow depth of field" for cinematic vibe. Do not alter the golden hour, posture, or bokeh. Optionally vary accessories; for example, changing necklace to "gold chain" could produce luxurious visuals, leading to moody outcomes, whereas "scarf" might add seasonal elements with calm, alert results.

Prompt

portrait of me standing in the middle of a city street during golden hour, wearing a fitted ribbed gray sweater, my body slightly turned to the left while my head looks back over my right shoulder, arms relaxed by my sides, soft urban lights and bokeh in the background, buildings fading into the distance, a calm but alert posture, casual silver necklace visible, soft natural lighting with a moody, cinematic vibe, shallow depth of field emphasizing the city atmosphere behind me. same face

Why this prompt works

This prompt will work because it captures a candid golden hour moment in an urban setting creating a moody and cinematic vibe the description of a fitted gray sweater soft urban lights and bokeh background adds a casual yet stylish aesthetic the pose with the body turned and head looking back creates dynamism and storytelling the shallow depth of field focuses on the subject while blending the city atmosphere making the image feel both personal and expansive this prompt is perfect for lifestyle photography fashion blogs or social media content that aims to convey a calm yet alert demeanor.

Prompt Variations

  • Clothing: Swap “fitted ribbed gray sweater” → “a classic brown leather jacket” for a tougher look. Swap “gray sweater” → “a vibrant red hoodie” for a pop of color.
  • Location: Swap “city street” → “a bridge overlooking a city” for a more expansive backdrop. Swap “city street” → “a quiet, foggy pier” for a more melancholic mood.
  • Action: Swap “head looks back over my right shoulder” → “looking down at a watch on my wrist” for a narrative of time. Swap “arms relaxed by my sides” → “holding a coffee cup”.
  • Time: Swap “golden hour” → “blue hour” just after sunset for cooler tones and more neon. Swap “golden hour” → “a rainy night” for reflective streets and dramatic lights.
