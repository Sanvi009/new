Generate a dramatic ultra-wide 10mm fisheye portrait with extreme high-angle perspective. Perfect for achieving editorial fashion shots with bold colors and dominant attitude.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a masterclass in directing an AI to generate a specific, high-fashion aesthetic by combining technical photography terms with detailed stylistic descriptions. It's designed to solve the common problem of AI image generation: getting a very specific, non-generic result. Instead of a simple 'portrait of a person,' this prompt builds a world. It starts by establishing the foundational 'shot' with the ultra-wide 10mm fisheye and extreme high-top down angle. This isn't just a cool effect; it's the core compositional element that dictates how the rest of the elements will be perceived. The distortion it creates—enlarging the head and compressing the body—is a deliberate stylistic choice borrowed from street and skate photography, lending a dynamic, edgy feel. The prompt then layers in the narrative through the subject's actions: the left hand subtly adjusting the sunglasses and the confident upward gaze. This transforms the subject from a passive model into an active character, conveying a story of dominance and self-assurance. The description of the outfit is where the prompt truly shines. By specifying 'bold color-block puffer jacket' with exact color names ('electric blue, vibrant yellow, burnt orange'), it gives the AI clear, non-ambiguous anchors to work with. These are colors the AI has likely been trained on extensively, ensuring it can render them accurately. Pairing the vibrant jacket with 'fitted black tee,' 'thick gold chain,' and 'clean white sneakers' perfectly captures the 'street-luxury' vibe—a mix of high-end and casual streetwear. The background and lighting are described with the precision of a studio photographer's notes. The 'bold deep red studio backdrop with smooth gradient falloff' and 'seamless cyclorama feel' create a clean, professional environment that makes the subject pop. The lighting recipe—'sharp high-contrast editorial lighting, strong key light from upper front, subtle rim light'—is crucial for achieving that glossy, magazine-worthy look. It explains exactly how to create the shadows and highlights that give the image depth and drama, like the 'glossy highlights on sunglasses.' Common mistakes when using a prompt like this include not respecting the technical parameters. If you omit '10mm' or 'fisheye,' the AI might default to a standard portrait lens, losing the intended distortion. Another mistake is being vague about colors; saying 'colorful jacket' leaves too much to the AI's imagination, potentially leading to muddy or unexpected hues. Users can tweak this further by playing with the lighting adjectives. Swapping 'sharp high-contrast' for 'soft, ambient' would completely change the mood. They could also modify the background, moving it from a studio to a specific location like 'a gritty subway station' to test how the 'street-luxury' style interacts with different environments. The power of this prompt lies in its structure: it feeds the AI a complete, coherent vision, leaving little to chance and resulting in an image that feels intentionally designed, not randomly generated.

The Prompt 

Convert uploaded photo into an ultra-wide 10mm fisheye portrait shot from an extreme high top-down angle, creating strong perspective distortion (slightly enlarged head, naturally compressed body proportions, realistic lens curvature). Subject wearing oversized matte black sunglasses and a plain black cap. Left hand subtly adjusting the sunglasses, confident upward gaze toward camera, calm but dominant expression. Outfit: bold color-block puffer jacket (electric blue, vibrant yellow, burnt orange panels), fitted black tee underneath, thick gold chain necklace, clean white sneakers. Street-luxury styling. Background: bold deep red studio backdrop with smooth gradient falloff, subtle vignette, seamless cyclorama feel. Lighting: sharp high-contrast editorial lighting, strong key light from upper front, subtle rim light separating subject from background, glossy highlights on sunglasses, cinematic shadow depth. Ultra-detailed skin texture, realistic fabric folds, natural light reflections, crisp focus, fashion magazine editorial look, hyper-real clarity, 8K resolution, dramatic, powerful, modern street icon energy. ar-9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To get the best results from this prompt, start with a clear, well-lit photo of your subject. Upload it to your preferred AI image generator, such as Midjourney or Stable Diffusion. Copy the entire prompt and paste it into the parameter or prompt field. The key to success is the extreme top-down angle; ensure the AI understands this by keeping the '10mm fisheye' and 'high top-down' descriptors intact. If the initial result doesn't perfectly capture the distortion, you can add a '--w 0.5' or similar parameter to adjust the weight of the original image, balancing between the photo and the prompt's description. For the color-block jacket, if the AI mixes the colors, you can specify 'blocks of electric blue, vibrant yellow, and burnt orange' more clearly. Finally, using the aspect ratio parameter '--ar 9:16' is crucial for that vertical, magazine-cover feel. Experiment with the '--stylize' value (e.g., '--s 750') to push the dramatic, editorial look further.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt is engineered for success because it breaks down a complex visual idea into distinct, machine-readable components. It doesn't just ask for a 'cool portrait'; it specifies the lens (10mm fisheye), the angle (extreme high top-down), and the exact effect of that angle (enlarged head, compressed body). This level of specificity guides the AI's latent space towards a precise output, reducing randomness. The clothing description uses strong, unambiguous color names ('electric blue', 'vibrant yellow') and texture cues ('matte black', 'glossy highlights'), which the AI associates with specific visual concepts. The lighting setup is described like a studio recipe: 'sharp high-contrast editorial lighting, strong key light from upper front, subtle rim light'. This structured approach—breaking down Subject, Action, Outfit, Background, and Lighting into clear sections—mimics how a photographer would brief a team, making it easy for the AI to parse and assemble the final image. The inclusion of technical quality tags like '8K resolution', 'hyper-real clarity', and 'crisp focus' at the end acts as a final quality filter, pushing the engine to prioritize detail and sharpness.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does '10mm fisheye' mean in an AI prompt?
    It's a reference to an ultra-wide-angle lens in photography. Telling the AI to use a '10mm fisheye' instructs it to create a distinct barrel distortion, making the center of the image bulge out and bending straight lines, which gives the portrait its dramatic, warped perspective.
  • Why is the aspect ratio 'ar-9:16' specified?
    The '9:16' aspect ratio is the vertical orientation used for magazine covers, smartphone wallpapers, and social media stories like Instagram Reels. Adding this parameter ensures the composition fits that tall, narrow frame perfectly, enhancing the editorial feel.
  • How can I make the AI focus more on my original uploaded photo?
    In Midjourney, you can adjust the image weight parameter. Adding '--iw 2' at the end of your prompt tells the AI to pay twice as much attention to your original photo compared to the text prompt, which is useful for preserving a person's likeness.
