The Prompt 

A hyper-realistic 8K 30 render, photorealistic product shot of uploaded image, standing upright at the center of the frame. The bottle's shape is precisely slender and tall, matching its original form and proportions perfectly. It is surrounded by a lush bed of vibrant, deep green moss, forming a circular island. Smooth, wet, grey river stones of various sizes, some subtly speckled with moss, are meticulously arranged around the bottle within the moss. A swirling current of translucent, deep emerald green water gently flows around the moss island and stones, creating soft ripples and reflections. A gnarled, ancient-looking tree root with fine details and a few delicate green leaves extends from the upper right, gracefully arching over the water and moss. The scene is bathed in dramatic, ethereal cinematic lighting, with soft volumetric rays illuminating the bottle and textures, creating a sense of depth and tranquility. Shot from a slightly elevated top-down perspective with a wide aperture, ensuring crisp focus on the bottle and its immediate surroundings while maintaining a slightly softer background. Rendered with Octane Render and Unreal Engine 5, showcasing incredibly detailed textures on the moss, water, stones, and tree root, with ray tracing for realistic reflections and refractions. Extremely sharp details, natural color grading. --ar 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for bottle product shots, surreal nature scenes, or eco-branding in beverage and art. Preserve "hyper-realistic 8K 30 render," "uploaded image," "lush bed of vibrant, deep green moss," and "--ar 9:16" for ethereal depth. Do not change the swirling water or cinematic lighting. Optionally vary root position; for example, left side could yield balanced visuals, resulting in ray-traced reflections, while right keeps tranquil, detailed outcomes.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt is effective due to its ethereal and natural setting that enhances the product’s premium quality the slender bottle surrounded by lush moss and emerald water creates a serene and luxurious scene the dramatic cinematic lighting and volumetric rays add depth and tranquility making the image feel almost magical the technical specifications ensure a photorealistic render perfect for high-end product launches nature-inspired branding or wellness campaigns that emphasize purity and elegance.

Prompt Variations

  • Bottle Type: Swap “uploaded image” → “a bottle of fine wine” for a different product. Swap “uploaded image” → “a bottle of aged whiskey”.
  • Liquid: Swap “deep emerald green water” → “crystal clear water” to focus on the bottle. Swap “emerald green water” → “a peaty, brown water” for a whiskey theme.
  • Natural Elements: Swap “lush bed of vibrant, deep green moss” → “a bed of smooth white quartz stones”. Swap “gnarled, ancient-looking tree root” → “a cluster of wild mushrooms”.
  • Perspective: Swap “slightly elevated top-down perspective” → “a low-angle perspective looking up at the bottle” for a more monumental feel.
