This makes it an ideal asset for premium branding campaigns, exclusive restaurant menu features, or high-end social media content that seeks to differentiate a product from its competitors by appealing to aspirational lifestyles. The Prompt 

A luxurious 8K food photography image in 3:4 aspect ratio. A can of [beverage brand] is styled for a high-end restaurant photo shoot. The can sits on a polished dark wooden table with soft ambient lighting. Behind it, a blurred fine-dining setting with elegant tableware, a folded white linen napkin, and subtle gold accents. Studio lighting highlights the glossy texture of the can, with condensation droplets for a cold, refreshing effect. 
Shallow depth of field, cinematic focus, and a warm, sophisticated tone evoke luxury and nostalgia. Perfect for a high-end beverage campaign.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it in high-end beverage campaigns, restaurant menus, or luxury product shoots for nostalgic and sophisticated branding. Use AI tools by replacing "[beverage brand]" with "Coca-Cola" or "Pepsi," keeping "luxurious 8K food photography," "3:4 aspect ratio," "polished dark wooden table," and "shallow depth of field" intact for cinematic focus. Do not modify the condensation droplets or warm tone. For instance, changing to "whiskey brand" could produce elegant bar visuals, resulting in gold-accented shots, whereas "soda" might create casual yet premium displays with nostalgic, inviting effects.

Prompt Variations

  • Beverage Brand: Swap “[beverage brand]” → “[Coca-Cola]” for a classic nostalgia feel. Swap “[beverage brand]” → “[Perrier]” for a premium sparkling water.
  • Setting: Swap “blurred fine-dining setting” → “a luxurious spa setting with towels and stones”. Swap “elegant tableware” → “vintage books and a leather armchair” for a club-like feel.
  • Surface: Swap “polished dark wooden table” → “a white marble table” for a brighter, modern luxury.
  • Condensation: Enhance condensation: “heavy condensation with water droplets racing down the side”. Swap “condensation droplets” → “a light frost covering the can” for a super-chilled effect.
