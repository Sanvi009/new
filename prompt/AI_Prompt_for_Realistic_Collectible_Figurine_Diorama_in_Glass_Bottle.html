How to use this prompt

Upload your reference photo so the figurine matches the original character’s face and attire. Keep the glass bottle diorama element for a premium, collectible look. You can adjust the environment — swap the beach for a forest, desert, or cityscape — but keep the scale, realism, and lighting consistent. This ensures the collectible feel stays intact.

Prompt

"A 1/7 scale commercialized collectible figure of the character from the photo, crafted in a highly realistic style. The figure is placed in a detailed beach environment with sand, seashells, and gentle ocean waves. The entire toy display is enclosed inside a clear souvenir glass bottle, giving it a premium miniature diorama look, with realistic lighting and shadows."

Why this prompt works

The glass bottle instantly gives it a souvenir, display-worthy quality while the realistic beach diorama adds storytelling and atmosphere. Combining collectible design with real-world environments makes the output look like high-end toy photography or a professional figure showcase. It’s a clever way to blend art, nostalgia, and realism into one image.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace “beach” → “snowy mountain base” for a winter collectible vibe.
  • Switch “glass bottle” → “crystal cube” for a modern, premium display.
  • Change “seashells” → “tiny corals and starfishes” for a more tropical feel.
  • Add “soft glowing LED lights inside the bottle” for a magical display effect.

FAQ

Do I have to use a beach setting for the diorama?No, you can replace it with any environment — desert, forest, city, or even outer space. The key is keeping it scaled, realistic, and enclosed.

Why use a glass bottle instead of just a diorama?The glass bottle makes the piece feel collectible and premium. It also creates unique lighting effects and adds that “souvenir” vibe people associate with high-quality miniature art.
