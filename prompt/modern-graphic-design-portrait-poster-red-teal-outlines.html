Create a stylized black-and-white poster portrait that maintains your exact facial features, hair, and sunglasses. The image adds bold red graphic outlines, teal vector hair, a 'thinking' pose, and the word 'BRAINLOOM' on a red rectangle behind your head. Urban editorial digital art style.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is expertly crafted to transform a standard portrait into a bold piece of personal branding and editorial design. Its creative purpose is to generate a stylized poster that is instantly recognizable, visually striking, and deeply personal, by merging a realistic base image with graphic design elements. The result is intended to be a modern, 'Instagram-ready' piece of digital art that can serve as a profile picture, a brand logo, or a piece of promotional material. The prompt functions by layering distinct visual instructions. It starts with the foundation: a 'black-and-white base photo' of the subject with 'exact same facial and physical appearance.' This grounds the image in reality and ensures the final product is a personalized portrait, not a generic model. Onto this base, it instructs the AI to add graphic overlays: 'bold red graphic outlines around the body' and 'hair illustrated in teal-blue vector style.' This creates a stunning contrast between the photorealistic face and the flat, illustrative elements, which is the hallmark of the 'modern graphic design look.' The pose is locked in as a classic 'thinking' gesture with a hand on the chin, which conveys intellect, contemplation, and a sense of style. The background is not left to chance; it's a 'clean indoor wall with a large red rectangle behind the head containing the word BRAINLOOM in tall white condensed typography.' This creates a powerful focal point and integrates the branding directly into the composition. The lighting is described as 'high contrast' and the composition as 'cinematic,' which ensures the black-and-white base has the depth and drama of a professional photograph, preventing it from looking flat and dull. The core, unchangeable elements of this prompt are the personal identity and the fundamental graphic design concept. The requirement for the 'exact same facial and physical appearance' is non-negotiable for a personalized branding piece. Similarly, the combination of a realistic base with graphic overlays (the outlines and vector hair) is the central artistic conceit. Changing this would result in a different kind of image, perhaps a pure photograph or a pure illustration, losing the unique blended style. The brand name 'BRAINLOOM' is also a fixed element for this specific branding exercise. However, the prompt is brilliantly modular for creative exploration. The color scheme can be completely overhauled: teal hair can become neon pink or gold, and red outlines can shift to electric blue or yellow, completely changing the visual energy. The background can be swapped for other urban environments like a brick wall or city skyline to alter the context. The typography and the word itself can be changed to any personal name or brand, making this a versatile template. The hand gesture can be modified to arms crossed or pointing, which would change the attitude of the portrait. To achieve the highest accuracy, users must follow the instruction to upload a clear, front-facing photo. The AI needs a high-quality reference to replicate the subject's features precisely. In tools like Midjourney, using the image prompt feature is essential. A practical tip is to be prepared for the AI to sometimes misinterpret the 'vector hair' style. If the hair doesn't look sufficiently 'illustrated' or 'vector,' adding terms like 'flat color, stylized hair' can help. Similarly, the red outlines might be too thin or inconsistent. The FAQ wisely suggests adding a phrase like 'thick red contour line' to reinforce the effect. A common mistake is to omit the 'black-and-white base' description. Without this, the AI might try to render the entire image in color, including the face, which would clash with the graphic red outlines and create a messy, incoherent result. Another error is to use a complex background that competes with the subject; the prompt specifies a clean wall for a reason, as it allows the graphic elements and the figure to pop. This prompt is a masterclass in instructing an AI to act as a graphic designer. It clearly defines the layers of the composition, the interaction between photographic and illustrative elements, and the precise placement of branding, resulting in a piece of digital art that is both a personal portrait and a powerful piece of modern, urban-inspired design.

The Prompt 

A stylized poster portrait of me with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image in a modern graphic design look. The subject is me with same hair and sunglasses, posing in a “thinking” gesture with one hand on the chin. Black-and-white base photo with bold red graphic outlines around the body. Hair illustrated in teal-blue vector style. High contrast lighting, cinematic composition. Background is a clean indoor wall with a large red rectangle behind the head containing the word “BRAINLOOM” in tall white condensed typography. Urban, editorial, digital art poster style, sharp focus, ultra-detailed, 4K, dramatic mood, Instagram-ready, modern branding aesthetic aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear front-facing photo of yourself (or your subject). 2. Paste the full prompt into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 3. Make sure the AI tool supports image prompts if you need exact facial match (Midjourney / Face Swap tools). 4. Generate at 9:16 aspect ratio. 5. Adjust colors in Canva or Photoshop if needed for precise brand matching.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt locks the subject's identity ('exact same facial and physical appearance') so the result is personal and usable for branding. It combines a classic black-and-white base with high-impact graphic elements (red outlines, teal hair) that are easy for AI to render. Specifying typography, pose, and layout leaves little to chance, producing a polished editorial poster in one generation.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace teal hair with neon pink or gold vector hair
  • Change red outlines to electric blue or yellow
  • Use a different urban background: brick wall, city skyline, or concrete
  • Swap 'BRAINLOOM' with another brand name or personal name
  • Try other hand gestures: arms crossed, pointing, or holding a prop
  • Glitch effect or halftone dots instead of solid outlines

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make the AI keep my exact face?
    Use a tool that supports image prompts (Midjourney --imagine with image URL, or Stable Diffusion with img2img + face swap extension). Upload your reference photo along with the text prompt.
  • Can I change the word 'BRAINLOOM' to my own name?
    Yes, simply edit the prompt and replace 'BRAINLOOM' with your brand name, name, or any short word.
  • What if the AI doesn't draw the red outlines correctly?
    Try adding 'thick red contour line around figure' or 'red stroke effect'. You can also add the outlines in Photoshop after generation.
  • Which AI works best for this style?
    Midjourney v6 or v7 handles 'editorial digital art' and typography well. DALL-E 3 is good for clean vector hair and text placement.
  • What is the 9:16 aspect ratio for?
    9:16 is the standard vertical format for Instagram Stories, Reels, and mobile-friendly posters.
