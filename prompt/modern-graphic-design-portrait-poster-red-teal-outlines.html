How to Use

1. Upload a clear front-facing photo of yourself (or your subject). 2. Paste the full prompt into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 3. Make sure the AI tool supports image prompts if you need exact facial match (Midjourney / Face Swap tools). 4. Generate at 9:16 aspect ratio. 5. Adjust colors in Canva or Photoshop if needed for precise brand matching.

FAQ

  • How do I make the AI keep my exact face?
    Use a tool that supports image prompts (Midjourney --imagine with image URL, or Stable Diffusion with img2img + face swap extension). Upload your reference photo along with the text prompt.
  • Can I change the word 'BRAINLOOM' to my own name?
    Yes, simply edit the prompt and replace 'BRAINLOOM' with your brand name, name, or any short word.
  • What if the AI doesn't draw the red outlines correctly?
    Try adding 'thick red contour line around figure' or 'red stroke effect'. You can also add the outlines in Photoshop after generation.
