Generate a luxury fashion editorial portrait that preserves your exact face, olive skin, and athletic build with 100% accuracy. The subject wears a charcoal pinstripe suit, burgundy tie, and has subtle neck and finger tattoos. Cinematic studio lighting, deep burgundy background, modern mafia aesthetic, 8K photorealistic quality.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a comprehensive brief for generating a high-end, luxury fashion editorial portrait with a specific narrative edge. Its creative purpose is to transform an individual into a character from a modern mafia film, combining impeccable style with a sense of relaxed, dominant confidence. The prompt_text is exhaustive, leaving little to chance. It opens with the most critical directive: preserve the subject's exact facial and physical appearance with 100% accuracy. This anchors the entire image in reality, ensuring the result is a personal portrait rather than a generic model. The physical attributes, such as olive skin and a lean athletic build, are specified to guide the AI in rendering the body consistently with the face. The wardrobe is described in meticulous detail, from the tailored charcoal-grey three-piece pinstripe suit down to the specific accessories like a gold tie bar, a luxury metal watch, and a silver ring on the index finger. This level of sartorial precision is essential for achieving the luxury fashion editorial style. The pose is equally specific, with the left hand in the pocket and the right hand holding a toothpick near the lips, a gesture that conveys the desired relaxed yet dominant posture. The inclusion of subtle neck and finger tattoos adds a layer of character and edge, reinforcing the mafia aesthetic. The lighting setup is described in professional terms, calling for a soft frontal key light, subtle fill, and a gentle rim light to create a polished, studio-finish look. The deep burgundy gradient background with a smooth matte texture provides a sophisticated, non-distracting backdrop that complements the suit and the warm cinematic color grading. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's core identity—face, skin tone, and build—as well as the fundamental pose and the presence of the specified tattoos. The AI must treat the uploaded image as the sole identity source. Elements that can be safely modified are numerous, as shown in the promptVariations. The suit color, tie, prop (toothpick can become a cigar or pen), background, and even the addition of accessories like a fedora can all be changed to explore different moods within the same aesthetic framework. Practical tips for achieving 100% accuracy involve using tools with strong image reference capabilities, such as Midjourney with a high image weight, or combining generation with a dedicated face-swap tool. To improve visual quality, following the suggestion to use upscaling tools for an 8K finish is key. A common mistake to avoid is using a low-resolution or poorly lit reference photo, which would make precise identity preservation difficult. Another is neglecting the interplay of the lighting setup; simply asking for studio lighting without specifying key, fill, and rim lights might result in a flat, un-cinematic image. This prompt masterfully integrates keywords like ultra-realistic high-fashion, modern mafia aesthetic, and cinematic studio lighting, transforming them into a detailed, actionable recipe for a premium, character-driven portrait.

The Prompt 

Create a ultra-realistic high-fashion studio portrait of me with exact same facial and physical apperance from the uploaded image as the only identity source, preserving the face with 100% accuracy and no alteration. The subject has olive skin, a lean athletic build, and is framed waist-up, centered, eye-level, with an 85mm portrait lens look. i wears a tailored charcoal-grey three-piece pinstripe suit, white spread-collar shirt, deep burgundy tie with a gold tie bar, a white pocket square, a luxury metal watch, and a silver ring on the index finger. my left hand rests in my trouser pocket while my right hand is raised near my lips holding a toothpick, conveying a relaxed yet dominant posture. A black ink neck tattoo is visible along with subtle finger tattoos. Lighting is professional cinematic studio lighting with a soft frontal key light, subtle fill, and a gentle rim light. The background is a deep burgundy gradient with a smooth matte texture. Apply warm cinematic color grading, shallow depth of field, ultra-sharp focus on the face, extreme realism, luxury fashion editorial style, modern mafia aesthetic, 8K photorealistic quality, aspect ratio 9:16, raw style, high detail, and premium studio finish. do according to my gender

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a high-quality reference photo of your face. 2. Use Midjourney with the image prompt + this text, or Stable Diffusion with img2img + face swap. 3. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 4. Generate multiple iterations and select the sharpest facial match. 5. Use upscaling tools (Topaz, Magnific) for 8K finish.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt eliminates guesswork by specifying every brand-able detail: exact facial preservation, suit color and cut, accessories, tattoos, pose, lighting, background, and color grading. Phrases like '100% accuracy', '85mm lens', 'cinematic studio lighting', and '8K photorealistic' force the AI to produce high-end, usable commercial portraits rather than generic fashion images.

Prompt Variations

  • Change suit color to navy or midnight blue
  • Replace burgundy tie with black or deep green
  • Swap toothpick for a cigar or fountain pen
  • Use a different background: smoke, marble, or cityscape at night
  • Add a fedora or overcoat
  • Try a seated pose in a leather armchair

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get 100% accurate face preservation?
    Use Midjourney with your reference photo URL + the prompt and set image weight high (--iw 2.0). For perfect results, combine with a face-swap tool like InsightFaceSwap after generation.
  • What does 'do according to my gender' mean in the prompt?
    It instructs the AI to use the gender from your uploaded photo. If you upload a male face, the model will generate a male portrait. Remove or modify this if your use case differs.
  • Can I remove the tattoos?
    Yes, delete 'A black ink neck tattoo is visible along with subtle finger tattoos' from the prompt.
  • What is 'modern mafia aesthetic'?
    It combines luxury tailoring, confident body language, subtle menace (toothpick), and cinematic mood—inspired by films like The Godfather or TV series Peaky Blinders.
  • Which AI model gives the most realistic skin texture?
    Midjourney v6 / v7 and Stable Diffusion with realistic checklists (Juggernaut XL, Realistic Vision) produce high-quality olive skin and fabric details.
