The Prompt 

Create a ultra-realistic high-fashion studio portrait of me with exact same facial and physical apperance from the uploaded image as the only identity source, preserving the face with 100% accuracy and no alteration. The subject has olive skin, a lean athletic build, and is framed waist-up, centered, eye-level, with an 85mm portrait lens look. i wears a tailored charcoal-grey three-piece pinstripe suit, white spread-collar shirt, deep burgundy tie with a gold tie bar, a white pocket square, a luxury metal watch, and a silver ring on the index finger. my left hand rests in my trouser pocket while my right hand is raised near my lips holding a toothpick, conveying a relaxed yet dominant posture. A black ink neck tattoo is visible along with subtle finger tattoos. Lighting is professional cinematic studio lighting with a soft frontal key light, subtle fill, and a gentle rim light. The background is a deep burgundy gradient with a smooth matte texture. Apply warm cinematic color grading, shallow depth of field, ultra-sharp focus on the face, extreme realism, luxury fashion editorial style, modern mafia aesthetic, 8K photorealistic quality, aspect ratio 9:16, raw style, high detail, and premium studio finish. do according to my gender

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a high-quality reference photo of your face. 2. Use Midjourney with the image prompt + this text, or Stable Diffusion with img2img + face swap. 3. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 4. Generate multiple iterations and select the sharpest facial match. 5. Use upscaling tools (Topaz, Magnific) for 8K finish.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt eliminates guesswork by specifying every brand-able detail: exact facial preservation, suit color and cut, accessories, tattoos, pose, lighting, background, and color grading. Phrases like '100% accuracy', '85mm lens', 'cinematic studio lighting', and '8K photorealistic' force the AI to produce high-end, usable commercial portraits rather than generic fashion images.

Prompt Variations

  • Change suit color to navy or midnight blue
  • Replace burgundy tie with black or deep green
  • Swap toothpick for a cigar or fountain pen
  • Use a different background: smoke, marble, or cityscape at night
  • Add a fedora or overcoat
  • Try a seated pose in a leather armchair

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get 100% accurate face preservation?
    Use Midjourney with your reference photo URL + the prompt and set image weight high (--iw 2.0). For perfect results, combine with a face-swap tool like InsightFaceSwap after generation.
  • What does 'do according to my gender' mean in the prompt?
    It instructs the AI to use the gender from your uploaded photo. If you upload a male face, the model will generate a male portrait. Remove or modify this if your use case differs.
  • Can I remove the tattoos?
    Yes, delete 'A black ink neck tattoo is visible along with subtle finger tattoos' from the prompt.
  • What is 'modern mafia aesthetic'?
    It combines luxury tailoring, confident body language, subtle menace (toothpick), and cinematic mood—inspired by films like The Godfather or TV series Peaky Blinders.
  • Which AI model gives the most realistic skin texture?
    Midjourney v6 / v7 and Stable Diffusion with realistic checklists (Juggernaut XL, Realistic Vision) produce high-quality olive skin and fabric details.
