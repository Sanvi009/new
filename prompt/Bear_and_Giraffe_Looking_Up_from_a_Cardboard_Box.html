How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, deploy it for humorous animal art, box packaging, or curiosity-themed ads in wildlife and decor. Preserve "[Bear & Giraffe]," "looking directly up from inside a cardboard box," "4:5," and "perspective from below" for surprise. Do not modify the bright lighting or curious expressions. Changing animals to "Cat & Dog" could yield pet visuals, leading to gazes, while original creates humorous, composed outcomes.

Prompt

Bear & Giraffe] are looking directly up from inside a cardboard box. They are positioned in the center of the image, with their heads and necks poking out of the top of the box. They are facing the viewer, their curious expressions directed upwards toward a partly cloudy blue sky visible through the opening. They both have a medium build and are looking directly at the camera. The box is light brownish-tan cardboard. The lighting is bright and natural, casting no significant shadows. The perspective is directly from below, looking up into the box. The overall composition is humorous, drawing attention to the [Bear & Giraffe] curious gazes and the unexpected scene. A sense of surprise and curiosity is conveyed. 4:5

Prompt Variations

  • Animals: Swap “[Bear & Giraffe]” → “[Panda & Elephant]” for different animals. Swap for a single animal: “[Just a Bear]”.
  • Box Material: Swap “light brownish-tan cardboard” → “a colorful printed cardboard box” . Swap “cardboard box” → “a wooden crate” .
  • Perspective: Swap “perspective is directly from below, looking up” → “perspective is from the side, at their eye level” .
  • Lighting: Swap “bright and natural, casting no significant shadows” → “dramatic lighting from below, casting strong upward shadows” for a funnier, spookier effect.
