The Prompt 

"A luxurious, romantic flat lay composition set on soft, glossy light blue satin fabric, with elegant folds and a luminous sheen. At the center, a light blue gift box tied with a perfect satin ribbon bow sits gracefully. Beside the box is a crystal-clear perfume bottle with a golden spray cap, filled with vibrant blue liquid. The bottle has the word “SOWAD” written directly on the glass in a clean, elegant font,without any paper label. Below “SOWAD,” in smaller letters, the word “Perfume” appears. On the left, a fresh bouquet of delicate light blue forget-me-not flowers with yellow centers and green stems adds a natural, gentle touch. The entire scene is styled in harmonious shades of blue and gold, evoking sophistication, romance, and celebration. Ratio 9:16.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it for perfume advertising, gift packaging, or romantic branding in beauty and celebration industries. Implement it with "9:16 ratio," preserving "luxurious, romantic flat lay," "light blue gift box," "crystal-clear perfume bottle," and "SOWAD Perfume" for sophisticated harmony. Do not change the blue and gold shades or bouquet. Changing flowers to "roses" could yield passionate visuals, resulting in elegant folds, while forget-me-nots keep gentle, celebratory outcomes.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt is effective due to its romantic and luxurious flat lay composition the use of light blue satin fabric with elegant folds and a luminous sheen creates a soft and sophisticated background the centered gift box and crystal-clear perfume bottle with golden cap add elegance and focus the fresh bouquet of forget-me-not flowers introduces a natural gentle touch enhancing the celebratory mood this image is perfect for perfume launches gift guides or wedding content that requires a harmonious and upscale aesthetic.

Prompt Variations

  • Color Theme: Swap “harmonious shades of blue and gold” → “shades of blush pink and rose gold”. Swap “blue and gold” → “emerald green and gold” for a different luxurious feel.
  • Perfume Bottle Shape: Swap “crystal-clear perfume bottle” → “an opaque ceramic perfume bottle” for a different texture.
  • Flowers: Swap “light blue forget-me-not flowers” → “white gardenias” for a more classic, luxurious flower. Swap “forget-me-nots” → “lavender sprigs” for a different scent association.
  • Gift Box: Swap “light blue gift box” → “a white gift box with silver ribbon” .
