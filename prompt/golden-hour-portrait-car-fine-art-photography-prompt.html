Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does '--style raw --v 6' mean in this prompt?
    These are specific commands for Midjourney AI. '--style raw' reduces Midjourney's default artistic styling for more control. '--v 6' tells it to use version 6 of the AI model for higher quality and better prompt understanding.
  • Can I use this without a reference photo for the face?
    Yes. The phrase '100% face identity lock to reference photo' is optional. The AI will generate a realistic face based on the rest of the description. You can remove that part if you don't have a reference image.
  • How do I get the creamy background blur (bokeh)?
    The prompt instructions 'shallow depth of field' and 'creamy background blur' specifically tell the AI to mimic a camera with a wide aperture, blurring the background and focusing sharply on the subject and car.
