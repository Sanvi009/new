How to Use

This prompt works best when you fill in the bracketed placeholders with specific, vivid details before submitting it to Gemini AI. Start by uploading a clear, front-facing reference photo where your face is well-lit and unobstructed to ensure exact identity replication. In the prompt text, replace `[core vibe]` with one clear adjective like 'mysterious' or 'chaotic' to set the mood. Be specific with the `[hairstyle]` and `[outfit description]`—the AI responds better to concrete terms like 'distressed black leather jacket' rather than just 'streetwear.' For the sidebar details, you can leave the optional fields blank or provide real or fictional data to build out the character's story. To enhance the poster layout, the AI may generate text for the title and sidebar. If the text appears garbled or misspelled, you can use a secondary photo editing tool to overlay clean typography on the generated image. Ensure your output settings are locked to 4:5 aspect ratio for the best editorial framing.

FAQ

  • How can I make the AI generated text on the poster look clean and readable instead of garbled?
    AI image models are excellent at visual composition but notoriously bad at rendering long strings of coherent text within an image. You will likely get blurred letters or nonsense words in the sidebar and title areas. The best workflow is to treat the AI output as the 'plate' or background plate for your poster. Generate the image with the prompt to lock in the perfect lighting, pose, and layout structure. Then, import that high-resolution 8K image into a photo editing tool like Photoshop, Canva, or Photopea. Use clean text layers to manually overlay the 'BRAINLOOM' title, the tagline, and all the character detail text. This guarantees perfect typography while maintaining the ultra-realistic character rendering.
  • The pose and outfit look great, but the face doesn't look exactly like me. What should I adjust?
    Facial identity in AI generation can drift when the prompt includes very strong styling elements like 'sharp features,' 'attitude-filled gaze,' or heavy makeup changes. To improve the likeness, first ensure your uploaded reference image is as close as possible to the desired angle and lighting. If the output still misses the mark, try reducing the strength of the 'styling' words in the prompt slightly—for example, change 'razor-sharp contouring' to 'light contouring' or remove makeup descriptors entirely. For the most precise results, use a dedicated 'Face Swap' or 'Inpainting' tool on the final image to restore the exact reference face while keeping the AI-generated hair, outfit, and cinematic lighting.
  • Can I use this prompt for a male character or a different gender presentation?
    Absolutely. The prompt is written with gender-neutral placeholders like 'me' and 'they are styled' specifically so it adapts to any uploaded reference image. The AI model will analyze the facial structure, bone structure, and physical identity from your uploaded photo and apply the described outfit, hair, and vibe accordingly. Whether your reference is masculine, feminine, or androgynous, the prompt will generate a cinematic poster matching that exact physical identity. Just be mindful when filling out the optional `[outfit description]` bracket to use terms that align with the look you want, such as 'tailored three-piece suit' or 'corset and cargo pants.'
