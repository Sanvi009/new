Prompt Explanation

This prompt is arguably the most technically complex and artistically ambitious one we've covered. It's not just asking for an image; it's asking for a piece of graphic design that integrates hyper-realistic portraiture, precise typography, and a sophisticated masking effect, all while maintaining absolute fidelity to a specific person's identity. The prompt's structure mimics a professional creative brief, with clear headings, prioritized sections, and explicit 'do not' commands. This structured approach is essential for guiding a large language model and image generator through a multi-step conceptual process. The first and most critical battle is identity preservation. The 'Face Match & Identity Preservation (STRICT)' section is a fortress of specificity. It lists every conceivable facial feature, from 'jawline' to 'hairline,' and demands 'exact' replication. It explicitly forbids beautification or stylization. This is necessary because AI image generators are trained on millions of idealized faces and will default to making the subject more conventionally attractive unless aggressively stopped. The 'True skin texture, pores, and natural imperfections' instruction is a direct counter-command to the AI's smoothing algorithms. Following this, the 'Editorial Grooming Refinement' section performs a delicate balancing act. It asks for a 'professional editorial portrait' with 'controlled structure' and visible 'individual hair strands,' but immediately reinforces the ban on 'plastic smoothing.' This tells the AI to refine the presentation without losing the authentic human details established in the previous section. The 'Eye Detail Rendering (EXTREME PRIORITY)' section is where the portrait gains its soul. The level of detail here—'iris texture,' 'radial patterns,' 'ciliary filaments' (implied by eyelash strands), 'realistic moisture reflections'—is extraordinary. It ensures the eyes are not just correctly shaped but are rendered with the depth and life of a professional photograph. This focus on micro-detail is what elevates an image from a simple portrait to an 'editorial' one. The 'Split Composition Layout' is described with geometric precision: 'exactly 50/50 vertically,' 'clean vertical edge cut at exact center of face,' 'no feathering or blending.' This ensures the design has a sharp, modern, magazine-quality graphic punch. The right half is a 'hyper-realistic black-and-white portrait,' a classic editorial choice. The left half is a 'pure white background,' creating maximum contrast and a clean canvas for the text. The 'Typography Layout' is equally precise, specifying the exact text ('FOLLOW ME FOR MORE AI PROMPT'), font style ('bold modern sans-serif'), and arrangement ('tight vertical stacking'). This leaves no room for the AI to invent its own text or layout. The final and most brilliant section is the 'Typography Masking Effect.' This is a conceptually rich instruction that fuses the typography and the portrait. Describing letters as 'cut-out mask windows' that reveal 'faded grayscale fragments of the same subject's face' is a masterful way to translate a complex Photoshop technique into a machine-readable prompt. It creates a layered, intriguing design where the text literally contains the image of the person, suggesting that the message and the identity are one and the same. Common mistakes when using this prompt include the AI failing to achieve the split composition correctly, often blending the face across the center. If this happens, you must re-emphasize 'no feathering' or 'hard edge.' Another common issue is the AI not understanding the masking effect, instead simply placing colored text over the image. In that case, you might need to use a platform like Photoshop with AI generation, or a tool specifically strong at typography like Ideogram, and then combine elements. The prompt's ultimate power is its ability to demand both photographic realism and graphic design sophistication in a single, coherent output, creating a piece of art that feels personal, professional, and conceptually deep.

The Prompt 

Create a minimalist, high-contrast editorial poster combining a hyper-realistic portrait with of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image with bold motivational typography using a perfectly centered vertical split composition. Face Match & Identity Preservation (STRICT) Use the uploaded reference image as the identity source. Preserve with extremely high accuracy: Exact facial structure, proportions, and head shape. Natural jawline, cheekbone definition, and chin shape. Exact eye shape, spacing, eyelid form, and gaze alignment. Exact nose structure and lip shape. Exact hairline and hairstyle pattern. True skin texture, pores, and natural imperfections. Do NOT beautify, stylize, or alter identity. Maintain absolute identity fidelity. Editorial Grooming Refinement (Subtle, Realistic) Refine appearance into a professional editorial portrait while preserving identity: Subject wears a clean matte black crew-neck t-shirt, minimal texture controlled structure. Individual hair strands clearly visible, especially along hairline. Skin must retain: Visible pores, Micro skin texture, Fine tonal variations, Natural realism. No plastic smoothing. Face Position & Crop (CRITICAL) Face is perfectly front-facing. Head centered exactly on the vertical split line. Crop includes: Top hair visible, Full forehead, Eyes centered horizontally, Nose centered on split line, Lips fully visible, Chin and slight upper neck visible. Portrait occupies the entire right half vertically. Neutral, calm, confident editorial expression. Direct eye contact with camera. Eye Detail Rendering (EXTREME PRIORITY) Eyes must be ultra-sharp and highly detailed: Clear iris texture and radial patterns, Crisp pupil edges, Natural sclera tone and subtle veins, Defined eyelash strands, Individual eyebrow hairs visible, Realistic moisture reflections, Small clean catchlight reflection in pupil. Eyes must appear sharp, alive, and emotionally strong. Split Composition Layout Canvas divided exactly 50/50 vertically. Right Half: Hyper-realistic black-and-white portrait. Clean vertical edge cut at exact center of face. No feathering or blending across split edge. Left Half: Pure white background. Clean, flat, neutral white tone. No gradients or texture. Typography Layout (Exact Structure) Display quote stacked vertically: FOLLOW ME FOR MORE AI PROMPT. Typography must have: Bold modern sans-serif font, Heavy weight, Tight vertical stacking, Clean alignment, Large scale text filling entire left half. Typography Masking Effect (CRITICAL DETAIL) Typography letters act as cut-out mask windows. Inside letters show faded grayscale fragments of the same subject's face from the left side. Requirements: Facial fragments align anatomically correctly. AR-9:16

How to Use This Prompt

This is an advanced, highly detailed prompt that requires precision. Start by uploading a very clear, high-resolution, front-facing photo of yourself. The success of this entire design hinges on the AI capturing your exact features, as the prompt contains extremely strict identity preservation instructions. Copy and paste the entire prompt into your AI image generator. Do not edit or simplify it—every section serves a purpose. The prompt is structured like a creative brief, with sections like 'Face Match,' 'Eye Detail,' and 'Typography Layout.' This helps the AI process complex instructions in stages. If the initial results don't perfectly split your face down the middle, or if the typography masking effect is missing, you may need to run multiple generations. Pay close attention to the eyes—the prompt demands 'ultra-sharp' detail with iris texture and catchlights. If the eyes look soft, add a parameter like '--q 2' (quality) if using Midjourney. The 'typography masking effect' is the most complex part. If the letters don't show face fragments, you might need to emphasize 'letters act as cut-out mask windows' or try a different generator better suited for typography, like Ideogram. The final text is specified as 'FOLLOW ME FOR MORE AI PROMPT'—ensure this exact text appears in your prompt.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'typography masking effect' mean in this prompt?
    It's a sophisticated design technique where the letters of the text act like windows or stencils. Instead of solid color, the inside of each letter reveals a faded, grayscale fragment of the subject's face. This creates a powerful visual connection between the text and the portrait.
  • Why is there so much detail about preserving skin texture and pores?
    The goal is a 'hyper-realistic' editorial portrait, not an AI-smoothed, idealized version. By explicitly demanding 'visible pores,' 'micro skin texture,' and 'natural imperfections,' the prompt overrides the AI's default tendency to beautify, ensuring the final image looks authentically human and photographically real.
  • What does 'clean vertical edge cut at exact center of face' achieve?
    This creates a stark, graphic split composition. Half of the face is visible on the right, and the left half is replaced by the typography and white background. The clean, unfeathered edge makes the design feel sharp, modern, and editorially bold, like a high-end magazine cover.
