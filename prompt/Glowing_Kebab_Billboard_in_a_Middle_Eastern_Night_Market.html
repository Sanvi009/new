How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it in billboard design, outdoor advertising, or experiential marketing for food trucks and restaurants, particularly in Middle Eastern-themed campaigns or urban night market promotions. Feed it into image generation software by keeping the hyper-realistic 3D elements like "giant kebab wrap bursts," "glowing spice particles," and "billboard frame with geometric patterns" intact, while optionally adjusting minor details if the brand changes. Do not alter the warm golden light, bustling plaza setting, or blend of traditional and modern vibes to preserve the immersive charm. Prompt

Hyper-realistic 3D billboard in a bustling Middle Eastern night market plaza, glowing in warm golden light. A giant kebab wrap bursts from the screen mid-air — grilled meat, fresh vegetables, sauces, steam, and glowing spice particles flying. Surrounded by shards of golden mosaic tiles, ember trails, and swirling spice dust.Billboard frame with geometric patterns, brand name "Sultan’s Kebab". Below, a cozy kebab shop with copper lanterns, open-grill flames, patterned tiles, and warm light. Street filled with market stalls, hanging lights, and pedestrians, blending traditional Middle Eastern charm with modern city vibes
