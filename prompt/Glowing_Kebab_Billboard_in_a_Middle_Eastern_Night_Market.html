How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it in billboard design, outdoor advertising, or experiential marketing for food trucks and restaurants, particularly in Middle Eastern-themed campaigns or urban night market promotions. Feed it into image generation software by keeping the hyper-realistic 3D elements like "giant kebab wrap bursts," "glowing spice particles," and "billboard frame with geometric patterns" intact, while optionally adjusting minor details if the brand changes. Do not alter the warm golden light, bustling plaza setting, or blend of traditional and modern vibes to preserve the immersive charm. For instance, replacing "Sultan’s Kebab" with "Falafel Delight" could yield explosive vegetarian ads, resulting in dynamic visuals that attract crowds in street food festivals or digital signage.

  • Brand Name: Swap “Sultan’s Kebab” → “Cairo Grill” for a different brand identity. Swap “Sultan’s Kebab” → “Desert Bite” for a more modern name.
  • Surrounding Elements: Swap “golden mosaic tiles” → “intricate arabesque metalwork” for a different cultural motif. Swap “ember trails and spice dust” → “flying banknotes and gold coins” for a theme of wealth and value.
  • Time: Swap “night market” → “a bustling daytime market” for a brighter, sunlit atmosphere. Swap “warm golden light” → “cool blue hour light” just after sunset.
