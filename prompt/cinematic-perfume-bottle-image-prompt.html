An AI image generation prompt for creating hyper-realistic, dark luxury photos of a crystal perfume bottle with dramatic lighting and smoke.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic cinematic product image of an elegant crystal perfume bottle placed on reflective black glass. Soft smoke curls around the bottle, subtle light streaks in the background, tiny floating dust particles catching the light. Dark luxury mood, dramatic rim lighting, ultra-sharp reflections, shallow depth of field, premium fragrance campaign style, 90mm lens look, no text, no watermark.

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt text into your AI image generator like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL-E. For best results, use the negative prompt 'text, watermark, logo, people, background clutter'. Adjust the aspect ratio for social media (1:1) or ad banners (16:9).

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it uses specific photography terms like '90mm lens look' and 'shallow depth of field' which AI models recognize for realistic bokeh. 'Reflective black glass' and 'rim lighting' create high-contrast, commercial-grade reflections. Descriptors like 'ultra-realistic cinematic' and 'premium fragrance campaign style' trigger the AI's training on professional ad photography.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'crystal perfume bottle' to 'gold atomizer' or 'matte black fragrance bottle'.
  • Replace 'dark luxury mood' with 'bright minimalist studio lighting' for a clean look.
  • Swap 'soft smoke' for 'sprinkled water droplets' or 'falling flower petals'.
  • Try 'aerial top-down view' instead of the side view for a flat lay style.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Which AI image generator is best for this perfume bottle prompt?
    Midjourney V6 and Stable Diffusion XL are excellent for realistic product shots with fine details like reflections and smoke.
  • How can I remove the watermark or text from the generated image?
    Always include 'no text, no watermark' in your prompt. If text appears, use an inpainting tool in Stable Diffusion or the 'Vary (Region)' feature in Midjourney to erase it.
  • Why is my perfume bottle not looking realistic?
    Ensure your prompt includes 'ultra-realistic' and 'cinematic'. Using a quality model like 'realistic vision' in Stable Diffusion or '--style raw' in Midjourney also helps.
  • Can I use this AI image for a real perfume ad?
    Yes, but check the AI generator's terms of service for commercial use. For brands, it's best to use the image as a mood board or mock-up concept.
