Upload a clear photo of your face. Copy the prompt text into your AI image generator. The prompt will create a vertical (9:16) fashion portrait that applies artistic motion blur and dynamic lighting effects to your likeness, resulting in an abstract, editorial-style image.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by using a concentrated list of highly stylized photography and visual art keywords that AI models associate with specific aesthetics. Terms like 'cinematic motion blur', 'slow shutter effect', 'motion streaks', and 'high contrast lighting' are direct instructions for creating a blurred, dynamic image. The inclusion of 'editorial fashion photography' and 'viral image style' further guides the AI toward a modern, shareable look that prioritizes mood and movement over perfect clarity.

Prompt Variations

  • Combine with a specific setting, e.g., 'in a neon-lit tunnel' or 'on a city street at night'.
  • Intensify the effect with 'extreme motion blur' or soften it with 'subtle movement trails'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a 'slow shutter effect' in AI?
    In photography, a slow shutter speed captures motion as blur. In AI, using this term instructs the model to generate streaks of light or blurred elements around the subject, simulating that photographic technique to convey movement and energy.
  • Will my face be recognizable with all the motion blur?
    Yes, the instruction 'Use the provided image as face' combined with 'dynamic portrait' typically leads the AI to keep the core facial structure recognizable while applying blur to the hair, clothing, or background. The face is often the sharpest element in the center of the motion.
  • Is this prompt good for profile pictures?
    It creates a very artistic and attention-grabbing image, perfect for social media profiles where you want to stand out. It's less suitable for formal profiles where a clear, sharp headshot is required.
