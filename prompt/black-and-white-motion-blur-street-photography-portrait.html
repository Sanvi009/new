Black and White Motion Blur Street Photography Portrait

AI Image Prompts 2026-05-14 Brainloom

This prompt produces a striking, vintage-style black and white photograph that perfectly captures the frantic energy of a busy city street. The subject is seen walking through a blurred crowd, anchored in sharp or semi-sharp focus while everyone else becomes a ghost of motion. It mimics the distinct look of a Leica M3 film camera, utilizing an ultra-thin lens effect, monochrome grading, and a slight overexposure to create an ethereal, antique glow. You will get a highly artistic, nostalgic piece of street photography that feels both timeless and deeply personal.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Black and white photograph of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image  walking through a crowd, motion blurred, taken from street level in the distance, with an ultra-thin lens, dressed in modern clothes, with other people moving in the background, taken with a Leica M3 film camera, monochrome, with high exposure and an antique glow effect. ar. 9:16.

How to Use

To get the best results with this prompt, load it into an AI tool that allows image referencing, like Midjourney or Gemini, so it accurately captures your face. If your generator doesn't use reference images, simply replace the 'uploaded image' text with a physical description of yourself. You can customize the mood by changing 'modern clothes' to something like '1920s trench coat' or 'cyberpunk streetwear.' If the image comes out too bright, remove the 'high exposure' keyword to bring back more contrast and deeper shadows in the monochrome aesthetic.

FAQ

  • Why is the motion blur affecting my face instead of just the background?
    AI generators can sometimes struggle to separate the subject from the background blur. If this happens, try adding phrases like 'sharp focus on the subject's face' or 'shallow depth of field with the crowd blurred out' to force the AI to isolate you from the movement.
  • How do I remove the antique or vintage film look?
    Simply remove the keywords 'Leica M3 film camera,' 'monochrome,' and 'antique glow effect.' You can replace them with terms like 'modern digital photography,' 'vibrant natural colors,' and 'crisp high-definition lighting' to modernize the final image.
  • Can I change the aspect ratio for an Instagram square post?
    Yes! The current prompt uses 'ar. 9:16', which is perfect for mobile screens, Reels, or TikTok. If you want a square post, change it to 'ar 1:1'. For a cinematic horizontal landscape shot, change it to 'ar 16:9'.
