How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for cinematic portraits, lifestyle photography, or noir-themed ads in fashion and film. Preserve "cinematic low-light portrait," "man sitting relaxed on a couch," "open button silk shirt," and "moody, dramatic atmosphere" for cool attitude. Do not change the cigarette lighting or dark background. Optionally vary drink; for example, wine could yield elegant visuals, leading to shadows, while whiskey keeps calm, detailed outcomes.

Prompt

A cinematic low-light portrait of a man sitting relaxed on a couch, wearing an open button silk shirt with sweat marks, sunglasses, and a necklace. Wearing Cap backwords,He is holding a cigarette in his mouth while lighting it with a metallic lighter in one hand and holding a glass of dark whiskey in the other hand. The background is dim and shadowy, emphasizing a moody, dramatic atmosphere. His facial expression is calm, slightly tilted back, enjoying the moment with a cool attitude.The lighting is warm, dark, and focused on his face and chest, creating a cinematic noir look. Keep the same framing, same background, same color tone, same size, and same details as the original photo.
