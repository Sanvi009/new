Generate a high-fashion Vogue-style portrait using an extreme wide-angle fisheye lens in a vintage bathroom setting, with precise lighting, retro styling, and your exact facial features.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a masterclass in combining technical photography knowledge with creative direction for AI image generation. At its core, it's designed to solve a common problem: how to make an AI-generated portrait feel authentic, professionally shot, and personally identifiable all at once. The solution lies in layering instructions that address different aspects of the final image, from the camera hardware to the lighting conditions to the subject's styling. The prompt begins with the lens choice because the fisheye perspective fundamentally changes how the entire image will be composed. Unlike a standard portrait lens that tries to flatter the subject by minimizing distortion, a 14mm fisheye deliberately exaggerates features near the edges and creates a sense of immersive depth. This is a bold creative choice that immediately sets the image apart from typical portraits. By specifying 'camera slightly below eye level and tilted upward,' the prompt adds a dynamic angle that makes the viewer feel like they're looking up at the subject, which creates a subtle power dynamic and adds visual interest. The mid-sip action is a clever storytelling element. Instead of a static pose, we have a captured moment—the subject is in the middle of a simple, human action. This adds a layer of realism and approachability to what might otherwise be a very stylized, almost cold fashion image. The matte turquoise coffee cup isn't just a prop; it's a color anchor that will interact with the teal bathroom tiles and the warm skin tones. Color theory plays a huge role here: teal and warm orange/burgundy are complementary colors on the color wheel, meaning they create visual tension and vibrancy when placed together. The prompt explicitly calls out 'cinematic teal-and-warm color grading,' which is a hugely popular look in modern film and photography because it makes skin tones pop against cool backgrounds. The setting—a vintage bathroom with teal tile and frosted windows—is unexpectedly perfect for a fashion shoot. Bathrooms offer interesting textures, reflective surfaces, and often unique lighting from windows. The frosted panels create 'subtle geometric shadows,' which add visual texture without overwhelming the subject. This shows an understanding that great photography isn't just about the person; it's about how light and environment interact to create mood. Word choice throughout is deliberate and professional. Terms like 'realistic skin texture,' 'sharp focus,' 'shallow depth of field,' and 'ultra high resolution' are all quality indicators that the AI associates with professional photography. By stringing them together, you're essentially telling the AI to generate an image that would pass muster with a fashion magazine photo editor. The inclusion of 'subtle film grain' is particularly smart—it adds a touch of analog warmth and prevents the image from looking too digitally perfect, which can sometimes read as artificial. Common mistakes users might make with this prompt include uploading a low-quality reference image that doesn't provide enough detail for the AI to work with. Another mistake is forgetting that the fisheye lens will distort facial features near the edges of the frame, so if you want your face to appear relatively normal, you need to ensure the composition keeps your face near the center where distortion is minimal. The prompt accounts for this by describing the subject 'leaning toward the camera,' which naturally centers the face. Users can tweak this prompt by adjusting the color palette—changing turquoise to red and teal to gold would create a completely different mood. The sunglasses could be removed for a more direct eye-contact shot, or changed to mirrored lenses for a more mysterious vibe. The bathroom setting could become a vintage diner, a subway station, or any location with interesting reflective surfaces and lighting potential. Each change ripples through the generation, creating a uniquely personal image while maintaining the technical foundation that makes the prompt work so well.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic editorial fashion portrait shot with an extreme wide-angle 14mm fisheye lens, dramatic close-up perspective with strong lens distortion subject leaning toward the camera mid-sip while holding a matte turquoise ceramic coffee cup near my lips, camera slightly below eye level and tilted upward for immersive depth; ,me as subject with exact same facial and physical identity from the uplaoded image , with , minimal hand tattoos and a gold ring, wearing oversized amber/yellow tinted aviator sunglasses with a thin gold frame, styled in a textured brown tailored blazer over a deep burgundy shirt for a modern 70s retro-meets-luxury street aesthetic; set inside a vintage bathroom with teal ceramic tiled walls and cream upper walls, frosted window panels above casting soft natural daylight and subtle geometric shadows, cinematic teal-and-warm color grading, soft diffused window light from the top right, realistic skin texture, sharp focus on face and cup with shallow depth of field while maintaining wide-angle distortion, intimate confident high-fashion Vogue-style composition, ultra high resolution 8K RAW photography, HDR, subtle film grain, highly detailed textures, no watermark, no text, aspect ratio 9:16.

How to Use This Prompt

Start by uploading a clear, high-resolution photo of yourself. The AI needs a quality reference to match your facial features and body structure accurately. Make sure your face is well-lit and visible without obstructions like sunglasses or hands, since the final image will include these elements but the reference should be clean. Copy the entire prompt exactly as written. Do not edit or shorten it, as every detail serves a purpose. Paste it into your AI image generator of choice, such as Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion. If the platform supports image prompts, upload your photo first and then add the text. Some tools let you use an image URL, so hosting your photo on a site like Imgur can be helpful. After submission, the AI will begin generating. This prompt is complex with many specific elements, so it may take a moment. Generate multiple variations by using the re-roll or generate again feature. Compare the results and look for the one that best captures your likeness while following the lens distortion and lighting instructions. If the facial features are slightly off, many platforms now offer editing tools like inpainting. Select the face area and regenerate just that part while keeping the rest. You can also adjust the color grading if the teal-and-warm tones aren't prominent enough by adding a comment like 'stronger teal shadows' in a follow-up edit. Pay attention to the hand tattoos and gold ring details, as these small elements add authenticity to the 70s retro aesthetic.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt succeeds because it combines precise technical photography terms with rich descriptive language that AI image generators understand well. Starting with 'Ultra-realistic editorial fashion portrait' sets a high standard for quality and style right from the beginning. The AI knows that 'editorial' implies a polished, magazine-ready look, while 'ultra-realistic' pushes it away from overly smooth or artificial results. The specific lens choice—'extreme wide-angle 14mm fisheye lens'—is brilliant because it tells the AI exactly what distortion effect to apply. Fisheye lenses have a very distinctive look with curved lines and exaggerated perspective, and by naming the exact focal length, you're tapping into the AI's training data which includes thousands of images tagged with this information. Adding 'strong lens distortion' reinforces this instruction. The composition details are equally precise: 'camera slightly below eye level and tilted upward for immersive depth' gives the AI clear geometric instructions. This creates a dynamic, engaging perspective that feels intimate and slightly unconventional, perfect for fashion editorial work. The pose description—'leaning toward the camera mid-sip'—captures a candid, in-between moment that feels more authentic than a static pose. Color is handled masterfully in this prompt. The 'matte turquoise ceramic coffee cup' introduces a cool accent color that will later be echoed in the 'cinematic teal-and-warm color grading' and the 'teal ceramic tiled walls' of the bathroom. This creates visual harmony across the image. The amber sunglasses and deep burgundy shirt provide warm contrast, and the instruction for 'soft natural daylight' from frosted windows ensures the lighting feels organic rather than artificially staged. The technical specifications at the end—'8K RAW photography, HDR, subtle film grain'—act as quality filters. RAW photography implies maximum detail and editing flexibility, HDR ensures a wide dynamic range, and film grain adds a touch of analog authenticity that prevents the image from looking too digitally sterile. The 'no watermark, no text' instruction is practical, preventing the AI from adding distracting elements that sometimes appear in generated images.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does the AI sometimes ignore the lens distortion request?
    Some AI models are trained primarily on standard lens perspectives and may not generate extreme fisheye distortion consistently. Adding 'extreme distortion, curved lines' alongside the lens specification can help reinforce the effect.
  • How do I ensure my face looks exactly like my uploaded photo?
    Use the highest quality reference photo possible with consistent lighting. Some platforms offer face-swapping features after generation, or you can use inpainting tools to refine facial features while keeping the rest of the image intact.
  • What does 'HDR' mean in the prompt and why include it?
    HDR stands for High Dynamic Range, which means the image will have good detail in both very bright and very dark areas. Including it tells the AI to avoid blown-out highlights or completely black shadows, preserving texture throughout.
