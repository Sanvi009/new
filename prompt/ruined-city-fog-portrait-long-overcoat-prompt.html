Cinematic Portrait

Generate a cinematic full-body portrait of you standing alone in an empty ruined city street. Dense fog, desaturated teal/green-gray palette, long dark overcoat, scarf, hands in pockets, serious distant expression. 9:16.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a powerful, cinematic full-body portrait that evokes themes of solitude, resilience, and dystopian atmosphere. Its creative purpose is to place the subject within a narrative of urban decay and isolation, creating an image that feels like a still from a post-apocalyptic film or a piece of melancholic concept art. The focus is on the human figure as a small, solitary element within a vast, ruined environment, emphasizing a mood of quiet contemplation or somber watchfulness. The 'prompt_text' works by layering directives for identity, environment, atmosphere, and composition to build this specific world. It begins with the essential, unchangeable command: 'cinematic full-body urban portrait, of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image.' This ensures the resulting image is a personal, rather than generic, representation. The setting is then powerfully defined: 'standing alone in the middle of an empty ruined city street.' The key words here are 'alone,' 'empty,' and 'ruined,' which immediately establish a sense of desolation and abandonment. The subject's demeanor is specified with 'serious distant expression' and 'messy wavy hair,' adding to the unkempt, weathered feel. The clothing is chosen to fit the cold, moody atmosphere: a 'long dark overcoat and scarf/high collar' suggests warmth and protection against the elements, while 'hands in pockets' reinforces a closed-off, contemplative body language. The environmental and atmospheric details are what truly make this prompt cinematic. 'Dense fog and haze' is not just weather; it's a tool to obscure detail, add mystery, and create a sense of depth and scale, softening the harshness of the ruined buildings. The 'desaturated teal/green-gray color palette' is a specific color grade, borrowed from dystopian cinema, that cools the image and drains it of vibrant life, enhancing the feeling of decay and melancholy. The compositional instruction, 'tall buildings on both sides creating a corridor,' is crucial for directing the viewer's eye. This creates a forced perspective, a tunnel-like view that leads directly toward the subject, emphasizing their isolation and the sheer scale of the ruined architecture surrounding them. This use of leading lines is a classic compositional technique that adds dynamism and focus. The fixed elements are the core components that define the prompt's unique identity: the ruined urban setting, the dense fog, the specific desaturated teal/green-gray palette, and the full-body shot of a solitary figure. Changing these would fundamentally alter the image's theme and mood. However, the prompt is highly adaptable in other ways. The variations provided are excellent examples: changing the 'ruined city' to an 'abandoned factory' or 'flooded street' would keep the dystopian theme but shift the specific context. Replacing the desaturated teal with 'cold blue or sepia tones' would alter the emotional temperature from melancholy to perhaps icy or nostalgic. Modifying the weather from fog to 'light rain or snow' would change the tactile feel of the scene, adding elements of discomfort or cleansing. For best results, using a high image weight (like --iw 2 in Midjourney) is a practical tip to ensure the subject's identity is preserved against the strong pull of the atmospheric scene. A common mistake is to not generate enough iterations to achieve the desired 'dense fog' effect; fog can be unpredictable for AI, so multiple attempts are often necessary. Another is to forget that 'ruined' implies damage, so the final image should show cracked pavement and broken windows, not a pristine street. By combining a strong narrative concept with precise atmospheric controls and compositional geometry, this prompt successfully directs the AI to create a deeply evocative cinematic portrait that tells a story of solitude in a forgotten world.

The Prompt 

cinematic full-body urban portrait, of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image standing alone in the middle of an empty ruined city street, serious distant expression, messy wavy hair, wearing a long dark overcoat and scarf/high collar, hands in pockets, cold moody atmosphere, dense fog and haze, desaturated teal/green-gray color palette, tall buildings on both sides creating a corridor . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16. 5. Use high image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney) to preserve facial identity and body type. 6. Generate multiple times to achieve dense atmospheric fog.

Why This Prompt Works

It creates a strong sense of isolation by placing a single figure in a ruined urban corridor. The desaturated teal/green-gray palette signals a specific cinematic mood that AI color models understand. It describes clothing that suits the environment (long overcoat, scarf) and a natural pose (hands in pockets). Dense fog simplifies background details while adding atmosphere. Tall buildings on both sides create leading lines that draw focus to the subject.

Prompt Variations

  • Change ruined city to abandoned factory or flooded street
  • Replace desaturated teal with cold blue or sepia tones
  • Modify weather from fog to light rain or snow
  • Change overcoat to leather jacket or hooded coat
  • Add subtle light source like distant streetlamp
  • Make expression contemplative instead of distant
  • Add environmental details like rubble or cracked pavement

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'tall buildings on both sides creating a corridor' mean?
    The street is framed by vertical architecture on left and right, leading the eye toward the subject or deep into the background. Creates a tunnel-like perspective.
  • How ruined should the city look?
    Prompt says 'ruined city street' - expect cracked pavement, damaged building facades, debris, broken windows. Not pristine urban environment.
  • What is 'desaturated teal/green-gray color palette'?
    Muted, low-saturation colors with a cool tint. Inspired by dystopian films and melancholy cinematography. Skin tones remain natural but shifted cool.
  • Will the fog cover the subject?
    Dense fog and haze should be atmospheric but the subject remains clearly visible. Fog sits between buildings and in background, softening edges.
  • What is 'scarf/high collar' style?
    Either a wrapped scarf or an overcoat with a tall, turned-up collar that covers the neck. Adds to cold, protected silhouette.
  • Can I use this for book covers or album art?
    Yes, this aesthetic is commonly used for dystopian fiction, cinematic music videos, and atmospheric editorial fashion.
