Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create a specific type of image: a stylized 3D character render that maintains your exact facial likeness while transforming your body into a cute, playful character. The user intent is likely personal branding, social media avatars, gaming profiles, or just creative fun. People searching for this kind of prompt probably want to see themselves reimagined as a video game character or collectible figurine. The prompt's structure is carefully organized to handle multiple complex requirements. It begins with the core concept: "A high-quality 3D stylized 3D character render of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image." The repetition of "3D" and the emphasis on "100% likeness" establish the primary goal. The AI must prioritize facial accuracy above all else. The next part describes the character's body and pose: "depicted as a cute, slightly chubby character in a playful crouching pose, standing on one foot." This is a specific character design choice. The "cute" and "slightly chubby" descriptors create an approachable, endearing quality. The crouching pose on one foot is dynamic and whimsical—it suggests movement and playfulness, making the character feel alive rather than static. The instruction about the finger sign is notable because it references a second uploaded image. This acknowledges a limitation of text-only prompts: complex hand gestures are hard to describe accurately. By providing a visual reference, you're giving the AI the exact information it needs. The "winking at the camera" adds another layer of personality, making the character feel like it's interacting with the viewer. "Keep the face unchanged" is a crucial reinforcement. After describing the body transformation, this reminder ensures the AI doesn't alter your facial features to match the cute body style. Your face should remain recognizable even as the body becomes stylized. The outfit description is highly detailed: bright lime green beanie with a "GreenDay" patch, grey zip-up hoodie over white t-shirt, tan cargo pants, white sneakers with red accents. Each item is specified with color and sometimes brand or style. The "GreenDay" patch is a nice personal touch—it could reference a favorite band or just be a fun detail. This level of specificity prevents the AI from generating generic clothing and ensures the final character has a distinct, personalized look. The solid vibrant orange background is a deliberate choice. In character design, solid backgrounds are often used in concept art and character sheets to keep focus on the character. The vibrant orange creates strong color contrast with the outfit's greens, greys, tans, and whites, making every detail pop. The technical specifications guide the rendering quality. "Soft cinematic lighting" ensures the character is flatteringly lit without harsh shadows. "Smooth 3D textures" tells the AI to aim for a polished, non-grainy surface quality. "High detail" and "8K resolution" demand maximum fidelity. "Octane render style" references a specific high-end rendering engine known for its realistic materials and lighting, pushing the AI toward that professional look. Word choice throughout is precise. "Stylized," "cute," "playful" set the tone. "Crouching pose, standing on one foot" describes the action. "Lime green," "grey," "tan," "white with red accents" specify colors. "Soft cinematic lighting," "smooth textures," "Octane render" are technical terms that guide quality. Each word pulls the AI in a specific direction. Users can adapt this prompt by changing the character's body type, outfit, or background while keeping the core concept of a stylized 3D render with accurate facial likeness. A different pose, like jumping or sitting, would change the energy. A different background, like a cartoon environment, would add context. The "GreenDay" patch could become any personal detail—a name, a logo, a symbol. Common mistakes include using a low-quality face reference that doesn't capture your features clearly, or a hand gesture reference that's ambiguous. Another pitfall is not specifying "keep the face unchanged," which might result in the AI altering your features to match the cute style. Some users might forget to include both image references, leaving the AI to guess at the hand sign. For better results, choose a face reference with good lighting and a neutral expression, so the AI has a clear baseline for your features. The hand gesture reference should be well-lit and clearly show the finger position. Run multiple generations and look for the one where your face is accurately rendered, the pose is balanced, and the outfit details are correct. You might need to regenerate if the AI struggles with the one-foot balance or the wink. The combination of facial accuracy and stylized body is challenging, so be patient and experiment with different seeds.

The Prompt 

A high-quality 3D stylized 3D character render of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image , maintaining a 100% likeness of my facial features, . i depicted as a cute, slightly chubby character in a playful crouching pose, standing on one foot. i making finger sign [as second photo uploaded preference] with one hand and winking at the camera. keep the face unchanged. i wearing a bright lime green beanie with a small “GreenDay” patch, a grey zip-up hoodie over a white t-shirt, tan cargo pants, and white sneakers with red accents. The background is a solid, vibrant orange. Soft cinematic lighting, smooth 3D textures, high detail, 8K resolution, Octane render style, ar-9:16.

How to Use This Prompt

This prompt requires two uploaded images for best results. First, you need a clear reference photo of your face to maintain your exact facial identity. Second, you need a reference image showing the specific finger sign or hand gesture you want the character to make. Upload both images at the beginning of your prompt in tools like Midjourney that support multiple image prompts. The prompt is designed to transform you into a stylized 3D character while preserving your likeness. The phrase "maintaining a 100% likeness of my facial features" and "keep the face unchanged" are critical instructions that tell the AI to prioritize facial accuracy even as it stylizes the rest of the body. The character description is specific: cute, slightly chubby, in a playful crouching pose standing on one foot. This creates a dynamic, energetic posture. The wink at the camera adds personality and charm. The outfit is detailed with colors and small touches like the "GreenDay" patch on the beanie, which adds a personal or nostalgic element. The background is a solid vibrant orange, which creates strong contrast with the character's outfit colors. Soft cinematic lighting ensures the character is well-lit without harsh shadows. Smooth 3D textures and high detail push the quality, while Octane render style tells the AI to aim for that specific high-end 3D rendering aesthetic. The 9:16 aspect ratio sets the vertical format. Run multiple generations to get the best combination of likeness, pose, and style. The AI might need several tries to accurately replicate the finger sign from your second reference image while keeping your face recognizable.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt succeeds because it clearly separates what must stay realistic (your face) from what can be stylized (the body and overall aesthetic). By explicitly stating "maintaining a 100% likeness" and "keep the face unchanged," you're telling the AI that facial accuracy is non-negotiable, even as it transforms you into a cute, chubby character. The pose description is specific and dynamic. "Playful crouching pose, standing on one foot" gives the AI a clear physical action to render. This is much more effective than just saying "dynamic pose" because it describes exactly how the body should be positioned. Adding "winking at the camera" gives the character a specific expression that adds personality and charm. The outfit details are precise: bright lime green beanie with a "GreenDay" patch, grey zip-up hoodie over white t-shirt, tan cargo pants, white sneakers with red accents. Each item is described with color and sometimes texture. This level of detail ensures the AI doesn't invent generic clothing but instead renders exactly what you want. The "GreenDay" patch adds a personal touch that makes the character uniquely yours. The solid vibrant orange background is a smart choice. It creates maximum contrast with the outfit colors, making the character pop. It also simplifies the scene, letting the character's pose and expression be the sole focus. Soft cinematic lighting ensures the character is flatteringly lit, while smooth 3D textures and Octane render style guide the AI toward a high-end, professional 3D aesthetic. The inclusion of a second reference image for the finger sign is crucial. Hand gestures are notoriously difficult for AI to generate accurately from text alone. By providing a visual reference, you're giving the AI exactly what it needs to replicate that specific hand pose.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why do I need two reference images for this prompt?
    One image establishes your facial features for likeness, and the second shows the specific finger sign you want. Hand gestures are complex, so a visual reference helps the AI replicate them accurately.
  • What is Octane render style and why is it mentioned?
    Octane Render is a high-end 3D rendering engine known for realistic lighting and materials. Mentioning it tells the AI to aim for that polished, professional 3D aesthetic.
  • Can I change the "GreenDay" patch to something else?
    Absolutely. You can replace it with any text or logo that's meaningful to you. Just keep the description format similar so the AI knows it's a patch on the beanie.
