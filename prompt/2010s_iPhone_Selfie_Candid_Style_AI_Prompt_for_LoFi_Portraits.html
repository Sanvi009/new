Prompt Explanation

This prompt is ingeniously crafted to generate a piece of digital nostalgia, specifically targeting the lo-fi, candid aesthetic of early 2010s smartphone photography. Its creative purpose is to create a personalized, almost dreamlike selfie that places the user in an intimate and slightly surreal moment with celebrities like Kirsten Stewart and Billie Eilish, all while wrapped in the warm, imperfect glow of a decade-old iPhone camera. The image is not meant to be a polished, professional portrait; rather, it seeks to capture the feeling of an accidental, imperfect snapshot from a night out or a casual hangout, complete with all the charming flaws that make such memories feel authentic. The prompt text guides the AI by constructing a very specific, yet intentionally loose, narrative. It sets the scene as a 2010s iPhone selfie of Kirsten Stewart in a lo-fi background, with the user (defined by a reference image for facial structure) perched on Billie Eilish's shoulder. This surreal element—being on someone's shoulder—immediately signals that the image is not bound by realism but by a dreamy, fantastical logic, perfect for a creative or artistic portrait. The instruction for the user to have a blank expression and for the photo to have no clear subject or particular composition is a direct attempt to mimic the spontaneity of a candid shot, where the photographer wasn't trying to frame the perfect picture. The core of the aesthetic is then defined by a series of technical imperfections: slightly blurry, flash photography, grainy texture, edited and soft focus. This combination of terms—blur, grain, flash, soft focus—is a powerful recipe for the AI to create an image that feels like it was taken with an older phone camera in low light, possibly with a filter applied later. The mention of optional accessories like eyeliner, jewelry, or face stickers adds a layer of personalized detail and further roots the image in the specific fashion and photo trends of that era, where such adornments were common in casual selfies. The lighting and composition are central to the prompt's success. The reliance on flash photography in what is implied to be a dim or lo-fi setting creates the characteristic harsh, direct light that flattens features and can cause red-eye or overexposed areas, which are embraced as part of the aesthetic. The soft focus and slight blur work together to create a dreamy, slightly out-of-focus look that is the antithesis of modern, razor-sharp smartphone photography. The 9:16 aspect ratio is not an afterthought; it is a crucial compositional element that mirrors the vertical orientation of a smartphone selfie, making the output immediately recognizable and suitable for social media platforms. The elements that must not be changed are the foundational aesthetic qualities: the 2010s iPhone selfie style, the lo-fi background, and the core imperfections of slight blur, grain, and flash photography. The surreal element of being on a celebrity's shoulder is also a key part of this specific prompt's unique character. However, the prompt is highly modular. The most obvious variation is swapping Kirsten Stewart and Billie Eilish for other celebrities or even fictional characters, creating a whole new personalized fantasy. The intensity of the blur can be adjusted from slightly blurry to more motion blur, making the image feel more chaotic and dynamic. Changing the light source from flash photography to natural daylight would transform the mood from a late-night party snapshot to a softer, more casual daytime selfie. Replacing the lo-fi background with an urban street background would shift the context from an intimate indoor setting to a trendy, city-style candid shot. For improved accuracy, users must provide a clear reference image of their own face, as the prompt relies on matching facial structure. When generating, it's important to not over-emphasize clarity or realism; the goal is imperfection. A common mistake is to try and correct the blur or grain, which would defeat the entire purpose. Another is to be too prescriptive about the composition; the prompt explicitly asks for no clear subject or particular composition, so trying to force a specific pose can make the image feel staged and inauthentic. Users might also find that some AI models struggle to consistently generate two specific celebrities in one frame. In such cases, emphasizing both names strongly in the prompt or using a model fine-tuned on celebrity faces can help. Ultimately, this prompt succeeds by understanding that the most emotionally resonant photos are often the imperfect ones, and it provides a perfect framework for the AI to generate a personalized, nostalgic, and artistically compelling image that feels like a cherished memory from a bygone digital era.

The Prompt 

A 2010s iPhone selfie of Kirsten Stewart with a lo-fi background, with me ( as same facial structure from the refernce image ) on Billie Eilish's shoulder, my expression blank, with no clear subject or particular composition—just an unintentional candid shot. Slightly blurry. Flash photography. Grainy texture. Edited and soft focus. Capturing an imperfect moment. Uses (eyeliner, jewelry, face stickers, etc.)

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt and replace any placeholders with your reference images or desired elements. Paste it into your AI image generator (MidJourney, DALL·E, or Stable Diffusion) and render in 9:16 aspect ratio to preserve the iPhone selfie composition. Emphasize features like slight blur, grain, flash, and soft focus to achieve the authentic lo-fi look. Add optional accessories like eyeliner, jewelry, or stickers to match your style.

Why This Prompt Works

9.16 aspect ratioThis prompt leverages AI’s ability to mimic spontaneous, imperfect photography from the 2010s. By including slight blur, grainy textures, soft focus, and unintentional composition, it produces images that feel authentic and nostalgic. Adding a real-world reference for your facial structure ensures personalized and recognizable results. The 9:16 aspect ratio mirrors modern phone selfies, making the output perfect for social media or digital art projects.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “Kirsten Stewart” to another celebrity or character → get a personalized candid selfie with anyone
  • Change “slightly blurry” to “more motion blur” → get a more chaotic, dynamic candid effect.
  • Change “flash photography” to “natural daylight” → get a softer, casual selfie vibe.
  • Change “lo-fi background” to “urban street background” → get a trendy, city-style candid shot.

  • Will the AI always include Billie Eilish and Kirsten Stewart?
