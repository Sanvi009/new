How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, utilize it for superhero fan art, horror crossovers, or movie posters in comic and film industries. Preserve "famous superhero with a [large “S” on the suit]," "decaying creature," "8K hyper-realistic style," and "Hollywood movie" lighting for realism. Do not change the background buildings or arch-enemy. Optionally vary decay level; for example, more rot could yield zombie visuals, resulting in faded enemies, while subtle creates eerie, focused outcomes.

Prompt

Create an image of a famous superhero with a \[large “S” on the suit] who looks like a decaying creature. Everything should be in 8K hyper-realistic style, it must look absolutely real. Match the background to the superhero, include typical buildings and his most famous arch-enemy, faded in the background. The focus should be on the superhero. Lighting and atmosphere should look like in a high-quality Hollywood movie.

Why this prompt works

This prompt is effective due to its unique twist on a famous superhero portraying them as a decaying creature which adds depth and intrigue the hyper-realistic style and typical buildings with a faded arch-enemy in the background create a narrative-rich scene the Hollywood-quality lighting and atmosphere ensure a professional and compelling image that can spark conversation and interest this concept is perfect for comic book adaptations movie posters or artistic explorations that reimagine familiar characters in new ways.
