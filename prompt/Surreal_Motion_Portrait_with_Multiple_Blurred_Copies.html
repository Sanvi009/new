Surreal Motion Portrait

AI Image Prompts 2025-09-20 Brainloom

Generated Images

The Prompt

Create a surreal vertical portrait of the person (use uploaded face). Keep the main face sharp in the center. Add blurred, semi-transparent copies of the face and upper body fading out on both sides to show motion or shifting identity.
Background should be soft light blue or grey. Dress the person in a dark, streetwear outfit and rounnd black glasses with comma hair style. Use dramatic lighting on the face.
Mood: cinematic, moody, and mysterious. 8k resolution.

How to Use

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into motion portraits, identity art, or surreal photography for personal and editorial. Preserve "surreal vertical portrait," "use uploaded face," "blurred, semi-transparent copies," and "8k resolution" for mysterious mood. Do not modify the sharp center or light blue background. Changing outfit to "suit" could yield professional visuals, leading to shifts, while streetwear keeps cinematic, moody outcomes.
