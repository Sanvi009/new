The Prompt 

Analyze my drink photo and transform it into a refreshing visual (uploaded drink photo)

dynamically falling into a plastic cup, with splashes of liquid from the drink, cinematic food photography, professional studio lighting, contrasting backdrop, advertising-style composition, ultra-detailed textures, hyper-realistic, 8K UHD resolution, sharp focus, toppings gracefully scattering, vibrant colors, mouth-watering presentation, motion frozen in time.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, integrate it into drink transformations, ad photography, or product dynamics for beverage brands. Preserve "analyze my drink photo," "dynamically falling into a plastic cup," "cinematic food photography," and "8K UHD resolution" for mouth-watering. Do not modify the splashes or sharp focus. Changing toppings to "fruits" could yield fresh visuals, leading to scatters, while original creates vibrant, frozen-motion outcomes.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by transforming a drink photo into a dynamic refreshing visual with splashing liquid and frozen motion the cinematic photography and professional lighting ensure a mouth-watering and high-quality result perfect for beverage advertising social media campaigns or menu displays that need to emphasize refreshment and appeal.

Prompt Variations

  • Drink Type: Assume “[uploaded drink photo]” is a bubble tea. Swap for “a fruit smoothie” for a different color and texture. Swap for “a glass of beer” for a different splash dynamic.
  • Container: Swap “plastic cup” → “a tall glass” for a more elegant splash. Swap “plastic cup” → “a ceramic mug” for a coffee-themed image.
  • Backdrop: Swap “contrasting backdrop” → “a matching colorful backdrop” for a vibrant, less contrasting look.
  • Toppings: Swap “toppings gracefully scattering” → “fruit slices gracefully scattering” for a smoothie. Swap “toppings” → “hops and barley grains” for beer.
