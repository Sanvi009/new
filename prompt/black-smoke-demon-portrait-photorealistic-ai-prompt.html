Prompt Explanation

This prompt is expertly engineered to generate a complex, high-concept piece of photorealistic dark character art. Its creative purpose is to merge contemporary streetwear fashion with dark fantasy elements, creating a striking and ominous portrait that feels both grounded and supernatural. The goal is to produce an image of a man who exudes a dominant, powerful presence, visually amplified by a massive, realistic black smoke demon looming behind him. This isn't just a portrait; it's a visual statement blending modern style with mythical horror in a cinematic, hyper-realistic style. The prompt_text is structured in a highly organized, almost modular way, which is a key factor in its effectiveness. By clearly labeling sections like '(Subject)', '(Outfit)', '(Pose)', '(Supernatural Element)', '(Environment)', '(Lighting)', and '(Tech Specs)', it provides the AI with a clear, digestible framework. This prevents the instructions from becoming a confusing block of text and ensures each critical component receives focused attention. The subject line establishes the need for a 'photorealistic portrait of a young man' with 'exact facial identity preserved,' and includes specific features like 'beard, sharp jawline' to guide the AI towards a particular masculine archetype, especially when combined with a celebrity lookalike suggestion. The outfit and pose work together to establish the character's attitude. 'All-black streetwear' with specific items like an 'oversized hoodie with minimal text' and 'loose black wide-leg pants' creates a modern, minimalist, and slightly edgy fashion base. The 'luxury black sunglasses' add a layer of cool detachment. The pose is described as 'dominant stance, shoulders squared, one hand relaxed near pocket, other hand slightly clenched, facing forward,' which perfectly communicates confidence, readiness, and a controlled sense of power. This precise physical language helps the AI construct a believable and attitude-filled figure. The supernatural element is the centerpiece and is described with great care to avoid common AI pitfalls. 'A massive black smoke demon standing directly behind him, glowing white eyes aligned above man's head' gives the creature scale, position, and a focal point. The critical instructions are 'thick realistic smoke wrapping around shoulders and arms, smoke texture ultra-realistic, no cartoon style.' This explicitly directs the AI to treat the demon not as a cartoon monster, but as a tangible, physical phenomenonâ€”dense, volumetric smoke with realistic light interaction. This is further reinforced by the extensive negative prompt, which explicitly bans 'cartoon, anime, illustration, painting, 3d render,' ensuring the output stays firmly in the photorealistic realm. The environment and lighting are chosen to support and enhance the main subject. A 'minimal indoor background, dark wall, empty space, grey concrete floor' provides a stark, non-distracting stage that emphasizes the isolation and power of the figure and his smoky companion. The 'dramatic low-key lighting' with 'soft front light on face' and 'deep shadows' is a classic technique for creating mood and drama. It sculpts the subject's face, keeping him visible and detailed while plunging the background into shadow, which perfectly complements the dark fantasy theme. The technical specs demanding '8k resolution,' 'hyper-realistic,' and 'highly detailed texture' push the AI to generate a final image with exceptional clarity and depth. In this prompt, the core concept and subject are fixed. The figure must be a young man with a preserved identity, and the supernatural element must be a 'massive black smoke demon' with 'glowing white eyes' positioned directly behind him. Changing these would create a fundamentally different image. The 'photorealistic' and 'no cartoon style' directives are also non-negotiable for achieving the intended aesthetic. However, there is significant room for creative variation. As suggested, the smoke demon could be replaced with another entity like a 'shadow creature or spirit,' altering the nature of the threat. The character's attire can be changed from streetwear to a 'suit or leather jacket,' which would shift his persona from urban cool to something more classic or rugged. The environment can be swapped for a 'rainy alley or abandoned building' to add a different atmospheric context. Even the smoke's color can be changed to 'blue or purple,' which would create a more ethereal or magical feel. To improve accuracy and visual quality, using a very high-quality reference image for the subject's face is crucial, as the AI needs clear data to preserve the 'exact facial identity.' Paying close attention to the negative prompt and adapting it for the specific AI tool being used can help eliminate artifacts like distorted hands or extra fingers. A common mistake is generating an image where the smoke demon looks like a flat, painted-on effect rather than volumetric, realistic smoke. Emphasizing keywords like 'volumetric lighting' and 'ultra-realistic smoke texture' in the main prompt, if the initial results are lacking, can help. Another potential issue is the demon's 'glowing white eyes' looking cartoonish. Specifying that they should have a 'subtle, menacing glow' can guide the AI towards a more realistic and sinister effect. This prompt is a masterclass in controlled complexity, using structured input and targeted negative prompts to guide an AI towards generating a sophisticated, genre-blending portrait that is both visually stunning and narratively compelling.

The Prompt 

(Subject) Photorealistic portrait of a young man, [insert celebrity lookalike or specific description if needed to aid AI], exact facial identity preserved, beard, sharp jawline. (Outfit) Wearing all-black streetwear, black oversized hoodie with minimal text, loose black wide-leg pants, dark sneakers, luxury black sunglasses.(Pose) Head raised straight, dominant stance, shoulders squared, one hand relaxed near pocket, other hand slightly clenched, facing forward. (Supernatural Element) A massive black smoke demon standing directly behind him, glowing white eyes aligned above man's head, thick realistic smoke wrapping around shoulders and arms, smoke texture ultra-realistic, no cartoon style. (Environment) Minimal indoor background, dark wall, empty space, grey concrete floor. (Lighting) Dramatic low-key lighting,soft front light on face, deep shadows, cinematic high contrast. (Tech Specs) 8k resolution, aspect ratio 3:4, shot on 35mm lens, hyper-realistic, highly detailed texture. Negative Prompt: cartoon, anime, illustration, painting, 3d render, distorted face, changing face, reshaping face, beauty filter, smile, bright background, sunlight, car, city, messy, blur, noise, watermark, text, bad hands, extra fingers.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Copy the entire prompt text. 2. Paste into your AI image generator (Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, DALL-E). 3. For a specific person, replace the bracketed text with their name or description. 4. Generate the image. 5. Adjust negative prompts if needed for your tool.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt uses specific photography terms (35mm lens, low-key lighting) that AI recognizes for realistic style. Separating elements like Subject, Outfit, and Lighting helps the AI process each detail. The negative prompt removes common AI art problems like cartoon styles and distorted faces.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace smoke demon with shadow creature or spirit
  • Change streetwear to suit or leather jacket
  • Swap indoor background for rainy alley or abandoned building
  • Try blue or purple smoke instead of black
  • Add glowing tattoos or supernatural eyes to the man

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What AI image generator works best with this prompt?
    This prompt works well with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. Adjust negative prompts for your specific generator.
  • How do I make the smoke look more realistic?
    Use keywords 'thick realistic smoke,' 'ultra-realistic texture,' and 'volumetric lighting' in your prompt. The negative prompt removes cartoon styles.
  • Can I use this prompt for a female character?
    Yes, change 'young man' to 'woman' and adjust clothing descriptions. The demon and lighting elements will work the same.
  • Why is my generated face distorted?
    Ensure 'exact facial identity preserved' is in the prompt. Use the negative prompt terms 'distorted face, changing face' to prevent this.
