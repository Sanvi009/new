Urban Street Motion Blur Portrait with Identity Preservation AI Prompt

AI Image Prompts 2026-04-22 Brainloom

This prompt creates an ultra-realistic cinematic street photograph where the user's exact facial identity stands in sharp focus at the center of a chaotic, fast-moving urban crowd. The subject maintains a calm, introspective expression with hands tucked casually into the pockets of loose blue jeans, wearing a slightly oversized black casual blazer over a white fitted tank top. A black belt with a visible metallic keychain detail, matte black over-ear headphones resting around the neck, and a crossbody bag complete the urban casual aesthetic, with a small rolling suitcase positioned nearby. The background is filled with pedestrians in motion, rendered with intentional slow-shutter motion blur streaks that contrast dramatically against the subject's crisp, static presence. Soft overcast or late afternoon light bathes the scene in muted, cinematic tones with cool teal shadows and natural warm skin highlights. The shallow depth of field at f/1.8 to f/2.2 ensures strong subject separation, while subtle film grain and natural skin texture maintain a raw, unpolished realism reminiscent of indie film stills and documentary street photography. The prompt strictly prohibits facial alteration, beautification filters, and artificial lighting to guarantee the result resembles a genuine candid photograph of the specific individual.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic cinematic photograph of a person (replace with my exact face, gender, facial structure, proportions, and skin tone — strict identity preservation). No beautification, no stylization that alters identity. Preserve natural asymmetries, pores, skin texture, eye shape, nose structure, lips, and all unique facial features. The result must look like a real candid photo of me, not an idealized version.
Escena y composición:
A busy urban street filled with pedestrians in motion, captured from a slightly elevated frontal angle (slight top-down perspective). The subject stands still in the center of the frame while the crowd moves rapidly around them, creating strong motion blur streaks in all directions. The subject is sharply in focus, isolated visually from the chaotic environment.
Pose y expresión:
Relaxed, neutral posture, standing upright, shoulders slightly forward. Hands casually inside front pockets of jeans. Facial expression calm, introspective, slightly serious, with a detached or observant gaze looking directly into the camera. Lips slightly parted (optional subtle detail: holding a small object like a toothpick or cigarette, depending on preference).
Vestuario y styling:by Lagz.
Black casual blazer (slightly oversized fit)
White or off-white fitted tank top underneath
Loose blue jeans (relaxed fit, slightly high waist)
Black belt with visible metallic ring/keychain detail hanging
Over-ear headphones resting around the neck (matte black)
Crossbody bag hanging on one side
Small rolling suitcase placed beside or slightly behind the subject
Minimal accessories, urban casual aesthetic
Entorno:
Crowded city street or pedestrian avenue. Background filled with people walking in different directions. No individual faces in focus — only motion-blurred silhouettes. Ground is pavement or tiled sidewalk. Urban, slightly gritty but realistic atmosphere.
Iluminación:
Soft natural light, overcast or late afternoon. Diffused lighting with no harsh shadows. Subtle cinematic color grading with muted tones, slightly cool-green/teal shadows and warm skin tones. Natural contrast, not overprocessed.
Cámara y técnica: by Lagz.
Lens: 35mm or 50mm
Aperture: f/1.8 – f/2.2 (shallow depth of field)
Shutter: slow shutter effect for motion blur in background (subject remains sharp)
Focus locked on subject's face
Slight film grain for realism
High dynamic range but natural
Efectos clave (MUY IMPORTANTE):
Motion blur ONLY on background pedestrians
Subject completely sharp (no blur on face or body)
Strong subject-background separation
Cinematic depth of field
Realistic skin texture (no smoothing)
No artificial glow or fantasy effects
Color grading / estilo visual:
Cinematic, moody, urban realism. Slight desaturation. Film-like tones. Inspired by street photography and indie film stills.
Negative prompt (para mejores resultados):
cartoon, anime, illustration, over-smooth skin, beauty filter, unrealistic face, distorted proportions, extra limbs, duplicate face, low quality, blurry subject, overexposed, oversharpen, artificial lighting, studio look

How to Use

To use this prompt effectively, begin by uploading a clear reference photo of your face to the AI image generator. Locate the text 'replace with my exact face' and substitute it with a brief, objective description of your specific facial features to reinforce the identity lock. The prompt is designed for a vertical 9:16 frame, making it ideal for social media stories or phone wallpapers. The key technical challenge is achieving the motion blur effect without blurring the subject. If the AI makes the subject blurry too, add emphasis to the line 'Subject completely sharp (no blur on face or body).' You can customize the environment by changing 'busy urban street' to 'train station platform' or 'airport terminal' for a different travel narrative. Adjust the outfit colors easily—swap the 'black blazer' for 'camel coat' or 'denim jacket' while keeping the 'motion blur' and 'shallow depth of field' instructions intact to maintain the cinematic look. If the background crowd looks too sparse or static, add 'rush hour foot traffic, dozens of blurred figures moving swiftly' to increase the density of motion.

FAQ

  • How do I ensure the background crowd blurs but my face and body stay perfectly sharp?
    This is the most technically specific aspect of this prompt and relies on the AI correctly interpreting 'slow shutter effect' combined with 'subject completely sharp.' If the AI mistakenly blurs the subject, you need to reinforce the contrast in the prompt. Add an explicit instruction like: 'The subject is frozen in time by a fast flash sync or high-speed capture, while the long exposure captures the movement of the crowd.' Additionally, make sure the subject's pose is described as 'standing completely still' and 'static.' Any hint of movement in the subject's description ('leaning slightly' or 'turning head') can cause the AI to apply the motion blur logic to the subject as well. If problems persist, try moving the 'Motion blur ONLY on background pedestrians' line to the very beginning of the prompt to increase its weight in the generation process.
  • Can I change the rolling suitcase to a different prop like a backpack or a guitar case?
    Yes, the prop is a flexible part of the narrative. The rolling suitcase helps reinforce the 'traveler' or 'commuter' story, which justifies why the subject is standing still amidst a rushing crowd. You can replace 'small rolling suitcase' with any similar stationary object that grounds the scene. Good alternatives include: 'a large worn leather duffel bag at their feet,' 'a bicycle held by the handlebars beside them,' or 'a camera tripod set up next to them.' If you remove the prop entirely, the subject might appear too disconnected from the environment. The prop provides a visual anchor that explains why this person is pausing while everyone else moves. Just ensure the new prop matches the 'urban casual aesthetic' and doesn't introduce distracting colors that pull focus from the face.
  • Why does the prompt include a negative prompt section at the end?
    The negative prompt is crucial for overriding the AI model's default training biases. Most image generation models are fine-tuned on datasets of highly polished, often artificially lit or illustrated images. Without the negative prompt, the AI might default to rendering the scene with 'studio lighting,' 'perfect skin smoothing,' or 'cartoonish proportions.' By explicitly forbidding terms like 'beauty filter,' 'over-smooth skin,' and 'artificial lighting,' you are forcing the rendering engine to stay within the constraints of documentary realism and candid street photography. This is especially important for identity preservation prompts because models tend to 'idealize' faces, erasing the very unique features you are trying to keep. If the result still looks too clean, you can add more terms to the negative prompt like 'high fashion,' 'magazine retouching,' or 'perfect symmetry' to push it further toward a raw, unposed look.
