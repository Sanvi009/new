How to Create a Ultra-Realistic Cinematic Streetwear Portrait Prompt

AI Image Prompts 2026-02-22 Brainloom

Generate a high-end fashion portrait with golden hour lighting, luxury streetwear styling, and hyper-realistic skin details in a modern city setting.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic cinematic daytime portrait, of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image  low-angle hero shot, confident intense gaze directly at camera, sitting on urban bench with hands forward resting on knees. Wearing oversized dark pinstripe blazer unbuttoned, layered thick silver chains and pendants, multiple silver rings, black fitted tee underneath, streetwear luxury vibe. Modern city street background with skyscrapers, blurred pedestrians and cars, golden hour warm sunlight from low side, strong dramatic shadows, subtle lens flare, teal-blue sky accents, film grain texture, shallow depth of field, 35mm lens, high contrast fashion editorial, hyper-realistic skin details, 8K professional photography, ar-9:16

How to Use

Start by preparing your reference image. This prompt relies heavily on maintaining your exact facial and physical identity, so you need a high-quality uploaded photo of yourself. A front-facing shot with good lighting works best because it gives the AI clear data about your features. Place this image URL at the beginning of your prompt in tools like Midjourney that support image prompting. The prompt itself is dense with specific instructions, so copy it exactly as written. It's structured to guide the AI through multiple layers: the subject's pose and expression, the outfit details, the background environment, the lighting conditions, and finally the camera and quality settings. Each part tells the AI what to prioritize. For the low-angle hero shot, the AI needs to understand that the camera is looking up at you. This creates a sense of power and importance. Your pose is also clearly described: sitting on an urban bench with hands forward on your knees. This isn't a casual lean—it's a deliberate, confident posture. The intense gaze directly at camera adds to the confrontational, editorial feel. The outfit description is highly specific. The oversized dark pinstripe blazer, unbuttoned, layered with thick silver chains and pendants, multiple silver rings, and a black fitted tee underneath all work together to create that streetwear luxury vibe. These details matter because they define the style. If you left out the chains or rings, the AI might generate a simpler look. The background and lighting section sets the scene: modern city with skyscrapers, blurred pedestrians and cars for motion, golden hour sunlight from a low side creating strong shadows, and teal-blue sky accents for color contrast. The subtle lens flare and film grain texture add authenticity, while shallow depth of field keeps focus on you. Ending with 8K professional photography and the 9:16 aspect ratio ensures maximum quality and the right format. Run multiple generations and pick the one that best captures your likeness and the mood. You may need to adjust the seed or regenerate if the AI struggles with the outfit layers or lighting complexity.

FAQ

  • What makes this prompt 'ultra-realistic' compared to standard portrait prompts?
    The combination of hyper-realistic skin details, 8K resolution, specific lens choice, and detailed lighting instructions pushes the AI toward photographic accuracy rather than artistic interpretation.
  • How important is the uploaded image for this prompt?
    Essential. Without your face reference, the AI will generate a random person. The prompt explicitly says 'exact same facial and physical identity,' so the AI needs that visual anchor.
  • Can I use this prompt for different outfits or locations?
    Absolutely. The structure works as a template. Replace the outfit description and background details while keeping the pose, lighting, and technical settings to get a completely different look.
