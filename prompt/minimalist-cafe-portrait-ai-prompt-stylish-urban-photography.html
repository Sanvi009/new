Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create a warm, inviting, and deeply textured portrait that feels like a candid moment captured in a stylish urban cafe. Its creative purpose is to generate an image that is both a fashion statement and a slice of life, focusing on the interplay of comfortable, high-quality clothing, a relaxed atmosphere, and the authentic details of a real person. Unlike the high-glamour, studio-controlled looks of other prompts, this one aims for a sophisticated yet approachable aesthetic, evoking the feeling of a lazy afternoon spent people-watching in a trendy neighborhood spot. The final image should feel like a still from an indie film, rich with mood and character. The prompt_text is a masterful blend of descriptive storytelling and technical precision, all working together to build a cohesive scene. It begins with the non-negotiable foundation: 'exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image.' As with all these prompts, preserving the subject's unique identity is paramount. But here, the realism is taken a step further with the instruction to 'Focus on facial details, skin texture, as well as the textures of the clothing.' This dual focus on the person and their clothes is key. It tells the AI that the materiality of the scene—the wool of the sweater, the crispness of the shirt collar, the leather of the loafers—is just as important as the person's face in creating a believable, high-quality image. The outfit description is extraordinarily detailed and tactile. It's not just a 'red sweater'; it's a 'textured dark red woolen sweater.' The AI is prompted to render the very feel of the yarn. It's worn over 'a crisp white collared shirt with the sleeves rolled up,' a small detail that adds a layer of casual, relaxed style. The 'dark grey high-waisted trousers with a thin stripe' are a specific, fashion-forward choice. The 'thin black leather belt,' 'polished black leather horsebit loafers,' and 'faded dark blue baseball cap with a white cursive embroidered logo, tilted slightly forward' are all meticulously described, painting a complete picture of a person with a curated, intentional personal style. The inclusion of the cap being 'tilted slightly forward' is a wonderful touch that adds a hint of personality and attitude. The pose and setting are integrated seamlessly: 'sitting on a chair in a minimalist cafe' and 'holds a glass of iced latte with a straw in my hands.' This action adds life and narrative to the portrait. The subject is not just posing; they are engaged in a quiet, everyday activity, which makes the image feel more authentic and less staged. The final, and perhaps most crucial, layer is the lighting and atmosphere description: 'Atmospheric soft indoor lighting with warm highlights, cinematic aesthetic, grainy film texture, sophisticated urban atmosphere. Slight blur and graininess.' This is where the prompt truly shines. It's not asking for clinical, perfect lighting. It's asking for atmosphere. 'Soft indoor lighting with warm highlights' suggests the golden, flattering light of a late afternoon sun streaming through a cafe window, or the warm glow of interior lamps. The 'cinematic aesthetic' and 'grainy film texture' are deliberate choices to move the image away from a sterile digital look and towards something more artistic and timeless. The 'slight blur and graininess' are not mistakes to be avoided but desired qualities that add to the mood and realism, mimicking the characteristics of a beautiful film photograph. The lighting logic is therefore all about creating mood and warmth. It must be soft and directional enough to create gentle shadows that define the features and the textures of the clothing, but without the high contrast of a studio setup. The warm highlights are essential for creating a cozy, inviting feel. The composition is a 'full-length shot' in a 9:16 aspect ratio, which is perfect for a vertical portrait that captures the entire outfit and the subject's relaxed posture in the chair. The realism level is anchored in 'realistic,' 'detailed textures,' and the deliberate addition of film grain and blur. The elements that must NOT be changed are the subject's facial identity and the core concept of a relaxed, textured portrait in a cafe. However, the promptVariations show that the setting and mood are highly adaptable. The cafe can be swapped for a bookstore for a more intellectual vibe, or a rainy window view for added melancholy. The lighting can be changed from soft indoor to cozy fireplace or bright summer sun, completely altering the emotional tone. The outfit is also a variable; it could be changed to a casual hoodie and jeans for an even more relaxed look, or to something more formal to shift the style. To improve accuracy and visual quality, the reference photo should ideally show the subject in a similar relaxed pose and with good, even lighting on the face. For the clothing textures, being specific in the prompt, as it already is, is key. A common mistake is to forget the 'slight blur and graininess' instruction, resulting in an image that is too sharp and digital, losing the intended cinematic, film-like quality. Another mistake is to use a reference photo where the subject's hairstyle or glasses are different from the described ones, which can confuse the AI. Users might also overlook the importance of the 'minimalist' cafe description; if the background becomes too cluttered, it can distract from the subject and the sophisticated urban atmosphere. This prompt is a beautiful example of how to use language to paint a complete sensory picture for an AI, guiding it to create not just a portrait, but a feeling—a moment in time captured with style, warmth, and an appreciation for the beauty of everyday textures and light.

The Prompt 

Full-length shot of me with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image  sitting on a chair in a minimalist cafe. i wearing a textured dark red woolen sweater over a crisp white collared shirt with the sleeves rolled up. i has dark grey high-waisted trousers with a thin stripe, belted with a thin black leather belt. On my feet are polished black leather horsebit loafers. On my head is a faded dark blue baseball cap with a white cursive embroidered logo, tilted slightly forward. Strictly maintain the look from the attached first photo, without distortions, Focus on facial details, skin texture, as well as the textures of the clothing. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16
i holds a glass of iced latte with a straw in my hands. Atmospheric soft indoor lighting with warm highlights, cinematic aesthetic, grainy film texture, sophisticated urban atmosphere. i wearing black glasses. Slight blur and graininess.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text exactly. 3. The AI will create a matching portrait in a cafe setting. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. For best results, use high-quality reference photos.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt includes specific clothing details and lighting instructions that AI models recognize. Terms like 'textured dark red woolen sweater' and 'soft indoor lighting with warm highlights' provide clear visual guidance. The 9:16 aspect ratio ensures proper composition for portraits.

Prompt Variations

  • Change setting to bookstore with wooden shelves and warm lamps
  • Try rainy window cafe view with water droplets on glass
  • Create evening version with cozy fireplace lighting
  • Make it summer cafe with outdoor seating and sunlight
  • Change outfit to casual hoodie and jeans with sneakers

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How accurate is the facial likeness from my reference photo?
    Very accurate with modern AI tools. DALL-E 3 and Midjourney v6 handle facial matching well when you upload reference images.
  • What if I want to change the clothing but keep my face?
    Replace the clothing descriptions but keep the 'exact same facial appearance' part of the prompt.
  • Why 9:16 aspect ratio?
    This vertical format works best for portraits and mobile viewing, similar to smartphone photos.
