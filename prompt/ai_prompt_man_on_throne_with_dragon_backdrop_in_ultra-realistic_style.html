Create an ultra-realistic 8K AI image of an Asian man sitting on a golden throne. He wears a sharp black suit with a red tie, holding a lit cigar with smoke rising. Around him stand six women in black dresses embroidered with red dragons, posing with confidence. The scene takes place in a dark Japanese-style room, lit with golden lights and a giant dragon painting on the wall. This AI prompt captures power, authority, and cinematic drama.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is crafted to produce an image of supreme authority and dark opulence, blending classical symbolism with a modern, cinematic aesthetic. Its creative purpose is to place the subject in a scene that communicates raw power, danger, and majesty, reminiscent of a high-budget film or a video game's final antagonist. It's designed for users who want to project an image of ultimate control, luxury, and intimidating presence, making it ideal for conceptual art, character design, or striking personal statements. The prompt_text is a dense, highly detailed set of instructions that builds the world from the ground up. It begins by setting the technical benchmark with ultra-realistic photos of 8K quality, immediately signaling the need for maximum detail and fidelity. The critical instruction to keep the original face, hair, shape, and expression exactly like the reference ensures the power fantasy is personally anchored. The subject's posture and attire are meticulously described: seated on a gilded classical throne, wearing an elegant black suit with a blood red tie. The red tie is a deliberate splash of color and symbolism, evoking danger, passion, and power. The pose, with left foot resting on the right thigh and a lit cigar with thick smoke rising, adds a layer of relaxed, almost arrogant confidence that underscores his dominion. The prompt then populates the scene with six beautiful women in tight black dresses embroidered with red dragons, posed seductively around him. This element introduces a dynamic of desire, loyalty, and further reinforces his status. The red dragons on their dresses echo the larger theme and create a visual motif. The backdrop is equally important, described as a modern Japanese-style room that is dark, with dim gold pendant lights and black wood ornaments. This setting provides a cultural and aesthetic frame that is both luxurious and ominous. The centerpiece is the large painting of a golden dragon behind the throne, a universal symbol of power, wisdom, and formidable force. The final, crucial instruction is for high contrast dramatic light, which emphasizes the atmosphere of a dark world that is majestic, dangerous, and full of authority, unifying all elements under a single, powerful mood. The non-negotiable elements are the throne, the suit and red tie, the cigar, and the high-contrast dramatic lighting. The throne establishes his royalty, the suit and tie his modern, refined power, and the cigar his casual command. The lighting is the glue that creates the dark, majestic, and dangerous atmosphere. Removing any of these would fundamentally alter the power dynamic the prompt seeks to create. The subject's face, as per the ultra-realistic requirement, must also remain unchanged from the reference to maintain the personal connection to the fantasy. Despite its specificity, the prompt offers clear avenues for modification. The red tie can be changed to a white tie for a more classic, mafia-style look, shifting the symbolism from blood to a more corporate or ceremonial power. The six women can be replaced with two bodyguards in suits, transforming the scene from one of seductive allure to one of stark, protective power. The Japanese room can be swapped for a luxury casino interior, giving the image a modern, high-stakes gambling twist. Adding glowing red neon lights can push the aesthetic into cyberpunk territory, blending the classic power symbols with a futuristic, digital edge. To achieve the best results, users must provide a high-quality reference photo. When generating, ensuring the AI understands high contrast is vital; if the image comes out too flat, one can add terms like deep shadows and bright, focused highlights. A common mistake is to allow the background to overpower the subject. The prompt specifies that the women are around him and the dragon is behind him, ensuring the subject remains the focal point. Another mistake is neglecting the expression; the prompt calls for a face that is cold and full of power. The user's reference photo or additional keywords like stoic or commanding are essential to convey this. By carefully balancing these core elements and chosen variations, one can create a stunningly realistic and powerfully evocative portrait of authority.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic photos of 8K quality, 9:16 ratio. Using [picture reference], keep the original face, hair, shape, and expression exactly like the reference. An Asian man sat on a gilded classical style throne, wearing an elegant black suit with a black shirt and a blood red tie. His face was cold and full of power. The left foot rests on the right thigh, the right hand holds a large cigar that is lit with thick smoke rising. Six beautiful women dressed in tight black dresses embroidered with red dragons with open cleavage, posed seductively around her. The backdrop of a modern Japanese-style room is dark with dim gold pendant lights, black wood ornaments, and a large painting of a golden dragon behind it. High contrast dramatic light, emphasizing the atmosphere of a dark world that is majestic, dangerous, and full of authority.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload your reference photo so the AI keeps the original face and expression. Keep the throne, suit, cigar, and red tie since these give the image its powerful look. You can change details like the background, number of women, or clothing designs to make it fit your own style, but keep the lighting dramatic for the best result.

Why This Prompt Works

This setup mixes luxury, power, and dark atmosphere in one frame. The throne and dragon painting add authority, while the women and lighting bring energy and contrast. It looks like a scene from a high-budget movie, making it perfect for anyone who wants cinematic, stylish AI portraits.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the red tie → “white tie” for a more classic mafia-style look.
  • Replace the six women → “two bodyguards in suits” for a stronger power image.
  • Switch the Japanese room → “luxury casino interior” for a modern twist.
  • Add glowing red neon lights for a cyberpunk-style version.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this prompt without a reference photo?
    Yes, but if you don’t upload a reference, the AI will generate a random face instead of keeping the one you want.
  • Can I make the scene less dark and more stylish?
    Of course — just adjust the lighting to be brighter or replace the backdrop with a modern luxury setting.
