The Prompt 

A photorealistic 8K cinematic portrait of me with exact facial identity preserved, with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image. i stand facing forward in a dominant, confident pose, head raised, shoulders squared, one hand relaxed near the pocket and the other slightly clenched.
He is dressed in all-red luxury streetwear: an oversized red hoodie with minimal text, loose red wide-leg pants, dark sneakers, and luxury black sunglasses.
Behind him stands a massive red smoke demon, formed from ultra-realistic volumetric smoke, with glowing white eyes aligned above his head. Thick, realistic smoke wraps around my shoulders and arms, maintaining a dark, cinematic, non-cartoon look.
The setting is a minimal indoor environment with a dark wall, empty space, and grey concrete floor.
Lighting is dramatic low-key, with soft frontal light on the face, deep shadows, and high-contrast cinematic mood.
Shot on a 35mm lens, aspect ratio 9:16 , hyper-realistic textures and details An elegant glossy gold handwritten signature “NK” appears in the bottom corner, premium and luxury-styled

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the full prompt into your AI image generator. First, upload a clear reference photo of your face. The AI will use that photo to match your facial features in this dramatic portrait with the smoke demon effect.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt provides extremely specific visual instructions: from the subject's pose and clothing ('dominant pose', 'all-red luxury streetwear') to the supernatural element ('volumetric smoke', 'glowing white eyes'). Professional terms like 'low-key lighting', '35mm lens', and 'hyper-realistic textures' guide the AI to produce a cohesive, high-quality cinematic image.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'red smoke demon' to 'blue energy aura' or 'shadow creature'.
  • Replace 'red streetwear' with 'black tailored suit' or 'white armor'.
  • Use 'smiling casually' instead of 'dominant, confident pose' for a different expression.
  • Try 'abandoned warehouse' or 'city rooftop' instead of 'minimal indoor environment'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the AI to copy my face exactly?
    You must upload a high-quality, well-lit photo of your face as an image reference when running the prompt. The AI uses this to replicate your facial identity.
  • What does 'volumetric smoke' mean in the prompt?
    It tells the AI to create smoke that looks three-dimensional, dense, and realistic—like real fog or smoke in a photo—rather than a flat, cartoonish drawing.
  • Can I remove or change the 'NK' signature?
    Yes, simply delete the last sentence about the signature from the prompt text before generating the image.
