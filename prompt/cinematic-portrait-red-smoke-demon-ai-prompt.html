Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create a striking, hyper-realistic portrait that blends contemporary street fashion with a dark, fantastical element. Its creative purpose is to empower the user by depicting them as a powerful figure commanding an otherworldly presence, merging the aesthetics of a luxury fashion editorial with a high-concept fantasy character poster. The prompt_text operates on multiple levels to achieve this. The foundation is the personalization directive: A photorealistic 8K cinematic portrait of me with exact facial identity preserved. This is non-negotiable for the prompt's core function, ensuring the generated image is a representation of the user, not a generic model. The description of the subject's pose and attire—dominant, confident, with head raised and shoulders squared in all-red luxury streetwear—immediately establishes a tone of power and contemporary style. The specific clothing items, like the oversized red hoodie and luxury black sunglasses, provide the AI with clear visual anchors for a modern, fashion-forward look. The most creative element is the massive red smoke demon behind the subject. The prompt is meticulous in its description: formed from ultra-realistic volumetric smoke with glowing white eyes. This instructs the AI to render the demon not as a solid, cartoonish monster, but as a dense, three-dimensional, and ethereal entity, made of the same substance as real smoke. The instruction that the smoke wraps around the subject's shoulders and arms physically connects the person to the fantastical element, making them appear to be generating or controlling it. The command for a non-cartoon look is critical to maintaining the photorealistic integrity of the entire scene. The lighting is described as dramatic low-key, with soft frontal light on the face and deep shadows. This is a crucial technical detail. Low-key lighting is characterized by high contrast and a dark palette, which perfectly suits the dark, cinematic mood. The soft frontal light on the face ensures the subject's features are clearly visible and well-defined, preventing them from being lost in the shadows. The setting is kept intentionally minimal—a dark wall, empty space, and grey concrete floor—to prevent distraction and keep the focus entirely on the subject and the demon. The 35mm lens specification suggests a wide-angle perspective, which can add a sense of scale and dynamism to the scene. Elements that must not be changed are the core personalization (the face) and the fundamental concept of the smoke demon as a volumetric, non-cartoon entity. Altering this would change the image from a unique fantasy portrait to something else entirely. The specifics of the color and style, however, are highly modifiable. Changing the red smoke demon to a blue energy aura or a shadow creature completely transforms the mood and the implied nature of the power. Replacing the red streetwear with a black tailored suit shifts the aesthetic from streetwear luxury to classic, sleek formality. Modifying the pose from dominant to casually smiling changes the character's attitude from commanding to approachable. Practical tips for improving accuracy include using a high-quality, front-facing reference photo with consistent lighting to help the AI better understand and replicate the user's facial structure. In Midjourney, using the --cref (character reference) parameter along with the image upload can significantly improve facial consistency. A common mistake is to be imprecise about the nature of the fantastical element; simply saying smoke demon might yield a cartoonish result. The inclusion of ultra-realistic volumetric smoke is what guides the AI toward a photorealistic interpretation. Another potential pitfall is the signature; if not desired, it must be explicitly removed from the prompt to prevent the AI from hallucinating text. By carefully balancing personalization, fashion, and fantasy with precise technical and atmospheric instructions, this prompt creates a powerful template for generating a truly unique and cinematic piece of art.

The Prompt 

A photorealistic 8K cinematic portrait of me with exact facial identity preserved, with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image. i stand facing forward in a dominant, confident pose, head raised, shoulders squared, one hand relaxed near the pocket and the other slightly clenched.
He is dressed in all-red luxury streetwear: an oversized red hoodie with minimal text, loose red wide-leg pants, dark sneakers, and luxury black sunglasses.
Behind him stands a massive red smoke demon, formed from ultra-realistic volumetric smoke, with glowing white eyes aligned above his head. Thick, realistic smoke wraps around my shoulders and arms, maintaining a dark, cinematic, non-cartoon look.
The setting is a minimal indoor environment with a dark wall, empty space, and grey concrete floor.
Lighting is dramatic low-key, with soft frontal light on the face, deep shadows, and high-contrast cinematic mood.
Shot on a 35mm lens, aspect ratio 9:16 , hyper-realistic textures and details An elegant glossy gold handwritten signature “NK” appears in the bottom corner, premium and luxury-styled

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the full prompt into your AI image generator. First, upload a clear reference photo of your face. The AI will use that photo to match your facial features in this dramatic portrait with the smoke demon effect.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt provides extremely specific visual instructions: from the subject's pose and clothing ('dominant pose', 'all-red luxury streetwear') to the supernatural element ('volumetric smoke', 'glowing white eyes'). Professional terms like 'low-key lighting', '35mm lens', and 'hyper-realistic textures' guide the AI to produce a cohesive, high-quality cinematic image.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'red smoke demon' to 'blue energy aura' or 'shadow creature'.
  • Replace 'red streetwear' with 'black tailored suit' or 'white armor'.
  • Use 'smiling casually' instead of 'dominant, confident pose' for a different expression.
  • Try 'abandoned warehouse' or 'city rooftop' instead of 'minimal indoor environment'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the AI to copy my face exactly?
    You must upload a high-quality, well-lit photo of your face as an image reference when running the prompt. The AI uses this to replicate your facial identity.
  • What does 'volumetric smoke' mean in the prompt?
    It tells the AI to create smoke that looks three-dimensional, dense, and realistic—like real fog or smoke in a photo—rather than a flat, cartoonish drawing.
  • Can I remove or change the 'NK' signature?
    Yes, simply delete the last sentence about the signature from the prompt text before generating the image.
