FAQ

  • What is the 85mm lens effect?
    85mm is a portrait photography favorite. It creates a flattering perspective with minimal distortion, beautiful background compression, and allows for a shallow depth of field that keeps the subject sharp while softly blurring the background.
  • How do I get the bronze satin jacket to reflect light?
    The prompt includes 'satin bomber jacket in a rich bronze/copper color that reflects the studio lights.' For best results, add keywords like 'glossy, metallic sheen, specular highlights' to emphasize the reflective quality.
  • What makes teal and bronze complementary?
    Teal is opposite orange on the color wheel. Bronze/copper is in the orange family, so teal creates strong visual contrast. This color pairing is popular in cinematic and portrait photography for its striking, 'pop' effect.
