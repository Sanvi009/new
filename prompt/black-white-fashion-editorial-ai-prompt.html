Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a striking, high-fashion editorial image that is both dynamic and graphically powerful. Its creative purpose is to capture a subject in a bold, unconventional pose against a minimalist backdrop, using the timeless elegance of black and white photography to emphasize form, texture, and composition. The goal is to produce an image that looks like it belongs in a high-end fashion magazine spread, where the model's attitude and the clothing's silhouette are the primary focus, rather than a traditional, posed portrait. It's about creating art through stark contrast and dynamic human geometry. The prompt_text guides the AI by focusing intensely on three key areas: the preservation of identity within a stylized format, a precise and challenging physical pose, and the specific technical and aesthetic qualities of the final image. It begins with the crucial instruction, 'Editorial black-and-white fashion shot of me with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image.' This sets the expectation that the output must be a stylized fashion image, not a literal photograph, while still maintaining the user's recognizable likeness. The repetition of 'keeping his exact facial features identical' and the note that 'facial structure remains recognizable' even with sunglasses, underscores the importance of identity preservation. This is a complex request for an AI, as it must balance a highly stylized, monochrome aesthetic with the accurate rendering of a specific individual's features. The description of the pose is the most critical and challenging element. 'Crouching low in a deep squat' is a very specific and dynamic position that immediately creates visual interest and a sense of edgy, unconventional fashion. The prompt then adds more layers of complexity: 'One hand reaches forward toward the camera, palm open, while the other hand touches the floor for balance.' This detailed breakdown of limb placement gives the AI a clear blueprint to construct a physically plausible and aesthetically pleasing pose. The 'wide-angle perspective' is perfectly chosen for this pose, as it will exaggerate the reach of the forward hand and the depth of the squat, creating a dramatic, almost surreal sense of space and drawing the viewer directly into the composition. The clothing, environment, and technical specs are all chosen to support this central, dramatic pose. The 'black long-sleeve top, black cropped trousers, and patterned slip-on shoes' provide a sleek, monochromatic base that won't distract from the pose, while the 'patterned slip-on shoes' add a subtle textural detail. The 'white studio backdrop' is essential for the editorial look; it creates a clean, infinite space that isolates the subject and makes the wide-angle perspective even more striking. The demand for 'ultra-cinematic quality, 16k resolution' pushes the AI to render the image with exceptional sharpness, detail, and a polished, high-end finish that is characteristic of professional fashion photography. In this prompt, the core pose and composition are fixed. The subject must be in a 'deep squat' with the specified hand and arm positions, and the perspective must be 'wide-angle.' Changing the pose would fundamentally alter the image's dynamic energy. The 'white studio backdrop' and 'black and white' color scheme are also central to the minimalist, high-fashion aesthetic. Swapping these would create a completely different mood. However, several elements are designed for variation. The 'black and white' can be changed to 'high fashion color pop' for a vibrant, modern look. The studio backdrop can be swapped for an 'urban concrete environment' to add a grittier, street-style context. The pose itself can be replaced with 'jumping' or 'sitting on a stool,' which would change the dynamic but keep the editorial feel. The lighting, currently implied by the clean studio setup, can be explicitly changed to 'rim lighting' or 'hard dramatic shadows' to sculpt the subject differently. And of course, removing the 'sunglasses' would allow for a more direct connection through the subject's eyes. For improved accuracy, using a reference photo that clearly shows the user's body type and facial structure is vital, as the AI will need to map these features onto a complex, crouching pose. It's also helpful to generate multiple iterations, as the AI's first attempt at the hand reaching towards the camera might result in distorted proportions. A common mistake is the AI failing to render the hands correctly, a frequent issue with complex poses. The prompt's detailed description of the hand positions helps, but being prepared to regenerate or use inpainting tools to correct hand anatomy is a practical tip. Another potential issue is the 'facial structure remains recognizable' aspect getting lost in the deep shadows of the black and white conversion. Ensuring the prompt includes a phrase like 'face well-lit despite sunglasses' can help the AI balance the dramatic lighting with the need for recognizable features. This prompt is a brilliant example of using a bold, physical concept as the foundation for a fashion image, leveraging precise pose instructions and a clean aesthetic to create a portrait that is both personally identifiable and artistically compelling.

The Prompt 

Editorial black-and-white fashion shot of me with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image, keeping his exact facial features identical i crouching low in a deep squat wearing a black long-sleeve top, black cropped trousers, and patterned slip-on shoes. i wearing dark sunglasses but his facial structure remains recognizable One hand reaches forward toward the camera, palm open, while the other hand touches the floor for balance. Wide-angle perspective, white studio backdrop ultra-cinematic quality, 16k resolution. 
do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt text into your AI image generator. If the tool supports it, upload a clear reference photo of yourself when prompted. The AI will use your facial features to create a black and white editorial fashion shot in the described dynamic pose and style.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by using clear fashion photography keywords like 'editorial black-and-white fashion shot' and 'white studio backdrop' that AI models are trained on. Specifying a precise pose ('deep squat', 'hand reaches forward'), clothing items, and perspective ('wide-angle') gives the AI concrete visual elements to assemble into a coherent, stylized image.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'black and white' to 'high fashion color pop' for a vibrant look.
  • Swap 'white studio backdrop' for 'urban concrete environment' or 'industrial warehouse'.
  • Replace the crouching pose with 'jumping' or 'sitting on a stool'.
  • Try 'rim lighting' or 'hard dramatic shadows' for different lighting styles.
  • Remove 'sunglasses' for a clear view of the eyes and expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my face be recognizable even with sunglasses?
    The prompt specifically says 'facial structure remains recognizable', which guides the AI to keep your bone structure, jawline, and overall likeness accurate, even with dark sunglasses covering the eyes.
  • What does 'editorial' mean in this prompt?
    In photography, 'editorial' refers to images created to illustrate a story or concept, often found in fashion magazines. It typically implies a stylized, artistic, and sometimes dramatic look, as opposed to a simple portrait or catalog shot.
  • Do I need a special AI model for black and white images?
    No. Major generators like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion understand 'black-and-white' as a style directive. The prompt will naturally produce a monochrome image. You can also use 'monochrome' as a keyword.
