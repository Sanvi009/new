The Prompt 

Editorial black-and-white fashion shot of me with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image, keeping his exact facial features identical i crouching low in a deep squat wearing a black long-sleeve top, black cropped trousers, and patterned slip-on shoes. i wearing dark sunglasses but his facial structure remains recognizable One hand reaches forward toward the camera, palm open, while the other hand touches the floor for balance. Wide-angle perspective, white studio backdrop ultra-cinematic quality, 16k resolution. 
do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt text into your AI image generator. If the tool supports it, upload a clear reference photo of yourself when prompted. The AI will use your facial features to create a black and white editorial fashion shot in the described dynamic pose and style.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by using clear fashion photography keywords like 'editorial black-and-white fashion shot' and 'white studio backdrop' that AI models are trained on. Specifying a precise pose ('deep squat', 'hand reaches forward'), clothing items, and perspective ('wide-angle') gives the AI concrete visual elements to assemble into a coherent, stylized image.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'black and white' to 'high fashion color pop' for a vibrant look.
  • Swap 'white studio backdrop' for 'urban concrete environment' or 'industrial warehouse'.
  • Replace the crouching pose with 'jumping' or 'sitting on a stool'.
  • Try 'rim lighting' or 'hard dramatic shadows' for different lighting styles.
  • Remove 'sunglasses' for a clear view of the eyes and expression.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will my face be recognizable even with sunglasses?
    The prompt specifically says 'facial structure remains recognizable', which guides the AI to keep your bone structure, jawline, and overall likeness accurate, even with dark sunglasses covering the eyes.
  • What does 'editorial' mean in this prompt?
    In photography, 'editorial' refers to images created to illustrate a story or concept, often found in fashion magazines. It typically implies a stylized, artistic, and sometimes dramatic look, as opposed to a simple portrait or catalog shot.
  • Do I need a special AI model for black and white images?
    No. Major generators like Midjourney, DALL-E, and Stable Diffusion understand 'black-and-white' as a style directive. The prompt will naturally produce a monochrome image. You can also use 'monochrome' as a keyword.
