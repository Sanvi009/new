"Hyper-realistic portrait of a urban man (photo uploaded) wearing a green beanie, open army-green jacket, white t-shirt, brown cargo pants, and green slip-on shoes. How to Use This Prompt

Upload your photo reference to make the man look accurate. Keep the outfit and rifle details for consistency, since they define the futuristic style. You can experiment with the graffiti colors, light effects, or mural designs to change the mood while keeping the neon glow and comic-style background for that high-energy look.

Why This Prompt Works

It mixes street culture with sci-fi. The outfit feels real and urban, while the holographic rifle and neon lighting add a futuristic twist. The graffiti murals and comic-style floor make the scene vibrant, energetic, and eye-catching. This kind of mix — realistic people in fantasy environments — creates images that feel unique and memorable.

Prompt Variations

  • Change “neon blue and purple” → “red and gold glow” for a fiery, powerful vibe.
  • Replace “comic-style murals” → “cyberpunk city streets” for a darker, more futuristic look.
  • Switch “holographic rifle” → “glowing energy sword” for a fantasy-warrior style.
  • Add “floating neon graffiti symbols in the air” for an even more surreal atmosphere.
