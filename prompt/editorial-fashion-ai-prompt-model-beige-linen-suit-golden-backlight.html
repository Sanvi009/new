The Prompt 

Use uploaded photos strictly as reference for face identity, facial features, body anatomy, proportions, skin tone. High-end editorial fashion photograph.me as model from reference photos, exact facial features, skin texture, and body anatomy. Full-body shot,facing camera,oversized beige linen suit, black graphic t-shirt,patterned sneakers. Intense warm golden-orange side/back lighting, long shadows on a minimalist concrete studio floor. Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.2, cinematic film. aspect ratio 9:16. do according to my gender

How to Use This Prompt

Upload one or more clear reference photos of yourself. Copy the prompt into your AI image generator. It will create a vertical (9:16) full-body editorial photo using your likeness, dressed in the specified outfit, with dramatic golden backlighting and a clean studio background.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by leading with a strong, clear instruction about how to use the reference image ('strictly as reference for face identity... body anatomy'), which sets the priority on accuracy. It then combines high-fashion keywords ('high-end editorial fashion photograph', 'oversized beige linen suit') with specific photographic equipment and lighting terms ('Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.2', 'intense warm golden-orange side/back lighting'). This gives the AI a clear blueprint for both the subject's appearance and the professional photographic style.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the outfit color from 'beige linen' to 'black wool' or 'white textured suit'.
  • Swap the 'minimalist concrete studio' for a 'luxury loft' or 'urban rooftop' setting.
  • Alter the lighting from 'golden-orange backlight' to 'cool blue rim light' or 'soft window light'.
  • Change the camera angle from 'facing camera' to a 'three-quarters profile' or 'walking pose'.
  • Replace the 'black graphic t-shirt' with a 'plain white tank top' or 'silky button-up shirt'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'side/back lighting' mean for the photo?
    It means the main light source is positioned behind or to the side of the subject, pointing back toward the camera. This creates a bright rim or halo of light around the edges of the subject (especially the hair and shoulders), deep shadows on the front, and a very dramatic, high-contrast look.
  • Why specify 'Canon EOS R5' and '85mm f/1.2'?
    These are specific camera and lens models known for high-quality portrait photography. Using them as keywords instructs the AI to mimic the sharpness, depth of field (the blurred background from f/1.2), and color rendering associated with that professional gear, leading to a more realistic editorial photo style.
  • How many reference photos should I upload?
    One clear, well-lit front-facing photo is often enough. For best body anatomy accuracy, you could upload one face shot and one full-body shot. The AI will blend the reference information to create the final image.
