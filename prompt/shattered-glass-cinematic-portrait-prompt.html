Prompt Explanation

This prompt is meticulously engineered to generate a high-end cinematic portrait that is as much a study in optical physics as it is a piece of editorial fashion or psychological drama. Its creative purpose is to produce an image where the subject's identity is not only preserved but also multiplied and distorted through the refractive medium of shattered glass, creating a layered, introspective, and visually arresting composition. The prompt_text serves as a highly detailed directive for the AI, functioning less like a simple description and more like a technical brief for a photoshoot. It begins with the non-negotiable command to preserve the subject's identity exactly, using phrases like "preserve exactly the original facial features and hairstyle" and "the subject must remain the same person as the reference image." This anchors the entire generation process, ensuring that despite the complex visual effects, the output remains a personal and recognizable portrait. The text then builds the scene with precise instructions: the subject is placed in a dark, elegant aesthetic with black clothing, which is crucial for creating high contrast and ensuring the subject doesn't blend into the deep red background. This specific color combination of red and black is chosen for its psychological intensity and visual pop, making the subject and the glass fragments leap off the canvas. The top-down camera angle is a key compositional choice, adding a sense of vulnerability or editorial detachment, framing the face and upper body in an unconventional and dramatic way. The true genius of the prompt lies in its description of the shattered glass. It doesn't just ask for glass; it demands "realistic shattered glass fragments suspended in mid-air" that "reflect and refract fragments of the original reference photo itself." By specifying that each shard must contain portions of the face, eyes, and clothing, the prompt ensures the effect is integral to the subject, not just an added filter. The request for "optically correct" refraction with "mirrored angles, light bending, depth variation, and sharp glass edges" pushes the AI to simulate real-world physics, elevating the image from a simple digital effect to a believable, tangible phenomenon. The lighting is described in cinematic terms: dramatic, with controlled highlights on the glass and soft shadows on the subject, guiding the AI to create depth and mood. The demand for ultra-realistic skin texture with visible pores ensures that the subject remains grounded in reality, providing a sharp, human contrast to the abstract, shattered glass. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's core identity and the fundamental aesthetic of dark, elegant, cinematic portrait photography. Modifying elements like the background color to emerald green or midnight blue, as suggested in the variations, would maintain the high-contrast, dramatic feel but shift the overall color psychology from passionate intensity to calm depth or mystery. Changing the shattered glass to cracked ice would alter the texture from sharp, dangerous edges to something more crystalline and fragile, changing the metaphor from psychological breaking to one of coldness or purity. To improve accuracy, users must upload a high-quality reference photo with clear lighting and a neutral expression. Common mistakes include using a low-resolution source image or not emphasizing the image weight parameter in their chosen AI tool, which can lead to the AI inventing new facial features rather than preserving the original. The prompt's strength is its fusion of strict identity preservation with a complex, physically descriptive visual effect, forcing the AI to reconcile the concrete (the person) with the abstract (the refracted light), resulting in a powerful and unique portrait that sits perfectly at the intersection of premium photography, hyperrealism, and psychological drama.

The Prompt 

Transform the reference photo into a high-end cinematic portrait with a shattered glass foreground.
Preserve exactly the original facial features and hairstyle. The subject must remain the same person as the reference image, preserving facial structure, skin tone, and identity.
Do not modify or invent any elements.
The subject should be wearing black clothing only, styled in a bold, high-fashion editorial way. Maintain a dark, elegant aesthetic.
The composition should be a close-up camera shot from above (top-down angle), tightly framed around the subject’s face and upper body.
Background should be clean and minimal, preferably a deep solid red color that contrasts with black clothing and skin tones, without distractions.
Realistic shattered glass fragments should be suspended in mid-air around the border of the composition. Each glass shard must reflect and refract fragments of the original reference photo itself.. portions of the subject’s face, eyes, skin, clothing, and lighting.. creating multiple distorted reflections of the same subject across the broken glass.
The reflections must feel optically correct: realistic refraction, mirrored angles, light bending, depth variation, and sharp glass edges.
The subject behind the glass remains sharp, powerful, and emotionally intense, while the reflections in the shards echo the same identity from different angles.
Lighting is cinematic and dramatic, with controlled highlights on the glass, soft shadows on the subject, and film-grade colour grading.
Ultra-realistic skin texture, visible pores, natural imperfections, realistic fabric detail.
Style: cinematic movie poster, psychological drama, premium photography, hyperrealism, ultra-detailed, professional lighting, sharp focus, 4K resolution, no distortion.
Aspect Ratio 9:16 (portrait)
Image quality: ultra-high resolution, photorealistic, editorial finish

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference portrait as an image prompt. 2. Copy the full prompt text exactly as written. 3. Paste into Midjourney, DALL-E, or Stable Diffusion. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16 (portrait mode). 5. Use highest quality / resolution settings. 6. Generate multiple times to get optimal glass refraction details.

Why This Prompt Works

It explicitly commands identity preservation so the output remains a recognizable portrait. It describes a physical phenomenon (refraction in glass) in concrete optical terms, which advanced AI image models can simulate. It anchors the scene with opposing colors (red/black) for visual impact. It uses professional photography terminology (top-down angle, film-grade grading, ultra-realistic skin) to guide the model toward a polished editorial aesthetic.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace red background with deep emerald green or midnight blue
  • Change shattered glass to cracked ice or crystalline shards
  • Switch black clothing to white or metallic silver
  • Alter mood from dramatic to ethereal with softer lighting
  • Add subtle water droplets on glass for rainy window effect
  • Use gold foil fragments instead of glass for luxury editorial
  • Make the reflections show alternate expressions of same subject

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will this prompt work without a reference photo?
    No, this prompt is designed to be used with a reference image. The phrase 'transform the reference photo' and 'preserve identity' require an uploaded source portrait.
  • Which AI generator handles the glass refraction best?
    Midjourney v6 or Stable Diffusion with ControlNet typically produce the most optically accurate glass refraction and identity preservation.
  • Can I use this for commercial fashion editorials?
    Yes, but you must own the rights to the reference photo. The output is suitable for album art, posters, or fashion campaigns.
  • Why does my subject's face change despite the prompt?
    Some AI models struggle with strict identity lock. Try using a higher image weight (--iw 2 in Midjourney) or face-swap extensions in Stable Diffusion.
  • What does 'top-down angle' mean?
    The camera is positioned above the subject looking downward. It creates a dramatic, editorial perspective often used in high-fashion photography.
