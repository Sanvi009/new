The Prompt 

Vertical collage of three stacked frames of me with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image, captured on a rooftop at night. Candid smartphone style, high ISO, slight grain. Top Frame: View from behind and side.  i touching the back of my neck, fingers in hair. Black leather jacket texture visible. Middle Frame: Extreme close-up of the face (eyes and forehead).  Intense, slightly blurry candid look. Skin texture and pores visible. Bottom Frame: Mid-shot. Leaning casually against a concrete rooftop wall. Wearing black leather jacket and black bootcut pants. Looking sideways (profile view), calm expression. 

Background: Dark night sky, distant city lights, concrete rooftop floor. Lighting: Soft ambient urban glow, no flash do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy this prompt into your AI image generator. Upload your reference photo when possible. Change 'i' to 'he' or 'she' based on your subject's gender. The prompt creates a vertical (9:16) three-frame collage with candid, smartphone-style photography captured on a rooftop at night.

Why This Prompt Works

Clear structural instructions ('vertical collage of three stacked frames') help AI understand the multi-image layout. Specific frame descriptions (top, middle, bottom) provide detailed composition guidance. Photography terms ('high ISO, slight grain', 'candid smartphone style') create consistent aesthetic results across different AI models.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'rooftop at night' to 'beach at sunset' or 'city street daytime'
  • Replace 'black leather jacket' with 'denim jacket' or 'hoodie' for different styles
  • Try 'four frames' instead of 'three frames' for more complex collages
  • Swap 'smartphone style' for 'film camera style' or 'DSR photography'
  • Change 'distant city lights' to 'neon signs' or 'starry sky' for different night backgrounds

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I create a portrait collage with AI?
    Use clear structural phrases like 'vertical collage of three stacked frames'. Describe each frame separately (top, middle, bottom) with specific poses and compositions for best results.
  • Which AI generators handle collages best?
    Midjourney can create collages with careful prompting. Stable Diffusion may require separate image generation and manual assembly. DALL-E 3 understands multi-frame concepts well.
  • Can I make this collage without a reference photo?
    Yes, but change 'me' to 'a person' or 'a man/woman'. The collage structure will still work, but facial features won't match your specific appearance.
  • What's the best aspect ratio for vertical collages?
    9:16 works well for mobile viewing and mimics smartphone portrait orientation. You can also try 2:3 or 4:5 for different layout proportions.
