How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, apply it for canned drink ads, splash photography, or energy product campaigns in marketing. Preserve "hyper-realistic product advertisement," "tall green 'Four Loko Sour Apple' can," "splashing neon-green liquid," and "4:5" for freshness. Do not modify the glossy surface or vibrant backdrop. Changing flavor to "Grape" could produce purple visuals, leading to floating slices, while Apple keeps dynamic, ultra-detailed results.

Prompt

A hyper-realistic product advertisement of a tall green "Four Loko Sour Apple" can, tilted diagonally in mid-air, surrounded by splashing neon-green liquid and floating fresh green apple slices. The can has a glossy surface with bold, large "SOUR" lettering and camouflage-like patterns in green shades. Crisp water droplets and liquid splashes wrap dynamically around the can, creating a sense of motion. In the background, whole and sliced green apples float, some with leaves attached. Lighting is vivid and dramatic, with strong reflections on the can and apples. The backdrop is a smooth green gradient, transitioning from light green on the left to deep green on the right, emphasizing freshness and energy. Ultra-detailed, 8K resolution, cinematic product photography style, high contrast, perfect sharpness, and vibrant colors. 4:5

Prompt Variations

  • Flavor/Product: Swap “Four Loko Sour Apple” → “Four Loko Gold” for a different flavor/color. Swap “green apple slices” → “peach slices” for a peach flavor.
  • Liquid Dynamic: Swap “splashing neon-green liquid” → “a cascading waterfall of the drink” for a different motion.
  • Background Gradient: Swap “smooth green gradient” → a “black background” to make the neon green pop even more.
  • Can Design: Swap “camouflage-like patterns” → “circuit board patterns” for a tech-inspired design. Swap “glossy surface” → “a matte surface” with glossy droplets.
