Casual Urban Fashion Portrait with Vintage Car

AI Image Prompts 2026-04-25 Brainloom

This premium AI image prompt generates a highly realistic, editorial-style full-body portrait of a person casually leaning against a classic vintage car in a quiet European neighborhood. The composition captures a relaxed, candid moment with the subject sipping coffee, dressed in a light blue linen shirt and tailored gray trousers. Perfect for creating lifestyle photography with natural daylight, authentic skin textures, and a soft, muted color grading that mimics high-end fashion magazines.

The Prompt

Ultra-realistic full-body portrait of a person (replace with my exact face, gender, facial structure, proportions, and skin tone — strict identity preservation). Do NOT beautify, stylize, or alter facial features. Preserve natural asymmetries, pores, skin texture, fine lines, and real facial proportions. Maintain true likeness and identity consistency.
Escena y composición:
Urban street scene in a quiet European-style neighborhood, soft natural daylight. The subject is casually leaning against the rear side of a parked light-colored vintage car (off-white or cream tone), positioned on a clean street curb. Behind them, a minimalist building façade with a dark decorative door and a window with white framing.
The subject is standing in a relaxed contrapposto pose: one leg crossed casually over the other, back slightly resting on the car, one hand inside the pocket, the other hand holding a takeaway coffee cup near their mouth in a mid-sip gesture. Head turned slightly sideways (profile or 3/4 angle), gaze directed away from the camera, creating a candid, effortless vibe.
Outfit (clave estética):by Lagz.
Light blue button-up shirt, slightly unbuttoned at the top (relaxed, breathable fabric like linen or cotton), sleeves rolled casually to the forearms
High-waisted tailored trousers in a soft gray tone, slightly loose fit with clean pleats
Black chunky leather loafers (modern, slightly thick sole)
Minimal accessories: subtle necklace(s), possibly a ring
Optional: black tote bag hanging from one shoulder for a casual urban feel
Colores y estética (muy importante):
Soft, muted color palette inspired by editorial fashion photography:
Light desaturated blue (shirt)
Neutral gray tones (pants)
Cream/white car
Subtle warm-gray street tones
Balanced color grading, slightly warm-neutral with soft contrast. No oversaturation.

Natural daylight, diffused lighting (overcast or soft sun), no harsh shadows. Even skin tones, realistic highlights, soft shadow transitions. Slight cinematic softness.
Cámara y estilo fotográfico:
Shot on a full-frame camera
35mm or 50mm lens look
Medium-full body framing
Slight depth of field (background softly blurred but still readable)
Editorial fashion photography style
Sharp focus on subject
Detalles realistas:
Natural fabric wrinkles on clothing
Realistic posture (not stiff)
Coffee cup slightly tilted mid-drink
Hair naturally styled (slightly messy but intentional)
No artificial smoothing or plastic skin
Negative prompt (MUY IMPORTANTE):
Do not alter identity, no beautification filters, no exaggerated jawline, no unrealistic symmetry, no cartoonish rendering, no over-sharpening, no HDR effect, no distorted proportions, no extra limbs, no unnatural poses.

How to Use

To use this prompt effectively, copy the entire text and paste it into your preferred AI image generator like Gemini, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion. You will need to replace the placeholder text regarding facial features with a detailed description of your own face, including specific details like a short beard or neck tattoos to ensure strict identity preservation. You can easily modify the clothing colors, the make of the vintage car, or the background setting to match your specific creative vision while maintaining the effortless, candid vibe.

FAQ

  • How do I ensure the AI generates my exact face using this prompt?
    You need to replace the placeholder text in the prompt with a highly detailed, objective description of your physical features. In some advanced tools, you can also use image reference features or face-swapping capabilities in conjunction with this prompt to guarantee strict identity preservation.
  • Can I change the color of the clothing or the vintage car?
    Yes, the prompt is highly modular. You can easily swap "light blue button-up shirt" or "soft gray tone" trousers with your preferred colors and fabrics. Similarly, you can change "off-white or cream tone" to a different vintage car color without breaking the overall cinematic composition.
  • What AI image generator works best for this specific style?
    This prompt is optimized for high-end, photorealistic models like Midjourney v6, Stable Diffusion with realistic checkpoints, or Google's latest image models. The detailed instructions regarding lighting, camera lenses (35mm/50mm), and natural skin textures will yield the best editorial fashion results on these advanced platforms.
