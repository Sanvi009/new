How to Use

Customizing this prompt allows you to completely shift the narrative and aesthetic. Start by modifying the wardrobe; you can change the 'white tuxedo, black shirt, white bow tie' to a 'classic black tuxedo' or a 'red velvet smoking jacket' for a different vibe. To alter the setting, replace the 'upscale casino-style interior' with a '1920s underground speakeasy' or a 'modern penthouse overlooking a city'. You can also change the floating objects from 'playing cards suspended mid-air' to 'casino chips', 'floating gold coins', or even 'glowing dice'. Finally, experiment with lighting by swapping 'warm ambient lighting' for 'dramatic spotlight' or 'moody neon lights'.

FAQ

  • How do I ensure the face matches my uploaded selfie perfectly?
    To get the best facial resemblance, use an AI generator that supports image referencing or character consistency. In Midjourney, you can use the `--cref` (character reference) parameter followed by the URL of your selfie. Ensure your uploaded photo has clear lighting and your face is fully visible without heavy shadows or obstructions.
  • How do I make the floating cards look realistic instead of distorted?
    Rendering multiple distinct objects like playing cards in mid-air can sometimes cause blending issues in AI generation. To improve this, use descriptive keywords like 'sharp focus on cards' and 'crisp details'. If using an advanced tool like Stable Diffusion, utilizing a ControlNet depth map can help the AI understand the spatial positioning of the floating objects.
  • Can I change the clothing from a white tuxedo to something casual?
    Absolutely! The prompt is highly customizable. Simply locate the wardrobe keywords ('WHITE TUXEDO, BLACK SHIRT, WHITE BOW TIE') and replace them with your desired outfit, such as 'BLACK LEATHER JACKET, WHITE T-SHIRT' or 'NAVY BLUE TAILORED SUIT'. Ensure your new description matches the vibe you want for the final image.
