FAQ

  • What does 'co-ord' mean in the outfit description?
    'Co-ord' is short for 'co-ordinates', meaning a matching set (like a jacket and pants or a top and skirt) designed to be worn together. Here, it's a black pinstripe oversized matching set.
  • How does the AI handle 'translucent' for the ghost?
    The term 'translucent' instructs the AI to make the ghost semi-transparent, so you can see the background and parts of your body through it, creating a spectral, non-solid appearance that blends with the scene.
  • What is the aspect ratio for this prompt?
    The prompt specifies (3:4), which is a standard horizontal/portrait ratio, different from the common 9:16. This is closer to a traditional photograph or magazine page proportion.
