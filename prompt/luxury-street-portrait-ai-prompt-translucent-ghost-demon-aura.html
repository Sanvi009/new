Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a fascinating exercise in contrast, aiming to blend the grounded reality of luxury street fashion photography with the ethereal, unsettling presence of supernatural entities. Its creative purpose is to create a narrative-driven portrait where the subject is not just a model in an outfit, but a character in a story, existing in a world where the mundane and the paranormal coexist. The scene is set on a city sidewalk outside a modern café, a relatable, everyday location that immediately establishes a sense of realism. Into this familiar setting, the prompt introduces two key surreal elements: a translucent 'See No Evil' ghost floating close behind the subject, and a subtle purple shadow demon aura. This juxtaposition creates a mysterious cinematic mood, transforming a simple street style snapshot into a piece of visual storytelling that hints at hidden dimensions or a personal spiritual narrative. The prompt_text is a masterclass in prioritization. Its opening lines are the most critical: 'Face 100% original and unchanged—no reshaping or filters, real skin texture preserved.' This is an absolute, non-negotiable command to the AI. Before any consideration is given to the ghost, the aura, or even the outfit, the AI is instructed to treat the uploaded image with sacred reverence. This anchors the entire fantasy in a bedrock of reality, ensuring the final image feels like a photograph of a real person who happens to have a ghost behind them, rather than a cartoonish or fully synthetic creation. The emphasis on preserving skin texture is particularly astute, as it's often the first thing AI models smooth over in pursuit of an idealized aesthetic. By demanding its preservation, the prompt guarantees a level of grit and authenticity that is essential for the 'ultra-realistic' tag. Following this, the prompt builds the real-world elements: the 'black pinstripe oversized co-ord, white chunky sneakers, black sunglasses' and the casual, confident pose of 'one hand at neck, one in pocket.' This outfit description is precise, dictating not just the items but their style—'oversized' and 'chunky' are key contemporary fashion keywords that signal a specific, trendy aesthetic. The pose adds to the nonchalant, cool attitude expected in luxury street style photography. The lighting and mood are subtly guided by the phrase 'mysterious cinematic mood,' which tells the AI to employ dramatic, possibly overcast or shadow-dappled lighting, rather than harsh, direct sunlight, to enhance the ethereal quality of the ghost and aura. The supernatural elements are described with a specificity that prevents them from becoming generic or overpowering. The ghost is not just a ghost; it is a 'translucent ‘See No Evil’ ghost with hands covering eyes, floating close.' The term 'translucent' is vital, instructing the AI on the materiality of the spirit—it must be semi-transparent, allowing the background and parts of the subject to be visible through it. The 'See No Evil' posture gives it a distinct character and purpose, suggesting themes of secrecy, willful ignorance, or protection. The 'subtle purple shadow demon aura aligned behind' is an even more ethereal layer. It is not a solid entity but an 'aura,' a field of energy. The color purple is often associated with mystery, spirituality, and the arcane, perfectly complementing the mood. The instruction for it to be 'aligned behind' ensures it frames the subject without distracting from them. The elements that absolutely must not be changed are the foundational rules of photorealism: the subject's face must remain 100% identical to the reference, and the real skin texture must be preserved. Changing these would break the contract of the prompt. The core concept of a ghost and an aura is also sacrosanct. However, many elements can be creatively modified. The promptVariations section offers excellent starting points: swapping the ghost for 'Hear No Evil' or 'Speak No Evil' spirits would change the narrative theme. Altering the aura's color would shift the emotional tone from mysterious to, say, envious with green or angry with red. The background can be changed from a modern café to a rainy alleyway or a neon-lit street, which would drastically alter the mood from sophisticated urban to film-noir or cyberpunk. The outfit is also highly variable; changing it from a pinstripe co-ord to a colorful tailored suit or elegant dress would transform the subject's personal style. To improve accuracy and visual quality, the reference photo is paramount. It should be a high-resolution, well-lit image where the face is clear and the skin texture is visible. For the supernatural elements, if the AI struggles to make the ghost translucent or to create a cohesive aura, using a tool like Photoshop to composite separately generated elements can yield superior results. A common mistake is to overlook the aspect ratio specification of 3:4, which is crucial for a traditional, print-friendly composition. Another mistake is to provide a reference photo where the pose or framing is wildly different from the described scene, confusing the AI. Users also often forget the instruction 'Do according to my gender,' which is a simple but effective way to ensure the clothing is applied appropriately, especially in models that might default to a specific gender presentation. Ultimately, this prompt is a brilliant example of how to construct a narrative within a single frame, using the precision of language to command an AI to balance hyper-realism with imaginative surrealism, creating a portrait that is as thought-provoking as it is stylish.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic outdoor luxury portrait (3:4). of me with exact same facial ad physical appearance from the uploaded image Face 100% original and unchanged—no reshaping or filters, real skin texture preserved. on a city sidewalk before a modern café, same framing as reference. Wearing black pinstripe oversized co-ord, white chunky sneakers, black sunglasses; one hand at neck, one in pocket. Behind me: a translucent ‘See No Evil’ ghost with hands covering eyes, floating close. Subtle purple shadow demon aura aligned behind, mysterious cinematic mood. Do according to my gender

How to Use This Prompt

Upload a clear photo of yourself, ideally in a similar pose or setting. Copy the prompt into your AI image generator. It will create a horizontal (3:4) luxury fashion portrait using your exact facial features, placing you in the described outfit and supernatural scene with the ghost and aura.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by establishing a strong base of photorealism and fidelity ('Face 100% original and unchanged', 'real skin texture preserved') before introducing surreal elements. This helps the AI anchor the image in reality. The supernatural elements are described clearly and specifically ('translucent ‘See No Evil’ ghost', 'subtle purple shadow demon aura'), giving the AI a distinct visual concept to integrate. Combining 'luxury portrait' with 'mysterious cinematic mood' guides the overall tone and lighting toward a high-end, dramatic look.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the supernatural entity to a 'Hear No Evil' or 'Speak No Evil' monkey spirit.
  • Alter the aura color from 'purple' to 'green' or 'red' for a different emotional tone (envy, anger).
  • Swap the 'modern café' background for a 'rainy alleyway' or 'neon-lit street'.
  • Change the outfit from 'black pinstripe' to a 'colorful tailored suit' or 'elegant dress'.
  • Remove the supernatural elements entirely for a straightforward luxury street fashion portrait.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'co-ord' mean in the outfit description?
    'Co-ord' is short for 'co-ordinates', meaning a matching set (like a jacket and pants or a top and skirt) designed to be worn together. Here, it's a black pinstripe oversized matching set.
  • How does the AI handle 'translucent' for the ghost?
    The term 'translucent' instructs the AI to make the ghost semi-transparent, so you can see the background and parts of your body through it, creating a spectral, non-solid appearance that blends with the scene.
  • What is the aspect ratio for this prompt?
    The prompt specifies (3:4), which is a standard horizontal/portrait ratio, different from the common 9:16. This is closer to a traditional photograph or magazine page proportion.
