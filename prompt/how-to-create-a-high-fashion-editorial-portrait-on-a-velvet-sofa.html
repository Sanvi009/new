How to Create a High-Fashion Editorial Portrait on a Velvet Sofa

AI Image Prompts
Published: 2026-03-08
Author: Brainloom

Generate a sophisticated, ultra-realistic 8k image of yourself reclining on a burnt-orange velvet modular sofa in a moody industrial loft. This prompt creates a high-fashion editorial shot featuring specific clothing details like a crisp white t-shirt, pleated black trousers, and dark sunglasses, all under cinematic top-lighting. Perfect for AI artists and fashion enthusiasts looking to create personalized, magazine-quality portraits.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt, simply replace the core details to match your desired subject. Start by describing the person (e.g., 'a woman in her 30s with curly red hair' or 'a man with a beard'). You can then customize the clothing colors and styles, change the sofa color (e.g., emerald green, charcoal gray), or adjust the loft environment. For a different mood, modify the lighting from 'cinematic top-lighting' to 'soft window light' or 'neon accent lights'. You can also change the camera perspective or add unique objects to the background like a sculpture or a record player.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I change the person in the image to someone else?
    The prompt is designed to be easily customizable. Simply replace the phrase 'me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image' with a description of your desired subject. For example, you could write 'a 40-year-old man with a thoughtful expression and short brown hair' or 'a young woman with freckles and long blonde hair in a messy bun.' This flexibility allows you to create the perfect editorial portrait for any project.
  • What AI image generators work best with this prompt?
    This prompt is optimized for advanced AI image generators that excel at photorealism, such as Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. For best results, ensure the AI tool you are using supports specific aspect ratios (ar 9:16) and is capable of rendering ultra-realistic textures and complex lighting scenarios. If your tool supports image prompting, you can also upload a reference image for the subject's identity.
  • How can I adjust the lighting for a different mood?
    Lighting is key to the mood of your image. To create a brighter, more upbeat feel, change 'soft cinematic top-lighting' to 'soft, bright natural window light' or 'airy, diffused skylight'. For a more dramatic or mysterious look, try 'dramatic chiaroscuro lighting from a single lamp' or 'cool, moody light with deep, volumetric shadows'. Experimenting with lighting descriptions will dramatically alter the final atmosphere of your high-fashion portrait.
