The Prompt 

Create a high-resolution, hyperrealistic image of a [attached object] sealed inside a vacuum-packed, crinkled metallic or transparent plastic pouch. The packaging should tightly conform to the shape of the object, exaggerating its curves, edges, and texture as if suctioned in place. Add condensation or small creases around pressure points for realism. The object inside must retain a bold, clean look—graphic or iconic in shape (e.g. smiley face, toy, tool, accessory). Background should be white or soft gray to highlight reflections and surface tension. Add typographic overlays, SKU codes, or branded text for a dystopian, collectible archival aesthetic. Visual details: transparent or metallic vacuum plastic, harsh lighting, extreme detail silhouette, cinematic realism. Mood: experimental, edgy, collectible, post-consumer product photography.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload your reference object and copy the prompt into your generator. Keep the aspect ratio close to square or product-shot framing for emphasis. For Midjourney, add `--ar 1:1 --v 5 --q 2` for balanced product display. For Stable Diffusion, enable high-detail rendering for packaging creases and reflections. Make sure lighting is bright and directional to capture metallic or transparent vacuum plastic textures. Fine-tune negative prompts to avoid warped edges or distorted packaging folds.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it layers hyperrealistic packaging details with clean, iconic object design. The vacuum-sealed effect creates dramatic tension between form and texture, while harsh lighting and creases enhance realism. The archival, collectible aesthetic with codes and branding adds depth and storytelling, making the result visually striking and conceptually strong.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace 'transparent plastic pouch' with 'fully metallic silver pouch' for a futuristic industrial feel.
  • Replace 'white/soft gray background' with 'dark gradient background' for a moody product display.
  • Replace 'SKU codes or branding overlays' with 'handwritten labels and doodles' for an indie art style.
  • Replace 'smiley face or toy' with 'retro game controller' to give a nostalgic collectible look.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get realistic vacuum creases in the image?
    Include terms like 'crinkled metallic plastic', 'tight suctioned packaging', and 'creases around pressure points'. Bright directional lighting will help emphasize folds and texture.
  • Can I adapt this prompt for logos or flat designs?
    Yes. Place your logo or flat graphic as the object inside the vacuum pouch. The packaging effect will give it depth and a collectible, experimental look.
