iPhone Selfie with T-800 Robot AI Prompt for Gemini

AI Image Prompts 2026-04-21 Brainloom

Generate an ultra-realistic vertical iPhone-style selfie featuring the exact facial and physical identity from an uploaded reference photo with no alterations whatsoever. The image captures a casual, unpolished front-camera shot with slightly uneven framing and the characteristic slight lens distortion of a phone selfie. The subject wears a stylish modern outfit and stands in the foreground, while the background reveals a daylight apocalyptic scene populated with other T-800-style endoskeleton robots. The photo exhibits natural skin texture, subtle motion blur from handheld movement, and the authentic shallow depth of field typical of a smartphone front camera rather than a professional DSLR. The composition maintains a spontaneous, documentary feel with harsh daylight and the gritty context of a sci-fi survival scenario, all rendered in a 4:5 vertical aspect ratio.

The Prompt

Create an ultra-realistic 3D selfie in vertical aspect ratio from the attached photo of me with exact same facial and physical identity  in stylish modern outfit (use the provided image for accurate facial features) with a T-800-style robot. The photo should look like a typical iPhone selfie-slightly uneven framing, camera angle of view, slight distortion from the front camera, natural skin texture, daylight in the background, an apocalyptic scene with other T-800-style robots, subtle motion blur from hand movements, and authentic depth of field from the iPhone's front camera. Maintain a high level of realism and detail, but with the casual, unpolished look of a real phone photo rather than a professional DSLR. Do not change the facial features of the attached photo under any circumstances. 100% use my real own face, -- ar 4 :5

How to Use

This prompt is specifically engineered to replicate the look and feel of a spontaneous iPhone selfie rather than a polished studio portrait. For the best results in Gemini, upload a clear, front-facing reference photo. The AI will map your exact face onto the selfie composition. The key to this prompt's authenticity lies in the 'imperfections' described. To enhance the iPhone aesthetic, ensure the generation settings do not use any 'enhance' or 'upscale' filters that smooth out skin texture. If the background T-800 robots appear too sharp or perfectly placed, add the phrase `robots are slightly out of focus in the background depth of field.` You can adjust the 'stylish modern outfit' by specifying clothing items, for example, `wearing a distressed leather jacket` or `wearing a simple black hoodie,` to better fit the apocalyptic theme. The motion blur effect is subtle; if you want it more pronounced to simulate a quick snap, change `subtle motion blur` to `slight directional blur on the edges of the frame.`

FAQ

  • The selfie looks too perfect and sharp, like a DSLR photo. How do I make it look like a real iPhone shot?
    To degrade the image quality to authentic smartphone levels, you need to add more 'flaws' to the prompt. Include phrases like: `slight digital noise in the shadows,` `softness around the edges of the frame due to fixed focus lens,` and `slight overexposure on the highlights.` You can also specify the exact iPhone model, such as `shot on iPhone 14 Pro front camera,` which cues the AI to use the specific lens characteristics and processing look associated with that device. Adding `minor compression artifacts` or `image slightly softened by hand movement` also helps break the sterile, perfect rendering that AI defaults to.
  • The T-800 robot looks like a cartoon or a toy. How do I make it more menacing and realistic?
    The term 'T-800' is a pop culture reference, but AI might generate a generic 'shiny robot.' To get a more authentic, menacing endoskeleton, use more specific physical descriptors. Replace `T-800-style robot` with `hyper-realistic chrome combat endoskeleton with glowing red optical sensors, exposed hydraulic pistons, and a grimacing metal skull,` and add `battle-damaged with scorch marks and exposed wiring.` Placing the robot in the `shallow depth of field background` helps mask any minor anatomical imperfections, as the blur will naturally soften the details and make it feel more like a real, out-of-focus object caught in a snapshot.
  • I want to use this for a funny post, but the apocalyptic scene looks too scary. Can I make it lighter?
    Yes, you can easily pivot the mood from horror to humor by changing the subject's expression and the robot's posture. Change the subject's expression from neutral to `giving a peace sign and a goofy grin.` For the robot, describe it as `standing awkwardly in the background like a lost tourist` or `giving a thumbs up back.` You can even add a prop to the robot: `the T-800 is holding a 'Will Work for Oil' cardboard sign.` These contextual cues shift the AI's interpretation from a tense survival scenario to a comedic, meme-worthy selfie, while still preserving your exact face and the iPhone photo aesthetic.
