The outfit stands out clearly with a black oversized full-sleeve t-shirt featuring front design details, paired with white baggy jeans and black sports shoes. How to Use

Paste this prompt into Gemini AI, Midjourney, or any advanced image generation tool. Use the uploaded reference image so the model can match your facial structure and identity accurately. Keep aspect ratio set to 9:16 for a full portrait frame. If your tool allows, adjust lighting strength to keep the scene dark but not underexposed. You can tweak outfit details like shirt design or shoe style if you want variation. For stronger realism, emphasize keywords like low angle shot, natural skin tone, and cinematic lighting in advanced settings or prompt boosters.

FAQ

  • How do I make the face match my identity more accurately?
    Always upload a clear reference image and keep identity lock or face reference strength high in your tool settings. This helps the model preserve facial structure instead of reimagining it.
  • Can I change the outfit without breaking the realism?
    Yes, just swap clothing details while keeping lighting and pose consistent. Avoid overloading the prompt with too many style changes at once to maintain realism.
  • Why does lighting change the whole result so much?
    Lighting controls mood, shadows, and depth. Even small changes like warm vs cool lighting can completely shift the final atmosphere of the image.
