Ensure the instructions regarding 'exact same facial and physical identity' remain strong to preserve your identity.
  • How can I remove the cigar and lighter from the scene?
    To remove the cigar and lighter, simply delete the parts about 'lighting a thick cigar with a metal lighter, the flame illuminating my face while smoke curls upward in detailed wisps.' You can replace it with a different action, such as 'holding a smartphone' or 'adjusting sunglasses.'
  • What if the window reflection hides too much of my face?
    If reflections obscure your face, you can add instructions to 'prioritize sharp facial features despite the reflections.' You could also modify the angle slightly by specifying 'the reflection is subtle' or 'focus is on the face through the window.'
