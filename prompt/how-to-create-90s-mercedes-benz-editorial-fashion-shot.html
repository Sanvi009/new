Generate a high-end editorial photo of yourself leaning against a classic 90s Mercedes-Benz coupe. This prompt blends quiet luxury with streetwear aesthetics using specific wardrobe and lighting details.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to bridge the gap between personal identity and aspirational fashion photography using AI. At its core, it’s about placing a specific person—you—into a highly stylized, magazine-worthy scene that would be logistically difficult and expensive to produce in real life. Renting a classic Mercedes, hiring a stylist, and scouting a brutalist architecture location costs thousands. This prompt collapses that entire production into a single text string. The structure of the prompt is critical to its success. It begins by establishing the hero object: the '90s Mercedes-Benz coupe silver metallic.' This isn't just any car; specifying the decade, the model type, and the color provides the AI with a tight visual reference. The 90s Mercedes design language is distinct—sharp lines, boxy yet elegant—and the AI needs that anchor to avoid generating a generic or modern vehicle. The silver metallic finish is also key, as it interacts beautifully with light, which is controlled later in the prompt. Next, the prompt tackles the hardest part of AI image generation: identity. Most prompts rely on describing a generic 'handsome man' or 'stylish woman,' which leads to inconsistent, randomly generated faces. By instructing the AI to use the 'exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image,' you are essentially giving it a primary source to work from. This technique leverages the AI's image-to-image capabilities, forcing it to map your features onto the generated scene. The comma after 'serious expression' is intentional; it separates the identity instruction from the action, preventing the AI from blending the concepts. The wardrobe section reads like a lookbook description. 'Linen cream button-up shirt' tells the AI three things: fabric (texture), color (mood), and garment type (silhouette). 'Chocolate brown relaxed trousers' adds contrast to the cream and silver, while 'gold chains' introduces a streetwear element of flashiness. The 'black boots' ground the look, preventing it from becoming too formal. This careful selection of clothing creates the 'quiet luxury meets streetwear' fusion mentioned later. The environment, 'brutalist concrete background,' is just as important as the subject. Brutalism offers raw, textured, geometric lines that contrast with the smooth curves of the Mercedes and the softness of the linen shirt. This juxtaposition creates visual tension and depth, making the image more interesting than a plain wall or a natural landscape. Lighting and medium are the final touches. 'Soft diffused light' mimics the effect of a large studio softbox or an overcast day, which is flattering for both skin tones and metal surfaces. It eliminates harsh shadows and gives the image a professional, editorial finish. Adding 'film photography' at the end instructs the AI to apply a subtle grain and color grade reminiscent of analog stock, which adds warmth and nostalgia, moving it away from the sterile look of digital photography. A common mistake users make is being too vague. If you were to write 'cool car and guy,' you would get a chaotic result. This prompt succeeds by being a detailed checklist for the AI. Every word functions as a parameter. To tweak it further, users should think like directors. If you want a more aggressive look, change 'serious' to 'intense.' If you want a summer vibe, switch the concrete for a 'palm springs pool backdrop.' The ar-9:16 at the end is non-negotiable for this specific vertical format; forgetting it will result in a crop that doesn't suit the fashion theme. Ultimately, this prompt is a template for how to combine personal identity, product photography, and fashion styling into one cohesive AI generation.

The Prompt 

90s Mercedes-Benz coupe silver metallic, me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image leaning casually against car, serious expression, , linen cream button-up shirt, chocolate brown relaxed trousers, gold chains, black boots, brutalist concrete background, quiet luxury meets streetwear, editorial menswear fashion shot, soft diffused light, film photography, ar-9:16

How to Use This Prompt

To get the best results from this prompt, start by uploading a clear, well-lit photo of your face. The AI needs a high-quality reference image to accurately capture your facial identity. Once uploaded, paste the entire prompt into the text field. Do not edit out the part about your identity, as that links the generation to your uploaded photo. Next, pay attention to the order of details. The prompt lists the car first, then your appearance, then the clothing, and finally the environment and lighting. This hierarchy tells the AI what to prioritize. If you want the outfit to stand out more, you could move the clothing description earlier in the prompt. When the image generates, you may need to run it a few times to get the perfect 'lean' on the car. The phrase 'leaning casually' can sometimes produce varied results. If the pose looks stiff, try regenerating or slightly adjusting that wording to 'resting against' or 'lounging on'. Finally, note the aspect ratio at the end: ar-9:16. This is crucial for getting a vertical, portrait-oriented shot suitable for editorial use or social media. If you skip this, the AI might default to a square or landscape orientation, which won't fit the fashion magazine aesthetic you're aiming for.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works exceptionally well because it follows a proven structure: Subject + Action + Main Object + Environment + Style Modifiers. By starting with the specific car ('90s Mercedes-Benz coupe silver metallic'), it immediately anchors the image in a distinct era and luxury context. This gives the AI a strong visual foundation to build upon. The prompt also solves a common AI problem: identity consistency. By explicitly stating 'me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image,' you are forcing the AI to prioritize the reference photo over its generic idea of a person. This instruction reduces the chances of the AI creating a random stranger. Clarity is another strength. Instead of just saying 'nice clothes,' it lists specific items: 'linen cream button-up shirt,' 'chocolate brown relaxed trousers,' and 'black boots.' This level of detail eliminates ambiguity. The AI knows exactly what fabric, color, and fit to render, which creates a cohesive, stylist-approved look. The inclusion of contradictory yet complementary terms like 'quiet luxury meets streetwear' is a high-level prompting technique. It tells the AI to blend two aesthetics, resulting in a more nuanced and interesting image than a simple 'luxury' or 'streetwear' prompt would produce. Finally, technical specs like 'soft diffused light' and 'film photography' guide the mood and texture, ensuring the final image doesn't look like a flat digital snapshot.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need to own a Mercedes-Benz to use this prompt?
    No, you do not need to own the car. The AI generates the vehicle from scratch based on the text description. You are essentially styling a photoshoot with a virtual car.
  • Why isn't the AI keeping my face consistent across multiple generations?
    Ensure your uploaded reference photo is high quality and facing forward. Also, make sure the phrase regarding your identity is exactly as written and placed near the beginning of the prompt to give it high priority.
  • Can I change the outfit to something else?
    Absolutely. You can swap the clothing items for anything you like. Just keep the descriptive style (fabric, color, fit) to maintain the high-fashion quality of the result.
