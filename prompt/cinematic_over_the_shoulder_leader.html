How to use this prompt

Upload the reference face to ensure accuracy. Copy the prompt into your AI generator. Keep the framing for an over-the-shoulder close-up, emphasizing the table details, mask, and blueprint. For Midjourney, use `--ar 16:9 --v 5 --q 2` for cinematic horizontal framing. For Stable Diffusion, enable high-detail rendering for textures on clothing, table props, and mask. Use low-key moody lighting settings to create dramatic shadows, and apply negative prompts to avoid distorted faces or objects.

FAQ

Specify 'low-key lighting with strong spotlight' and ensure the AI prioritizes shadows and highlights on the subject and props. Adjust intensity and direction for depth and focus.

Yes. Replace maps and money with gadgets, blueprints, or other thematic items while keeping hand poses and over-the-shoulder framing to maintain cinematic storytelling.
