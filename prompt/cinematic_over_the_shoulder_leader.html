Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a highly dramatic and narrative-driven portrait, pulling inspiration from the iconic visuals of the series 'La Casa de Papel' (Money Heist). Its creative purpose is to place a specific individual, using a reference photo, into the role of the mastermind behind a grand heist. The scene is constructed to feel like a pivotal moment from a film, full of tension, strategy, and covert leadership. The image aims for an 8K hyper-realistic quality, focusing on the subject's intense concentration and the tangible details of their planning environment. The over-the-shoulder perspective is key, as it immediately draws the viewer into the subject's world, making them a silent observer in this high-stakes scenario, perfect for fan art, concept development, or personalized cinematic posters. The 'promptText' is a meticulously constructed blueprint that dictates every significant visual element. It starts by establishing the point of view: an 'over-the-shoulder close-up,' which is a classic cinematic shot used to create intimacy and a sense of shared perspective. The instruction to 'use reference face' is paramount, as it grounds the fantasy in a specific identity, making the generated image personally relevant or recognizable. The description of the subject includes 'messy hair partially covering the eye,' a small but powerful detail that suggests a character so consumed by his plan that he has no time for personal grooming, adding to the gritty, realistic portrayal. The outfit is specified as a 'money-heavy professional leadership outfit,' which is a cleverly ambiguous term that tells the AI to generate clothing that looks expensive, authoritative, and appropriate for a leader—perhaps a tailored vest, a high-end shirt, or a textured jacket—without being overly specific. The setting is a 'wooden table filled with maps, blueprints, and bundles of money,' providing a wealth of detailed props that enrich the story and create visual interest. The action is dual: he 'holds the La Casa de Papel mask resting on the table while pointing at a blueprint with his other hand.' This combines an iconic prop from the source material with a gesture of planning and direction, perfectly capturing the character's dual role as a keeper of the symbol and the strategic leader. The background is carefully managed with 'red jumpsuit teammates faintly visible, blurred but focused on him,' which establishes a team dynamic and reinforces the heist theme without distracting from the central figure. Finally, the lighting—'moody, low-key light with strong spotlight'—is essential for creating dramatic shadows that sculpt the subject's face and the objects on the table, focusing the viewer's attention and heightening the cinematic tension. The interplay of composition, lighting, and prop detail is what makes this prompt so effective. The over-the-shoulder shot is a powerful narrative tool, immediately establishing a point of view and creating a sense of voyeuristic tension. The low-key lighting with a strong spotlight acts like a theatrical follow-spot, isolating the subject and his immediate planning area from the shadowy background where the team waits. This creates a clear visual hierarchy: the leader and his plan are in the light, while the executors are in the periphery. The props—maps, blueprints, money—are not just filler; they are storytelling devices that communicate the scale and nature of the operation. The hyper-realistic requirement demands that these items be rendered with sharp, tangible detail—the texture of the old paper, the crispness of the banknotes, the reflective quality of the iconic mask. Certain elements are critical to the prompt's core identity and should not be changed: the over-the-shoulder framing, the use of a reference face, the presence of the La Casa de Papel mask as a central prop, and the low-key spotlight lighting. Altering these would fundamentally change the narrative or the aesthetic. However, the prompt offers significant flexibility for creators to adapt the core concept to different genres and aesthetics. The 'La Casa de Papel mask' could be replaced with a 'glowing holographic map,' instantly shifting the scene from a realistic heist to a futuristic, sci-fi planning session. Changing the 'red jumpsuit teammates' to 'business-suited assistants' transforms the narrative from a heist to a high-stakes corporate takeover or a military strategy scene. The setting itself can evolve; swapping the 'wooden table filled with maps and money' for a 'metallic table with high-tech gadgets' would create a sleek, cyberpunk heist aesthetic. Even the subject's appearance can be adjusted, such as replacing 'messy hair over eye' with 'slicked-back hair,' which would portray a more polished, controlled, and professional mastermind rather than a gritty, obsessive one. To achieve the best results, users must strictly adhere to the 'howToInstructions.' Uploading a clear, well-lit reference photo is the most critical step for facial accuracy. For Midjourney, using `--ar 16:9 --v 5 --q 2` is non-negotiable for achieving the correct cinematic widescreen format and high resolution. In Stable Diffusion, enabling high-detail rendering modes will help with the complex textures of the clothing, wooden table, paper, and mask. A common mistake is to neglect the lighting description, resulting in a flat, evenly lit scene that lacks drama. Another pitfall is making the background teammates too distinct, which would pull focus from the main subject. The prompt specifies they should be 'faintly visible, blurred but focused on him,' meaning they are an atmospheric element, not secondary characters. By carefully managing these technical and creative aspects, this prompt consistently delivers a powerful, cinematic over-the-shoulder portrait that successfully merges a specific cultural reference with a personalized, high-stakes narrative.

The Prompt 

8K hyper-realistic cinematic over-the-shoulder close-up of the person (use reference face), messy hair partially covering the eye, dressed in a money-heavy professional leadership outfit. Sitting at a wooden table filled with maps, blueprints, and bundles of money. He holds the La Casa de Papel mask resting on the table while pointing at a blueprint with his other hand. Background: Red jumpsuit teammates faintly visible, blurred but focused on him. Lighting: moody, low-key light with strong spotlight. Cinematic, high-resolution, sharp, realistic skin texture, 8K.

How to Use This Prompt

Upload the reference face to ensure accuracy. Copy the prompt into your AI generator. Keep the framing for an over-the-shoulder close-up, emphasizing the table details, mask, and blueprint. For Midjourney, use `--ar 16:9 --v 5 --q 2` for cinematic horizontal framing. For Stable Diffusion, enable high-detail rendering for textures on clothing, table props, and mask. Use low-key moody lighting settings to create dramatic shadows, and apply negative prompts to avoid distorted faces or objects.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it emphasizes reference-based facial accuracy and combines cinematic composition with dramatic lighting and detailed props. The over-the-shoulder perspective creates narrative tension, while the surrounding teammates and table details provide context and storytelling. The low-key spotlight enhances realism and highlights the subject as the central figure.

Prompt Variations

  • Replace 'La Casa de Papel mask' with 'glowing holographic map' for a futuristic heist aesthetic.
  • Replace 'red jumpsuit teammates' with 'business-suited assistants' for a corporate strategy mood.
  • Replace 'wooden table filled with maps and money' with 'metallic table with high-tech gadgets' for a cyberpunk heist look.
  • Replace 'messy hair over eye' with 'slicked-back hair' for a more polished professional appearance.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make the cinematic lighting look realistic?
    Specify 'low-key lighting with strong spotlight' and ensure the AI prioritizes shadows and highlights on the subject and props. Adjust intensity and direction for depth and focus.
  • Can I change the table props for a different story?
    Yes. Replace maps and money with gadgets, blueprints, or other thematic items while keeping hand poses and over-the-shoulder framing to maintain cinematic storytelling.
