Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a hyperrealistic, fashion-editorial headshot that feels like it was pulled from the pages of a high-end magazine or an arthouse film still. Its creative purpose is to transform a standard reference photo of a person into a dramatic, cinematic portrait that emphasizes texture, mood, and sophisticated lighting, all while maintaining the subject's authentic identity. The prompt achieves this by weaving together specific photography terminology, detailed environmental descriptions, and precise artistic direction, effectively simulating the workflow of a professional photoshoot within the AI's generation process. The prompt text guides the AI by breaking down the image into core components: identity preservation, composition, lighting, styling, and technical render quality. The instruction to preserve 'facial structure, proportions, asymmetry, skin texture, pores, and natural imperfections' is critical. It acts as a direct countermeasure to the AI's tendency to default to smooth, idealized representations, ensuring the output remains a portrait of a real person rather than a generic, plastic mannequin. This focus on photorealism is reinforced by technical terms like '64K DSLR-quality', 'Octane render', and 'Unreal Engine 5', which signal to the AI to aim for the highest possible fidelity and material accuracy. Lighting is the primary driver of the mood in this composition. The prompt specifies a single, hard directional light source passing through blinds to cast 'distinct diagonal shadow patterns'. This creates a chiaroscuro effect, a high-contrast style that adds graphic structure and visual tension, transforming a simple headshot into a study of light and shadow. The color temperature is precisely set between 4300–4800K to ensure warm highlights, while the color grading calls for 'cool green shadows' and 'deep blacks', establishing a sophisticated and slightly desaturated editorial tonality. The interplay of light with the 'round metal-frame sunglasses' is another key detail, as the lenses are meant to 'catch and refract the key light', adding a layer of complexity and realism that prevents the accessory from looking like a flat, pasted-on element. The composition is tightly controlled with a '9:16 aspect ratio' and a 'tight head-and-shoulders' frame. The slight upward angle and off-center positioning of the subject enhance presence and add a dynamic quality to the shot. The shallow depth of field, achieved by specifying a '50mm–85mm portrait lens' at a 'wide aperture f/1.8–f/2', focuses all attention on the eyes, ensuring they are 'razor-sharp' while the background and even parts of the hair fall into a 'soft cinematic blur'. This mimics the exact behavior of professional portrait photography equipment. The styling is described with equal care: 'dark, muted jacket', 'neutral-toned shirt', and 'matte fabric textures' with 'minimal logos' create an understated, premium aesthetic that doesn't distract from the subject's face or the dramatic lighting. Elements that must not be changed are those that define the core identity and mood of the piece: the strict adherence to preserving the subject's unique physical characteristics, the directional lighting setup with its diagonal shadows, the specific color grading, and the shallow depth of field. Altering these would fundamentally change the image from a 'moody cinematic portrait' to something else entirely. However, many elements can be safely modified to create variations. For example, changing the jacket to a 'white linen shirt' would shift the feel from dramatic to more ethereal and summery. Swapping the 'deep green-gray wall' for 'concrete texture' would introduce a raw, urban edge, while a 'rainy window background' could add a layer of melancholy. Changing the expression from 'introspective' to a 'confident direct gaze' would alter the narrative from quiet contemplation to assertive presence. For practical tips to improve accuracy, using a high-resolution, well-lit reference photo is paramount. The AI's ability to preserve identity is directly tied to the quality of the input image. In tools like Midjourney, utilizing a high image weight parameter will further enforce facial likeness. A common mistake is to neglect the negative space or the specific material descriptions; ensuring the AI understands that the jacket fabric is 'matte' prevents unwanted shiny or plastic-like reflections. By meticulously describing every aspect from the lens type to the exact wall color, this prompt effectively constrains the AI's creative freedom within a very specific and desirable aesthetic, turning a simple portrait into a piece of cinematic, fashion-editorial art.

The Prompt 

Transform this image [UPLOAD YOUR IMAGE] into a 64K DSLR-quality shot resolution Ultra-hyperrealistic cinematic using a real person’s photo as the exact identity reference image, preserve the subject’s facial structure, proportions, asymmetry, skin texture, pores, and natural imperfections with no beautification or facial alteration. Create a moody, cinematic fashion-editorial portrait in a vertical 9:16 aspect ratio, framed as a tight head-and-shoulders composition. The subject’s face is positioned slightly off-center within the frame, captured from a subtle upward camera angle to enhance presence and quiet confidence.
Pose & Expression: The subject’s head is tilted back a few degrees, chin gently raised, with the face turned only minimally toward the primary light source. Shoulders remain relaxed, posture natural and unforced. Facial expression is introspective, calm, and emotionally distant, eyes relaxed  behind tinted lenses, lips slightly parted with no smile, conveying quiet contemplation and cinematic restraint.
Styling & Wardrobe: Understated, modern casual-fashion styling with editorial refinement. A dark, muted jacket with soft structure layered over a neutral-toned shirt (beige, stone, or soft gray), matte fabric textures with minimal logos or distractions. Styling feels effortless, contemporary, and premium.
Accessories: Round metal-frame sunglasses with warm, lightly tinted  lenses. The lenses catch and refract the key light, producing soft amber reflections and subtle glare without obscuring the eyes completely.
Background & Environment: Minimalist interior setting featuring a smooth wall in deep desaturated green-gray tones (#2F3A33). Hard directional light passes through unseen window blinds, casting distinct diagonal shadow patterns across the face, clothing, and background, adding graphic structure and visual tension Lighting: A single, strong natural-style key light enters from the upper-left, color temperature between 4300–4800K. Lighting creates a high-contrast chiaroscuro effect, with sharp shadow stripes, warm highlights on skin and eyewear, and deep shadow falloff on the opposite side of the face. No fill light, no rim light — contrast remains bold, dramatic, and intentional.
Camera, Optics & Depth of Field: Shot on a 50mm–85mm portrait lens, wide aperture f/1.8–f/2. Extremely shallow depth of field with eyes, eyelashes, and sunglasses in razor-sharp focus, while hair, ears, and background fall into a soft cinematic blur. Natural perspective compression, no distortion.
Texture & Atmosphere: Visible organic film grain, finely detailed skin texture, subtle micro-contrast, and a slightly dusty atmosphere catching the light for added cinematic depth. No plastic skin, no over-smoothing. Color & Grading: Professional cinematic color grading with cool green shadows, warm skin highlights, controlled saturation, deep blacks, and soft highlight roll-off. Editorial tonality inspired by high-end fashion magazines and arthouse cinema Overall Style: Modern, minimalist, high-detail cinematic composition. Ultra-realistic textures, premium fashion-editorial aesthetic, dramatic yet restrained mood, photographed like a professional fashion campaign still rather than a casual portrait. Octane render, Ultra-hyperrealistic & Unreal Engine 5.

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear, well-lit headshot of a person to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste this entire prompt. 3. Ensure the tool you're using supports image prompting (like Midjourney's /describe or Stable Diffusion's img2img). 4. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 vertical. 5. Generate the image. For different looks, try the variations below.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works because it gives the AI specific photography terms (like '50mm portrait lens', 'f/1.8', 'chiaroscuro') that create a realistic photo. Telling it to preserve 'pores' and 'natural imperfections' stops the AI from making skin look plastic. The detailed lighting and color instructions force the AI to mimic a real camera's behavior.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'moody, cinematic' to 'bright, cheerful studio lighting' for a different mood.
  • Replace 'dark muted jacket' with 'white linen shirt' or 'black leather jacket' for new styles.
  • Try 'blue hour lighting' or 'neon sign lighting' instead of the natural key light.
  • Change 'introspective expression' to 'confident direct gaze' or 'subtle smile'.
  • Swap 'deep green-gray wall' for 'concrete texture' or 'rainy window background'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What AI image generator works best for this prompt?
    This prompt works in any advanced generator that supports image prompting and detailed text, like Stable Diffusion, Midjourney V6, or DALL-E 3. Use img2img features for best results.
  • Why does my AI image look unrealistic?
    If the image looks fake, your starting photo might be low quality. Use a high-resolution, clear headshot. Also, some AI tools smooth skin by default; add keywords like 'skin texture' and 'pores' to your prompt to fix this.
  • Can I use this prompt without a reference photo?
    Yes, but results will vary. The prompt needs a reference image to copy the person's exact face. Without it, the AI will invent a random person but still follow the lighting, style, and composition instructions.
  • How do I get the dramatic shadow lines?
    The shadows come from the 'hard directional light' and 'window blinds' in the prompt. If shadows are soft, add words like 'sharp shadows', 'hard light', or 'chiaroscuro' to strengthen the effect.
