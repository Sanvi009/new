The Prompt 

A dark psychological horror scene, ame with exact same facial and physical appearance from the uploaded image in a black hoodie standing sadly in a dim room, head down. Behind me, a mirror shows his terrifying reflection screaming and pressing hands on the glass. Foggy atmosphere, cinematic moody lighting, teal dark tones, ultra-realistic, dramatic shadows, 8K cinematic look. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will create a horror scene with your likeness. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. Adjust 'teal dark tones' if you prefer different color schemes.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt combines clear visual storytelling ('mirror shows terrifying reflection screaming') with specific cinematic terms ('teal dark tones', '8K cinematic look', 'dramatic shadows'). The 9:16 aspect ratio creates vertical framing ideal for horror tension, and 'exact same facial appearance' ensures character consistency.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I use this without a reference photo?
    Yes, replace 'exact same facial appearance from uploaded image' with a description like 'young man' or 'woman with long hair'.
  • What AI tool works best for horror scenes?
    Midjourney excels at cinematic horror aesthetics, while Stable Diffusion offers more control over dark tones and details.
  • How do I make the reflection more terrifying?
    Add descriptors like 'demonic eyes', 'distorted facial features', or 'supernatural glow' to the mirror reflection part.
