Generate a cinematic, ultra-photorealistic portrait of yourself as a time traveler standing in a futuristic ruin engulfed by a sandstorm. This prompt delivers 8K quality, natural skin texture, and exact facial identity with no stylization or beautification.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to create nothing less than a cinematic masterpiece—a frame so real, so detailed, so emotionally resonant that it could be mistaken for a promotional still from a major science fiction film. The goal is to place you, exactly as you are with all your natural features and imperfections, into a world of spectacular ruin and hope. You're not playing a character in the traditional sense; you're yourself, transported into a future moment, facing the horizon with quiet determination. The expected result is an image that feels both epic and intimate, fantastical and deeply human. The structure of this prompt reveals an understanding that when you want something extraordinary from AI, you must provide extraordinary direction. It opens with a cascade of quality specifications: 'Ultra-high-end cinematic sci-fi portrait, vertical 9:16, live-action film still, ultra-photorealistic, 8K resolution.' Each term reinforces the others. 'Live-action film still' tells the AI this isn't animation or illustration. 'Ultra-photorealistic' demands the highest level of realism. '8K resolution' specifies detail density. Together, they set an unshakeable expectation of quality. The camera specification—'full-frame cinema camera aesthetic with natural 85mm lens compression and shallow depth of field'—adds technical authenticity. This isn't just any photo; it's shot with professional cinema equipment, with the lens characteristics that entails. The identity preservation section is the most comprehensive in this entire series. It doesn't just say 'keep my face.' It lists every aspect of your face that must remain unchanged: 'facial structure, identity, bone structure, skin tone, eye shape, pores, proportions.' It then adds positive requirements: 'preserve natural skin texture and imperfections.' And then it adds negative prohibitions: 'no beautification, no stylization, no face modification.' This level of specificity is necessary because AI has strong defaults toward smoothing, idealizing, and homogenizing faces. Every word here pushes back against those defaults. The pose description is remarkably precise for an AI prompt. 'Framed from mid-thigh up, body angled 25–35 degrees away from camera, weight shifted subtly to one leg, shoulders naturally tilted by wind pressure.' These aren't vague directions—they're specific enough to create consistency, but not so rigid that they prevent natural variation. The coat 'flowing sideways from strong desert wind' adds dynamic movement and sells the sandstorm environment. The expression—'mature, reflective, quietly determined'—gives emotional direction without dictating exact facial muscles. The prop description is a lesson in grounding fantasy in reality. The compass is 'open foldable pocket compass of realistic size, classic hinged design, brushed champagne-gold metal with subtle wear, fine engraved markings, glass slightly dusty but intact, angled toward him, no glow, no digital elements.' Every detail works against the AI's tendency to make props look like video game items. 'Subtle wear' suggests age and use. 'Glass slightly dusty' adds realism. 'No glow, no digital elements' ensures it stays mechanical, not magical. The environmental description—'devastated futuristic skyline partially buried in sand, collapsed skyscrapers fading into dusty atmosphere, debris silhouettes barely visible'—creates a world with history. These ruins tell a story. The sandstorm specification—'no rain, only dense wind-driven sandstorm with fine dust swirling dynamically'—prevents the AI from defaulting to rain, which is easier but wrong for this concept. The lighting description is where this prompt truly shines. 'Warm golden sunlight breaking dramatically through storm clouds behind him, creating strong rim light outlining his silhouette, volumetric light rays cutting through airborne sand, balanced cool shadows for depth, cinematic teal-tinted shadows and warm highlights while preserving accurate natural skin tones.' This is a complete lighting plan. Rim light separates you from background. Volumetric light rays make the sandstorm visible and beautiful. The teal-orange contrast creates that cinematic look while the instruction to preserve natural skin tones ensures you don't become a teal monster. The extensive negative list at the end—'no cartoon, no anime, no illustration, no painterly effect, no plastic skin, no face alteration, no glowing eyes, no fantasy armor, no superhero stance, no neon oversaturation, no extreme lens flare, no blurred face, no duplicate person, no extra limbs, no bad anatomy, no watermark, no logo, no text'—is essential. Each term blocks a specific AI failure mode. Together, they create a force field around the image, preventing it from drifting into any of the countless ways AI images can go wrong. Users can tweak this prompt by understanding its modular architecture, but caution is needed. This prompt is carefully balanced. Changing one element affects others. If you change the lighting, adjust the mood accordingly. If you change the prop, ensure it fits the 'grounded mechanical' requirement. If you change the environment, maintain the emotional tone. A common mistake would be adding fantasy elements that contradict the 'grounded sci-fi realism' brief—glowing weapons, impossible architecture, magical effects. Another mistake is using a low-quality reference photo; for ultra-realism, your reference must be sharp and clear. A third mistake is neglecting the negative instructions; they're not optional—they're essential guardrails. For the best results, approach this as a film director would. Cast yourself (your reference photo). Build the world (the environment description). Light the scene (the lighting plan). Choose the prop (the compass). Direct the performance (the expression and pose). Then let the AI execute, and refine through iteration until every element aligns. The final image should be you—exactly you—standing at the end of the world, compass in hand, facing the horizon with quiet determination, caught in a moment of cinematic perfection.

How to Use This Prompt

This is an advanced, highly detailed prompt that requires careful attention to achieve its ambitious vision. Start by uploading the clearest, most natural reference photo of your face you have—ideally one with neutral expression and even lighting that shows your natural skin texture. Copy the image link and paste it at the very beginning of your prompt field. Then add the complete prompt text. This prompt contains multiple layers of instruction: technical specifications (8K, 85mm lens, shallow depth of field), compositional details (body angle, wind effects, expression), identity preservation (exact face, no alterations, natural texture), prop description (the vintage compass), environmental setting (sandstorm, ruined city), lighting direction (golden rim light, teal shadows), and a long list of negative instructions (what NOT to do). When you run the first generation, check each element systematically. First and most importantly: is your face exactly right—same bone structure, skin texture, imperfections? If not, the prompt needs stronger identity emphasis. Then check the compass—is it mechanical, champagne-gold, angled toward you? Then the coat and wind effects, then the sandstorm and city ruins, then the lighting. This is too complex to perfect in one generation. Use the closest result and generate variations, or use it as a new reference image. You may need to break this into stages: first get the figure and environment right, then refine the compass and lighting. The --ar 9:16 is already included. Run multiple iterations, adjusting one element at a time, until every detail meets the brief.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I ensure my face remains exactly the same with all these effects?
    The prompt includes extremely detailed identity preservation language. If your face still isn't perfect, try adding 'reference image is the sole source for facial features, do not combine with other faces' and emphasize 'exact match required.' You can also use the closest image as a new reference and regenerate with stronger identity instructions.
  • What does 'teal-tinted shadows and warm highlights' achieve?
    This is a classic cinematic color grading technique called orange-and-teal. Warm highlights (on skin, rim light) contrast with cool shadows (in the environment, shaded areas), creating visual depth and emotional complexity. It makes the image feel like a professional film still.
  • Why so many negative instructions (no cartoon, no plastic skin, etc.)?
    AI generators have default tendencies—they often smooth skin, add fantasy elements, or drift toward illustration styles. The negative instructions explicitly block these defaults, forcing the AI to stay in the ultra-realistic, grounded space this prompt requires. They're essential guardrails.
