Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to solve a specific and complex visual problem: creating a cinematic portrait that shows both the subject's back and their facial reflection simultaneously. Its creative purpose is to capture a moment of modern introspection, using the mirror as a storytelling device to reveal the subject's expression and identity while maintaining the compositional intrigue of a rear view. This setup creates a layered narrative, inviting the viewer to consider the person in the scene and the person in the reflection as two parts of a whole, captured in a quiet, powerful moment. The prompt guides the AI by first establishing the non-negotiable goal of likeness with the phrase 'exact same facial features and physical appearance from uploaded image.' This is immediately reinforced with the blunt instruction 'match my face accurately. dont change anything,' which serves as a powerful guardrail against the AI's tendency to creatively reinterpret a face. This emphasis on fidelity is crucial for a personalized portrait. The core of the prompt's logic lies in the spatial relationship it describes. It asks for a 'mirror portrait... shot from behind with clear reflection.' This is a sophisticated directive that requires the AI to construct a 3D space in which the camera sees the back of a figure, but that figure is facing a mirror that reflects their front. The prompt supports this construction by detailing the pose: 'leaning forward in a powerful, introspective pose.' This action naturally brings the subject closer to the mirror, making the reflection the dominant and logical focal point of the image. The environmental details are minimal but effective. The 'modern LED-lit mirror' is not just a prop; it is the primary light source. By specifying 'soft cinematic LED lighting,' the prompt defines the quality and source of the illumination. The light comes from the mirror itself, which will create a unique, forward-facing glow on the subject's reflected face while leaving their back in relative shadow, naturally creating the 'deep shadows, high contrast' look that is requested. This is a clever way to integrate lighting and set design. The 'minimal dark background' ensures that nothing distracts from the central visual relationship between the figure, the mirror, and the reflection. The subject's styling is carefully chosen to support the 'luxury editorial look.' The 'black tailored outfit' with a 'slightly unbuttoned black shirt' and 'thin chain' creates a silhouette that is both sleek and slightly relaxed, adding a hint of texture and personality without introducing color that might distract from the interplay of light and dark. The final technical instructions for 'photorealistic skin texture, sharp focus, shallow DOF' ground the image in reality and professionalism, ensuring the reflection, where the face is visible, is rendered with the same care and detail as any standard portrait. The elements that must not be changed are the subject's identity and the fundamental structure of the scene: the mirror, the back view, and the clear reflection. The lighting logic that emanates from the LED mirror is also central to the mood and must be preserved. However, the stylistic details offer ample room for variation. The mirror itself can be changed to something like an 'antique gold frame' or a 'cracked vintage mirror,' which would alter the narrative from modern luxury to something more historical or melancholic. The outfit is a primary variable; a 'white robe' would create a completely different, softer contrast and mood, while a 'colorful silk shirt' would make the subject pop against the dark background. The subject's expression in the reflection can also be directed, with variations like a 'confident smirk' or 'surprised glance' changing the emotional core of the image. The background can be enriched with elements like a 'dimly lit room' or a 'city skyline view' to provide context and depth. For practical tips to improve accuracy, the uploaded reference photo should be a clear, front-facing shot with good lighting, as this gives the AI the best possible data to replicate in the reflection. It is also helpful to generate the image multiple times, as the AI's interpretation of the 'leaning forward' pose and the angle of reflection can vary. A common mistake is not providing a reference image, which will result in a generic person. Another is to change the lighting description to something that conflicts with the mirror as a light source, such as adding a strong overhead light, which would confuse the scene. By respecting the prompt's clever spatial logic and its unwavering demand for facial accuracy, users can consistently produce a striking and personal portrait that feels both intimate and cinematic.

The Prompt 

Ultra-realistic cinematic mirror portrait, vertical. of me with exact same facial features and physical appearance from uploaded image  in a black tailored outfit, slightly unbuttoned black shirt with thin chain, standing before a modern LED-lit mirro–≥. match my face accuratly. dont change anything Shot from behind with clear reflection, leaning forward in a powerful, introspective pose. Soft cinematic LED lighting, deep shadows, high contrast, minimal dark background. Photorealistic skin texture, sharp focus, shallow DOF, luxury editorial look. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Upload a clear photo of your face. Copy and paste the entire prompt. The AI will generate a vertical (9:16) portrait showing your back and the reflection of your face in an LED-lit mirror, with accurate facial features and the described outfit and mood.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by clearly defining a complex scene with a specific camera angle ('shot from behind'), a key prop ('modern LED-lit mirror'), and a detailed lighting setup ('soft cinematic LED lighting, deep shadows'). The instruction 'match my face accurately. dont change anything' combined with 'photorealistic skin texture' strongly guides the AI toward fidelity. The terms 'cinematic', 'luxury editorial look', and 'shallow DOF' ensure a polished, professional style rather than a casual snapshot.

Prompt Variations

  • Change the mirror style from 'modern LED-lit' to 'antique gold frame' or 'cracked vintage mirror'.
  • Alter the outfit from a 'black tailored outfit' to a 'white robe' or 'colorful silk shirt' for contrast.
  • Swap the 'introspective pose' for a 'confident smirk' or 'surprised glance' in the reflection.
  • Change the lighting color from neutral LED to 'cool blue' or 'warm golden' tones.
  • Add elements to the background like a 'dimly lit room' or 'city skyline view' instead of 'minimal dark background'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does the AI show 'from behind' but also my face?
    The prompt specifies a 'mirror portrait... shot from behind with clear reflection'. This instructs the AI to compose the image with your back to the camera, but your face and upper body are visible in the mirror's reflection in front of you.
  • Why is the instruction 'dont change anything' included?
    It's a strong reinforcement to use the uploaded reference image as closely as possible, aiming for maximum likeness and preventing the AI from drifting into generating a similar-looking but different person. It emphasizes accuracy over artistic reinterpretation.
  • What kind of chain is meant by 'thin chain'?
    It typically refers to a thin metal necklace, common in modern fashion. The AI will interpret it as a subtle jewelry detail on the neck over the unbuttoned black shirt, adding to the 'luxury editorial' aesthetic.
