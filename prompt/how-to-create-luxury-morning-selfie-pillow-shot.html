Generate an intimate, ultra-realistic selfie of yourself half-buried in a white pillow. This prompt captures a sleepy, candid morning moment with a luxurious, high-end aesthetic.

Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to capture something surprisingly difficult: an intimate, authentic moment that still looks visually stunning. Most AI portraits fall into two categories: either they look like generic stock photos, or they're so stylized they feel artificial. This prompt bridges that gap by combining the vulnerability of a waking moment with the polish of luxury aesthetics. The structure of the prompt is built around a central tension: exposure versus concealment. The subject is shirtless and in bed, which implies vulnerability. But half the face is hidden in a pillow, which adds mystery and prevents the image from feeling too revealing. This balance makes the image intriguing rather than uncomfortable. The 'half open eyes looking directly into the camera' are crucial here—they create connection despite the partial concealment, drawing the viewer in. Identity preservation is the technical heart of this prompt. AI image generators, when left to their own devices, will create a generic 'attractive person' based on their training data. By repeatedly anchoring the generation to your uploaded photo, you're fighting against that default behavior. The phrasing 'exact same facial and physical identity' is stronger than just 'same face' because it includes body type, skin tone, and other physical characteristics. This is especially important for a 'shirtless' shot, where body identity matters as much as facial features. The choice of props and environment is deliberately minimal but highly specified. The pillow isn't just a pillow—it's 'luxurious white.' The wall isn't just a wall—it's 'white, expensive, luxurious.' This repetition of 'white' and 'luxurious' creates a cohesive visual theme. The AI understands it should render textures that suggest quality: high thread-count pillowcases, smooth painted walls, subtle shadows that imply depth and space. This minimalism also serves a practical purpose: fewer elements mean fewer chances for the AI to generate distracting or nonsensical details. Lighting is where this prompt really shines. 'Soft, diffused natural morning light' is a specific instruction that does several things. 'Morning light' implies a low sun angle, which creates gentle shadows rather than harsh overhead light. 'Soft' and 'diffused' tell the AI to avoid direct sunlight streaming in, instead suggesting light filtered through curtains or clouds. This creates a flattering, even illumination on skin while still maintaining a sense of naturalism. The result is skin that looks like skin—pores, texture, and all—rather than the waxy, airbrushed look that plagues many AI portraits. The technical finish—'ultra realistic, 8K high resolution'—is the final piece. These terms push the AI toward higher detail generation. When combined with 'candid smartphone photography style,' they create an interesting paradox. Smartphone photos aren't typically 8K, but the AI understands this as 'technically perfect quality with the compositional spontaneity of a real selfie.' This means you get the sharpness of a high-end camera with the authenticity of a casual moment. Common mistakes when using this prompt include using a poor reference photo. If your uploaded image is blurry or poorly lit, the AI has nothing good to work with. Another mistake is removing the 'candid' instruction, which often results in stiff, posed images that look like they belong in a catalog rather than a personal moment. To improve results further, users can experiment with adding specific emotional cues like 'peaceful' or 'just woke up,' or adjust the pillow position by being even more descriptive about what part of the face remains visible. The 9:16 aspect ratio is also non-negotiable for this intimate, vertical composition—changing it would fundamentally alter the feeling of the shot.

The Prompt 

A close up selfie of me with exact same facial and physical identity from the uploaded image, shirtless, with a sleepy and relaxed expression. my half open eyes look directly into the camera. The lower half of my face is buried in a luxurious white pillow, and my left bare shoulder is slightly visible. The background is a white, expensive, luxurious bedroom wall. Soft, diffused natural morning light, candid smartphone photography style, ultra realistic, 8K high resolution. Use the provided image to create an image of me. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Begin by uploading a clear, front-facing photo of yourself. This reference image is crucial because the prompt explicitly demands the AI maintain your 'exact same facial and physical identity.' A good reference photo should have even lighting and show your face clearly without obstructions. Once uploaded, paste the entire prompt into the text field. Notice how the prompt structures the scene: it starts with your identity, then your pose, then the pillow, then the background, and finally the lighting and technical specs. This hierarchy helps the AI understand what matters most. The phrase 'Use the provided image to create an image of me' at the end reinforces the identity instruction. The pose is very specific: 'half open eyes look directly into the camera' and 'lower half of my face is buried in a luxurious white pillow.' If the first generation doesn't capture this exact positioning, you can regenerate or slightly adjust the wording to 'face resting on pillow' or 'cheek pressed into pillow' to guide the AI. Pay attention to the lighting description. 'Soft, diffused natural morning light' tells the AI to avoid harsh shadows. If your results look too dark or too bright, you can modify this to 'soft window light from the left' to control the direction. Finally, the 'aspect ratio 9:16' ensures the image is vertical, perfect for smartphone screens or social media stories. Run the generation multiple times to get the most natural, candid expression.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt succeeds because it solves one of the hardest problems in AI image generation: identity consistency. By repeatedly stating 'me with exact same facial and physical identity' and ending with 'Use the provided image to create an image of me,' it creates multiple anchor points that force the AI to prioritize your reference photo over its generic understanding of a face. The prompt also excels at creating a specific mood through contrast. It pairs a vulnerable, sleepy pose ('half open eyes,' 'face buried in pillow') with luxurious elements ('expensive, luxurious bedroom wall,' 'white pillow'). This combination creates an intimate yet aspirational feel, like a candid moment captured in a high-end lifestyle shoot. The level of detail in the pose prevents the AI from defaulting to a standard portrait. Instead of 'lying in bed,' it specifies exactly how much of the face is visible, where the shoulder is, and the direction of the gaze. This precision gives the AI a clear blueprint to follow, reducing the randomness of the output. The technical specifications are also perfectly balanced. 'Candid smartphone photography style' tells the AI to avoid overly posed, studio-lit looks. It should look like a real morning selfie. Yet 'ultra realistic, 8K high resolution' ensures that despite the casual style, the image quality is pristine. This blend of casual authenticity with technical perfection is what makes the image feel both real and aspirational. Finally, the color palette is intentionally minimal: white pillow, white wall, skin tones. By limiting the colors, the prompt reduces the variables the AI has to manage, allowing it to focus on texture, lighting, and facial details. The soft morning light then adds warmth and dimension to this monochrome scene, preventing it from looking flat or clinical.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does the prompt repeat the instruction about using my identity?
    Repetition reinforces priority. AI models weigh words based on position and frequency. Stating your identity at both the beginning and end ensures the AI consistently references your uploaded photo throughout the generation process.
  • The pillow keeps covering too much of my face. How do I fix this?
    Adjust the prompt to specify 'only the lower half of my face is buried' or 'tip of nose visible above pillow.' You can also try 'cheek resting on pillow' for a different composition that shows more of the face.
  • What does 'candid smartphone photography style' actually do?
    It tells the AI to avoid the perfectly lit, posed look of a studio portrait. It encourages slight imperfections like natural skin texture, softer focus, and a more spontaneous framing that mimics how people actually take selfies.
