How to Use This Prompt

Copy the prompt into your AI image generator (like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or DALL·E). For best results, use an image of your face for reference in tools that support it. The prompt will generate a hyper-realistic scene of a person with an alligator in an urban setting.

This prompt works by combining specific, realistic details: a clear subject (man with alligator), detailed clothing (brown hoodie, ripped jeans), a strong setting (graffiti parking lot), and professional photography terms (cinematic lighting, low angle shot, 8K). These detailed instructions guide the AI to create a coherent, high-detail image with a specific mood and style.
