FAQ

  • How do I use my own face in this AI prompt?
    Use the 'image reference' feature in your AI tool. Upload a clear photo of your face, then use this prompt, ensuring it includes phrases like 'of me from the uploaded image'.
  • Which AI image generator is best for this prompt?
    Midjourney and DALL-E 3 are excellent for realistic character scenes. Stable Diffusion with a photorealistic model checkpoint is also highly effective for this gritty, detailed style.
  • How can I make the alligator look more realistic?
    The prompt already includes key details like 'realistic textures' and 'high detail'. For even better results, you can add terms like 'detailed scales', 'wet reptilian skin', or 'sharp claws'.
