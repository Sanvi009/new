How to Use

To recreate this dreamy sleepcore aesthetic, load the prompt into a capable AI image generator like Midjourney or Gemini. FAQ

  • How can I change the text that appears in the image?
    You can easily change the text by replacing the phrase 'LAUNDRY NAP' in the prompt with your desired words. To ensure the AI generates the text clearly, keep the typography descriptions like 'rounded, bubbly, cute' right next to your new text in the prompt.
  • Why are the floating stickers blending into the background?
    If the stickers lack definition, it usually means the AI is blending them into the lighting. Add keywords like 'high contrast stickers,' 'sharp outlines,' or '2D graphic overlays' to force the generator to separate the floating graphics from the realistic 3D environment.
  • Can I make the scene look less pastel and more dark and moody?
    Yes. To shift away from the pastel aesthetic, remove keywords like 'pastel color grading' and 'gentle glow.' Replace them with terms like 'deep shadows,' 'moody cinematic lighting,' and 'rich jewel tones' while keeping the core sleeping pose intact.
