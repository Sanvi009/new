Prompt Explanation

This prompt is meticulously constructed to generate a cinematic, narrative-driven portrait that places the subject directly into a specific lifestyle scene: that of a stylish, rebellious biker. Its creative purpose is to create a character study, blending fashion photography with a gritty, urban atmosphere to evoke a sense of attitude and cool confidence. The prompt aims to produce an image that feels like a still from an independent film, where the subject's personality is conveyed through their attire, their environment, and the overall moody color palette. It's about crafting a visual story, not just a simple portrait. The prompt_text guides the AI output by layering specific details across several key areas: subject, action, attire, setting, and photographic style. It begins with a clear directive: 'Cinematic full-body portrait me with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image as biker confidently riding on a black cruiser motorcycle.' This establishes the non-negotiable coreâ€”the subject's identity is to be preserved from a reference image, they are to be shown in a full-body shot, and they are actively engaged in the defining action of riding a specific type of motorcycle. This level of specificity is crucial; it prevents the AI from generating a static, posed shot and pushes it towards a dynamic, in-scene capture. The description of the attire is incredibly detailed: 'dark oversized T-shirt, loose wide-leg denim pants, patterned white sneakers, minimal jewelry, edgy street style aesthetic.' Each item is chosen to contribute to the 'edgy street style' look. The 'dark oversized T-shirt' and 'loose wide-leg denim pants' create a relaxed, contemporary silhouette, while the 'patterned white sneakers' add a pop of visual interest and detail. Specifying 'minimal jewelry' prevents the look from becoming overly accessorized, keeping it authentically street-style. This precise clothing guide ensures the AI generates a coherent and fashionable outfit that aligns with the prompt's overall aesthetic. The setting and photographic style are equally well-defined. 'Urban cafe background visible through glass window with faint typography, city view slightly blurred' places the action in a specific, relatable location while using depth of field to keep the focus on the subject. The 'faint typography' adds a layer of realistic urban detail. The core mood is then locked in with the color and texture directives: 'Moody teal-gray color grading, gritty textures, soft natural light, high-contrast shadows, rebellious attitude, 35mm film photography.' This combination of terms is powerful. 'Teal-gray color grading' gives the image a distinct, modern cinematic color palette. 'Gritty textures' and '35mm film photography' introduce a sense of realism and analog authenticity, moving it away from a clean, digital look. The juxtaposition of 'soft natural light' and 'high-contrast shadows' creates a dramatic yet believable lighting scenario, perfectly suited for conveying the 'rebellious attitude' the prompt seeks. In this prompt, the core concept and subject are fixed. The user's identity must be preserved, and they must be depicted as a biker confidently riding a motorcycle. Altering this fundamental premise would result in a different scene entirely. The specified 'teal-gray color grading' and '35mm film photography' are also central to the intended mood. Changing the color grading to something else, like 'desaturated vintage,' would significantly alter the image's emotional tone. However, many elements are designed for variation. As suggested, the motorcycle can be swapped for a 'sport bike' or 'vintage cafe racer,' changing the character's subculture. The urban cafe background can be replaced with a 'rain-wet city street at night' for a more noir-ish feel or a 'desert highway' for a sense of open-road freedom. The pose can shift from riding to 'leaning against the motorcycle,' creating a more static, portrait-like image. The attire is also highly adaptable; changing the 'dark oversized T-shirt' to a 'leather jacket' would immediately evoke a classic, more traditional biker archetype. For improved accuracy, using a high-resolution reference photo with good lighting is paramount, especially for preserving facial features in a dynamic, full-body shot. It's also helpful to specify the desired gender in the prompt if the reference image is ambiguous, even with the 'do according to my gender' instruction. A common mistake is generating an image where the face is blurred or unrecognizable due to the motion implied by 'riding.' To mitigate this, one might add a phrase like 'sharp focus on face' if the initial results are too blurry. Another mistake is the background details overpowering the subject. The prompt's use of 'slightly blurred' helps, but iterating with different emphasis on the background blur can refine the result. Overall, this prompt's strength lies in its comprehensive, layered approach, building a complete and compelling visual narrative that transforms a simple portrait into a cinematic character study, perfectly capturing the essence of street style and rebellious attitude within a moody, photographic frame.

The Prompt 

Cinematic full-body portrait me with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image as biker confidently riding on a black cruiser motorcycle. Attire: dark oversized T-shirt , loose wide-leg denim pants, patterned white sneakers, minimal jewelry, edgy street style aesthetic. Urban cafe background visible through glass window with faint typography, city view slightly blurred. Moody teal-gray color grading, gritty textures, soft natural light, high-contrast shadows, rebellious attitude, 35mm film photography. do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear photo of yourself to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the entire prompt. 3. The prompt will adapt to your gender based on your uploaded image; you can also add 'male' or 'female' explicitly. 4. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 for a vertical mobile-friendly portrait. 5. Generate and iterate if needed to refine details like the clothing or background blur.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt provides a complete character scene with specific, layered details that AI image models understand. It defines the subject (you as a biker), their precise attire, the action (riding), the setting (urban cafe), and the photographic style (35mm, teal-gray, gritty). Keywords like 'cinematic', 'high-contrast shadows', and 'soft natural light' are strong stylistic triggers for generating a photorealistic image with a specific mood.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'teal-gray color grading' to 'orange and teal' or 'desaturated vintage' for a different cinematic look.
  • Swap the 'urban cafe background' for a 'rain-wet city street at night' or 'desert highway'.
  • Replace the 'black cruiser motorcycle' with a 'sport bike' or 'vintage cafe racer'.
  • Try a different pose like 'leaning against the motorcycle' or 'standing next to it'.
  • Change the attire to a 'leather jacket', 'hoodie', or 'graphic tee' to shift the street style.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I make the AI use my face in this biker portrait?
    Use the image upload feature in your AI tool (like Midjourney's /describe or DALL-E's upload). Provide a clear front-facing photo, then use this prompt to generate the scene with your likeness.
  • What does '35mm film photography' do in the AI prompt?
    It tells the AI to mimic the aesthetic of photos taken with classic film cameras, including natural grain, specific color rendition, and a slightly imperfect, authentic look.
  • Can I use this prompt for a female character?
    Yes. The prompt instructs the AI to 'do according to my gender' based on your uploaded reference. If you don't use a reference, simply add 'female biker' or 'woman' to the beginning of the description.
