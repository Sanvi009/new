FAQ

  • What is cel-shading?
    Cel-shading is a rendering style that makes 3D graphics appear flat like a comic book or cartoon. It uses hard, solid colors without gradients to create a bold, graphic look.
  • What are halftone details?
    Halftone is a printing technique that uses dots to create gradients and shading. In illustrations, it adds a vintage comic book or pop art texture.
  • How do I preserve my exact facial identity?
    Use AI tools with strong face preservation features. Midjourney's --cref parameter, Stable Diffusion with IP-Adapter Face ID, or DALL-E 3 with reference images work best. The prompt explicitly requires exact identity.
