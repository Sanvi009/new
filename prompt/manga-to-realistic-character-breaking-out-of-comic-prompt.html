How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload your reference photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. Replace [PERSON AS PHOTO REFERENCE] with a description like 'young woman' or 'athletic man'. 4. Generate the image. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3.

Why This Prompt Works
