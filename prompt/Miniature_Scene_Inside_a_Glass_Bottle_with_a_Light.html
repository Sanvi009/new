How to use this prompt

To use this prompt professionally, employ it for miniature art, decorative items, or whimsical photography in crafts and gifts. Preserve "miniature scene inside a transparent glass bottle," "tiny light bulb," "miniature figure of a cute girl," and "handheld art piece" for surprise. Do not change the cork lid or selfie pose. Optionally vary activity; for example, "reading book" could yield studious visuals, resulting in detailed rocks, while Starbucks keeps casual, creative outcomes.

Prompt

A miniature scene inside a transparent glass bottle with a cork lid. At the top of the bottle, there's a small light bulb emitting a glow. Beneath the light, a miniature figure of a cute girl is seated on a pile of rocks. The person appears to be having selfie and drinking Starbucks. The bottle is being held by a hand, suggesting it's a handheld art piece or decorative item.

Why this prompt works

This prompt is highly effective for a whimsical miniature scene that tells a story inside a glass bottle the small light bulb and figure creating a selfie and drinking Starbucks add a touch of modern life and nostalgia the hand holding the bottle provides scale and context making the scene feel intimate and handmade this image is perfect for art prints social media content or promotional materials that aim to evoke curiosity and charm.

Prompt Variations

  • Light Source: Swap “small light bulb” → “a glowing LED crystal” for a fantasy element. Swap “light bulb” → “a tiny candle” for a rustic, warm glow.
  • Miniature Activity: Swap “having selfie and drinking Starbucks” → “reading a tiny book”. Swap the activity → “painting a miniature landscape”.
  • Bottle Content: Swap “a pile of rocks” → “a tiny armchair and rug” for a cozy interior. Add “tiny plants growing inside the bottle”.
  • Holder: Swap “a hand” → “delicate metal stand” as if it’s a museum piece.
