Grunge Mirror Selfie AI Prompt: Scrapbook Style with Chalk Doodles

AI Image Prompts 2026-02-07 Brainloom

An AI image prompt that transforms your mirror selfie into grunge scrapbook art with chalk doodles, film grain, and dreamy aesthetic effects.

Generated Images

The Prompt

Transform this image into a detailed aesthetic mirror selfie artwork with oft grunge+dreamy scrapbook vibe.
Keep the subject exactly the same: same face, skin tone, expression, hair, outfit, pose, and phone.
Darken and slightly desaturate the background to reduce brightness while keeping store details visible.
Add depth by applying subtle vignette and film grain to the background only.
Surround the subject with multiple layered hand-drawn chalk doodles and scrapbook elements:

How to Use

1. Upload your mirror selfie photo to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste the prompt text. 3. The AI will add grunge scrapbook effects while keeping your face unchanged. 4. Works in Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3 for artistic edits.

FAQ

  • Can I use any photo or only mirror selfies?
    Works best with mirror selfies showing a phone, but you can use any portrait. The 'mirror selfie' part helps the AI understand the composition.
  • How do I change the doodle style?
    Replace 'hand-drawn chalk doodles' with 'ink sketches', 'pencil drawings', 'digital graphics', or 'watercolor paintings' for different effects.
  • What if I want more color?
    Remove 'slightly desaturate the background' or change to 'vibrant colorful background' while keeping the grunge scrapbook elements.
