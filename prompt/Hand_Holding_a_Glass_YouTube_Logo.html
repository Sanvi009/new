The Prompt 

Create a photorealistic, portrait-oriented image showing a human hand holding a 3D-printed glass object version of the youtube logo, rendered in the brand's original vibrant red color. The logo must be held at a noticeable angle - slightly rotated or tilted (not upright) - to clearly show its depth, thickness, and transparent colored glass material, as if it's a solid, premium-quality physical object. The hand should hold it naturally using two or three fingers, possibly with a slight twist in the wrist. The logo must preserve its exact shape, proportions, and vibrant red hue no distortion - with a polished, clean surface finish.

Lighting:

Use soft natural daylight from one side to cast realistic reflections and subtle refractions through the red glass. The translucent surface should glow softly, with internal light scatter that enhances the logo's curves and waveform design. Reflected light should create faint red highlights on the fingertips and surrounding skin for added realism.

Background:

Set the background to a softly blurred modern cityscape in daylight. Include subtle architectural shapes and sky tones with bokeh, but ensure all background elements remain out of focus. This keeps the attention on the detailed glass logo and hand in the foreground, creating a professional, editorial feel.

Rendering Style:

Rendered in 4K photorealistic quality using Octane Render, Redshift, or Blender Cycles. Use a macro lens effect, with shallow depth of field, soft bloom, and realistic material shaders. The glass must have accurate refraction, faint beveling, and clear edge light. The hand should show natural pores, soft shadows, and micro-details to complete the realism. Vertical composition optimized for product showcase.

Conceptual meaning:

A sleek and tactile reinterpretation of the youtube brand blending transparency, sound, and design into a minimalist, touchable object that symbolizes modern digital presence.

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, leverage it for logo physicalizations, brand showcases, or digital presence art in media and tech. Preserve "photorealistic, portrait-oriented image," "human hand holding a 3D-printed glass object version of the youtube logo," "4K photorealistic quality," and "vertical composition" for realism. Do not modify the tilt angle or shallow depth. Changing logo to "Instagram" could produce social visuals, leading to refractions, while YouTube keeps red, tactile results.
