Dreamy Blurred Portrait AI Prompt with Motion Effect | Brainloom

Artistic

This prompt creates a moody, cinematic portrait with intentional motion blur and a teal color grade. It transforms your photo into a dreamy, analog-style image with a hoodie, soft haze, and film grain.

Generated Images

The Prompt 

cinematic blurred portrait, me as subject with exact same facial and physical features from the uploaded image wearing light hoodie, hood up, neutral background, teal green color grading, warm light leak on left side, face partially smeared with lateral motion blur, double exposure blur effect, soft haze, low contrast, shallow depth of field, film grain, moody atmosphere, melancholic expression, minimal composition, dreamy realism, analog photography feel, subtle vignette . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear photo of yourself to your AI image generator. 2. Copy and paste this entire prompt. 3. Set the aspect ratio to 9:16 vertical. 4. Generate the image. This works well in AI tools that support artistic styles and image prompting, like Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, or Leonardo.AI.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt works by using specific photographic defect terms like 'motion blur', 'light leak', 'film grain', and 'low contrast' to force the AI away from a clean, digital look. The 'analog photography feel' instruction cues the AI to mimic the imperfections of film, creating a dreamy and emotional atmosphere.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'teal green color grading' to 'warm amber' or 'cool blue' for a different mood.
  • Replace 'light hoodie' with 'denim jacket' or 'oversized sweater'.
  • Try 'happy contemplative expression' instead of 'melancholic'.
  • Swap 'motion blur' for 'lens flare' or 'rain streaks' for other effects.
  • Change 'neutral background' to 'city lights at night' or 'forest backdrop'.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I get the motion blur effect on the face in AI?
    Use the exact phrases 'face partially smeared with lateral motion blur' and 'double exposure blur effect'. If the blur is too strong or weak, adjust by adding 'slight motion blur' or 'heavy motion blur' to your prompt.
  • What AI generator is best for this artistic, blurry style?
    Midjourney and Stable Diffusion are excellent for this because they handle stylistic keywords like 'film grain' and 'dreamy realism' very well. DALL-E 3 can also create it but may produce less pronounced blur effects by default.
  • Can I use this prompt without a hoodie?
    Yes. Simply change 'wearing light hoodie, hood up' to your desired clothing, like 'wearing a t-shirt' or 'wearing a beanie'. The prompt will still apply the blur and color effects.
  • Why does my image look too sharp and digital?
    The AI might be prioritizing 'detailed' keywords. Emphasize the style words by putting them first: start your prompt with 'cinematic blurred portrait, soft haze, low contrast, film grain...' to give them more weight.
