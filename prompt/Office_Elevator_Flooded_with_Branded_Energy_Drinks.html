Prompt Explanation

This prompt is a template for creating a hyperrealistic, surreal advertising image that visually exaggerates a brand's presence to an absurd, memorable degree. Its creative purpose is to generate viral-worthy, conceptual art for marketing campaigns, social media, or editorial content by placing a massive quantity of a branded product in an unexpected, everyday location. The prompt_text functions as a fill-in-the-blank script, guiding the AI to construct a specific, high-impact scene. It dictates the core concept: stylish office elevator doors, slightly ajar, revealing an interior flooded with a specific product. The inclusion of placeholders like [x:y] for aspect ratio and [uploaded image] for the product makes it a versatile tool for different brands and campaigns. The technical directives are crucial for achieving the desired professional look. The "dynamic low-angle perspective" makes the flood of products appear more imposing and chaotic, emphasizing the overwhelming quantity. "Dramatic studio lighting creating sharp contrasts and deep shadows" ensures the image has a polished, commercial photography feel, preventing it from looking like a simple digital render. The instruction to focus the color palette on the brand's signature hues with metallic accents is a key branding element; for Red Bull, this would mean its characteristic blues and silvers, reinforcing brand recognition even in a surreal context. The demand for an "ultra-detailed, photorealistic style with crisp textures and vibrant colors" pushes the AI towards a final output that is visually arresting and suitable for high-quality print or digital display. The elements that must not be changed are the foundational pillars of the concept: the hyperrealistic poster format, the slightly ajar elevator doors revealing a flooded interior, the dramatic studio lighting, and the photorealistic rendering style. Changing the perspective or the lighting would fundamentally alter the image's visual impact and professional sheen. However, every other element is designed to be a variable. The product itself ([uploaded image]), the brand ([Red Bull]), and the environment (sleek, modern office) are all open to substitution. Swapping Red Bull for Nike and the generic uploaded image for sneakers transforms the ad from an energy drink promotion to a sportswear fantasy. Changing the office environment to a sports stadium locker room further reinforces this new theme. Adding a terrified office worker, as suggested in the variations, introduces a human element and scale, making the flood even more impactful and humorous. To improve accuracy, users should provide a high-quality source image of the product they want to flood the elevator with, as the AI will use this to replicate its shape, texture, and logo placement. A common mistake is not specifying the desired quantity clearly; using phrases like "overwhelming amount" is good, but adding "thousands of" or "countless" can reinforce the sense of a deluge. Another pitfall is forgetting to specify the aspect ratio by replacing the [x:y] placeholder, which can result in an oddly cropped final image. By strategically filling in the placeholders while preserving the core visual directives, this prompt consistently produces striking, memorable brand advertising that captures attention through its sheer, surreal excess.

The Prompt 

Hyperrealistic [x:y] poster depicting stylish office elevator doors slightly ajar, revealing an interior completely flooded with an overwhelming amount of [uploaded image], each prominently featuring the logo and brand name of [Red Bull]. The scene is captured from a dynamic low-angle perspective, with dramatic studio lighting creating sharp contrasts and deep shadows. The background blends seamlessly into a sleek, modern office environment, while the color palette focuses on the brand's signature hues with metallic accents. Rendered in ultra-detailed, photorealistic style with crisp textures and vibrant colors for maximum visual impact. 4:5

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, incorporate it into brand advertising, surreal marketing, or office-themed promotions for energy drinks like Red Bull. Use tools with uploaded images, replacing "[x:y]" and "[uploaded image]" with "4:5" and "energy cans," keeping "hyperrealistic [x:y] poster," "office elevator doors slightly ajar," "dramatic studio lighting," and "photorealistic style" for visual impact. Do not modify the low-angle perspective or color palette. Changing "[Red Bull]" to "Monster" could produce rival brand visuals, leading to flooded interiors, whereas original yields logo-heavy chaos with sharp, contrasting results.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt will work because it creates a surreal and humorous scene flooded with branded objects which is visually striking and memorable the dynamic low-angle perspective and dramatic lighting enhance the impact making the image feel overwhelming and chaotic yet polished the focus on brand colors and metallic accents ensures consistency and recognition this concept is perfect for viral marketing campaigns social media ads or editorial content that aims to playfully exaggerate brand presence in everyday life.

Prompt Variations

  • Flooding Object: Swap “[uploaded image]” → “a specific product, e.g., sneakers” (implies “[object]” is sneakers). Swap “[uploaded image]” → “burgers” for a food-themed flood.
  • Brand: Swap “[Red Bull]” → “[Nike]” for a different brand. Swap “[Red Bull]” → “[Heineken]” for a beverage brand.
  • Environment: Swap “sleek, modern office environment” → “a luxurious penthouse apartment” . Swap “office environment” → “a sports stadium locker room”.
  • Perspective: Swap “dynamic low-angle perspective” → “a eye-level perspective looking directly into the elevator”. Add “a terrified office worker visible inside the elevator” for scale and story.
