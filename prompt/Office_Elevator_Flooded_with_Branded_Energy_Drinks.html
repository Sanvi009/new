The Prompt 

Hyperrealistic [x:y] poster depicting stylish office elevator doors slightly ajar, revealing an interior completely flooded with an overwhelming amount of [uploaded image], each prominently featuring the logo and brand name of [Red Bull]. The scene is captured from a dynamic low-angle perspective, with dramatic studio lighting creating sharp contrasts and deep shadows. The background blends seamlessly into a sleek, modern office environment, while the color palette focuses on the brand's signature hues with metallic accents. Rendered in ultra-detailed, photorealistic style with crisp textures and vibrant colors for maximum visual impact. 4:5

How to Use This Prompt

To use this prompt professionally, incorporate it into brand advertising, surreal marketing, or office-themed promotions for energy drinks like Red Bull. Use tools with uploaded images, replacing "[x:y]" and "[uploaded image]" with "4:5" and "energy cans," keeping "hyperrealistic [x:y] poster," "office elevator doors slightly ajar," "dramatic studio lighting," and "photorealistic style" for visual impact. Do not modify the low-angle perspective or color palette. Changing "[Red Bull]" to "Monster" could produce rival brand visuals, leading to flooded interiors, whereas original yields logo-heavy chaos with sharp, contrasting results.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt will work because it creates a surreal and humorous scene flooded with branded objects which is visually striking and memorable the dynamic low-angle perspective and dramatic lighting enhance the impact making the image feel overwhelming and chaotic yet polished the focus on brand colors and metallic accents ensures consistency and recognition this concept is perfect for viral marketing campaigns social media ads or editorial content that aims to playfully exaggerate brand presence in everyday life.

Prompt Variations

  • Flooding Object: Swap “[uploaded image]” → “a specific product, e.g., sneakers” (implies “[object]” is sneakers). Swap “[uploaded image]” → “burgers” for a food-themed flood.
  • Brand: Swap “[Red Bull]” → “[Nike]” for a different brand. Swap “[Red Bull]” → “[Heineken]” for a beverage brand.
  • Environment: Swap “sleek, modern office environment” → “a luxurious penthouse apartment” . Swap “office environment” → “a sports stadium locker room”.
  • Perspective: Swap “dynamic low-angle perspective” → “a eye-level perspective looking directly into the elevator”. Add “a terrified office worker visible inside the elevator” for scale and story.
