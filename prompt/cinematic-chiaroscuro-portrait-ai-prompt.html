The Prompt 

Medium, centered portrait shot using a 35mm lens from a slightly low angle. me with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image , visible natural imperfections, and neutral ethnicity. i stands still with an intense, inward-focused gaze. Wearing dark, textured clothing that absorbs light. Black ash-like particulate matter drifts from deep shadow into the light around his face and body. Lit by a strong directional key light cutting through darkness, creating high-contrast chiaroscuro with controlled falloff into shadow. Set in an underground parking garage ramp with curved concrete geometry. Cold, muted color palette with subtle grain and atmospheric haze. Mad-real surreal cinematic portrait, ARRI Alexa-style grading matched to the environment, emphasizing pores and skin texture. FG grain present  do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy and paste this prompt into your AI image generator. For best results, upload your reference photo when the tool allows it. Adjust 'me' to 'man' or 'woman' based on your subject's gender. The prompt will create a vertical portrait (9:16 ratio) with dramatic lighting and atmospheric effects.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt uses specific cinematography terms (chiaroscuro, 35mm lens, ARRI Alexa) that AI models recognize as visual styles. Concrete details like 'underground parking garage ramp' and 'black ash-like particulate matter' give the AI clear elements to render. Lighting instructions ('strong directional key light') create consistent high-contrast results across different generators.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'underground parking garage' to 'industrial warehouse' or 'abandoned subway tunnel'
  • Replace 'ash particles' with 'falling snow' or 'floating dust motes'
  • Try 'warm golden hour light' instead of 'cold muted palette'
  • Swap 'intense inward gaze' for 'direct eye contact with viewer'
  • Change clothing to 'light-colored linen' to alter contrast

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is chiaroscuro lighting in AI prompts?
    Chiaroscuro means strong contrast between light and dark areas. In AI prompts, it creates dramatic, moody portraits with deep shadows and bright highlights.
  • Which AI image generators work best with this prompt?
    This prompt works well with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. All understand cinematography terms like 'ARRI Alexa-style' and '35mm lens.'
  • How do I get the ash particles to look realistic?
    Use the exact phrase 'black ash-like particulate matter drifts from deep shadow into the light.' AI models recognize this as floating atmospheric elements.
  • Can I use this prompt without a reference photo?
    Yes, but the 'me' reference works best with an uploaded image. Without one, change 'me' to 'a man' or 'a woman' for a generic portrait.
