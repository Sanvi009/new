Prompt Explanation

This prompt is designed to generate a dramatic, high-contrast cinematic portrait that places the subject in a specific atmospheric environment, an underground parking garage ramp, to create a mood of intense introspection and surreal realism. The creative purpose is to merge the classic painting technique of chiaroscuro, which plays with extreme contrasts of light and shadow, with modern cinematography to produce an image that feels both timeless and technologically advanced. It aims to capture not just a likeness but a feeling, using environmental elements like drifting ash and curved concrete geometry to frame the subject in a narrative of solitude and quiet power. The prompt guides the AI by breaking down the image into its core components: the subject's exact features from an uploaded image, the precise lighting conditions, the atmospheric details, and the overall color and texture grading. The instruction for a 'medium, centered portrait shot using a 35mm lens from a slightly low angle' establishes the foundational composition, ensuring the subject is the focal point while the low angle adds a subtle sense of grandeur or intensity. The mention of 'visible natural imperfections' and 'skin texture' is crucial for steering the AI away from the overly smooth, plastic-like renderings that can plague AI-generated portraits, pushing it toward photorealism and authenticity. The phrase 'dark, textured clothing that absorbs light' works in tandem with the lighting setup to prevent the subject from becoming a distracting reflective surface, keeping the viewer's focus on the face and the interplay of light and shadow across it. The lighting description is the heart of this prompt. By specifying a 'strong directional key light cutting through darkness, creating high-contrast chiaroscuro with controlled falloff into shadow,' it gives the AI a very clear technical directive. This is not flat, even lighting; it is sculptural light that defines form through shadow. The 'controlled falloff' ensures the transition from light to dark is intentional and smooth, adding depth. The environment of the 'underground parking garage ramp with curved concrete geometry' provides a perfect setting for this light to play against, as the curved surfaces will catch the light in interesting ways, reinforcing the three-dimensionality of the scene. The 'black ash-like particulate matter' is a stroke of genius in prompt engineering. It adds a tangible, physical quality to the air, giving the light something to cut through and making the scene feel alive and textured. It moves the image from a simple portrait to a moment captured within a specific, slightly dystopian atmosphere. The color palette is strictly defined as 'cold, muted' with 'subtle grain and atmospheric haze,' which, combined with the 'ARRI Alexa-style grading,' tells the AI to emulate the look of high-end digital cinema, which is known for its dynamic range and color science. This, along with the instruction to emphasize pores and skin texture, ensures the final image has a professional, editorial quality rather than a digital art aesthetic. The elements that must not be changed are the core identity of the subject, as the prompt explicitly states to match the facial and physical features from the uploaded image without alteration. The fundamental lighting concept and the presence of the ash particles are also central to the image's mood and should be preserved. However, several elements can be safely modified to explore different creative directions. The location can be swapped for other industrial or abandoned spaces like an 'industrial warehouse' or 'abandoned subway tunnel,' which would change the geometry and reflective qualities of the environment. Replacing 'ash particles' with 'falling snow' or 'floating dust motes' would alter the atmosphere from gritty to ethereal or melancholic. The color palette is a primary variable; shifting from 'cold, muted' to 'warm golden hour light' would completely transform the emotional tone from isolation to nostalgia or even hope. The subject's pose and gaze can also be adjusted, with variations like 'direct eye contact with viewer' creating a more confrontational and engaging piece. Changing the subject's clothing to something like 'light-colored linen' would dramatically alter the contrast, making the subject stand out more against the dark background but potentially losing the light-absorbing quality that deepens the shadows on the figure. For practical tips to improve accuracy, it is essential to use a high-quality, well-lit reference photo where the face is clear and unobstructed. When generating, it can be helpful to run the prompt multiple times to see how the AI interprets the 'ash' and 'grain,' as these elements can sometimes vary. A common mistake is not including a reference image and relying on the 'me' pronoun, which will lead to a generic figure. Another is over-correcting the prompt by adding too many conflicting lighting terms, which can confuse the AI. It is also a mistake to ignore the gender instruction; explicitly stating 'do according to my gender' is vital for accurate representation. By adhering to the prompt's specific technical language and respecting its core visual pillars, users can consistently produce a powerful, cinematic chiaroscuro portrait that feels both personal and professionally crafted.

The Prompt 

Medium, centered portrait shot using a 35mm lens from a slightly low angle. me with exact same facial and physical features from uploaded image , visible natural imperfections, and neutral ethnicity. i stands still with an intense, inward-focused gaze. Wearing dark, textured clothing that absorbs light. Black ash-like particulate matter drifts from deep shadow into the light around his face and body. Lit by a strong directional key light cutting through darkness, creating high-contrast chiaroscuro with controlled falloff into shadow. Set in an underground parking garage ramp with curved concrete geometry. Cold, muted color palette with subtle grain and atmospheric haze. Mad-real surreal cinematic portrait, ARRI Alexa-style grading matched to the environment, emphasizing pores and skin texture. FG grain present  do according to my gender . aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

Copy and paste this prompt into your AI image generator. For best results, upload your reference photo when the tool allows it. Adjust 'me' to 'man' or 'woman' based on your subject's gender. The prompt will create a vertical portrait (9:16 ratio) with dramatic lighting and atmospheric effects.

Why This Prompt Works

This prompt uses specific cinematography terms (chiaroscuro, 35mm lens, ARRI Alexa) that AI models recognize as visual styles. Concrete details like 'underground parking garage ramp' and 'black ash-like particulate matter' give the AI clear elements to render. Lighting instructions ('strong directional key light') create consistent high-contrast results across different generators.

Prompt Variations

  • Change 'underground parking garage' to 'industrial warehouse' or 'abandoned subway tunnel'
  • Replace 'ash particles' with 'falling snow' or 'floating dust motes'
  • Try 'warm golden hour light' instead of 'cold muted palette'
  • Swap 'intense inward gaze' for 'direct eye contact with viewer'
  • Change clothing to 'light-colored linen' to alter contrast

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is chiaroscuro lighting in AI prompts?
    Chiaroscuro means strong contrast between light and dark areas. In AI prompts, it creates dramatic, moody portraits with deep shadows and bright highlights.
  • Which AI image generators work best with this prompt?
    This prompt works well with Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and DALL-E 3. All understand cinematography terms like 'ARRI Alexa-style' and '35mm lens.'
  • How do I get the ash particles to look realistic?
    Use the exact phrase 'black ash-like particulate matter drifts from deep shadow into the light.' AI models recognize this as floating atmospheric elements.
  • Can I use this prompt without a reference photo?
    Yes, but the 'me' reference works best with an uploaded image. Without one, change 'me' to 'a man' or 'a woman' for a generic portrait.
