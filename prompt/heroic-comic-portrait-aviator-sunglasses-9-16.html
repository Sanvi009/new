The Prompt 

Ultra-detailed digital illustration of me with exact same identity from the uploaded image wearing reflective aviator sunglasses, i dressed in a dark zip-up jacket with green shoulder accents over a deep red shirt. The perspective is slightly low-angle, giving me a heroic, cinematic presence. my expression is calm and self-assured, looking slightly upward. Art style is graphic novel / comic book realism with strong cross-hatching, inked linework, and textured brushstrokes. High contrast lighting with warm highlights on the skin and dramatic shadows. Sunglasses reflect a warm golden cityscape. Background features abstract textured color blocking with teal, orange, and red tones blended in a gritty, painterly style. Sharp details, bold outlines, dynamic shading, rich textures, dramatic lighting, 4K resolution, highly detailed illustration, modern comic art style, cinematic color grading. aspect ratio 9:16

How to Use This Prompt

1. Upload a clear front-facing photo of yourself. 2. Copy the full prompt into Midjourney, DALL·E, or Stable Diffusion. 3. Keep 'me' if your tool accepts image references, or replace with your name. 4. Set aspect ratio to 9:16 (--ar 9:16 in Midjourney). 5. Generate and upscale for 4K detail.

Why This Prompt Works

The prompt combines specific comic art techniques (cross-hatching, inked linework, textured brushstrokes) with cinematic color grading and a reflective element (cityscape in sunglasses) that AI models execute reliably. Low-angle composition signals 'heroic' intent. The teal/orange/red palette is proven to generate visually striking, professional comic art.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does 'reflective aviator sunglasses' add to the prompt?
    It creates a storytelling element. The AI generates a warm golden cityscape reflected in the lenses, which adds depth, mystery, and a cinematic feel without needing a complex background.
  • Do I need to describe the cityscape in more detail?
    No. The prompt says 'warm golden cityscape' which is enough for Midjourney and Stable Diffusion to generate skyline-like reflections. Over-specifying can confuse the AI.
  • Can I get this style in DALL·E 3?
    Yes, but DALL·E 3 tends to produce softer, less 'inked' results. For strong cross-hatching and bold outlines, Midjourney or Stable Diffusion with a comic LoRA works best.
  • What is 'textured color blocking'?
    It means large areas of flat color (teal, orange, red) applied with visible brush texture, not smooth gradients. This gives a gritty, painterly, modern comic book feel.
  • Why 9:16 aspect ratio for a comic portrait?
    9:16 is vertical, ideal for phone wallpapers, Instagram Stories, and vertical comic covers. It also emphasizes the heroic low-angle composition better than square.
