Prompt

Cinematic portrait of a young man inspired by a realistic version of Monkey D. Luffy, sitting in a heavily graffitied subway or train. He wears a straw hat with weathered texture and black messy hair peeking out beneath it. His face is slightly bruised and dirty, with blood stains and scratches, yet he shows a faint confident smirk, exuding toughness. He wears a blood-stained off-white t-shirt with bold black kanji characters on it, under a glossy red windbreaker jacket. His pants are dark, loose-fitting, also dirtied and stained with battle marks. He sits with legs spread, slouched against a metal subway wall, surrounded by gritty urban elements: scratched glass, graffiti tags, dim lighting with warm orange tones reflecting off metallic surfaces. His shoes are white high-top sneakers with red stripes, scuffed and worn. The lighting is dramatic, with sharp contrast and golden-hour tones illuminating his skin and clothes. The atmosphere is raw, urban, intense, with cinematic depth of field and ultra-detailed textures. Shot from a low angle to emphasize dominance and energy. Hyper-realistic, live-action anime adaptation style.
