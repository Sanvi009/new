Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does '64K resolution' mean in an AI prompt?
    64K is an extremely high resolution reference (far beyond current consumer displays). In AI prompts, it's used to instruct the generator to create images with maximum detail, sharpness, and texture clarity. It pushes the AI to render fabric weaves, skin pores, and stitching with ultra-fine precision.
  • Why are 'Octane render' and 'Unreal Engine 5' included?
    These are professional 3D rendering terms. Octane Render is known for photorealistic lighting and materials. Unreal Engine 5 is a game engine famous for its realistic graphics. Including them tells the AI to emulate the quality of high-end 3D renders, resulting in more believable lighting, shadows, and material textures.
  • How do I get the exact color blocking shown in the prompt?
    The prompt specifies exact colors: mustard yellow hoodie, dark green jacket, red bins, orange background, black pants, white sneakers. AI generators trained on fashion photography understand these color terms. For best results, use a model like Midjourney that has strong color accuracy and fashion understanding.
  • What is the difference between this and a regular portrait prompt?
    This prompt is designed for commercial fashion photography results. It includes specific styling (streetwear layering), prop placement (stacked bins), color theory (color blocking), and technical rendering terms (Octane, Unreal). It's optimized for creating images that look like they belong in a fashion campaign or editorial spread.
