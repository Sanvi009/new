Founder & Vision

Sanvi Al Evan

Founder & Lead Curator

As an AI enthusiast and developer, Sanvi founded Brainloom to solve a common problem: the time-consuming process of crafting effective AI prompts. With a background in machine learning and content creation, she recognized the need for a centralized, quality-vetted prompt library that could serve both beginners and experts in the AI space.

"Brainloom was born from the belief that the right prompt can transform how we interact with AI. Our goal is to democratize access to powerful AI tools by providing the linguistic keys that unlock their full potential."
