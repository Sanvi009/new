Our Mission

Our curated collection provides users with ready-to-use prompt templates that can be copied and used instantly with various AI image generation tools. Each prompt is carefully crafted to produce specific visual styles and effects.

Our Process

1. Idea Generation: We brainstorm creative prompt ideas for different visual styles and effects.

2. Testing & Refinement: Each prompt is tested with multiple AI models and refined until it produces optimal results.

3. Categorization: Prompts are organized by style, theme, and difficulty for easy navigation.

4. Quality Assurance: If a prompt doesn't give expected results, users can contact us for assistance and prompt adjustment.

Note: AI image generation results can vary between platforms and models. We continually test and update our prompts to ensure compatibility with popular AI image generators.

Founder & Vision

Sanvi Al Evan

Founder, Developer & Lead Curator

Dhaka, Bangladesh

I'm a Computer Science student currently in my 2nd year of bachelor's studies, with a passion for web development and artificial intelligence. Brainloom was launched in July 2025 as a personal project to solve a common problem I encountered: the time-consuming process of crafting effective AI image prompts.

As a web developer, I built Brainloom from scratch to create a centralized, quality-vetted prompt library that serves both beginners and experts in AI image generation. My background in CSE provides me with the technical understanding of how AI models interpret and process prompt instructions.

"Brainloom was born from the belief that the right prompt can transform how we create visual content with AI. Our goal is to democratize access to powerful AI image generation tools by providing the precise linguistic instructions that unlock their full creative potential."
